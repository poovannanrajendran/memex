Title: 5 Shows That Fix Your Money Mindset (Budget, Debt, Stocks)
URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ioaZc7Th0G8

Description (Fallback):
Save this list. If you watch these 5 series when you’re bored, you’ll start understanding money, investing, stock market, and business without “studying finance.”

Get Smart with Money (Netflix)
Budgeting, debt control, and practical steps to start investing.

Eat the Rich: The GameStop Saga (Netflix)
Shows how market moves can be driven by crowd emotion, hype, FOMO, and short squeezes.

Undercover Billionaire (Discovery+)
Real business survival lessons: sales, networking, negotiating, team building.

Inside Job (2010) (Prime Video / rent)
A simple breakdown of the 2008 financial crisis and how systems fail.

Money, Explained (Netflix)
Credit card traps, scams, retirement planning, gambling psychology—easy explanations.
