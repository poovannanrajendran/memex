## Overview
Project Digits is the codename for NVIDIA's new Mini PC designed for AI and machine learning tasks. It aims to offer high-end performance in a compact form factor.

## Role in this knowledge base
The new Mini PC announced by NVIDIA that houses the GB10 Grace Blackwell Superchip.

## Key facts
- Project Digits is designed to be a compact alternative to larger DGX systems for AI professionals.
