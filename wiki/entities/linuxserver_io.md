## Overview
LinuxServer.io provides pre-built Docker containers for a wide range of applications, simplifying their deployment and management. They focus on creating well-maintained and user-friendly container images.

## Role in this knowledge base
Provides many of the container images demonstrated in the video for applications like web browsers, Obsidian, and LibreOffice.

## Key facts
- They offer high-quality Docker images for numerous applications, often used in the video's examples.
