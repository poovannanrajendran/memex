## Overview
HeyGen is an AI-powered video generation platform that allows users to create professional-looking videos with AI avatars. It offers features for generating realistic avatars, customising voices, and producing videos for various business and personal use cases.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is mentioned as an AI avatar video app that creators can use to scale their brand by producing AI-generated content.

## Key facts
- It can be used with ElevenLabs to create an AI avatar.
