## Overview
Higgsfield is an entity offering AI skills and tools, particularly for video prompt generation. They are known for their 'Seed Dance 2.0' product and have developed a pack of 15 skills designed to work with Claude code.

## Role in this knowledge base
Provider of AI skills and tools that enhance prompt generation for video creation, working in conjunction with LLMs like Claude.

## Key facts
- Offers a 70% discount on unlimited access to Seed Dance 2.0.
