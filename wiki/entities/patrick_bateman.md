## Overview
Patrick Bateman is a fictional character, the protagonist of Bret Easton Ellis's 1991 novel "American Psycho" and its 2000 film adaptation. He is portrayed as a wealthy, young New York City investment banker who leads a double life as a serial killer.

## Role in this knowledge base
This character is frequently cited and used within 'sigma male' internet culture as an example of the archetype due to his perceived extreme self-reliance, ambition, and sociopathic tendencies.

## Key facts
- He is the main character in the novel and film 'American Psycho'.
