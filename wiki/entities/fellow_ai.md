## Overview
Fellow AI is a newly developed autonomous browser designed for deep research and task automation. It functions by understanding user intent, visiting websites, clicking buttons, collecting information, and completing tasks automatically.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is presented as a revolutionary AI tool that automates browsing tasks, aiming to significantly enhance user productivity, particularly for professionals involved in research and online tasks.

## Key facts
- It is described as the world's autonomous browser built for deep research and task automation.
