## Overview
The Time Variance Authority is a fictional organisation from Marvel Comics and the Marvel Cinematic Universe, responsible for monitoring and protecting the multiverse from deviations in the timeline.

## Role in this knowledge base
The organisation that apprehends Loki, judges him, and attempts to reset him, demonstrating their authority over time and individual actions.

## Key facts
- Magic powers are ineffective within the TVA's jurisdiction.
