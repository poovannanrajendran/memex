## Overview
Warren Buffett is a renowned American investor, businessman, and philanthropist, often referred to as the 'Oracle of Omaha.' He is the chairman and CEO of Berkshire Hathaway, a multinational conglomerate holding company.

## Role in this knowledge base
He is cited as an authority on financial principles, specifically regarding the wisdom of leasing depreciating assets like cars versus buying appreciating assets.

## Key facts
- Buffett's principle suggests leasing depreciating assets and buying appreciating assets.
