## Overview
Lever is an applicant tracking system (ATS) that offers a comprehensive suite of tools for recruiting and hiring. It focuses on providing a modern and integrated experience for both recruiters and candidates.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is identified as one of the platforms on which the job application tool is effective.

## Key facts
- The job application tool is compatible with job postings on the Lever platform.
