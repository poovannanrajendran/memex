## Overview
The HyperDrive 6-in-1 USB-C Hub is a portable docking station for iPads and other USB-C devices. It provides essential ports like headphone jack, HDMI, and SD card slots.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended as a portable and effective dock for connecting multiple peripherals to an iPad, offering essential ports in a compact form factor.

## Key facts
- Includes a 3.5mm headphone jack, HDMI output, and SSD card slots.
