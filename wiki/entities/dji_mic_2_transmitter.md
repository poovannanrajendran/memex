## Overview
The DJI Mic 2 Transmitter is a wireless microphone transmitter that can connect to DJI action cameras via Bluetooth, offering high-quality audio recording.

## Role in this knowledge base
An accessory that enhances the audio capabilities of compatible DJI cameras, especially the Action 5 Pro.

## Key facts
- The DJI Osmo Action 5 Pro can connect to two DJI Mic 2 transmitters simultaneously.
