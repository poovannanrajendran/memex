## Overview
Raajkamal Films International is an Indian film production company founded by veteran actor Kamal Haasan. The company has produced several critically acclaimed and commercially successful films in Tamil cinema.

## Role in this knowledge base
Co-producer of the film 'Amaran'.

## Key facts
- It has produced films like 'Chachi 420', 'Hey Ram', and 'Vishwaroopam'.
