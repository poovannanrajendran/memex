## Overview
MediaTek is a Taiwanese fabless semiconductor company that provides systems-on-a-chip (SoCs) for wireless communications, smart TVs, and other electronic devices. They are known for their mobile chipsets.

## Role in this knowledge base
Collaborated with NVIDIA on the design and integration of the Project Digits Mini PC.

## Key facts
- MediaTek collaborated with NVIDIA on the design of the Project Digits Mini PC, contributing to its interconnects and overall assembly.
