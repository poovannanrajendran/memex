## Overview
AirServer is software that allows devices to mirror their screen content wirelessly to another device, typically a computer. It is commonly used for presentations, tutorials, and screen sharing.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the tools used for screen capture to record the iPad's display.

## Key facts
- It facilitates wireless screen mirroring between different platforms.
