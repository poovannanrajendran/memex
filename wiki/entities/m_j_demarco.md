## Overview
M.J. DeMarco is an author and entrepreneur known for his critiques of traditional financial advice and advocacy for wealth creation outside conventional employment. His work focuses on escaping the 'rat race' through entrepreneurial ventures.

## Role in this knowledge base
Author of 'Unscripted', offering advice on breaking free from conventional employment and achieving financial independence.

## Key facts
- Coined the term 'CENTS' (Control, Education, Network, Time, Scale) as a framework for building wealth in his book 'Unscripted'.
