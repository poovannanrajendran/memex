## Overview
Cillian Murphy is an Irish actor acclaimed for his roles in films and television, notably playing Thomas Shelby in "Peaky Blinders." He is known for his distinctive performances and ability to convey complex characters.

## Role in this knowledge base
He is mentioned as the actor who portrayed Thomas Shelby, a character often linked to the 'sigma male' archetype.
