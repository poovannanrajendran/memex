## Overview
Ruspolia nitidula, the Large Conehead, is a species of bush cricket found in parts of Europe and Asia. It is known for its distinctive conical head and its ability to blend in with its surroundings.

## Role in this knowledge base
The subject of the video and a rare find in Ukraine.

## Key facts
- It is a type of bush cricket belonging to the family Tettigoniidae.
