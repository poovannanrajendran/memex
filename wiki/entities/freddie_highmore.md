## Overview
Freddie Highmore is an English actor, best known for his roles as Dr. Shaun Murphy in the medical drama series 'The Good Doctor' and Norman Bates in the drama-thriller series 'Bates Motel'. He has also received acclaim for his earlier work in films like 'Finding Neverland' and 'Charlie and the Chocolate Factory'.
