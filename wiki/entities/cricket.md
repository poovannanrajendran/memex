## Overview
Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field at the centre of which is a 22-yard (20-metre) pitch. It is a sport that requires significant skill, strategy, and physical prowess.

## Role in this knowledge base
The sport in which the backfoot punch shot is a key technique.

## Key facts
- The game involves batting, bowling, and fielding elements.
