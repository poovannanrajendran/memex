## Overview
The Crucial X9 Pro is a compact and high-performance external SSD. It is noted for its small size, making it easily mountable.

## Role in this knowledge base
Presented as an alternative compact external SSD solution for the iPad, which the presenter has modified with a phone mount.

## Key facts
- Its small size allows for creative mounting solutions, like magnetically attaching it to the iPad.
