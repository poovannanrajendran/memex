## Overview
The Toyota Camry is a mid-size sedan manufactured by Toyota, known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and reputation for reliability. It is a popular choice for families and commuters.

## Role in this knowledge base
Included as an example of a slightly more premium, yet still affordable and dependable, car option for outright purchase.

## Key facts
- It is suggested for those who want a reliable car that is still economical.
