## Overview
MacBook trackpads are the integrated pointing devices on Apple's laptop computers, known for their multi-touch gestures and haptic feedback.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as an analogy to describe the multi-stage press functionality of the iPhone 16's Camera Control Button.

## Key facts
- The Camera Control Button's two-stage press is likened to the functionality of MacBook trackpads.
