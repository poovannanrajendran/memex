## Overview
A productivity application for macOS that acts as a search utility and workflow automation tool, replacing Spotlight with enhanced features.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a powerful application for optimising workflows, primarily used for launching applications and performing quick searches via keyboard commands.

## Key facts
