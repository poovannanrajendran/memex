## Overview
A.R. Rahman is an acclaimed Indian composer, singer, music producer, and philanthropist. He is best known for his work in Indian cinema, particularly Tamil and Hindi films, and has also composed for international projects.

## Role in this knowledge base
A.R. Rahman has composed the music for the film 'Raayan'.

## Key facts
- He is credited with the music direction for 'Raayan'.
