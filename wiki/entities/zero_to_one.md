## Overview
'Zero to One: Notes on Startups, or How to Build the Future' is a book by Peter Thiel and Blake Masters. It explores the importance of monopolies and creating new things, rather than competing in existing markets.

## Role in this knowledge base
This book is highly recommended by Ritesh Agarwal for entrepreneurs.

## Key facts
- The book argues that every moment in the present is a moment of singularity.
