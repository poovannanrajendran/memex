## Overview
A Kunai is a Japanese gardening trowel that was also used as a multi-purpose tool and sometimes as a weapon by shinobi. The 'Batman Kunai' is likely a fictionalised or themed version of this tool, inspired by the character Batman.

## Role in this knowledge base
A specific, notable item within the knife collection being showcased.

## Key facts
- While historically a tool, the Kunai has been adopted into popular culture as a weapon, particularly within ninja lore.
