## Overview
The Intel Xeon Gold 6138 is a server processor from the Skylake-SP generation, offering 20 cores and 40 threads per CPU. It is designed for demanding enterprise workloads and high-performance computing.

## Role in this knowledge base
The CPUs chosen for the dual-socket Dell Precision 7820 build, providing a total of 40 cores and 80 threads.

## Key facts
- The build uses two Intel Xeon Gold 6138 CPUs, each with 20 cores and 40 threads, for a total of 40 cores and 80 threads.
