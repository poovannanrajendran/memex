## Overview
The World Economic Forum is an international organisation for public-private cooperation. It brings together politicians, business leaders, and other stakeholders to discuss and address global issues.

## Role in this knowledge base
Cited as a source for data and projections regarding job market changes due to AI.

## Key facts
- It projects that 92 million jobs will be lost by 2030 due to AI.
