## Overview
Uttam Singh is a respected Indian music composer and music director, particularly known for his work in Hindi cinema. He has composed music for many successful films, often collaborating with directors like Yash Chopra.

## Role in this knowledge base
He composed the music for the film 'Dil To Pagal Hai', including the song 'Le Gayi'.

## Key facts
- He is known for his contributions to Bollywood music soundtracks.
