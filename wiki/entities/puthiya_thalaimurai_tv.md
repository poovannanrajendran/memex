## Overview
Puthiya Thalaimurai TV is a 24/7 Tamil live news channel launched on August 24, 2011. It is known for its independent editorial stance and aims to provide unbiased and accurate information to the common man, covering news on politics, sports, business, and international affairs.

## Role in this knowledge base
The news source providing the content about the 'ordinary person who triumphed'.

## Key facts
- It became popular within days of its launch due to its independent editorial stance.
