## Overview
Kazuho Monster is a Japanese dancer and model born on November 17, 1994. He stands at 182cm and weighs 67kg. He is known for his diverse dance styles, including moonwalk, animation dance, wave, and pantomime.

## Role in this knowledge base
The subject of the YouTube video, showcasing his dance talent and professional background.

## Key facts
- He is the world champion of the moonwalk.
