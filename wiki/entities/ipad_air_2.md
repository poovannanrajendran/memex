## Overview
The iPad Air 2 is a tablet computer developed and marketed by Apple. It is known for its slim design, powerful performance for its time, and high-resolution Retina display, making it suitable for various applications including drone control.

## Role in this knowledge base
The device used to control the drone and view the Litchi mission interface during the tutorial.

## Key facts
- It offered a good balance of portability and screen real estate for mobile applications.
