## Overview
KBRA (Kroll Bond Rating Agency) is a full-service global rating agency that provides transparent and independent ratings. They offer insights into credit risk.

## Role in this knowledge base
KBRA contributes to the independent assessment of Lloyd's financial strength, supporting the market's reputation for security and stability.

## Key facts
- Lloyd's has a financial strength rating of AA- (Very Strong) with a stable outlook from KBRA.
