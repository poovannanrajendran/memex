## Overview
A revered and powerful warrior-statesman in the Mahabharata, son of Shantanu and Ganga, known for his formidable vow of celibacy and unwavering loyalty to the Kuru throne.

## Role in this knowledge base
His birth is a key event in the Kuru dynasty's ancestry to be explored in Season 2.

## Key facts
- His birth and life choices are integral to the unfolding drama and destiny of the Kuru kingdom.
