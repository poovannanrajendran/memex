## Overview
Hepi is a Greek restaurant located in London, highly acclaimed for its food and atmosphere. It features a central grill custom-built in 1966 and is known for its popular dishes, particularly the milkfed lamb.

## Role in this knowledge base
The subject of the review, presented as a top contender for the best Greek restaurant in London.

## Key facts
- The restaurant's grill was custom-built by hand in 1966 when the restaurant first opened.
