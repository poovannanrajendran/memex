## Overview
Schnitle Forever is a food vendor that prepares schnitzels, offering a £3 takeaway deal through the Neotaste app. Their schnitzels are hand-bashed to order and can be pork or chicken.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is featured for its affordable schnitzel deal, including free garlic butter and lemon.

## Key facts
- Each schnitzel is hand-bashed to order.
