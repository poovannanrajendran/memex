## Overview
A note-taking application developed by Apple Inc. for its operating systems, allowing users to create and organise text, images, and sketches.

## Role in this knowledge base
Utilised for taking notes during reading and research, forming part of Apple's integrated productivity suite.

## Key facts
- It is part of Apple's productivity suite which works great offline and syncs nicely between devices.
