## Overview
Scribe is a browser extension and desktop application that automates the creation of process documentation. It records user actions, generates step-by-step guides with screenshots and text, and allows for easy sharing of these guides.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary tool discussed in the video for enhancing workplace productivity by streamlining the documentation of processes and reducing repetitive explanations.

## Key facts
- Scribe automatically generates guides with screenshots and text documentation by simply performing the steps of a process.
