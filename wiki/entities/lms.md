## Overview
A Learning Management System (LMS) is a software application for the administration, documentation, tracking, reporting, and delivery of educational courses or training programs.

## Role in this knowledge base
The type of platform the AI tool aims to enhance or replace with gamified course creation capabilities.

## Key facts
- LMS platforms are essential for organisations managing large-scale training and education.
