## Overview
Hiphop Tamizha is a musical duo consisting of Adhithya Ramachandran (Adhi) and Jeeva R. They are known for composing music, singing, and rapping, primarily in Tamil films.

## Role in this knowledge base
The music composer and singer of the song "Achacho".

## Key facts
- They are recognized for blending hip-hop elements with traditional Tamil music.
