## Overview
Lakshmi is the Hindu goddess of wealth, prosperity, fortune, and beauty. She is the consort of Lord Vishnu and is worshipped for blessings of material and spiritual wealth.

## Role in this knowledge base
Deity invoked during religious chants and prayers, particularly associated with blessings for the couple.

## Key facts
- The Sri Maha Lakshmi Ashtottara is recited, invoking her blessings for prosperity and well-being.
