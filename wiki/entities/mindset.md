## Overview
'Mindset: The New Psychology of Success' is a book by Carol S. Dweck that explores the power of our beliefs.

## Role in this knowledge base
A recommended resource for understanding how mindsets influence personal growth and achievement.

## Key facts
- The book differentiates between fixed and growth mindsets.
