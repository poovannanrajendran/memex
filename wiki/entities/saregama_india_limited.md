## Overview
Saregama India Limited, formerly The Gramophone Company of India, is one of the oldest music labels in India. It holds the rights to a vast catalogue of Indian music across various genres and languages.

## Role in this knowledge base
Label for the song 'Hey Minnale' and the film 'Amaran'.

## Key facts
- It is part of the RPSG Group.
