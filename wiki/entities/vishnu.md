## Overview
Vishnu is one of the principal deities of Hinduism, known as the preserver or protector of the universe. He is part of the Trimurti along with Brahma and Shiva. He is often depicted with a conch, discus, mace, and lotus.

## Role in this knowledge base
Deity invoked during religious chants and prayers, particularly in conjunction with Lakshmi.

## Key facts
- He is invoked as Narayana, a prominent form of Vishnu, and as the consort of Lakshmi.
