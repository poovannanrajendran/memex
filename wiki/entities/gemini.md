## Overview
A family of multimodal large language models developed by Google AI, designed for advanced reasoning and understanding across different data types.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the Large Language Models (LLMs) explicitly supported by the CrewAI framework for building AI agents.

## Key facts
- Gemini is a compatible LLM for AI agents built with CrewAI.
