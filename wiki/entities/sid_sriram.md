## Overview
Sid Sriram is an Indian-American singer, composer, and record producer who has gained significant popularity for his work in South Indian film music, particularly in Tamil and Telugu.

## Role in this knowledge base
Singer of the 'Sudari Sudari' song featured in the status clip.
