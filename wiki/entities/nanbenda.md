## Overview
'Nanbenda' is a 2015 Tamil comedy film directed by Jagadish. The film stars Udhayanidhi Stalin, Nayanthara, and Santhanam in lead roles, with Arya and Andrea Jeremiah in supporting roles. It was produced by Udhayanidhi Stalin under Red Giant Movies.

## Role in this knowledge base

## Key facts
