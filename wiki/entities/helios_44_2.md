## Overview
The Helios 44-2 is a vintage 58mm F2 lens, originally manufactured in the Soviet Union during the 1950s for film cameras. It is known for its distinctive optical characteristics and has gained a cult following among filmmakers and photographers.

## Role in this knowledge base
The specific lens used to film Dune 2 and tested in the video, demonstrating its capabilities despite its low cost and age.

## Key facts
- It is a 58mm F2 full-frame lens originally from the Soviet era.
