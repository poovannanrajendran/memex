## Overview
Charlie Munger is an American investor, businessman, and philanthropist, best known as the vice-chairman of Berkshire Hathaway and Warren Buffett's longtime business partner.

## Role in this knowledge base
His philosophy on compounding and the importance of not interrupting it is quoted to emphasize the value of focus and long-term commitment in achieving business success.

## Key facts
- He advocates for buying a company and being willing to have the stock market closed for years, embodying the principle of not interrupting compounding.
