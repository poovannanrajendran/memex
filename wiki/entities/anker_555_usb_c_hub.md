## Overview
The Anker 555 USB-C Hub is a multi-port adapter that expands the connectivity options of USB-C enabled devices. It typically includes ports such as HDMI, USB-A, USB-C, and SD card readers.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended for its comprehensive port selection and performance, enabling connection to external displays and peripherals.

## Key facts
- It features a 10 Gbps USB-C port and two 10 Gbps USB-A ports.
