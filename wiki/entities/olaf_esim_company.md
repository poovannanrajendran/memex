## Overview
Olaf is a company that provides travel eSIMs, allowing users to connect to mobile data networks in various countries without needing a physical SIM card. They focus on convenience and reliable data access for travelers.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended for travel eSIMs prioritizing convenience, customer service, and unlimited data plans in many destinations.

## Key facts
- Olaf offers unlimited data plans in many different destinations, tested even in remote locations like the Sahara Desert.
