## Overview
The five recognised sons of Pandu: Yudhishthira, Bhima, Arjuna, Nakula, and Sahadeva, who are the central protagonists of the Mahabharata.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary group of protagonists whose individual stories are highlighted as foundational to the epic.

## Key facts
- Each Pandava brother brings a unique dimension to the saga, setting the tone for future challenges.
