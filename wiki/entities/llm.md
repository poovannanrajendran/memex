## Overview
Large Language Models are artificial intelligence models trained on vast amounts of text data, capable of understanding and generating human-like text. They are used in various applications, including content creation and AI research.

## Role in this knowledge base
Running LLMs locally was tested as a demanding task on the M4 Mac Mini, assessing its capability and limitations in AI workloads.
