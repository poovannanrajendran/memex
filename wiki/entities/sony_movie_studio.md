## Overview
Sony Movie Studio is a consumer-level video editing software. It allows users to edit video footage, add effects, music, and titles to create professional-looking videos.

## Role in this knowledge base
The software used to compile and edit the footage for the tutorial video.

## Key facts
- It is designed for hobbyists and semi-professionals looking for robust video editing features.
