## Overview
ICANN Lookup (also known as WHOIS) is a public database that stores information about domain name registrants, including their name, address, email, and phone number. This information is publicly accessible unless privacy protection is enabled.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as the public database where domain owner information is listed, making privacy protection a necessary service for individuals who do not want their personal details exposed.

## Key facts
- The ICANN Lookup database can expose a domain owner's name, phone number, email, and physical address if privacy protection is not used.
