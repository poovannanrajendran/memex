## Overview
Sivagami is a name associated with the consort of Lord Shiva, typically Parvati or a manifestation of the Divine Feminine. The name itself combines 'Siva' and 'gami' (wife).

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned in the song lyrics, possibly referring to a form of the Divine Feminine or a consort of Shiva.

## Key facts
- The lyrics state that Sivagami is a 'part' of Shiva, implying a divine union or essential connection.
