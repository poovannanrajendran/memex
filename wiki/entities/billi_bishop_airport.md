## Overview
Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport is a small airport located on the Toronto Islands, serving the city of Toronto. It handles domestic flights and is accessible by ferry or tunnel.

## Role in this knowledge base
The airport from which a plane takes off, shown in the vlog.
