## Overview
DJI is a technology company known for its development and manufacturing of camera drones and other aerial photography equipment. They are a leading brand in the consumer and professional drone market.

## Role in this knowledge base
The DJI Mini 2 is the drone model featured and recommended for the tutorial.

## Key facts
- DJI holds a significant market share in the global consumer drone industry.
