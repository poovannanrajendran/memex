## Overview
A royal guru and master of advanced military arts in the Mahabharata, who taught both the Pandavas and Kauravas.

## Role in this knowledge base
He conducted a test that revealed Yudhishthira's honesty.

## Key facts
- He used a simple test with seeds to highlight Yudhishthira's spirit of truth.
