## Overview
G V Prakash Kumar is an Indian music composer, film producer, and actor who primarily works in Tamil cinema. He is the nephew of renowned music director A. R. Rahman and has composed music for numerous successful films.

## Role in this knowledge base
Music composer for the film 'Amaran' and the song 'Hey Minnale'.

## Key facts
- He made his acting debut in the film 'Penta Doctor' (2016).
