## Overview
Steve Jobs was a co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc. He is renowned for his visionary leadership and his ability to build teams that created groundbreaking products like the iPhone.

## Role in this knowledge base
Cited as an example of leadership focused on building a team capable of execution, rather than solely on individual technical skill, highlighting the importance of hiring and management.

## Key facts
- He knew how to build the team that could build the iPhone.
