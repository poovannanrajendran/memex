## Overview
The courts in Scotland that deal with minor criminal offences. They are presided over by Justices of the Peace.

## Role in this knowledge base
Identified as the Scottish equivalent of the Magistrates' Court for handling minor criminal cases, a detail relevant to the Life in the UK test's section on courts.

## Key facts
- Deals with minor criminal offences in Scotland.
