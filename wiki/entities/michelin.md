## Overview
Michelin is a French tyre manufacturing company and a highly respected publisher of restaurant and hotel guides. Their restaurant guides award stars to restaurants based on the quality of their food and dining experience.

## Role in this knowledge base
The restaurant's perpetual soup has earned it recognition from Michelin, specifically two Michelin plaques.

## Key facts
- Michelin awards plaques to restaurants that are highly regarded for their culinary offerings.
