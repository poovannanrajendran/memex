## Overview
The Nvidia GeForce RTX 4080 is a high-end consumer graphics card based on the Ada Lovelace architecture. It offers significant performance improvements for gaming and AI workloads.

## Role in this knowledge base
This GPU was tested on a Threadripper system to evaluate its capability in running local LLMs, demonstrating strong performance when properly configured with WSL2.

## Key facts
- It used 16GB of host memory and showed spikes of up to 100% GPU usage, averaging around 75% during LLM inference.
