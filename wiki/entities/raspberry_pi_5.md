## Overview
The Raspberry Pi 5 is the latest iteration of the popular single-board computer, offering significantly improved performance over its predecessors. It features a faster processor, more RAM options, and enhanced I/O capabilities.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary focus of the video, used as the core component for the demonstrated projects.

## Key facts
- The Raspberry Pi 5 starts at $60 and offers performance comparable to a basic cheap PC in a tiny form factor with extensive maker-friendly expansion options.
