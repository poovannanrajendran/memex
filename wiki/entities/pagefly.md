## Overview
PageFly is a popular page builder for Shopify that enables users to create custom landing pages and enhance their website's design without requiring coding knowledge.

## Role in this knowledge base
PageFly is recommended as a key tool for creating visually appealing and highly branded e-commerce websites, significantly improving user experience and conversion rates.

## Key facts
- It allows for the creation of fully custom landing pages that are outside the standard Shopify theme.
