## Overview
The DJI Mini 3 Pro is a compact and lightweight drone designed for aerial photography and videography. It offers advanced features such as obstacle sensing and a high-quality camera capable of shooting in 4K.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the specific drone models used to film the Leeds footage.

## Key facts
- It weighs less than 249 grams, avoiding certain registration requirements in many countries.
