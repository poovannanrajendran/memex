## Overview
The MacBook Pro 16, particularly upgraded configurations with an M-series Max chip, offers powerful GPUs, high memory bandwidth, and up to 128GB of unified memory. It is a premium, well-built laptop with excellent battery life and features.

## Role in this knowledge base
Highly recommended for demanding ML and AI model training due to its powerful integrated GPU, substantial unified memory, and overall performance, despite the lack of CUDA support and higher cost.

## Key facts
- Upgraded configurations with an M-series Max chip can combine a powerful GPU with up to 128GB of fast unified memory, ideal for ML/AI model training.
