## Overview
An open-source home automation platform that allows users to control and automate smart home devices. It emphasizes local control and privacy, offering extensive customization options.

## Role in this knowledge base
Discussed as a platform that can be integrated with LLMs for advanced home automation and as a central hub for local smart home devices, avoiding cloud dependencies.

## Key facts
- It can be integrated with LLMs via APIs, enabling voice control and complex automations.
