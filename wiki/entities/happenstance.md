## Overview
Happenstance is a digital platform that consolidates professional data such as LinkedIn connections, GitHub repositories, and educational history to create a more integrated and effective networking experience. It is designed for both individuals seeking to build connections and recruiters looking for suitable candidates.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary subject of the YouTube video, presented as a solution for simplifying and enhancing professional networking.

## Key facts
- It aims to make networking interactive and engaging through gamified elements.
