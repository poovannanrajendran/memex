## Overview
Augment is described as a general AI personal assistant that fits in your pocket. It can handle tasks like scheduling meetings, summarizing content, booking travel, and creating lists.

## Role in this knowledge base
Acts as a virtual personal assistant, adapting to user workflows and personal preferences to enhance productivity.

## Key facts
- It adapts to your workflow across all apps and learns from your personal content, preferences, and history.
