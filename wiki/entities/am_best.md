## Overview
AM Best is a global credit rating agency that provides financial strength ratings for insurance companies. Their ratings are crucial for market confidence.

## Role in this knowledge base
AM Best is one of the rating agencies that affirm Lloyd's financial strength, providing an independent assessment of its ability to meet policyholder obligations.

## Key facts
- Lloyd's maintains a financial strength rating of A+ (Superior) with a stable outlook from AM Best.
