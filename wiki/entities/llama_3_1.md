## Overview
Llama 3.1 is a family of large language models developed by Meta AI. It comes in various sizes, offering different performance and capability trade-offs.

## Role in this knowledge base
Llama 3.1 (specifically the 70 billion parameter version for most tests) was the primary LLM tested for its performance on different hardware configurations.

## Key facts
- The 70 billion parameter version is approximately 5 GB in size for download.
