## Overview
Hoyixi is a brand that produces laptop accessories, including sleeves and cases. They focus on functional designs at an affordable price point.

## Role in this knowledge base
Manufacturer of the laptop sleeve recommended for protecting the MacBook Air and doubling as a stand.

## Key facts
- The Hoyixi laptop sleeve features a foldable design that allows it to prop up the MacBook.
