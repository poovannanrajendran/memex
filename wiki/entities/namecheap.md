## Overview
Namecheap is a domain registrar known for its competitive pricing and inclusion of free Whois privacy protection. However, the video notes that renewal prices can be higher than advertised introductory rates.

## Role in this knowledge base
Presented as a better option than the previously mentioned registrars, offering free privacy protection, but still has renewal prices that are not as competitive as the top recommendations.

## Key facts
- A .com domain at Namecheap can be registered for $5.98, but the renewal price is $15.88.
