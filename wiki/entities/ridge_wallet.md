## Overview
The Ridge Wallet is a minimalist wallet designed for everyday carry, known for its durable metal construction, RFID-blocking technology, and compact size.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a product that prioritises small pockets and user convenience, drawing a parallel to the desire for well-designed, non-intrusive phone features.

## Key facts
- The Ridge Wallet can fit up to 12 cards and comes with RFID protection and a lifetime warranty.
