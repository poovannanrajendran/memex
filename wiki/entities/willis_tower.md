## Overview
A 108-story skyscraper in Chicago, Illinois. Designed by the architectural firm Skidmore, Owings & Merrill, it was completed in 1973 and was the world's tallest building for nearly 25 years.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as an example of a more modern, lighter skyscraper construction.

## Key facts
- It was formerly known as the Sears Tower.
