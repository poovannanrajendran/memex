## Overview
Timothy Ferriss is an American author, entrepreneur, and lifestyle guru. He is best known for his book 'The 4-Hour Work Week', which promotes a lifestyle of location independence and reduced working hours through automation and outsourcing.

## Role in this knowledge base
Author of 'The 4-Hour Work Week', providing a framework for passive income and lifestyle design.

## Key facts
- His book 'The 4-Hour Work Week' advocates for the 'muse' concept, where individuals create automated income streams.
