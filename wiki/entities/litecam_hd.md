## Overview
LiteCam HD is a screen recording software that allows users to capture video of their computer screen. It is often used for creating tutorials, gameplay videos, and software demonstrations.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the tools used for screen capture to record the iPad's display.

## Key facts
- It provides high-definition recording capabilities for screen content.
