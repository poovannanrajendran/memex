## Overview
Dream Machine is an AI video generation tool developed by Luma Labs. It allows users to create videos from text prompts, images, or a combination of both, with advanced features for animating transitions.

## Role in this knowledge base
A powerful AI video generation tool that is identified as the best option for image-to-video conversions.

## Key facts
- Dream Machine's image-to-video capabilities are highlighted, particularly its ability to generate compelling transformations between uploaded start and end frames.
