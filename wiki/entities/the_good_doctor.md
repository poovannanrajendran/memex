## Overview
'The Good Doctor' is an American medical drama television series based on a South Korean series of the same name. The show centers on Dr. Shaun Murphy, a young surgical resident with autism and savant syndrome at the fictional San Jose St. Bonaventure Hospital.

## Role in this knowledge base
Freddie Highmore plays the lead character in this series, and his experience on the show is the subject of the transcript.

## Key facts
- The series has been running for multiple seasons, allowing Highmore to play a surgeon for an extended period.
