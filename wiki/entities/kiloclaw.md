## Overview
Kiloclaw is presented as a hosted, 24/7 version of OpenClaw that simplifies usage by eliminating the need for setup. It is the platform enabling free access to certain models.

## Role in this knowledge base
The tool that offers a free way to use OpenClaw models.

## Key facts
- Kiloclaw offers the Minimax M2.5 model completely free, with no credits required.
