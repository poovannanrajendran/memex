## Overview
Pimsleur is a language learning method that focuses on listening and speaking skills through audio lessons. It emphasizes pronunciation and practical conversational phrases, aiming to enable users to communicate effectively from the start.

## Role in this knowledge base
An alternative to Duolingo for language learning, focusing on practical conversational skills for travelers through listening and repetition.

## Key facts
- Its main lessons are purely listening and repeating, training the user's ears and pronunciation for conversation.
