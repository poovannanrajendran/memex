## Overview
Patreon is a membership platform that allows creators to run a subscription content service. It allows artists and creators to earn a monthly recurring income directly from their most engaged fans.

## Role in this knowledge base
Platform used by the creator to offer exclusive content and early access to photos.

## Key facts
- It functions as a subscription service for creators and their fans.
