## Overview
Morgan is the narrator of the story and the protagonist. She is Rachel's sister and the aunt of Jake and Emma.

## Role in this knowledge base
Morgan is the intended recipient of her sister's children, but due to a mistaken address provided by Rachel, the children were dropped off at the wrong location. She is also a key witness in the custody hearing.

## Key facts
- She initially refused to babysit her niece and nephew due to a work conference but ultimately stepped in after they were found abandoned.
