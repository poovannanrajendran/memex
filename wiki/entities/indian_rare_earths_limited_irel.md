## Overview
Indian Rare Earths Limited is a Government of India undertaking. It is involved in mining and processing of rare earth minerals and atomic minerals.

## Role in this knowledge base
The organisation undertaking the nuclear mining project in Kanyakumari, extracting atomic minerals.

## Key facts
- IREL is involved in mining and processing of rare earth and atomic minerals.
