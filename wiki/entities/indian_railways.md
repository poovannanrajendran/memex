## Overview
Indian Railways is a state-owned enterprise of the Government of India that operates the nation's transportation network as a statutory body under the Ministry of Railways. It is the world's eighth-largest employer, and the fifth-largest railway network. The organisation is responsible for the operation of all rail transport in India.
