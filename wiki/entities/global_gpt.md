## Overview
Global GPT is a platform that allows users to access and utilize multiple Large Language Models (LLMs) through a single interface, often with cost-saving benefits. It's positioned as a tool for enhancing project planning.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended for project planning, enabling the use of diverse LLMs to generate comprehensive initial plans and strategies.

## Key facts
- It offers affordable access to various LLMs and includes integrated tools like Deep Research and Perplexity.
