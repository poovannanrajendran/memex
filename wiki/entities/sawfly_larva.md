## Overview
A young, immature form of a Sawfly, which is a type of insect. These larvae are often caterpillar-like in appearance and are known for their role in plant ecosystems.

## Role in this knowledge base
The subject of the macro photography presented in the video.

## Key facts
- Sawfly larvae are distinct from butterfly or moth caterpillars as they typically have more prolegs.
