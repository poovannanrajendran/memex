## Overview
Google AI Studio is a web-based tool that allows users to quickly prototype and build AI-powered applications. It offers features for real-time interaction, including the ability for the AI to analyse what is on a user's screen and provide guidance.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is presented as a versatile tool for learning and receiving interactive assistance, particularly for tasks involving visual screen analysis.

## Key facts
- It can look at what is on your screen and tell you what to do.
