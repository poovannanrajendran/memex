## Overview
A legendary ancient Indian royal lineage that is central to the Mahabharata epic, whose internal conflicts form the primary narrative.

## Role in this knowledge base

## Key facts
- The ancestry of the Kuru dynasty, from Shantanu to Bhishma, forms the bedrock of the Mahabharata's world.
