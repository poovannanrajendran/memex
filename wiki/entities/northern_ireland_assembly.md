## Overview
The legislative assembly for Northern Ireland, responsible for devolved matters within the region. It consists of elected members who make laws for Northern Ireland.

## Role in this knowledge base
The number of members in the Northern Ireland Assembly is a specific piece of factual information tested in the Life in the UK exam, relating to devolved administrations.

## Key facts
- The Northern Ireland Assembly has 90 members.
