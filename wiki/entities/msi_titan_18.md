## Overview
The MSI Titan 18 is a large 18-inch laptop designed for extreme performance, featuring four memory slots that allow for up to 128GB of system memory. However, its size and price make it comparable to a desktop.

## Role in this knowledge base
Represents the upper limit of laptop performance for ML/AI, though its sheer size and cost make it less practical than a desktop for most users requiring such extreme specifications.

## Key facts
- It has four slots for system memory, allowing configurations of up to 128GB.
