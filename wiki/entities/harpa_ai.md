## Overview
Harpa AI is a Chrome extension that acts as a helpful AI assistant, particularly for replying to emails. It allows users to choose the sentiment and tone of email responses and can learn the user's writing style.

## Role in this knowledge base
Streamlines email communication by automating responses, offering customization of tone and style, and integrating with the Arc browser.

## Key facts
- Replying to emails with Harpa AI takes as little as two clicks.
