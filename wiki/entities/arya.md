## Overview
Arya is a popular Tamil film actor who has appeared in numerous commercially successful films. He is known for his roles in films like 'Arinthum Ariyamalum', 'Boss Engira Bhaskaran', and 'Engaeyum Eppothum'.

## Role in this knowledge base
