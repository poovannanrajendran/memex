## Overview
Mark is the father of Jake and Emma and Rachel's partner or ex-partner. He is the one who initiates the custody proceedings.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mark is the other parent seeking custody of the children, acting to protect them from Rachel's negligence. He is a key figure in the custody hearing and expresses gratitude to Morgan.

## Key facts
- He filed for emergency custody of the children and was later awarded permanent custody.
