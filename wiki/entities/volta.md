## Overview
Volta is a company that manufactures portable power solutions, including high-capacity power banks designed to charge laptops and other devices on the go.

## Role in this knowledge base
Manufacturer of the portable charger recommended for extending battery life away from power outlets.

## Key facts
- The Volta portable charger has a large battery capacity, capable of charging a laptop multiple times.
