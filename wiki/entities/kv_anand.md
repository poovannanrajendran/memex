## Overview
KV Anand was a distinguished Indian cinematographer and film director who primarily worked in Tamil cinema. He was widely respected for his visual storytelling and contributions to several critically acclaimed and commercially successful films. Before transitioning to direction, he had a successful career as a cinematographer, earning accolades for his work.
