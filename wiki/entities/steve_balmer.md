## Overview
Steve Balmer served as the CEO of Microsoft after Bill Gates. He was a key early executive at the company.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as a counter-example to Bill Gates, illustrating that maintaining focus and not liquidating shares (staying invested in one core asset) can lead to comparable or even greater wealth accumulation over time.

## Key facts
- He is almost as wealthy as Bill Gates today because he stayed focused and never liquidated his shares in Microsoft.
