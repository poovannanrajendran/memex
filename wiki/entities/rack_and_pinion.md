## Overview
A rack and pinion system consists of a toothed gear (pinion) meshing with a linear toothed bar (rack). It converts rotational motion into linear motion or vice versa.

## Role in this knowledge base
Identified as a specific mechanism for converting motion types, with a common application in vehicle steering.

## Key facts
- Converts rotational motion to linear motion and is commonly used in steering systems.
