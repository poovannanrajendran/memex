## Overview
LTX 2.3 is an advanced, production-level AI video generation model that can be run locally on personal computing devices. It is presented as an open-source solution developed based on user feedback.

## Role in this knowledge base
This product is the central focus of the analysis, representing a new paradigm in AI video creation that shifts from cloud-based services to local execution.

## Key facts
- LTX 2.3 is an evolution of the previous LTX2 model, incorporating thousands of creator suggestions for enhanced performance and stability.
