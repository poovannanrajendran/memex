## Overview
An accessory for the iPad Pro that enhances its functionality by providing a keyboard, trackpad, and a hinged stand, mimicking a laptop-like experience.

## Role in this knowledge base
A crucial accessory that the creator believes, when combined with the iPad Pro and Apple Pencil Pro, makes the tablet a viable laptop replacement.

## Key facts
- The new Magic Keyboard for the iPad Pro features a larger trackpad and a metal finish on the inside.
