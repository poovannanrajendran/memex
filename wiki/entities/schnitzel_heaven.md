## Overview
Schnitzel Heaven is a food establishment specializing in schnitzel. It is noted for serving large, hand-pressed schnitzels made to order.

## Role in this knowledge base
Featured for its large schnitzels available for £12 each, presented as a substantial meal option.

## Key facts
- Serves schnitzels that are hand-pressed to order and are described as being as big as a torso.
