## Overview
A court in Scotland that handles both civil and criminal cases. It has a more extensive jurisdiction than the Justice of the Peace courts.

## Role in this knowledge base
The Sheriff Court in Scotland handles serious criminal offences with a jury of 15 people, a detail tested in the Life in the UK exam.
