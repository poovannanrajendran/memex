## Overview
The Steelcase Gesture is a high-end ergonomic office chair known for its advanced adjustability and ability to support a wide range of postures and user sizes. It is often considered a benchmark in ergonomic seating but comes at a premium price.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as a benchmark for comparison with the FlexiSpot OC6 chair, with the OC6 being presented as a significantly more affordable alternative that matches or exceeds some of its specifications.

## Key facts
- The FlexiSpot OC6 chair is positioned as a more affordable alternative to the Steelcase Gesture chair, costing less than a quarter of its price and having a higher weight capacity.
