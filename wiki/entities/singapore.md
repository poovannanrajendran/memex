## Overview
Singapore is a sovereign island city-state in Southeast Asia. It is a global financial centre and known for its efficient public services and innovative policies.

## Role in this knowledge base
The source mentions an experiment from Singapore related to rent payment, which was later adopted in the UK. This serves as a case study for behavioural economic interventions.

## Key facts
- Implemented a successful behavioural economic intervention where timely rent payments received a thank-you message.
