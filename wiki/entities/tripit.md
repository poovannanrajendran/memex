## Overview
TripIt is a travel organization application that automatically creates a master itinerary by scanning and processing booking confirmation emails. It consolidates flights, hotels, car rentals, and other travel plans into a single, easy-to-access schedule.

## Role in this knowledge base
Organizes travel plans and reservations into a one-stop shop itinerary.

## Key facts
- It can be accessed in a static version even when offline, as long as the user doesn't log out of the app.
