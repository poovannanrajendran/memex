## Overview
DeepSeek is presented as a highly cost-effective AI model, with DeepSeek R1 being particularly effective for planning and architecture, and DeepSeek V3 offering performance comparable to Claude 3.5 at a much lower price point. It's noted for its affordability for complex tasks.

## Role in this knowledge base
The presenter relies heavily on DeepSeek, especially DeepSeek R1 for planning and DeepSeek V3 for implementation, to achieve significant cost reductions, often using it for tasks that would otherwise require more expensive models.

## Key facts
- DeepSeek V3 can implement complex tasks for pennies, estimated at around 30 cents, compared to Claude's potential $2 cost for the same task.
