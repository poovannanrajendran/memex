## Overview
The Google Pixel is a line of consumer electronic devices developed by Google, primarily running the Android operating system and known for their camera capabilities and AI features.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a point of comparison for Apple's product launch strategy regarding features announced but not yet released.
