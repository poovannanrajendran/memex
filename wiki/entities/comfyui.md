## Overview
ComfyUI is an open-source, node-based graphical user interface for AI image generation and manipulation. It is highly flexible and offers extensive customisation for complex workflows.

## Role in this knowledge base
An open-source tool that serves as a foundation for advanced AI video generation workflows, offering significant customisation.

## Key facts
- ComfyUI, combined with AnimateDiff, enables complex, customisable AI video generation workflows that underpin some of the capabilities found in paid platforms.
