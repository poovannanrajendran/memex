## Overview
Google Maps is a web mapping service developed by Google. It offers satellite imagery, street maps, 360° interactive panoramic views of streets, real-time traffic conditions, and route planning for travel.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used for navigation, saving recommended locations with personal notes, and downloading offline maps.

## Key facts
- Users can save recommended pins with comments about who recommended them and why, to help piece together an itinerary.
