## Overview
Motionsites.ai is a platform that provides website templates, including hero templates, which can be either free or paid. Users can select and use these templates as a starting point for their website designs.

## Role in this knowledge base
The source of website templates used in the first step of the tutorial.

## Key facts
- Motionsites.ai offers great hero templates for free.
