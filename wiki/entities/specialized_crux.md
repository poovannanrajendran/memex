## Overview
The Specialized CruX is a cyclocross and gravel bike model. The author uses their own CruX to illustrate the impact of drivetrain choice on tire clearance.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as an example to demonstrate how a 1x drivetrain can allow for greater tire clearance compared to a 2x setup on the same frame.

## Key facts
- On the author's Specialized CruX, a 2x configuration limits tire clearance to approximately 43-44mm, whereas a 1x setup could accommodate up to 47mm or more.
