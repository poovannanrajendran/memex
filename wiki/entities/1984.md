## Overview
A dystopian novel by George Orwell, published in 1949. It is a seminal work of political fiction that explores themes of totalitarianism, surveillance, and censorship.

## Role in this knowledge base
Cited as an ironic example of a book being removed from Kindle devices, highlighting concerns about censorship and control over digital content.

## Key facts
- It was infamously removed from Kindle devices by Amazon.
