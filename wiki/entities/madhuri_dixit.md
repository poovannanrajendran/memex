## Overview
Madhuri Dixit is a celebrated Indian actress and dancer, widely regarded as one of the greatest actresses in Hindi cinema. She is known for her exceptional dancing skills and her captivating on-screen presence.

## Role in this knowledge base
She portrays Pooja in 'Dil To Pagal Hai', a character who believes in destiny and soulmates.

## Key facts
- She is renowned for her dancing prowess in Indian cinema.
