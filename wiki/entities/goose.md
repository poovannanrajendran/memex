## Overview
Goose is a fully open-source AI agent developed by Block that assists developers by installing, executing, editing, and testing code directly on their machine.

## Role in this knowledge base
It serves as an example of an accessible, open-source AI development tool, aiming to reduce costs and increase efficiency for developers.

## Key facts
- Goose connects to over 70 MCP servers and works with any LLM.
