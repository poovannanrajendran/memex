## Overview
The personification of the sacred river Ganges and a divine figure in Hindu mythology, mother of Bhishma.

## Role in this knowledge base
Her divine meeting with Shantanu is a crucial part of the Kuru ancestry to be explored in Season 2.

## Key facts
- Her divine union with Shantanu is a key event in the Kuru dynasty's origins.
