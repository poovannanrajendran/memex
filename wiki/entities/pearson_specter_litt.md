## Overview
Pearson Specter Litt is a fictional corporate law firm in New York City, featured in the TV series 'Suits'. It is known for its high-profile clients and intense legal battles.

## Role in this knowledge base
The law firm is the setting and context for the professional lives of Harvey, Louis, and Jessica, and the continuity of its business is a key concern.

## Key facts
- The firm has a system in place to ensure clients are not informed of Louis's medical emergency.
