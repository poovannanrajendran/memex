## Overview
The Nvidia RTX 6000 Ada Generation is a professional workstation GPU based on the Ada Lovelace architecture. It is designed for AI, data science, and high-end visualisation tasks, featuring a large amount of VRAM.

## Role in this knowledge base
This professional-grade GPU was paired with a 96-core Threadripper to test the performance of the largest Llama model (405 billion parameters).

## Key facts
- It is a high-end professional GPU used in the $50,000 Dell workstation.
