## Overview
Napkin AI is a tool that transforms text into visual content. It is designed to rapidly generate images and other visuals from written descriptions or data, significantly speeding up the creative process.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is recommended for its ability to quickly convert text into visuals, saving creators significant time compared to traditional methods like PowerPoint or video editing software.

## Key facts
