## Overview
Straight bevel gears have teeth that are straight and tapered, designed to transmit motion between intersecting shafts, typically at a 90-degree angle.

## Role in this knowledge base
Explained as a type of bevel gear for intersecting axes with straight teeth.

## Key facts
- Has a conical shape with straight teeth and transfers motion between intersecting axes.
