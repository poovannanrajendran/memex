## Overview
Generative Pre-trained Transformer, a series of large language models developed by OpenAI, widely recognised for its capabilities in natural language processing and generation.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the Large Language Models (LLMs) explicitly supported by the CrewAI framework for building AI agents.

## Key facts
- GPT models are compatible LLMs for AI agents built with CrewAI.
