## Overview
The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) is an Indian constitutional body responsible for recruiting civil servants for various government ministries and departments.

## Role in this knowledge base
The source directly addresses preparation strategies for exams conducted by this organisation.

## Key facts
- It conducts the Civil Services Examination, one of the toughest competitive exams in India.
