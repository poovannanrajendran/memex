## Overview
A high-performance prime lens for Fujifilm X-mount cameras, featuring a 56mm focal length and a wide f1.2 maximum aperture. It is favoured for its excellent bokeh and low-light capabilities.

## Role in this knowledge base
Presented as a comparative lens in terms of value.

## Key facts
- The f1.2 aperture allows for very shallow depth of field and exceptional performance in low light.
