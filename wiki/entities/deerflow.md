## Overview
Deerflow is a newly released AI agent that operates locally on a user's machine without needing cloud access. It is designed to function as a comprehensive agent capable of performing a wide range of tasks autonomously.

## Role in this knowledge base
Deerflow is presented as a free, powerful AI agent that can significantly boost productivity by handling complex tasks locally, offering an alternative to expensive paid software.
