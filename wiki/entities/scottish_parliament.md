## Overview
The devolved parliament of Scotland, responsible for legislating on devolved matters. It is based in Edinburgh.

## Role in this knowledge base
The number of members in the Scottish Parliament is a specific detail tested in the Life in the UK exam, related to the study of devolved administrations.

## Key facts
- The Scottish Parliament has 129 members.
