## Overview
Nayanthara is a highly successful Indian actress who primarily appears in Tamil films. She is often referred to as 'Lady Superstar' and has starred alongside many leading actors in the industry.

## Role in this knowledge base
Lead actress in the film "Mr. Local" and featured in the song video.

## Key facts
- She is one of the highest-paid actresses in South Indian cinema.
