## Overview
Ellen Langer is a Harvard psychologist known for her research on the psychology of the mind and its influence on the body. She often explores concepts like mindfulness and the power of expectation.

## Role in this knowledge base
While not explicitly named in the transcript, the 'Harvard scientist' conducting the age-reversal study is widely attributed to Ellen Langer, making her work highly relevant to the concepts discussed regarding thoughts creating reality.

## Key facts
- Her famous study involved elderly men living as if they were in their youth for a week, leading to observable age-reversal effects.
