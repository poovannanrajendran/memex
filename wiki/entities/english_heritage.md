## Overview
English Heritage is a charitable trust that manages over 400 historic monuments, buildings, and places in England. It is responsible for protecting and preserving England's historic environment.

## Role in this knowledge base
Investigated and solved the mystery of the Islington carvings.

## Key facts
- An investigator from English Heritage solved the mystery of the carvings in 2006.
