## Overview
The Keychron Q Series, specifically mentioning the Q2 and Q60 Max, are described as all-metal, substantial keyboards suitable for a full desktop setup. They support pairing with multiple Bluetooth devices.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended for users aiming for a full desktop mode setup with their iPad, offering robust build quality and multi-device connectivity.

## Key facts
- Can pair with up to three Bluetooth devices.
