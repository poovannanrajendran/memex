## Overview
StrictlyApple is an online store that sells Apple products, including iPads. They offer different conditions for their products, such as 'open box'.

## Role in this knowledge base
The retailer from which Tracy purchased the iPad Air 5.

## Key facts
- They offer 'open box' items in varying conditions, with 'best condition' being highlighted.
