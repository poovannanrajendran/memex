## Overview
A helical gear has teeth that are cut at an angle to the axis of rotation, resembling a helix. This design allows for smoother and quieter operation compared to spur gears.

## Role in this knowledge base
Presented as an improvement over spur gears, offering quieter and smoother operation at higher speeds.

## Key facts
- Features angled teeth, making it quieter and smoother, ideal for higher speeds.
