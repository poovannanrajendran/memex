## Overview
The A18 Pro chip is an enhanced version of the A18 chip designed for the iPhone 16 Pro models, providing even greater performance, particularly for camera and AI tasks.

## Role in this knowledge base
The processor powering the iPhone 16 Pro and 16 Pro Max.

## Key facts
- The A18 Pro chip includes improvements to calling and enhanced capabilities related to the camera.
