## Overview
The Banks Stand is an iPad stand that is magnetic and designed to function effectively as an angled platform, similar to a draftsman's board. It features a turning system for adjustment and can be magnetized to surfaces like desk shelves.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended for users who draw or take notes, offering a drafting-board-like experience.

## Key facts
- Its magnetic nature allows it to attach to surfaces like desk shelves.
