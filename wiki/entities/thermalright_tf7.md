## Overview
Thermalright TF7 is a thermal paste compound used to improve heat transfer between a CPU and its cooler. It is known for its performance in dissipating heat effectively.

## Role in this knowledge base
The thermal paste recommended and used in the installation process.

## Key facts
- Applying nine dots of the paste is suggested to cover the entire CPU surface.
