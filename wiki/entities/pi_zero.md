## Overview
The Pi Zero is an ultra-small and affordable version of the Raspberry Pi, designed for embedded applications and projects where space and power consumption are critical.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as an alternative Raspberry Pi model with specific use cases.

## Key facts
- The Pi Zero costs $15 and sips a tiny bit of power, making it great for remote sensors or time-lapse cameras.
