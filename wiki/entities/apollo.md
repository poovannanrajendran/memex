## Overview
Apollo is a sales automation tool that helps generate lead lists by filtering based on an Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).

## Role in this knowledge base
A tool for automating sales processes, specifically lead generation and cold emailing.

## Key facts
- It allows users to input their ICP and instantly creates a lead list.
