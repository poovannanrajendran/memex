## Overview
AVM Productions is a renowned Indian film production studio based in Chennai. Founded by Avichi Meiyappa Chettiar, it has produced numerous successful Tamil films across various genres since its inception.

## Role in this knowledge base
Production house of the film 'Samsaram Adhu Minsaram'.

## Key facts
- AVM Productions is one of the oldest and most respected film studios in South India.
