## Overview
The Mercedes-Benz EQB is a compact electric SUV that is available with an optional third row of seats, making it a more versatile and spacious option than the EQA. It is based on the B-Class platform.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the two electric vehicles being compared in the source document, highlighted for its practicality and space.

## Key facts
- The EQB can be configured as a seven-seater, offering more practicality than the EQA.
