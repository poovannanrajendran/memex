## Overview
MagSafe is a magnetic charging and accessory attachment system developed by Apple for iPhones, allowing for easy alignment and secure connection of chargers and accessories.

## Role in this knowledge base
A charging technology relevant to the ESR sponsor case.

## Key facts
- ESR cases claim the fastest MagSafe charging of any stand case due to their thin design.
