## Overview
Sai Pallavi is an Indian actress and dancer who appears in Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam films. She gained recognition for her natural acting style and dancing abilities, often portraying strong, independent female characters.

## Role in this knowledge base
Lead actress in the film 'Amaran' and featured in the song 'Hey Minnale'.

## Key facts
- She made her acting debut in the Malayalam film 'Premam' (2015).
