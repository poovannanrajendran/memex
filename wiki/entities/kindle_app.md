## Overview
Amazon's application for reading Kindle e-books on various devices, including computers and mobile devices.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used in conjunction with audiobooks and note-taking apps to enhance reading comprehension and information retention.

## Key facts
- It is used simultaneously with audiobooks to create a dual-input learning experience.
