## Overview
NoodlDB is a free, self-hostable, and open-source alternative to Airtable. It offers a simpler interface while still effectively managing databases and providing users with the benefit of keeping their data private on their own servers.

## Role in this knowledge base
Replaces Airtable for database management.

## Key facts
- It provides a free Community Edition that can be self-hosted, ensuring data privacy.
