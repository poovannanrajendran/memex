## Overview
Carol Dweck is a renowned psychologist and author, best known for her research on the concept of mindset.

## Role in this knowledge base
Author of the book 'Mindset', which is recommended for understanding personal development and ambition.

## Key facts
- She is a pioneer in the study of fixed versus growth mindsets.
