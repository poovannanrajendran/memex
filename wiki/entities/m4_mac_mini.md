## Overview
The M4 Mac Mini is a compact desktop computer from Apple that features their M4 chip, known for its performance and power efficiency, particularly in creative and productivity tasks.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as a point of comparison for price and performance with the NVIDIA Project Digits Mini PC.

## Key facts
- The M4 Mac Mini is mentioned as a reference for price and performance, with the Project Digits Mini PC being significantly more expensive but offering substantially higher AI computing capabilities.
