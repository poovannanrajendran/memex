## Overview
VW Financial Services is the financial arm of Volkswagen, providing financing and leasing options for Volkswagen vehicles.

## Role in this knowledge base
They are used as an example of a leasing company that charged significantly less for minor damage (e.g., alloy wheel refurbishment) upon the return of a leased car compared to local repair quotes.

## Key facts
- VW Financial Services charged £60 to refurbish a damaged alloy wheel on a returned lease car.
