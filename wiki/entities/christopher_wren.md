## Overview
A renowned English architect, scientist, and astronomer. He is credited with rebuilding 51 churches in the City of London after the Great Fire of London, as well as St Paul's Cathedral.

## Role in this knowledge base
Designer of the Triforium Library at St Paul's Cathedral.

## Key facts
- He collaborated with his protégé Nicholas Hawksmore on the design of the Triforium Library.
