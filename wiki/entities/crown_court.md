## Overview
A court in England, Wales, and Northern Ireland that deals with more serious criminal cases. It is where jury trials for indictable offences take place.

## Role in this knowledge base
The Crown Court is where serious criminal offences are handled in England, Wales, and Northern Ireland, involving a jury of 12 people, a key point in the Life in the UK test's court section.

## Key facts
- Handles serious criminal offenses in England, Wales, and Northern Ireland with a jury of 12.
