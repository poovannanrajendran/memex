## Overview
Software programmes leveraging artificial intelligence to perform tasks autonomously, often interacting with systems or users to automate workflows and provide analytical insights.

## Role in this knowledge base
They are becoming "digital colleagues" for underwriters and brokers, handling tasks from submission triage to claims processing.

## Key facts
- AI agents with long-context windows are enabling deep analysis of complex risk data.
