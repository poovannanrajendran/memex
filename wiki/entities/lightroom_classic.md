## Overview
Adobe Lightroom Classic is a desktop-focused application for photo editing and management. It is known for its intensive use of CPU, GPU, and RAM for tasks like batch editing and exporting large sets of high-resolution images.

## Role in this knowledge base
A critical real-world application used to test the devices' ability to handle large-scale photo editing and export tasks, simulating demanding productivity workloads.

## Key facts
- Used to export 500 high-resolution raw images, testing CPU, GPU, and memory performance.
