## Overview
Abhirami is a Hindu goddess, often associated with the Devi or Shakti. She is particularly revered in the context of the Abhirami Anthathi, a devotional hymn.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned in the song lyrics as a divine figure to whom the song is addressed or related.

## Key facts
- Referred to as a sacred and lullaby-singing deity in the song.
