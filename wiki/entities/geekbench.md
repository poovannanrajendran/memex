## Overview
Geekbench is a cross-platform benchmark tool used to measure the performance of CPUs and GPUs. It provides both single-core and multi-core scores for comparative analysis.

## Role in this knowledge base
A key benchmarking tool used in the video to evaluate the raw processing power of the AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395 and the Apple M4 Pro.

## Key facts
- It was used to compare single-core and multi-core CPU performance between the two chips.
