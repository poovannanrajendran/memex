## Overview
A display technology used in the 2024 iPad Pro, which utilizes tandem OLED panels to achieve high brightness and deep contrast, marketed as Apple's best consumer screen.

## Role in this knowledge base
A significant visual component of the iPad Pro, contributing to its premium feel and suitability for media consumption and creative work.

## Key facts
- The Ultra Retina XDR Display uses two OLED panels stacked together for improved contrast and brightness.
