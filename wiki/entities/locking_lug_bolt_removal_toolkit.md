## Overview
A specialized set of tools designed to remove locking lug bolts when the original key is lost or unavailable. It typically includes a centering guide, a hardened drill bit, and an extractor tool.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary toolset used in the video to successfully remove the locking lug bolt without the key.

## Key facts
- Includes a centering guide to ensure the drill bit penetrates the exact middle of the lug bolt.
