## Overview
Yash Raj Films (YRF) is one of India's largest film production houses, founded by Yash Chopra. It is known for producing a wide range of popular Hindi films, including romantic dramas and comedies, and has a significant global reach.

## Role in this knowledge base
The production house created and distributed the film 'Dil To Pagal Hai' and uploaded this song video.

## Key facts
- It is one of the most prominent film studios in India.
