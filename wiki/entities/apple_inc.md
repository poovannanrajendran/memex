## Overview
Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. It designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services.

## Role in this knowledge base
The manufacturer of the iPhone and the developer of the iOS operating system.

## Key facts
- Apple is one of the world's largest technology companies by revenue and market capitalisation.
