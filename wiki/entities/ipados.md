## Overview
The mobile operating system developed by Apple for iPads. It is based on iOS but tailored for the larger screen and enhanced capabilities of the iPad.

## Role in this knowledge base
The software environment in which the iPad Pro operates, influencing its usability, app compatibility, and multitasking features, and is a point of comparison with macOS.

## Key facts
- iPadOS is based on iOS and is designed to be simpler than macOS.
