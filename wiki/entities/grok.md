## Overview
Grok is an AI chatbot developed by xAI, known for its real-time access to information via the X (formerly Twitter) platform and its more direct, unfiltered responses.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the AI models that can be accessed through the LiteLLM proxy within the Open WebUI.

## Key facts
- Grok can be accessed via API through LiteLLM, providing another option in the unified AI interface.
