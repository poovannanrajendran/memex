## Overview
Albert Kolan is presented as the employer for whom Raymond Reddington worked during a summer job installing carpets. He is depicted as a discerning individual who recognizes and rewards commitment.

## Role in this knowledge base
The employer who, by recognizing Reddington's commitment and loyalty, reinforced the lesson of valuing loyalty above all else.

## Key facts
- He gave Reddington a bonus for completing the summer job, stating no other kid had made it through.
