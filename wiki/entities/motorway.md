## Overview
Motorway is an online service that helps individuals sell their cars by comparing offers from multiple dealers.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is listed alongside Carwow as a platform to obtain competitive bids when looking to sell a current car prior to taking delivery of a new lease vehicle.

## Key facts
- Motorway is used to compare dealer offers for selling a car.
