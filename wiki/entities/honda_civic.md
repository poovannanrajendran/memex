## Overview
The Honda Civic is a compact car produced by Honda, recognized for its reliability, fuel economy, and sporty handling. It often serves as a benchmark in its class for practical transportation.

## Role in this knowledge base
Cited as a prime example of an economical car suitable for purchase outright, and a vehicle that can be driven until it is no longer functional.

## Key facts
- It is one of the specific models recommended for its durability and low cost.
