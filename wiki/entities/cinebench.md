## Overview
Cinebench is a cross-platform benchmark tool that evaluates the performance of a computer's CPU and GPU. It simulates rendering tasks to provide comparable scores.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used to benchmark the overall CPU performance of the new workstation, comparing it to high-end consumer and HEDT CPUs.

## Key facts
- The workstation achieved an 'insane score' of 27,41 in Cinebench, nearing the performance of a Threadripper 2990 WX.
