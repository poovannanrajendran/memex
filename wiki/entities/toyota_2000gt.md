## Overview
The Toyota 2000GT is a highly sought-after, limited-production Japanese sports car produced by Toyota from 1967 to 1970. It is renowned for its elegant styling, advanced engineering for its time, and rarity.

## Role in this knowledge base
The central subject of the video; the father's car which sparks a journey of rediscovery and connection.
