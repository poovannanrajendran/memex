## Overview
Digilocker is a digital locker service launched by the Government of India. It provides individuals with a secure online space to store and share their important documents and certificates, linked to their Aadhaar identity.

## Role in this knowledge base
Serves as the authentication and login mechanism for accessing AI Kosh.

## Key facts
- Users must use Digilocker, linked to their Aadhaar, to log into AI Kosh.
