## Overview
Rajkumar Periasamy is an Indian film director and screenwriter who works predominantly in Tamil cinema. He made his directorial debut with the film 'Amaran'.

## Role in this knowledge base
Director of the film 'Amaran'.

## Key facts
- He has also written the screenplay for 'Amaran'.
