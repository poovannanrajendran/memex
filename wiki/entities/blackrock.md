## Overview
BlackRock is a multinational investment management corporation. It is one of the world's largest asset managers and a leading provider of financial technology services.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned in the context of a common misconception or sarcastic question about future generations owning nothing and allowing large institutions like BlackRock to control all assets.

## Key facts
- It is presented as an example of a large financial institution that some perceive as benefiting from a future where individuals own less.
