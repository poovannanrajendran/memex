## Overview
Red Dead Redemption 2 is a critically acclaimed action-adventure game known for its detailed open world and demanding graphics. It serves as a benchmark for PC hardware performance.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the games tested on the new workstation to gauge its performance, albeit not its primary intended use.

## Key facts
- Red Dead Redemption 2 ran at an average of 91.1 FPS at 1080p on the new workstation, with very low CPU usage but maxed GPU usage.
