## Overview
The Skoda Enyaq is an electric SUV known for its practicality, spacious interior, and comfortable ride, built on the Volkswagen Group's MEB platform.

## Role in this knowledge base
Considered one of the most practical family EVs, the Enyaq is recommended for its range, comfort, and build quality, with the reviewer preferring it over contemporary VW ID4/ID5 models.

## Key facts
- A 2021 IV80 model with 67,000 miles and over 330 miles of range is available for just over £16,500.
