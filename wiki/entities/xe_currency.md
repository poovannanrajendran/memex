## Overview
XE Currency is a popular app and website that provides real-time exchange rates for virtually all world currencies. It allows users to convert currencies quickly and easily, helping them understand the value of goods and services abroad.

## Role in this knowledge base
A tool for checking currency exchange rates when traveling.

## Key facts
- It comes in handy on days when users' brains are too tired to mentally calculate exchange rates.
