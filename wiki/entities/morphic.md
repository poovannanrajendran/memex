## Overview
Morphic is described as a next-generation AI filmmaking tool used to create video content from text prompts and reference images.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary AI tool used to generate the entire Karna death scene, from character animation to final video compilation.

## Key facts
- It is capable of converting static images into videos with described actions and generating synced cinematic moments for dialogues.
