## Overview
Chefs are culinary professionals responsible for preparing food in a restaurant or other establishment. In this context, chefs are seen marinating meat and seafood for tandoor cooking.

## Role in this knowledge base
Directly involved in the preparation of the food being reviewed.

## Key facts
- They are observed marinating meat and seafood which is then cooked in a charcoal tandoor.
