## Overview
The Nvidia GeForce GTX 650 is a budget-oriented graphics card from the Kepler generation, primarily designed for entry-level gaming. It offers basic graphics performance.

## Role in this knowledge base
A functional GPU used to diagnose the Dell Precision 7820 workstation's POST issues after the Tesla K20 failed.

## Key facts
- The GTX 650 was one of the few GPUs that would allow the Dell Precision 7820 to post, eventually leading to the diagnosis of a CPU seating issue.
