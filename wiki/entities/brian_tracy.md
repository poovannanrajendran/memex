## Overview
Brian Tracy is a globally recognised expert and author in the fields of sales, management, and personal development. He has written numerous books and produced hundreds of audio and video learning programs designed to help individuals improve their performance and achieve their goals.

## Role in this knowledge base
The presenter and authority figure providing the core content and methodology for achieving goals.
