## Overview
Citymapper is a public transit app and data service that offers detailed route planning, schedules, and real-time information for public transportation in major cities worldwide. It often provides more efficient and personalized route options than general mapping services.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended over Google Maps for public transportation in big cities in the US, Canada, or Europe due to more efficient routes, accurate timings, and pricing.

## Key facts
- It allows personalization of routes, such as choosing 'bus only' or 'train only', and offers a 'simple' option with the fewest transfers.
