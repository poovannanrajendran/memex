## Overview
The Hyundai Ioniq 5 is a distinctive electric hatchback/crossover featuring retro-futuristic styling and advanced 800V charging architecture.

## Role in this knowledge base
Praised for its roomy interior, modern design, and excellent fast charging capabilities due to its 800V system, making it a strong contender in the used EV market.

## Key facts
- A 2021 model with 315 miles of range can be found for around £20,000, with low mileage examples available.
