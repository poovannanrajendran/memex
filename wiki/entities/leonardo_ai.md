## Overview
Leonardo AI is a platform that leverages artificial intelligence to generate high-quality images from text descriptions. It offers various models and customization options.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used to create visual assets for the animated story by generating images from the prompts developed in ChatGPT.

## Key facts
- Offers a 'seed' feature to maintain style consistency across generated images, and provides 150 free tokens daily.
