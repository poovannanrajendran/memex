## Overview
A multitasking feature in iPadOS designed to improve window management and app interaction, particularly on external displays.

## Role in this knowledge base
A feature that aids in multitasking on the iPad Pro, especially when connected to larger screens, bridging the gap between tablet and desktop-like experiences.

## Key facts
- Stage Manager is designed for big screen multitasking on the iPad.
