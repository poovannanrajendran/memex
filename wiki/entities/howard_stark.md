## Overview
Howard Stark is a fictional character in Marvel Comics, the father of Tony Stark and a brilliant scientist and industrialist. He was a co-founder of S.H.I.E.L.D. and known for his innovative technological advancements.

## Role in this knowledge base
Tony Stark's father, who is quoted as having delivered the key piece of advice about the value of time.

## Key facts
- He told his son, Tony, that 'no amount of money ever bought a second of time'.
