## Overview
SpaceX is an American aerospace manufacturer and space transport services company headquartered in Hawthorne, California. It was founded by Elon Musk in 2002 with the goal of reducing space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars.

## Role in this knowledge base
SpaceX is cited as an example of a company heavily regulated in its operations, particularly regarding rockets and Starlink.

## Key facts
- Failure to comply with regulations for Rockets or Starlink would lead to SpaceX being shut down.
