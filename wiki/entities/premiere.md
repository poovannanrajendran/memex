## Overview
Adobe Premiere Pro is a professional video editing software application. It is widely used by filmmakers, editors, and content creators for its extensive features and integration with other Adobe Creative Cloud applications.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary video editing software used for testing and exporting the video project.

## Key facts
- A 4K export of a video project in Premiere took just over 14 minutes on the new workstation, compared to nearly an hour on the previous Ryzen 3700x system.
