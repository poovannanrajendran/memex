## Overview
LTX Studio is an AI-powered platform designed for rapid creation and editing of short films. It offers extensive control over characters, styles, and scenes, allowing for quick iteration and full project generation.

## Role in this knowledge base
A comprehensive AI tool for creating entire short films with a high degree of control and speed, including features for style transfer and detailed editing.

## Key facts
- LTX Studio can generate an entire short film from a script or prompt in a matter of minutes, offering full scene editability and style customisation.
