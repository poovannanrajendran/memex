## Overview
King of England, Scotland, and Ireland from the Restoration of the monarchy in 1660 until his death in 1685. He succeeded his father, Charles I, after a period of republican rule.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned in the context of the 'Restoration' which refers to the re-establishment of the monarchy under his rule, a historical concept relevant to the Life in the UK test.

## Key facts
- Charles II returned from exile to be re-established as monarch in 1660.
