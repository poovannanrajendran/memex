## Overview
A millimeter wave (mmWave) radar module is a device that uses high-frequency radio waves (typically in the millimeter range) to detect objects and measure their properties such as distance, speed, and angle. These modules are often used in advanced sensing applications.

## Role in this knowledge base
This is the core component enabling the GENIUS Way sensor to perform its human detection and tracking functions.

## Key facts
- It uses 24 GHz radio waves.
