## Overview
The Dacia Duster is a compact SUV known for its affordability and practicality. This review focuses on the latest generation, which boasts a completely revamped design, new powertrains including a hybrid option, and updated technology.

## Role in this knowledge base
The subject of the review, analysed for its improvements and value proposition in the small SUV market.

## Key facts
- The new Duster starts at £18,000, having doubled in price since its 2012 introduction, but is only £1,000 more than the previous model's end-of-life price.
