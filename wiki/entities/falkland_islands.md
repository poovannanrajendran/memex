## Overview
A British Overseas Territory in the South Atlantic Ocean, consisting of two main islands and numerous smaller ones. Known for its sheep farming and strategic location.

## Role in this knowledge base
Presented as an example of a British Overseas Territory, to help distinguish it from Crown Dependencies for the Life in the UK test.

## Key facts
- They are located off the coast of Argentina.
