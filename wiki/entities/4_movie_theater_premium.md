## Overview
A 4K laser TV projector with an ultra-short throw design, featuring ALPD RGB plus 4.0 technology, high brightness (2200 ISO lumens), wide colour gamut, and Dolby Vision/HDR10+ support. It also includes built-in Google TV and native Netflix.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a product that, like the Mac Mini, made the reviewer rethink his setup, in this case, his home entertainment system.

## Key facts
- It can project a 150-inch screen from just a few centimeters away.
