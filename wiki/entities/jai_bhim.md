## Overview
Jai Bhim is a salutation and slogan widely used by followers of B.R. Ambedkar, signifying allegiance to his ideals and the Dalit rights movement. It represents a call for justice, equality, and the liberation of oppressed communities.

## Role in this knowledge base
An ideological framework with which the speech's concept of freedom is associated.

## Key facts
- It is a powerful slogan and salute associated with the Dalit Buddhist movement and Ambedkarite followers.
