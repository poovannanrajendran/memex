## Overview
MePalace is an AI memory tool designed to give AI systems permanent memory by organising past conversations and projects. It uses a structured 'palace' metaphor with wings, halls, and rooms to store and retrieve information efficiently.

## Role in this knowledge base
The central subject of the analysis, demonstrating a novel approach to AI memory.

## Key facts
- It claims to be the highest-scoring AI memory system ever benchmarked.
