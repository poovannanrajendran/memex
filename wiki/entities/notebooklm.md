## Overview
NotebookLM is an AI-powered research and learning tool that allows users to upload documents and ask questions, receiving answers based solely on the provided source material. It helps organize notes, insights, and research projects within a closed database, minimizing hallucination.

## Role in this knowledge base
NotebookLM serves as the analytical engine in the workflow, providing focused insights derived exclusively from user-imported sources, thereby ensuring accuracy and reducing the risk of misinformation.

## Key facts
- NotebookLM only provides answers based on the sources you import, and the source reference will not change unless you import new sources.
