## Overview
Shiva is one of the principal deities of Hinduism, regarded as the Supreme Being in Shaivism. He is known as the destroyer and transformer within the Trimurti.

## Role in this knowledge base
Referenced in the song lyrics as the divine entity with whom Sivagami is connected.

## Key facts
