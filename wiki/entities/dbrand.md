## Overview
dbrand is a company that specialises in customisation products for electronics, most notably skins and cases that alter the appearance and protection of devices.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a provider of accessory products for the Samsung Galaxy S24.

## Key facts
- Known for its high-quality, often customisable, device skins and cases.
