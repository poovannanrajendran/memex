## Overview
The Maaveeran TVS 50 is a classic 50cc moped, known for its utility and durability, especially in rural settings. It was popular in the 1980s in India.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary subject of the YouTube video, representing a piece of automotive history and personal nostalgia.

## Key facts
- It was colloquially known as the 'King of Mopeds' due to its robust build and carrying capacity.
