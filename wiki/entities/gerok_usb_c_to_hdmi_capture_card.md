## Overview
A low-cost USB-C to HDMI capture card that allows video signals to be input into an iPad. It enables the iPad to act as a monitor for external devices or receive camera feeds.

## Role in this knowledge base
Highlighted as an affordable tool to turn an iPad into a monitor for consoles or a display for mirrorless cameras, enabling professional video calls or content creation workflows.

## Key facts
- Costs around $20 and requires iPadOS 17 or compatible apps like Capture Pro, Capture One, or Orion.
