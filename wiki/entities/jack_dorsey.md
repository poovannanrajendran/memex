## Overview
Jack Dorsey is a prominent figure in the tech industry, widely recognised as the co-founder and former CEO of Twitter (now X) and Square (now Block).

## Role in this knowledge base
He initiated the development of the open-source AI agent Goose through his company, Block, aiming to democratise AI tools.
