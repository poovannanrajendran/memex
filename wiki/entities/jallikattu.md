## Overview
Jallikattu is a traditional Tamil sport played during the Pongal festival. It involves taming a bull by holding onto its horns or hump and preventing it from escaping.

The cultural and sporting context within which the individual's story unfolds.

## Key facts
- It is a controversial sport with a long history, often involving significant risk.
