## Overview
The Raspberry Pi Foundation is a UK-based charity that promotes the study of computer science in schools and developing countries. It is best known for its line of single-board computers, which are small, affordable, and versatile computing devices.

## Role in this knowledge base

## Key facts
- Raspberry Pi offers multiple generations and form factors of its single-board computers, each with distinct features and price points.
