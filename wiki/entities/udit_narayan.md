## Overview
Udit Narayan is a highly acclaimed Indian playback singer whose songs have been featured in numerous Bollywood films. He is known for his melodious voice and has won several awards for his contributions to music.

## Role in this knowledge base
He provided vocals for the song 'Le Gayi' from 'Dil To Pagal Hai'.

## Key facts
- He is a multiple National Film Award and Filmfare Award winner.
