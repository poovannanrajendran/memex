## Overview
The Nvidia Titan Xp was a high-end consumer graphics card based on the Pascal architecture, offering significant gaming and professional performance. It featured 12GB of GDDR5X VRAM.

## Role in this knowledge base
The graphics card used in the new workstation, chosen for its 12GB of VRAM, which is sufficient for the creator's current editing needs.

## Key facts
- The creator reused their existing Titan Xp graphics card, which has 12GB of VRAM, finding it adequate for their video editing requirements.
