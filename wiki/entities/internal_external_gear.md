## Overview
An internal/external gear system involves a gear with internal teeth meshing with a gear with external teeth. This configuration is often compact and used in planetary gear sets.

## Role in this knowledge base
Described for its compact nature and suitability for specific applications like planetary gears, where teeth are on the inside of one gear.

## Key facts
- Has teeth inside a wheel that mesh with external teeth, making it compact and ideal for planetary gears.
