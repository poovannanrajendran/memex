## Overview
The Ambassador's Clubhouse is a restaurant in London that is claimed to serve some of the best Punjabi food in the city. It offers a distinctive ambiance, reminiscent of a royal embassy, and a menu featuring traditional Punjabi dishes prepared with a focus on charcoal grilling and rich flavour profiles.

## Role in this knowledge base
The subject of a food review, highlighted for its supposedly superior Punjabi cuisine.
