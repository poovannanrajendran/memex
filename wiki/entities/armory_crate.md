## Overview
Armory Crate is a utility software developed by Asus for ROG (Republic of Gamers) products. It allows users to manage system settings, lighting, and performance profiles.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used to check performance modes and fan settings on the ROG Flow Z13, specifically noting limitations on battery power.

## Key facts
- It shows that full performance modes on the Z13 are only available when plugged in.
