## Overview
Gigabyte Technology is a Taiwanese manufacturer and distributor of computer hardware. They are well-known for their motherboards, graphics cards, and laptops.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the motherboard manufacturers whose products are reviewed and recommended.

## Key facts
- Gigabyte's motherboards often feature robust power delivery and good cooling solutions.
