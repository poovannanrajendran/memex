## Overview
S&P Global is a leading provider of transparent and independent ratings for the global capital markets. Their ratings offer essential insights into risk and opportunity.

## Role in this knowledge base
S&P Global is among the rating agencies that recognise Lloyd's strong financial position, reinforcing confidence among investors and policyholders.

## Key facts
- Lloyd's holds a financial strength rating of AA- (Very Strong) with a stable outlook from S&P Global.
