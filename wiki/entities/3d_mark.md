## Overview
3DMark is a popular benchmarking suite for graphics cards and gaming performance on PCs. It includes various tests designed to stress different aspects of graphics processing.

## Role in this knowledge base
A benchmarking tool employed to test and compare the graphics and gaming performance capabilities of the Asus ROG Flow Z13 and the MacBook Pro.

## Key facts
- Used to test gaming performance with the 'Steel Nomad light' benchmark.
