## Overview
Quinn 2.5 VL is an AI model with vision capabilities. While not as powerful as models like Claude 3.5, it can describe parts of an image and feed that information into other models for implementation.

## Role in this knowledge base
The presenter uses Quinn 2.5 VL for its vision capabilities, particularly to extract information from mockups or images that can then be used by other models for implementation.

## Key facts
- Quinn 2.5 VL has vision capabilities that allow it to process image inputs.
