## Overview
Mercedes-Benz is a German luxury and commercial vehicle automotive brand. It is known for producing high-quality vehicles, including electric cars, and is part of the Daimler AG group.

## Role in this knowledge base
Manufacturer of the EQA and EQB electric vehicles being compared.

## Key facts
- The 'EQ' designation in their electric vehicles stands for 'Electric Intelligence'.
