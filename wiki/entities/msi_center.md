## Overview
MSI Center is a software application for MSI motherboards and laptops, providing system monitoring, performance tuning, and hardware control features.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used to monitor power gaps and clock speeds during the Cinebench stress test on the Asus ROG Flow Z13.
