## Overview
The Pi 400 is a unique Raspberry Pi product that integrates a Raspberry Pi 4 into a compact keyboard, offering a complete personal computer experience with minimal setup.

## Role in this knowledge base
Presented as a convenient all-in-one starter kit option.

## Key facts
- The Pi 400 kit includes a Pi built into a keyboard, a mouse, a power supply, and comes with Raspberry Pi OS pre-installed for immediate use.
