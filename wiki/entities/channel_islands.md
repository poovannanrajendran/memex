## Overview
A group of British Crown Dependencies located in the English Channel, comprising Guernsey and Jersey, each with its ownparliamentary, legal, and administrative systems.

## Role in this knowledge base
Identified as a Crown Dependency, which is a key distinction tested in the Life in the UK exam when differentiating from British Overseas Territories.

## Key facts
- They are located in the English Channel between the UK and France.
