## Overview
The Ford Mustang Mach-E is an electric crossover SUV styled to evoke the Mustang muscle car lineage, offering strong performance and range.

## Role in this knowledge base
The extended range version is noted for its significant range, though the reviewer expresses reservations about the Mustang badge. Early software issues are mentioned as resolved with updates.

## Key facts
- An extended range model with 335 miles of range and 72,000 miles can be found for £19,000 (2021 plate).
