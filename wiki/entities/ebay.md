## Overview
eBay is an American multinational e-commerce company that facilitates online consumer-to-consumer and business-to-consumer sales. It operates the website eBay.com, an online auction and shopping website where individuals and small businesses buy and sell a wide variety of goods and services worldwide.

## Role in this knowledge base
