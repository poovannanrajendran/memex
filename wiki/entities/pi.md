## Overview
Pi is an AI assistant designed to act as a confidant and empathetic personal assistant. Users can brainstorm ideas, get recommendations, ask questions, and even 'vent' to Pi.

## Role in this knowledge base
Serves as a friendly, empathetic, and versatile AI assistant for brainstorming, getting advice, and general conversation, functioning as a business and personal confidant.

## Key facts
- It has raised approximately one billion dollars in funding and is backed by major companies like Nvidia and Microsoft.
