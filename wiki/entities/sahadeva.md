## Overview
Sahadeva is the youngest of the Pandava brothers and the twin of [[nakula]]. He is a gifted astrologer and possessor of immense wisdom.

## Role in this knowledge base
Presented as a "quiet genius" in [[mahabharata_moments]], illustrating the burden of knowing the future while being unable to change it.

## Key facts
- Foresaw the events of the Mahabharata war but was bound to silence by fate.
