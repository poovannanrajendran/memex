## Overview
Helical bevel gears combine the characteristics of helical and bevel gears, featuring curved teeth that mesh smoothly. They are used for transmitting power between intersecting shafts.

## Role in this knowledge base
Presented as a combination of helical and bevel gear properties for smooth power transfer on intersecting axes.

## Key facts
- Is a conical gear with angled teeth that combines helical and bevel gear advantages for smooth power transfer.
