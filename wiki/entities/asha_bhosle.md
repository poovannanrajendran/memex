## Overview
Asha Bhosle is a legendary Indian playback singer who has recorded thousands of songs in various Indian languages. She is known for her versatility and has sung in a wide range of genres, from film music to pop.

## Role in this knowledge base
She provided vocals for the song 'Le Gayi' from 'Dil To Pagal Hai'.

## Key facts
- She is one of the most prolific and versatile playback singers in Indian cinema.
