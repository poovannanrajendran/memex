## Overview
Duolingo is a popular language-learning platform that uses gamification to make learning engaging and addictive. It offers courses in numerous languages through a website and mobile app.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a popular but less efficient language learning app for travelers, as it focuses heavily on reading and writing over conversational skills.

## Key facts
- After studying Portuguese daily for over a month, the user only knew phrases like 'The cat is under the table'.
