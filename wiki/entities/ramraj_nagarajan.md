## Overview
Ramraj Nagarajan is the founder of the Ramraj Group, a leading Indian textile conglomerate. He is widely recognised for establishing the highly successful Ramraj Veshti brand, which has become synonymous with traditional Indian attire.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary subject of the interview, sharing his experiences and insights into building his business empire.

## Key facts
- He founded the Ramraj Group and pioneered the mass-market branding of the veshti.
