Claude AI is a conversational AI model developed by Anthropic. It is designed to understand and generate human-like text, assist with various tasks, and engage in dialogue.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary tool used in the video for job searching by leveraging its AI capabilities to process resumes and find matching job postings.
