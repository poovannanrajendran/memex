## Overview
Intel Corporation is a multinational semiconductor chip manufacturer known for its central processing units (CPUs). In the context of data science laptops, Intel processors like Core Ultra and HX series are discussed for their performance capabilities.

## Role in this knowledge base
A major provider of laptop processors, with specific models like Core Ultra 9 and HX series recommended for data scientists requiring higher computing power.

## Key facts
- Laptops with Intel Core Ultra 9 or Intel HX processors are recommended for data scientists with potentially higher computing needs.
