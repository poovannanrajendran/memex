## Overview
The BMW iX is a battery electric mid-size luxury crossover SUV produced by BMW. It represents BMW's entry into the premium electric SUV segment, focusing on design, technology, and electric performance.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a class-leading all-electric SUV that the Kia EV9 is being compared against.

## Key facts
- It is BMW's first dedicated electric SUV offering.
