## Overview
A town in Tamil Nadu, India, famous for the Ranganathaswamy Temple, a significant Vaishnavite shrine dedicated to Lord Vishnu. It is considered one of the most important pilgrimage sites for Hindus.

## Role in this knowledge base
The destination of the procession and the setting for the divine events described.

## Key facts
- It is home to one of the largest functioning Hindu temples in the world.
