## Overview
China is a country in East Asia, known for its significant infrastructure projects and vast landmass, including challenging desert regions.

## Role in this knowledge base
The entity responsible for the construction and maintenance of the longest desert highway and its protective forest.

## Key facts
- China built a 565 km desert highway, known as the longest desert highway in the world.
