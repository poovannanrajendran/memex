## Overview
Netrikann is a 2021 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Milind Rau. It stars Nayanthara in the lead role and was produced by Vignesh Shivan's Rowdy Pictures.

## Role in this knowledge base
The film from which the song 'Sudari Sudari' is taken.

## Key facts
- The film is a remake of the 2018 South Korean film 'The Eyes of the Blind'.
