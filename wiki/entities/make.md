## Overview
Make (formerly Integromat) is a platform for visualising, designing, and automating complex workflows. It allows users to connect various apps and services to create sophisticated automated processes without extensive coding.

## Role in this knowledge base
Another essential automation tool for building an AI automation agency, offering advanced workflow capabilities.

## Key facts
- Enables the creation of complex automated workflows visually.
