## Overview
Studio Green is an Indian film production and distribution company, founded by K. E. Gnanavel Raja. It has produced numerous successful Tamil films.

## Role in this knowledge base
Production company for the film "Mr. Local".

## Key facts
- It is one of the leading production houses in the Tamil film industry.
