## Overview
Formula 1 is the highest class of international racing for single-seater formula racing cars, organised by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). It is known for its cutting-edge technology, global appeal, and high-speed competition.

## Role in this knowledge base
This organisation is the subject of the source document, detailing its plan to achieve carbon neutrality.

## Key facts
- Formula 1 has pledged to become carbon neutral by 2030.
