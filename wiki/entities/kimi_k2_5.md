## Overview
Kimi K2.5 is an AI model described as an 'OS of Agents' designed to enhance productivity. It can execute a variety of tasks, from visual-to-code generation to complex research, by deploying specialised AI agents.

## Role in this knowledge base
The central AI product being showcased, representing a new paradigm in productivity through agent swarms.

## Key facts
- It redefines productivity by functioning as an OS of Agents, capable of handling complex tasks like generating extensive literature reviews.
