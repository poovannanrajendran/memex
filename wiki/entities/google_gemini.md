## Overview
Google Gemini is a large language model developed by Google AI, similar in functionality to ChatGPT. It can assist with various text-based tasks, including content generation.

## Role in this knowledge base
Presented as a free alternative to ChatGPT for story and script generation if ChatGPT is unavailable.

## Key facts
- Works similarly to ChatGPT and is free to use.
