## Overview
Google Sheets is a cloud-based spreadsheet application that is part of the Google Docs Editors suite. It allows users to create, edit, and share spreadsheets online, enabling collaboration and data organisation.

## Role in this knowledge base
Serves as the initial trigger for the automation, where new article URLs are added, initiating the content creation process.

## Key facts
- The automation is triggered when a new row (containing an article URL) is added to a specified Google Sheet.
