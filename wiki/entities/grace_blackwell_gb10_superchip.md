## Overview
The Grace Blackwell GB10 is NVIDIA's latest superchip designed for AI and high-performance computing workloads. It integrates advanced GPU and CPU components to deliver significant computational power.

## Role in this knowledge base
The core processing unit of the new NVIDIA Project Digits Mini PC, providing Petaflop-level AI performance.

## Key facts
- The GB10 chip offers a Petaflop of AI computing performance at 4-bit precision.
