## Overview
Carwow is a UK-based online car marketplace that connects consumers with dealerships to buy and sell cars. They offer tools for comparing deals and negotiating prices.

## Role in this knowledge base
The platform and publisher of the review video, promoting its services for car purchasing and selling.

## Key facts
- Carwow offers a service to help consumers get a great price when selling their old car and finding a new one, simplifying the process of haggling and avoiding unpleasant surprises.
