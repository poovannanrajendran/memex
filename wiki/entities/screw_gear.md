## Overview
A screw gear is similar in form to a worm gear but is typically used to connect non-intersecting axes. It offers a compact design solution.

## Role in this knowledge base
Differentiated from worm gears by its application on intersecting axes and its compact design.

## Key facts
- Similar to worm gears but used for intersecting axes, offering a compact design.
