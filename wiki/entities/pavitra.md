## Overview
Pavitra is a colleague of Akshata, who initially appears to give Akshata career advice and suggests opportunities. However, later in the transcript, Pavitra is implicated in giving misleading advice and is the subject of complaints from Akshata and other team members.

## Role in this knowledge base
Pavitra's actions and advice to Akshata are a significant plot point, influencing Akshata's decisions and becoming a subject of scrutiny during the exit interview process.

## Key facts
- Pavitra is mentioned as having given Akshata career advice that may have been self-serving.
