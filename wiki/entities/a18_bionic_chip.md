## Overview
The A18 Bionic is a new processor developed by Apple for its consumer electronics, offering enhanced performance and efficiency.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is the new chip powering the standard iPhone 16 models, providing significant performance improvements.

## Key facts
- The A18 Bionic chip offers a 20-30% performance improvement and a two times faster neural engine compared to the previous generation.
