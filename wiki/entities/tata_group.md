## Overview
The Tata Group is an Indian multinational conglomerate headquartered in Mumbai, India. It is one of India's largest and most respected business conglomerates, with a presence in numerous sectors including steel, automobiles, IT, and hospitality.

## Role in this knowledge base
The organisation with which Ratan Tata is most famously associated, representing the context for his leadership and achievements.

## Key facts
- Ratan Tata served as the chairman of the Tata Group from 1991 to 2012.
