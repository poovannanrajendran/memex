## Overview
The Planar Tracker is a feature within DaVinci Resolve that analyses motion and perspective shifts in video footage. It allows users to track surfaces or planes within a video and attach elements to them.

## Role in this knowledge base
The specific tool within DaVinci Resolve that enables the tracking of elements in the video.

## Key facts
- It tracks planar surfaces, making it suitable for elements that move with the camera's perspective.
