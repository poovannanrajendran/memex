## Overview
Spider-Man is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The character is an orphaned teenager, Peter Parker, who gains superhuman abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider.

## Role in this knowledge base
The character whose role Tom Holland was preparing for, leading to his unique undercover experience.
