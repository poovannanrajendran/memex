## Overview
The Volkswagen ID3 is a compact electric car designed to be a successor to the Golf, offering a practical and efficient electric driving experience.

## Role in this knowledge base
The reviewer suggests looking for the 'Pro S' version with the 77 kWh battery for better range and notes that early software bugs have largely been resolved through updates.

## Key facts
- A 2021 Pro S model with 75,000 miles and 338 miles of range is available for approximately £13,300.
