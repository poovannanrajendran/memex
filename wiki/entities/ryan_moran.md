## Overview
Ryan Moran is an entrepreneur and author who advocates for a customer-centric approach to business. He suggests that understanding the target audience and their desired transformation is key to business success.

## Role in this knowledge base
The video's content is based on Ryan Moran's business philosophy, which is presented as a counterpoint to traditional product-focused entrepreneurship.

## Key facts
- He flips the typical entrepreneurial focus from 'what product to sell' to 'who to serve'.
