## Overview
The DJI Osmo Action 4 is the predecessor to the Action 5 Pro, noted for its balanced lighting and natural colours in normal video mode, but lacking an HDR mode and advanced features found in the new model.

## Role in this knowledge base
Serves as a benchmark for comparison with the newer DJI Action 5 Pro and other action cameras.

## Key facts
- It is the only camera among the tested models that lacks an HDR mode.
