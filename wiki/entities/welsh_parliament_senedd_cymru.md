## Overview
The devolved legislature of Wales. It makes laws for Wales on a range of devolved policy areas.

## Role in this knowledge base
The number of members and the election cycle for the Welsh Parliament are key details required for the Life in the UK test regarding devolved administrations.

## Key facts
- The Welsh Parliament has 60 members and holds elections every four years.
