## Overview
PostHog is an open-source product analytics platform that offers features like user tracking, feature flags, and A/B testing. It is often used as an alternative to proprietary analytics tools.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as an expensive and clunky analytics tool that was difficult to get accurate reports from, leading to its replacement by a custom PostgreSQL and Grafana setup.

## Key facts
- Described as expensive, super clunky, slow, and frustrating to use, often failing to report accurately.
