## Overview
Andreessen Horowitz (often referred to as a16z) is an American venture capital firm. It is known for investing in and advising technology companies.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned for its investment rule of only backing founders with prior experience in the problem they aim to solve, highlighting the value of deep understanding and lived experience.

## Key facts
- They now only invest in founders who already have experience with the problem their company aims to solve.
