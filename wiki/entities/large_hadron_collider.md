## Overview
The Large Hadron Collider is a massive particle accelerator located underground, used to speed up particles to near the speed of light and collide them to study fundamental physics.

## Role in this knowledge base
Provides a point of comparison for the scale and purpose of the new, miniature particle accelerator.

## Key facts
- It is a 17m underground tunnel.
