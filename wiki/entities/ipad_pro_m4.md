## Overview
The 2024 iteration of Apple's high-end tablet, featuring significant design and performance upgrades including an M4 chip and an Ultra Retina XDR display. It is positioned as a premium device with capabilities that challenge traditional laptop functionality.

## Role in this knowledge base

## Key facts
