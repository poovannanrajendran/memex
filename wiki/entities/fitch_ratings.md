## Overview
Fitch Ratings is a leading global provider of credit ratings, commentary, and research for the world's capital markets. They assess the creditworthiness of entities.

## Role in this knowledge base
Fitch Ratings is another key rating agency that provides an independent evaluation of Lloyd's financial health, contributing to market perception and trust.

## Key facts
- Lloyd's holds a financial strength rating of AA- (Very Strong) with a stable outlook from Fitch Ratings.
