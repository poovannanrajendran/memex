## Overview
An integrated development environment (IDE) developed by Apple for creating software for macOS, iOS, iPadOS, tvOS, and watchOS.

## Role in this knowledge base
Considered essential for Mac software development, even for those not directly using its IDE features, due to its integrated system tools.

## Key facts
- It is a large download and can take a significant amount of time to install.
