## Overview
An Italian luxury sports car manufacturer, famous for its racing success and high-performance vehicles.

## Role in this knowledge base
Ford's primary rival in the racing context for which the GT40 was developed.

## Key facts
- Ferrari achieved a 1-2-3 victory in the 1964 race where Ford's GT40s failed.
