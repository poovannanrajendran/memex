## Overview
AirPods Max are Apple's premium over-ear headphones, known for their audio quality and design.

## Role in this knowledge base
The video criticizes the AirPods Max for a lack of significant updates, particularly the retention of a Lightning port.

## Key facts
- The AirPods Max have been updated with a USB-C port and new color options, but retain the Lightning port from previous models and lack an on/off button.
