## Overview
The Ooid 8 series is a computing board presented as an alternative to the Raspberry Pi. It features a modular design with support for RAM, M.2 SSDs, eMMC, and SATA 3 connections, making it suitable for use as a NAS or media server.

## Role in this knowledge base
This is the product being recommended as a superior alternative to the Raspberry Pi.

## Key facts
- It is a modular, bandless design that supports M.2 SSDs and has SATA 3 ports.
