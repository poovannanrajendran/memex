## Overview
Pix4DCapture is a mobile application developed by Pix4D that enables users to plan and execute automated drone flights for mapping and surveying. It works with various drone models and is designed to collect high-quality aerial imagery for photogrammetric processing.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the top drone capture applications reviewed in the video.

## Key facts
- It is designed to generate precise 2D maps and 3D models from drone imagery.
