## Overview
Dogmost is a free and open-source documentation and notes tool that is presented as a self-hostable alternative to Notion's documentation features. It is designed to be simple and effective for managing internal wikis and notes.

## Role in this knowledge base
Replaces the internal wiki and documentation function previously handled by Notion.

## Key facts
- It focuses solely on the documentation and notes aspect, unlike Notion which aims to be an 'everything app'.
