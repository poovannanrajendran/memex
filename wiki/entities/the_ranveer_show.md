## Overview
The Ranveer Show (TRS) is a popular Indian podcast hosted by Ranveer Allahbadia. It features long-form interviews with a diverse range of guests, including entrepreneurs, celebrities, athletes, and authors, delving into topics such as self-improvement, success, mental health, and personal experiences.
