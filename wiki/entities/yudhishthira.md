## Overview
Yudhishthira is the eldest of the five Pandava brothers. He is often referred to as Dharmaraja due to his unwavering commitment to truth and righteousness.

## Role in this knowledge base
A central character explored in the [[mahabharata_moments]] podcast. His early tests of honesty and his fascination with dice are highlighted as foundational to his character arc.

## Key facts
- Passed Dronacharya’s seed test of honesty.
- Represents the theme of [[dharma]] in its most literal form.
