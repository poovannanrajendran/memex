## Overview
The Google App is a mobile application that provides access to Google's search engine and various other services. It often integrates features like augmented reality (AR) experiences and 3D model viewing directly into search results.

## Role in this knowledge base

## Key facts
- It allows users to search for and view 3D models of objects and animals within their real-world environment.
