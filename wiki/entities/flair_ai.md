## Overview
Flair.ai is a web-based tool that uses artificial intelligence to generate product photographs. It allows users to upload product images and place them in various AI-generated scenes and backgrounds.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the free AI product photography programs evaluated in the video.

## Key facts
- It enables users to create professional-looking product shots without traditional photography equipment.
