## Overview
SRAM is a manufacturer of bicycle components, including groupsets (drivetrains), brakes, and suspension. They are known for their innovative electronic shifting systems and a wide range of products for road, mountain, and gravel cycling.

## Role in this knowledge base
SRAM's gravel-specific groupsets, particularly the AXS XPLR line, are central to the author's re-evaluation of 1x drivetrains for gravel bikes.

## Key facts
- SRAM offers electronic groupsets like Red AXS and Force AXS, with the XPLR (Explorer) variant designed specifically for wider-range 1x gravel applications.
