## Overview
Cling is an AI video generation platform from China that offers quality comparable to Runway and Luma Labs. It has a substantial waitlist and requires a Chinese phone number for sign-up.

## Role in this knowledge base
A high-quality AI video generation tool that is difficult to access due to a large waitlist and sign-up restrictions.

## Key facts
- Cling provides AI video generation quality on par with leading Western tools but is hindered by a massive waitlist and complex sign-up process for international users.
