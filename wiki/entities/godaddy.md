## Overview
GoDaddy is one of the largest domain registrars and web hosting companies globally. While it used to charge for privacy protection, it now offers it for free, but may try to upsell other services like 'full domain protection'.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a registrar that now offers free privacy protection, but still attempts to upsell other services.

## Key facts
- GoDaddy offers free privacy protection, which prevents the owner's contact information from being publicly listed in the ICANN lookup database.
