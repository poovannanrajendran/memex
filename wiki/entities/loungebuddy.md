## Overview
LoungeBuddy is an application that helps travelers find and access airport lounges worldwide. Users can input their credit card information or loyalty programs to discover which lounges they are eligible for and get directions to them.

## Role in this knowledge base
Helps travelers find and access airport lounges by identifying eligibility based on credit cards and providing location maps and reviews.

## Key facts
- It tells you exactly which lounges you are allowed to enter and provides a map showing their exact location.
