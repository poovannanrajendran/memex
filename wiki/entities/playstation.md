## Overview
Sony Interactive Entertainment's gaming brand, offering consoles, games, and digital media services. They have faced criticism regarding media ownership and digital rights.

## Role in this knowledge base
Cited as an example of a company revoking access to purchased digital media, illustrating the problems with current digital ownership models and the lack of consumer outcry.

## Key facts
- PlayStation has removed access to some media that users had previously purchased.
