## Overview
"Building a StoryBrand" is a book by Donald Miller that outlines a framework for businesses to communicate their value proposition clearly. It teaches readers to position themselves as the guide in their customer's story, rather than the hero.

## Role in this knowledge base
The core subject of the video, recommended for its utility in idea generation and message crafting.

## Key facts
- It uses a seven-step framework to simplify brand messaging.
