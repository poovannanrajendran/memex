## Overview
Mirofish is an open-source engine designed to simulate a large number of AI agents within a digital environment. Each agent possesses its own memory and personality, allowing for complex emergent behaviours to be modelled.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is the central AI technology discussed in the source, used to simulate market behaviours and predict human reactions to events.
