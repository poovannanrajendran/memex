## Overview
Anand Bakshi was a prolific Indian lyricist who penned lyrics for over 600 Bollywood films. He was known for his simple yet effective writing style and penned some of the most iconic songs in Hindi cinema.

## Role in this knowledge base
He wrote the lyrics for the songs in 'Dil To Pagal Hai', including 'Le Gayi'.

## Key facts
- He was one of the most successful and popular lyricists in Bollywood history.
