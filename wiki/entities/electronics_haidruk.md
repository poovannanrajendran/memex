## Overview
The Electronics Haidruk is a 16-inch laptop offering extreme performance, comparable to the Legion Pro 7i but with the added option of water cooling for even greater performance. It supports up to 96GB of system memory using 48GB memory chips.

## Role in this knowledge base
Represents the pinnacle of performance for 16-inch laptops, designed for users who need the absolute most power available, including advanced cooling solutions.

## Key facts
- It offers the option of water cooling for its components, enabling even higher performance than similarly configured laptops.
