## Overview
K. Manikandan is an Indian screenwriter and actor predominantly working in the Tamil film industry. He is recognised for his roles in films like 'Jai Bhim', 'Good Night', and 'Lover', and for writing dialogues for 'Vikram Vedha'. His career also includes early work as a radio jockey and dubbing artist.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary subject of the interview, discussing his career and creative process.

## Key facts
- He penned the dialogues for the highly successful film 'Vikram Vedha' and also acted in it.
