## Overview
Doom (2016 or Eternal) is a fast-paced first-person shooter known for its demanding graphics and smooth performance scaling. It is often used as a benchmark for CPU and GPU capabilities.

## Role in this knowledge base
Tested on the new workstation, demonstrating excellent performance as expected from the game.

## Key facts
