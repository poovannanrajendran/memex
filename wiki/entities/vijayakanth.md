## Overview
Vijayakanth was a prominent Tamil actor and politician, known for his strong on-screen persona and later as the founder of the Desiya Murpokku Dravida Kazhagam (DMDK) party. He was often referred to as 'Captain'.

## Role in this knowledge base
An exemplar of respectful behaviour towards directors, presented as a role model.

## Key facts
- He was a highly popular actor before entering politics.
