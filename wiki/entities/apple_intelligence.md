## Overview
Apple Intelligence is a new AI platform integrated into Apple devices, designed to enhance user experience through personalized intelligence, context-aware assistance, and generative AI capabilities.

## Role in this knowledge base
A major new AI framework being introduced with the iPhone 16 generation, though not fully available at launch.
