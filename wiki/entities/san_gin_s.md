## Overview
San Ginés is a famous establishment in Madrid, Spain, known for its churros and chocolate. It has a long history, dating back to its opening in 1894, and operates continuously, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

## Role in this knowledge base
The source document centres on the churros served by this organisation, highlighting their fame and the unique preparation process.

## Key facts
- San Ginés was the first establishment to dip churros into chocolate when they opened in 1894.
