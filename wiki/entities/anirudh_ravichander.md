## Overview
Anirudh Ravichander is a highly popular Indian music composer and singer who predominantly works in Tamil cinema. He is known for his energetic and modern musical style that has resonated with younger audiences.

## Role in this knowledge base
Music composer for the 'Chikitu' song and the film 'Coolie'.

## Key facts
- He is one of the most sought-after music directors in the South Indian film industry.
