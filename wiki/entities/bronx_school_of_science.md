## Overview
The Bronx High School of Science is a specialized public high school in the Bronx, New York City, known for its strong academic programs, particularly in science and mathematics, and for admitting gifted students.

## Role in this knowledge base
The institution where Tom Holland attended undercover as a student to prepare for his role as Spider-Man.

## Key facts
- It is described as a school for 'genius kids'.
