## Overview
A British mathematician, computer scientist, logician, cryptanalyst, philosopher, and theoretical biologist. He was highly influential in the development of computer science and artificial intelligence.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as the inventor of the 'Touring machine' (Turing machine), a key 20th-century British invention relevant to the Life in the UK test. His story is also highlighted via the film 'The Imitation Game'.

## Key facts
- He developed the theoretical 'Touring machine' (Turing machine).
