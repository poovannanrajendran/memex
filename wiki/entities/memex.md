## Overview
A personal AI-maintained second brain system being developed by the author to address information overload and fragmented knowledge. It aims to ingest, cross-link, and synthesise information, functioning more like a research librarian than a simple retrieval system.

## Role in this knowledge base
The central product and project being built by the author, inspired by Vannevar Bush's original vision, to create an advanced knowledge management system.

## Key facts
- It is named after Vannevar Bush's hypothetical device and leverages a 1M-token context window and an AI agent as its 'librarian' to extend human memory.
