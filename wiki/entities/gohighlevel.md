## Overview
GoHighLevel is an all-in-one sales and marketing platform that includes tools for CRM, email marketing, website building, and more. It is often used by marketing agencies.

## Role in this knowledge base
A landing page builder where the final AI-generated HTML code is pasted to create a functional website.
