## Overview
Pantic AI is an open-source Python framework specifically designed for building AI agents. It aims to make the development of production-grade agents less painful by including features often overlooked in other frameworks.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary focus of the video, presented as a superior alternative to existing AI agent frameworks.

## Key facts
- It leverages the Pantic validation library at its core for robust data handling and validation within agents.
