## Overview
The Grace Hopper Superchip is a high-performance computing platform from NVIDIA that combines the Grace CPU and Hopper GPU on a single module, designed for AI and HPC workloads.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a predecessor or related product to the Grace Blackwell Superchip, highlighting NVIDIA's development in integrated CPU-GPU solutions.

## Key facts
- NVIDIA uses its proprietary NVLink chip-to-chip interconnect in products like the Grace Hopper Superchip, a technology also present in the Grace Blackwell architecture.
