## Overview
A British Overseas Territory in the South Atlantic Ocean, known for its remote location and unique wildlife.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as an example of a British Overseas Territory, to differentiate it from Crown Dependencies in the Life in the UK test.

## Key facts
- It is located off the coast of Africa.
