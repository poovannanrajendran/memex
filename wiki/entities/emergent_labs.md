## Overview
Emergent Labs is a company founded by Mukund and Madav that has developed a platform for building applications using AI. Their approach focuses on creating full-stack, production-ready applications rapidly from simple English descriptions.

## Role in this knowledge base
The company is a new entrant in the AI building space, attracting significant attention for its innovative approach to application development.

## Key facts
- Emergent Labs built over 10,000 apps in just two weeks.
