## Overview
The Hyundai Kona Electric is a compact electric SUV that offers a good balance of range, practicality, and value.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended for buyers seeking an EV with decent range that is slightly more SUV-like and less minimalist than a Tesla Model 3.

## Key facts
- A 2020 model with 73,000 miles and 279 miles of range can be purchased for under £10,000.
