## Overview
Bluehost is a well-known web hosting provider that also offers domain registration. It typically provides a free domain for the first year, but charges for renewals and privacy protection.

## Role in this knowledge base
Cited as an example of a hosting company with a common upsell tactic of a free domain for the first year followed by higher renewal fees and additional costs for privacy protection.

## Key facts
- Renewal costs for Bluehost domains can be around $19.99 per year, with an additional $15 per year for privacy protection.
