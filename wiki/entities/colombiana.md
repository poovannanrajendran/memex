## Overview
Colombiana is a 2011 American action thriller film directed by Olivier Megaton and produced by Luc Besson. The film stars Zoe Saldana as Cataleya Restrepo, a young woman who grows up to be a trained killer and joins her uncle's criminal organization in Chicago, while also working as a hitwoman to avenge the murder of her parents.
