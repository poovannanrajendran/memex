## Overview
General Purpose Input/Output pins are a fundamental feature of microcontrollers and single-board computers like the Raspberry Pi. They allow the device to interact with external electronic components, sensors, and actuators.

## Role in this knowledge base
Highlighted as a key feature that makes the Raspberry Pi versatile for hardware projects.

## Key facts
- GPIO pins are one of the Raspberry Pi's superpowers, enabling interaction with a wide range of electronic components.
