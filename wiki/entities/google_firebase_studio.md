## Overview
Firebase Studio is a web-based AI application builder developed by Google. It aims to streamline the entire development lifecycle for web and mobile apps by integrating AI agents for building both front-end and back-end components.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary subject of the analysis, evaluated for its features, usability, and potential as an AI app builder.
