## Overview
Overseas Citizens of India are individuals of Indian origin who hold an Indian passport but are citizens of another country. They are granted certain privileges in India.

## Role in this knowledge base
Eligible to use the FTITP programme for faster airport transit.

## Key facts
- OCI holders can access the FTITP service free of charge.
