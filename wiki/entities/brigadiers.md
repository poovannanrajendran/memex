## Overview
Brigadiers is an Indian restaurant located in London, known for its 'Desi pub' concept and offering a range of Indian dishes with a focus on grilled and smoked meats. It is part of the JKS Restaurants group.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary subject of the review, being assessed for its quality as a 'Desi pub'.

## Key facts
- Owned by the same group as Gymkhana.
