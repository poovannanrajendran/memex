## Overview
Google Domains was a domain name registrar service offered by Google, known for its straightforward pricing and included privacy protection. However, it ceased operations as a standalone registrar.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a previously good option for low-cost domains with free privacy protection, but it is no longer selling domains as it was sold to Squarespace.

## Key facts
- Google Domains was sold to Squarespace in September 2023.
