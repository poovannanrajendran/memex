## Overview
AirPods are Apple's popular line of wireless Bluetooth earbuds. The video discusses updates to both the base AirPods and AirPods Pro.

## Role in this knowledge base
The video covers new models of AirPods, detailing design changes, feature additions like active noise cancellation, and certifications.

## Key facts
- The new base AirPods (AirPods 4) will be available in two versions: one with a standard case and one with active noise cancellation and a wireless charging case with a Find My speaker.
