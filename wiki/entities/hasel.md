## Overview
Hasel is a budgeting app designed to help users track their expenses. It supports tracking in multiple currencies, which is particularly useful for travelers managing finances in different countries.

## Role in this knowledge base
A budgeting app for tracking expenses in multiple currencies.

## Key facts
- It is often used in its free version and works well for managing travel finances.
