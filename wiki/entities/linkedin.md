## Overview
LinkedIn is a social media platform focused on professional networking and career development. Users can create profiles, connect with colleagues, share industry news, and search for job opportunities.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the key data sources integrated by Happenstance to build a comprehensive professional profile.
