## Overview
Anker is a company that produces charging accessories, mobile power banks, and other consumer electronics. They are known for their quality and affordability.

## Role in this knowledge base
Manufacturer of the Anker 555 USB-C Hub, recommended for expanding MacBook connectivity.

## Key facts
- The Anker 555 USB-C Hub supports 4K 60Hz output via HDMI.
