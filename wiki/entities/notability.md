## Overview
Notability is another well-known note-taking application for iPad and macOS, offering features like handwriting, typing, audio recording synchronization, and PDF annotation.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned alongside GoodNotes as an example of an iPad note-taking app where digital planners and journaling templates can be utilized.
