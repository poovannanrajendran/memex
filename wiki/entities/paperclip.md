## Overview
Paperclip is an open-source AI agent orchestration layer designed to create an office environment for AI agents. It aims to enhance productivity by organising and managing these agents.

## Role in this knowledge base
Paperclip is presented as a tool to boost productivity for individuals and small businesses by providing an 'office environment' for AI agents.

## Key facts
- Paperclip is open-source and can be installed in 60 seconds.
