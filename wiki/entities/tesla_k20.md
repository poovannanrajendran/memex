## Overview
The Nvidia Tesla K20 was a professional compute accelerator card designed for servers and workstations, often used for scientific simulations and GPGPU tasks. It was based on the Kepler architecture.

## Role in this knowledge base
An older GPU that the creator attempted to use in the Dell Precision 7820 but found it incompatible and unable to post.

## Key facts
- A Tesla K20 GPU was tested but did not work with the Dell Precision 7820 workstation, preventing the system from posting.
