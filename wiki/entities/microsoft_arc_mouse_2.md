## Overview
The Microsoft Arc Mouse 2 is a portable and uniquely designed wireless mouse. It is praised for its functionality and portability, especially when paired with an iPad.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended as a portable mouse that significantly enhances iPad functionality, offering a less expensive alternative to Apple's keyboard options when paired with a Bluetooth keyboard.

## Key facts
- Offers excellent functionality and portability for mobile use.
