## Overview
The Apple M4 Pro is a powerful System on a Chip (SoC) designed by Apple, part of their 'Moment 4' generation. It features a 12-core CPU with high clock speeds and is built on a 3nm process.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary competitor chip to the AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395, used in the MacBook Pro for performance comparisons.

## Key facts
- It has a 12-core CPU with high clock speeds and is built on a 3nm process.
