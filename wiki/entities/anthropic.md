## Overview
Anthropic is an AI safety and research company that developed the Claude family of large language models.

## Role in this knowledge base
Anthropic designed and released the Model Context Protocol (MCP) as a standard for LLM communication.

## Key facts
- Anthropic is the creator of the Claude models and the designer of the MCP.
