## Overview
Hendra is an AI lip-syncing tool that generates expressive talking avatars from uploaded characters and audio. It offers a free tier for users to experiment with generating speech onto various character models.

## Role in this knowledge base
A lip-syncing tool notable for its expressive talking avatars, though it can struggle with non-human characters.

## Key facts
- Hendra can produce highly expressive lip-syncing for human avatars but faces challenges with non-human or less human-like characters.
