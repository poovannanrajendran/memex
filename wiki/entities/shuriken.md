## Overview
Shuriken are traditional Japanese weapons, often referred to as 'throwing stars' or 'ninja stars'. They are typically small, handheld implements designed for throwing, and can vary greatly in shape and size.

## Role in this knowledge base
A type of weapon included in the presenter's collection and mentioned in the video's hashtag.

## Key facts
- Shuriken were often used for distraction or as secondary weapons, rather than primary offensive tools.
