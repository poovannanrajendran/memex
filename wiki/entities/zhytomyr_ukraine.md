## Overview
Zhytomyr is a city in northern Ukraine, serving as the administrative centre of Zhytomyr Oblast. It is situated on the Teteriv River.

## Role in this knowledge base
The location where the Large Conehead was discovered.

## Key facts
- It is a significant regional administrative and cultural centre in Ukraine.
