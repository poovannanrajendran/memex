## Overview
A digital audio workstation (DAW) for macOS, developed by Apple. It is used for music production, recording, editing, and mixing audio.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as a benchmark to test the performance limits of the M4 Mac Mini models, particularly in handling a large number of tracks and third-party plugins.

## Key facts
- The base model M4 Mac Mini started to struggle past 150 very complex tracks in Logic Pro.
