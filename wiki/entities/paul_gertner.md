## Overview
Paul Gertner is a magician known for his contributions to card magic, particularly his teachings on the faro shuffle.

## Role in this knowledge base
Creator and teacher of the card trick routine.

## Key facts
- He is credited by Nate Jester as the source of his teachings on this trick.
