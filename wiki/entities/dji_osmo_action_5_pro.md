## Overview
The DJI Osmo Action 5 Pro is the latest action camera from DJI, featuring a new sensor and processor, built-in storage, advanced HDR capabilities, and superior battery life compared to its predecessors and competitors.

## Role in this knowledge base
A new generation action camera offering significant upgrades and unique features for vlogging and general use.

## Key facts
- It is the only action camera with built-in storage, eliminating the need for a memory card.
