## Overview
Yash Chopra was an iconic Indian film director and producer, renowned for his romantic dramas and visually rich filmmaking. He was the co-founder of Yash Raj Films and is considered one of the most influential figures in Indian cinema.

## Role in this knowledge base
He directed and produced 'Dil To Pagal Hai', shaping its romantic narrative and musical themes.

## Key facts
- He was a pioneer of the romantic genre in Bollywood.
