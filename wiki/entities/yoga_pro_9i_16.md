## Overview
The Yoga Pro 9i 16 is a high-performance Windows laptop with a mini-LED display that is high-resolution, bright, and has a fast refresh rate. It can be configured with up to an RTX 4070 GPU and 64GB of memory in some regions.

## Role in this knowledge base
An alternative to the ProArt P16 for those prioritising display quality (brightness, refresh rate) over portability, especially for users who need high-end graphical capabilities.

## Key facts
- Its mini-LED panel is high-resolution, bright, and features a fast refresh rate, making it excellent for data visualisation and high-performance tasks.
