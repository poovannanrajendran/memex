## Overview
Perplexity.ai is an AI-powered search engine and conversational AI that differentiates itself by its ability to access and process real-time web information. It provides summarised answers and insights derived from current online content.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used in the automation workflow to read and summarise new articles from the web, providing the core content for social media posts.

## Key facts
- It can access the web in real-time, providing more up-to-date information compared to LLMs with fixed knowledge cut-off dates.
