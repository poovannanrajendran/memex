## Overview
An English architect who was a significant figure in English Baroque architecture. He was a close associate and pupil of Sir Christopher Wren.

## Role in this knowledge base
Collaborator with Christopher Wren on the design of the Triforium Library.

## Key facts
- He was Wren's protégé and master craftsman.
