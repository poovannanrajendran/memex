## Overview
A self-governing British Crown Dependency located in the Irish Sea between England and Ireland. It has its own laws and government.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recognised as a Crown Dependency, a category important for differentiating from British Overseas Territories in the Life in the UK test.

## Key facts
- It is a self-governing British Crown Dependency in the Irish Sea.
