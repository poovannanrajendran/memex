## Overview
The Ramraj Group is an Indian conglomerate with a significant presence in the textile industry. It is best known for its dominant position in the market for veshtis and other traditional clothing.

## Role in this knowledge base
The business entity founded by Ramraj Nagarajan, whose success and legacy are the focus of the interview.

## Key facts
- The group has diversified interests but is most famous for its 'Ramraj Veshti' brand.
