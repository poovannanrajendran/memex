## Overview
Microsoft Teams is a unified communication and collaboration platform that combines chat, video meetings, file storage, and application integration.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used for team collaboration, managing shared tasks, and coordinating projects.

## Key facts
- Supports 'Loop tasks' for simple group task sharing and integrates with Planner for project management.
