## Overview
Bubble is a popular no-code development platform that allows users to build web applications without writing code. It requires users to understand database structures and workflow logic.

## Role in this knowledge base
A competitor to Base44, used as a point of comparison to highlight Base44's advantages in ease of use and integration.

## Key facts
- Bubble, unlike Base44, requires users to understand databases and workflows to build applications.
