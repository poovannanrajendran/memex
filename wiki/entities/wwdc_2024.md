## Overview
Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) is an annual event hosted by Apple Inc. for developers to learn about the latest advancements in Apple's platforms and technologies.

## Role in this knowledge base
The event where Apple Intelligence was first announced.

## Key facts
- Apple Intelligence was announced at WWDC 2024, nearly six months before the initial rollout.
