## Overview
Apple Inc. is a multinational technology company renowned for its consumer electronics, software, and online services. Its product line includes the iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, and Apple TV, alongside software such as iOS, macOS, and the Apple Intelligence platform.

## Role in this knowledge base
The manufacturer of the iPhones being reviewed, and the developer of the new Apple Intelligence features.

## Key facts
- Apple is positioning the iPhone 16 generation as the 'AI generation' with the introduction of Apple Intelligence.
