## Overview
The Magic Keyboard Folio is another keyboard accessory for iPads, offering a trackpad and keys similar to the Magic Keyboard but with a detachable design. The keyboard portion detaches from the back cover.

## Role in this knowledge base
Presented as an alternative to the Magic Keyboard, offering similar typing quality with the bonus of a detachable design for maintaining tablet functionality.

## Key facts
- The keyboard detaches from the back cover, allowing for a traditional tablet carrying system.
