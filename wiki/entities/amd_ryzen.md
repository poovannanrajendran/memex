## Overview
AMD Ryzen is a brand of central processing units (CPUs) designed by AMD for the consumer market. They are known for their multi-core performance and are popular choices for gaming and productivity PCs.

## Role in this knowledge base
The CPUs around which the motherboard selection revolves.

## Key facts
- Ryzen CPUs utilise the AM4 and AM5 socket types, requiring compatible motherboards.
