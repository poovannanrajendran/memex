## Overview
Fix-a-Flat is a tire sealant and inflator product designed for temporary repair of punctures up to a quarter-inch in diameter. It claims to be non-flammable and safe for tires.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the tire repair products tested for its effectiveness, ease of use, and potential side effects.

## Key facts
- Fix-a-Flat failed to adequately seal punctures during the testing, even after multiple applications.
