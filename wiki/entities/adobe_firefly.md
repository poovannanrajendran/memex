## Overview
Adobe Firefly is a family of creative generative AI models developed by Adobe. It is designed to be commercially safe and integrated into Adobe's creative products, allowing users to generate images, text effects, and other creative assets using natural language prompts.

## Role in this knowledge base
The core AI technology demonstrated in the video for generating and editing product images within Photoshop.

## Key facts
- It is trained on Adobe Stock images and openly licensed content, aiming to ensure copyright compliance.
