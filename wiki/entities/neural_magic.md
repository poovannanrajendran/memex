## Overview
Neural Magic is a company that develops software to optimize neural networks, enabling them to run efficiently on CPUs. Their technology aims to make AI more accessible and deployable on standard hardware.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as an example of innovation in AI, specifically for sparsifying neural networks to run on CPUs, thus making powerful AI models more accessible.

## Key facts
- Their software allows neural networks to be sparsified, making them runnable on CPUs.
