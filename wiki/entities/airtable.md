## Overview
Airtable is a cloud collaboration service that combines the features of a spreadsheet with the power of a database. It is highly flexible for organizing data and managing projects.

## Role in this knowledge base
A previously used tool for database management, replaced by NoodlDB for cost and self-hosting benefits.

## Key facts
- While a 'wonderful tool' with fair pricing, it was replaced as part of a broader initiative to adopt self-hostable alternatives.
