## Overview
LTX2 was a previous iteration of an open-source AI video generation model, noted for its popularity and high download rates.

## Role in this knowledge base
It serves as the predecessor to LTX 2.3, establishing a foundation and user base that informed the development of the newer version.

## Key facts
- LTX2 was one of the most downloaded open-source video models ever.
