## Overview
Open Montage is an AI-powered, agentic video production system that creates videos from plain English descriptions. It automates the entire video production workflow, including research, scripting, voice-over, visuals, and editing.

## Role in this knowledge base
This is the primary tool being discussed, representing a novel application of AI in automated content creation.

## Key facts
- It is described as the world's first agentic video production system.
