## Overview
Ramraj Veshti is a well-known brand of the traditional Indian lower garment, the veshti, manufactured and marketed by the Ramraj Group. It has achieved widespread recognition and market penetration across India.

## Role in this knowledge base
The central product and brand discussed in the interview, representing the core business of Ramraj Nagarajan.

## Key facts
- It is one of the most recognised brands for veshtis in India, known for its quality and accessibility.
