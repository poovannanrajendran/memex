## Overview
Kailash Nad is the CTO of Zerodha. He is the creator of the OUI UI library, which he developed to address issues of bloat and complexity in modern web development frameworks.

## Role in this knowledge base
Developer of OUI, CTO of Zerodha, promoting a philosophy of lightweight infrastructure and efficient development.

## Key facts
- He launched OUI, a minimal UI library designed to remove JavaScript bloat.
