## Overview
The Range Rover Evoque is a subcompact luxury crossover SUV produced by Jaguar Land Rover. Known for its stylish design, it shares its platform with the Land Rover Freelander 2.

## Role in this knowledge base
The subject of the video, bought cheaply and subsequently refurbished. Its purchase and repair journey exemplifies the financial risks associated with acquiring used luxury vehicles.
