## Overview
Michael Jackson was a globally renowned American singer, songwriter, and dancer, widely regarded as one of the most significant cultural figures of the 20th century. He achieved unparalleled success in pop music and is often referred to as the "King of Pop."

## Role in this knowledge base
The source highlights Michael Jackson's exceptional musical talent and creative process, specifically his ability to conceptualise and perform complex vocal arrangements for his song "Beat It" on a demo recording, despite not being proficient with musical instruments.

## Key facts
- Michael Jackson could not play musical instruments but could vocally arrange and perform all parts for a song's demo.
