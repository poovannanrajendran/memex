## Overview
Cisco Smart Spaces is a solution that utilizes location services and IoT devices within an environment to provide enhanced user experiences and operational insights. It can enable features like indoor navigation and space utilisation analysis.

## Role in this knowledge base
This product is given as an example application of the Ultra-wideband technology found in some of Cisco's new access points.

## Key facts
- Cisco Smart Spaces can use QR codes to provide directions within an office.
