## Overview
The Dwarkadhish Temple, also known as Jagat Mandir, is a Hindu temple dedicated to the god Krishna, who is worshipped here as Dwarkadhish, the 'King of Dwarka'. It is located in Dwarka, Gujarat, India. The temple is a revered pilgrimage site and a significant example of Indian temple architecture.
