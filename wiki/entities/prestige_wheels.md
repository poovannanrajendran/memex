## Overview
Prestige Wheels appears to be a specialist wheel refurbishment service. The presenter uses their services to restore the damaged wheels of the Range Rover Evoque.

## Role in this knowledge base
Provided a professional service to refurbish the kerbed wheels of the Evoque, a necessary cosmetic and functional repair.

## Key facts
- Prestige Wheels successfully refurbished the severely kerbed Evoque wheels to a 'good as new' condition.
