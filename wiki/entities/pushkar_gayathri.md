## Overview
Pushkar Ram Mohan Rao and Gayathri Raghuram are an Indian filmmaker duo who primarily work in Tamil cinema. They are known for their distinctive directorial style, with 'Vikram Vedha' being one of their most successful films.

## Role in this knowledge base
Directors of 'Vikram Vedha', for which K. Manikandan wrote dialogues.

## Key facts
- They wrote and directed the critically acclaimed film 'Vikram Vedha'.
