## Overview
Sanbox is an AI-powered tool for email management that automatically sorts and prioritizes emails in your inbox. It can be trained on the types of emails that matter most to the user.

## Role in this knowledge base
Automates email organization and prioritization, saving users time previously spent on inbox management.

## Key facts
- It is described as being very good right out of the box, requiring minimal training.
