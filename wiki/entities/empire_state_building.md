## Overview
A 102-story Art Deco skyscraper in Midtown Manhattan, New York City. Completed in 1931, it was the world's tallest building for nearly 40 years.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as a historical benchmark to illustrate older skyscraper construction and weight.

## Key facts
- It was once the world's tallest building.
