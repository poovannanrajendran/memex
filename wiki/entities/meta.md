## Overview
Meta Platforms, Inc. is an American multinational technology conglomerate headquartered in Menlo Park, California. It is the parent company of Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Oculus (now Meta Quest).

## Role in this knowledge base
The manufacturer of the Quest 3 and Quest 3S virtual reality headsets.

## Key facts
- Meta's virtual reality division was formerly known as Oculus.
