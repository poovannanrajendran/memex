## Overview
CarVertical is an online service that provides vehicle history reports by checking databases across numerous countries. It helps buyers identify issues such as theft, accident damage, mileage discrepancies, and outstanding finance.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used in the video to perform a crucial vehicle history check on the purchased Range Rover Evoque, confirming it was not stolen, written off, or subject to mileage rollback or finance issues.

## Key facts
- CarVertical checks databases in 35 different countries.
