## Overview
Viva Daily Briefing is an AI-powered email sent by Outlook that highlights commitments, follow-ups, and tasks derived from email communications.

## Role in this knowledge base
Leverages Outlook's AI to identify potential tasks and commitments from emails, prompting users to add them to their task list or mark them as completed.

## Key facts
- Analyses email communications to identify promises and commitments, suggesting actions within the email.
