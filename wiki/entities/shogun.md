## Overview
Shogun is another page builder tool for Shopify, similar to PageFly, that assists e-commerce businesses in creating custom page layouts and improving their online store's aesthetic and functionality.

## Role in this knowledge base
Shogun is mentioned as one of the popular page builder alternatives available on the Shopify App Store, alongside PageFly.

## Key facts
- It is listed as one of the most popular page builder tools for Shopify.
