## Overview
Motion is an AI tool designed for calendar and project management. It prioritizes tasks, time-blocks them on the user's calendar, and dynamically optimizes the schedule throughout the day.

## Role in this knowledge base
Manages calendar and project tasks by automatically prioritizing, time-blocking, and optimizing schedules, reducing the need for manual coordination and planning.

## Key facts
- It dynamically optimizes your schedule throughout the day all automatically.
