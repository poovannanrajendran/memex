## Overview
Sylli.io is an AI video generator that allows users to paste scripts and create videos. A key feature highlighted is its ability to schedule created videos up to two weeks in advance.

## Role in this knowledge base
This tool is used to generate the video content from the respun scripts and to efficiently schedule the content for publication.

## Key facts
- It enables scheduling content up to two weeks in advance, facilitating bulk content creation.
