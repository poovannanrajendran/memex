## Overview
Anuradha Goyal is an Indian author and the founder of IndiTales. She is recognised for her extensive travel and writing focused on ancient Indian history, spirituality, and cultural heritage. Her work often explores the spiritual and historical significance of ancient sites across India.

## Role in this knowledge base
Guest on The Ranveer Show, sharing her personal experiences and insights on spirituality and travel in India, including her visit to the Ram Janmabhoomi Temple.

## Key facts
- She is the author of IndiTales, a platform dedicated to exploring India's history and spirituality through travel writing.
