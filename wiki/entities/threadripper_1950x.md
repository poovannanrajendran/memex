## Overview
The AMD Ryzen Threadripper 1950X is an earlier generation HEDT processor with 16 cores and 32 threads. It was a powerful option for workstations and demanding tasks.

## Role in this knowledge base
Another high-end comparison point for the dual Xeon build, which the Xeon CPUs significantly outperform financially and in core count.

## Key facts
- The dual Xeon Gold CPUs in the build provide a significant core count advantage over a Threadripper 1950X for a much lower combined cost.
