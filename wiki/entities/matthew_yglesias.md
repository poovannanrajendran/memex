## Overview
Matthew Yglesias is an American journalist and commentator who writes about politics, economics, and technology. He is known for his prolific writing and engagement with public discourse.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned for his writings on media ownership and related topics, specifically referencing his books 'Chokepoint Capitalism' and 'The Rentier State' (though the specific title mentioned is 'Seize the Means of Computation').

## Key facts
- He has written extensively on media ownership and corporate power in books like 'Chokepoint Capitalism'.
