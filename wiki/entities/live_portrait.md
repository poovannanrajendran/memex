## Overview
Live Portrait is an AI tool, available on Hugging Face, that animates a source portrait using a driving video. It allows users to map facial expressions and movements from one video onto a still image.

## Role in this knowledge base
A tool for animating static portraits using a reference video, offering a degree of control over expressiveness.

## Key facts
- Live Portrait works best when mapping facial movements from a straight-on driving video onto a source portrait, though it can struggle with significant head movements or non-human subjects.
