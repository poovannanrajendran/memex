## Overview
Webflow is a visual web design tool that allows users to create and launch websites. It is known for enabling the creation of complex and responsive designs without extensive coding knowledge.

## Role in this knowledge base
A platform recommended for finding inspiration and examples of premium website designs that can be used as templates.

## Key facts
- Offers a wide variety of premium design templates for inspiration.
