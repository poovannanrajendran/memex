## Overview
A legal principle that allows the owner of a copyrighted work to sell or otherwise dispose of that particular copy of the work without the permission of the copyright holder. This applies to physical media like books and CDs.

## Role in this knowledge base
Discussed as a legal concept that should be extended to digital media to protect consumers' rights to own and resell their digital purchases, contrasting with current practices in streaming and digital licensing.

## Key facts
- It is a legal principle that allows the resale of physical copyrighted items, which advocates argue should be applied to digital media.
