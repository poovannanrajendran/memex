## Overview
Peter Lynch is a renowned American investor and fund manager. He is famous for his tenure at the Magellan Fund at Fidelity Investments, where he achieved exceptional returns. His investment philosophy often emphasizes investing in what you know.

## Role in this knowledge base
Author of 'One Up on Wall Street', offering insights into investment strategies.

## Key facts
- Managed the Magellan Fund from 1977 to 1990, turning it into the best-performing mutual fund in the world.
