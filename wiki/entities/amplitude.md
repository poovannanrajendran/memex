## Overview
Amplitude is a product analytics platform that provides tools for understanding user behavior and product performance. It is known for its comprehensive analytics capabilities.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as another expensive and clunky analytics tool that is difficult to get accurate reports from, leading to its replacement by a custom PostgreSQL and Grafana setup.

## Key facts
- Like PostHog, it is described as expensive and frustrating, with challenges in achieving accurate reporting.
