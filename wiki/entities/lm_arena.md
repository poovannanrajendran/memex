## Overview
LM Arena is a platform that enables side-by-side or direct comparison of different AI models through chat interactions, with user ratings contributing to leaderboards.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is offered as another tool to compare AI models by allowing users to chat with them and view leaderboards based on user feedback.

## Key facts
- Users can compare models by chatting with them side-by-side and contributing to a leaderboard.
