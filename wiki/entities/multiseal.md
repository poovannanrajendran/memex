## Overview
MultiSeal is a tire sealant that utilizes DuPont Kevlar and is specifically designed for off-road use, claiming to fix holes up to half-inch in diameter and last for over 10 years. It is advertised for easy cleanup with water.

## Role in this knowledge base
A high-performance tire sealant tested primarily for its robust sealing capabilities in off-road scenarios and its longevity.

## Key facts
- MultiSeal successfully sealed a 3/8 inch spike puncture, exceeding its advertised capability for smaller holes.
