## Overview
Ben Horowitz is an American entrepreneur, investor, and author. He is a co-founder of Andreessen Horowitz, a venture capital firm, and has founded and scaled several tech companies.

## Role in this knowledge base
He is the author of the book 'The Hard Thing About Hard Things', recommended by Ritesh Agarwal.

## Key facts
- He is a co-founder of Andreessen Horowitz.
