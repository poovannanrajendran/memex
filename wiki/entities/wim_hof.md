## Overview
Wim Hof is a Dutch extreme athlete known for his method of using controlled exposure to cold, breathing techniques, and commitment to training to improve physical and mental health. His method has gained popularity for its potential benefits in stress reduction, immune system support, and increased energy levels.

## Role in this knowledge base
The technique of using an ice bath for cooling boiled eggs is referenced, likely inspired by Wim Hof's cold exposure methods.

## Key facts
- He is known for his 'Wim Hof Method' which involves cold therapy.
