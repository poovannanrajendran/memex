## Overview
Flaxseed, also known as linseed, is a small seed from the plant Linum usitatissimum. It is a rich source of omega-3 fatty acids, fibre, and lignans, and is often consumed as a powder or oil.

## Role in this knowledge base
An ingredient in the detox drink, added as powder.

## Key facts
- Flaxseed is added to the drink in powder form.
