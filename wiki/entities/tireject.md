## Overview
TireJect is a tire sealant designed for both on-road and off-road applications, capable of sealing bead leaks and punctures up to a quarter-inch. The kit includes sealant, an applicator, and tools.

## Role in this knowledge base
A tire sealant product tested for its effectiveness in sealing different types of tire damage and its suitability for various driving conditions.

## Key facts
- TireJect effectively sealed punctures and maintained tire pressure after being subjected to a quarter-inch bolt.
