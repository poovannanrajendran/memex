## Overview
The Samsung S23 Ultra is a flagship smartphone manufactured by Samsung, part of their Galaxy S series. It is known for its large display, advanced camera system, S Pen stylus, and powerful Android performance. It is one of the two devices being compared for battery life.

## Role in this knowledge base
