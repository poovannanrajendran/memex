## Overview
Thomas Shelby is the protagonist of the BBC historical crime drama "Peaky Blinders." He is the leader of the Peaky Blinders gang, known for his intelligence, ruthlessness, strategic mind, and complex moral code as he navigates the post-World War I era in Birmingham.

## Role in this knowledge base
Thomas Shelby is often presented as a quintessential 'sigma male' in online discussions, admired for his independence, strategic prowess, leadership qualities, and enigmatic nature.

## Key facts
- He is the central character and leader of the Peaky Blinders gang.
