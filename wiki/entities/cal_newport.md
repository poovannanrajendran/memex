## Overview
Cal Newport is an American author and computer science professor at Georgetown University. He is known for his books on productivity and the impact of technology on cognitive skills.

## Role in this knowledge base
Author of the book 'Deep Work', which provided the framework for the speaker to build their coding habit.

## Key facts
- He advocates for structured approaches to combat distraction and enhance focus in a technology-saturated world.
