## Overview
Micro Center is a specialty retailer of computer hardware and consumer electronics, known for its wide selection of PC components, maker supplies, and knowledgeable staff.

## Role in this knowledge base
Sponsor of the video and the primary location for purchasing Raspberry Pi components and accessories.

## Key facts
- Micro Center has a dedicated 'maker section' where Raspberry Pi products are prominently featured.
