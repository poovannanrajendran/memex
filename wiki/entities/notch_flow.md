## Overview
Notch Flow is an application that transforms the MacBook's notch into a dynamic island, similar to a feature on some iPhones. It offers additional functionalities like playing music and setting timers.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is used to enhance the user interface and functionality of the MacBook by repurposing the notch.

## Key facts
- Transforms the MacBook notch into a dynamic island for music and timers.
