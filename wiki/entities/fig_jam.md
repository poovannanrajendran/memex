## Overview
Fig Jam is a collaborative whiteboarding tool offered by Figma. It is designed to facilitate brainstorming, ideation, and team discussions alongside design work.

## Role in this knowledge base
Fig Jam is presented as an example of Figma's strategy to expand its product suite and increase revenue per user.

## Key facts
- Fig Jam is one of the additional products Figma layered on top of its core design tool.
