## Overview
Tire Slime is an emergency flat tire repair sealant that claims to instantly seal tread punctures up to a quarter-inch. It is advertised as being safe for tires and wheels, non-flammable, and cleans up with water.

## Role in this knowledge base
A tire repair product evaluated for its sealing capabilities, durability, and potential impact on tire components.

## Key facts
- Tire Slime performed well in sealing punctures and holding tire pressure for on-road use.
