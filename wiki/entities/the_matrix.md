## Overview
The Matrix is a science fiction film series that explores themes of reality, choice, and control. It features concepts like simulated realities and a protagonist's journey of awakening.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as an analogy ('woman in the red dress') to explain how distractions or seemingly attractive new opportunities can actually be 'agents in disguise' that derail focus and progress.

## Key facts
- In the movie, 'agents' disrupt the system and can appear as attractive distractions, like the 'woman in the red dress'.
