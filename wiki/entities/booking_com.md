## Overview
Booking.com is an online travel agency (OTA) that facilitates the booking of accommodations, flights, car rentals, and attractions. It offers a wide selection of options and often provides in-app discounts.

## Role in this knowledge base
An accommodation booking app that often offers in-app discounts.

## Key facts
- Often offers in-app discounts to help save money on accommodation.
