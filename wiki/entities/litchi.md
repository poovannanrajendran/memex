## Overview
Litchi is a popular third-party flight control application for DJI drones. It offers advanced features such as waypoint missions, virtual reality (VR) modes, and automatic flight planning, often exceeding the capabilities of DJI's native applications.

## Role in this knowledge base
The core software tool being demonstrated and taught in the tutorial.

## Key facts
- Litchi allows for much more complex and customisable flight paths than standard DJI software.
