## Overview
A group of professionals brought in to diagnose and repair the malfunctioning air conditioning system in the building.

## Role in this knowledge base
Performed a suspicious and seemingly temporary repair on the AC system, refusing to provide details about their work, which raised concerns about its efficacy and legitimacy.

## Key facts
- Claimed to have fixed the AC by changing the refrigerant, but the repair appeared quick, did not provide a lasting solution, and they refused to explain their actions.
