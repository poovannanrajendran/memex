## Overview
An annual prize given to an artist born or based in Great Britain for an outstanding exhibition or other presentation of their work in the preceding twelve months.

## Role in this knowledge base
The Turner Prize is used as an example of a contemporary art award, contrasting with other awards in music, theatre, film, and literature, all of which are relevant to the Life in the UK test.

## Key facts
- It is an award for contemporary art.
