## Overview
Coursera is an American online learning platform founded by Stanford University professors Andrew Ng and Daphne Koller. It offers courses, specializations, and degrees from top universities and companies.

## Role in this knowledge base
Provider of the 'Algorithms 1 and 2' course, useful for interview preparation.

## Key facts
- Offers 'Algorithms 1 and 2' courses specifically to help users prepare for coding interviews.
