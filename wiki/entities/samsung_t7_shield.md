## Overview
The Samsung T7 Shield is a portable external solid-state drive (SSD) known for its durability, including drop and water resistance, and fast data transfer speeds.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended as an external storage solution to supplement the MacBook Air's internal storage.

## Key facts
- It is IP65 water and dust resistant.
