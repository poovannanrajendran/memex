## Overview
Large Language Models (LLMs) are a type of artificial intelligence capable of understanding, generating, and manipulating human language. They are trained on massive datasets and form the core of many advanced AI applications.

## Role in this knowledge base
LLMs are presented as the core technology enabling the automation of significant tasks, particularly legal work, which is foundational to the full-stack AI company concept.

## Key facts
- LLMs are now capable of automating a significant amount of legal work, according to the source.
