## Overview
A to-do list application developed by Apple Inc. for managing tasks and reminders on Apple devices.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as part of Apple's productivity suite for task management and setting reminders.

## Key facts
- It can be integrated with custom shortcuts for quick task additions.
