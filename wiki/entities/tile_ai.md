## Overview
Tile AI is an entity that provides resources and guides for accessing difficult-to-reach AI tools, such as Cling. They offer solutions for overcoming sign-up barriers like international phone number requirements.

## Role in this knowledge base
Provides guides and methods for users to navigate the sign-up process for exclusive AI tools like Cling.

## Key facts
- Tile AI offers a walkthrough for obtaining a burner number to bypass Cling's requirement for a Chinese phone number.
