## Overview
MSI (Micro-Star International) is a Taiwanese multinational information technology corporation. They design and manufacture a wide range of computer hardware, including laptops, motherboards, graphics cards, and monitors.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the motherboard manufacturers whose products are reviewed and recommended.

## Key facts
- MSI offers a broad portfolio of motherboards, often focusing on gaming features and aesthetics.
