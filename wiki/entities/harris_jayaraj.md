## Overview
Harris Jayaraj is a prominent Indian film composer, singer, and music producer who primarily works in Tamil cinema. He is known for his energetic soundtracks and melodious tunes.

## Role in this knowledge base
Composer of the song 'Makkamishi'.

## Key facts
- He has composed music for over 50 films in various Indian languages.
