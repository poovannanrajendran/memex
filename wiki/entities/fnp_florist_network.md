## Overview
FNP (formerly Ferns N Petals) is an Indian online retailer specializing in flowers, gifts, cakes, and plants.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as an example of an Indian online store from which Google's AI mode suggests products.

## Key facts
- FNP is identified as a vendor for gift suggestions within Google's AI mode for Indian users.
