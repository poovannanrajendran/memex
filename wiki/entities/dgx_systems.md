## Overview
NVIDIA DGX systems are high-performance AI supercomputers designed for deep learning and AI research. They are typically large, rack-mounted servers containing multiple high-end GPUs.

## Role in this knowledge base
Project Digits is described as a shrunk-down version of DGX systems, offering a more accessible entry point to NVIDIA's AI computing solutions.

## Key facts
- Project Digits is positioned as a smaller, more affordable alternative to the large DGX systems.
