## Overview
A powerful large language model developed by Google, characterised by its very large context window, allowing it to process extensive amounts of information in a single pass.

## Role in this knowledge base
Serves as the backbone for complex AI operations within memex, handling long-document ingestion, deep synthesis, and crucial contradiction resolution across sources.

## Key facts
- Features a 1M+ token context window, enabling one-shot analysis of full research papers or multi-document sets, fundamentally altering the architecture by eliminating chunking and retrieval hallucination.
