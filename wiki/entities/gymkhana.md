## Overview
Gymkhana is a highly acclaimed Indian restaurant in London, known for its contemporary take on classic Indian food. It is part of the JKS Restaurants group.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as a benchmark or point of reference for the quality of Brigadiers, as they share the same ownership group.

## Key facts
- Is a sister restaurant to Brigadiers under the JKS Restaurants umbrella.
