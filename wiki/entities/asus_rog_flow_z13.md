## Overview
The Asus ROG Flow Z13 is a versatile 2-in-1 gaming laptop that can be used as a tablet. It features a 2.5k, 180Hz display with DCI-P3 colour accuracy and can be configured with up to 128GB of RAM.

## Role in this knowledge base
The device hosting the AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395 APU, used for direct performance comparison against the MacBook Pro.
