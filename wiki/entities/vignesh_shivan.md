## Overview
Vignesh Shivan is an Indian film director, screenwriter, lyricist, and producer who works in Tamil cinema. He is also the founder of the production house Rowdy Pictures.

## Role in this knowledge base
Associated with the production of 'Netrikann' and potentially the promotion of its songs.

## Key facts
- He is the partner of actress Nayanthara.
