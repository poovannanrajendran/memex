## Overview
Unsplash is a website that provides a library of high-resolution, royalty-free stock images. It is often used by designers and content creators to find visuals for their projects.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a source for free stock photos that can be used as cover images within Notion.

## Key facts
- It offers a library of stock free photos that can be used for Notion covers.
