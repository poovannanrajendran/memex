## Overview
Toolify is a platform that aggregates and provides access to a wide range of AI tools and their respective websites. It aims to simplify the discovery and utilisation of AI resources by centralising them in a single, organised hub.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary subject of the YouTube video, presented as a solution for easily accessing and managing various AI tools.

## Key facts
- It allows users to access AI tools and their website links from one location and is updated daily with new tool launches.
