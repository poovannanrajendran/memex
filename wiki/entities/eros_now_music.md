## Overview
Eros Now Music is the music division associated with Eros International, a film production and distribution company.

## Role in this knowledge base
Producer of the soundtrack for 'R... Rajkumar' and the song 'Saree Ke Fall Sa'.

## Key facts
- Eros International is a major player in the Indian film industry.
