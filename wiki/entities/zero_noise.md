## Overview
Zero Noise is an application developed by the video's presenter, designed to help users filter out irrelevant information by fetching and aggregating content only when relevant changes occur.

## Role in this knowledge base
A practical application demonstrating the use of GPT-4o mini and prompt chaining for efficient information management and recommendation.

## Key facts
- It utilizes a GPT-4o mini fusion chain to perform an SEO-type search for related keywords, making the agentic workflow nearly free.
