## Overview
Ceramic Shield is a proprietary display glass technology developed by Apple, designed to offer enhanced durability and drop resistance compared to standard smartphone glass.

## Role in this knowledge base
A component of the iPhone screens, with an upgrade noted for the iPhone 16 models.

## Key facts
- The iPhone 16's Ceramic Shield is claimed to be 50% stronger than the first generation.
