## Overview
Hugging Face is a platform and community focused on artificial intelligence, particularly for natural language processing. It hosts a vast number of pre-trained models, datasets, and tools that developers can use and contribute to.

## Role in this knowledge base
The platform where the open-source Deepseek AI models can be found and downloaded.

## Key facts
- It is the primary repository for downloading open-source AI models like Deepseek.
