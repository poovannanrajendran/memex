## Overview
NVLink is a high-speed interconnect technology developed by NVIDIA that allows direct communication between GPUs and between CPUs and GPUs. It offers higher bandwidth and scalability compared to PCIe.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used for chip-to-chip interconnect within the Grace Blackwell Superchip in the Project Digits Mini PC, enabling efficient data transfer between CPU and GPU.

## Key facts
- NVLink is used for chip-to-chip interconnect in the GB10 Grace Blackwell Superchip to connect the CPU and GPU.
