## Overview
MBUX is Mercedes-Benz's proprietary infotainment system, featuring advanced artificial intelligence capabilities, touchscreens, voice control, and customizable displays. It is designed to enhance the driver's experience.

## Role in this knowledge base
The infotainment system present in both the EQA and EQB, central to the user interface and vehicle controls.

## Key facts
- The latest generation of MBUX (NTG 7) includes features like wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto.
