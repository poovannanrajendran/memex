## Overview
Selva Raghavan is an Indian film director, screenwriter, and producer known for his works in Tamil cinema. He often explores complex human relationships and societal themes in his films.

## Role in this knowledge base
The interviewee in the YouTube video, discussing his observations about women, particularly his mother, and their roles in society and his films.
