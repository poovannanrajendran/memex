## Overview
The Apple Vision Pro is a mixed-reality headset developed by Apple, designed for spatial computing and immersive experiences.

## Role in this knowledge base
A device for which the iPhone 16's spatial video recording capability is intended.

## Key facts
- The new vertical camera layout on the iPhone 16 enables recording spatial video viewable on the Apple Vision Pro.
