## Overview
Idiogram is an AI image generation tool praised for its realism, accuracy, and ability to create visually appealing images and logos. Notably, it can correctly spell words within generated images.

## Role in this knowledge base
Enables the creation of high-quality, realistic images and logos, with the unique capability of accurately rendering text within visuals.

## Key facts
- It is the only AI tool encountered that can spell words correctly in generated images.
