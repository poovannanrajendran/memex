## Overview
The Mercedes-Benz EQA is a compact electric SUV based on the A-Class platform. It offers a balance of electric performance, luxury, and practicality in a smaller SUV package.

## Role in this knowledge base
One of the two electric vehicles being compared in the source document.

## Key facts
- The 2024 EQA has seen a significant range improvement, going from around 260 miles to almost 350 miles.
