## Overview
The AMD Radeon Pro Vega Frontier Edition was a professional graphics card designed for workstations, featuring high memory bandwidth and capacity (16GB HBM2). It was aimed at content creation, machine learning, and data science tasks.

## Role in this knowledge base
The creator's initial idea for a GPU for the new workstation, chosen for its large VRAM capacity.
