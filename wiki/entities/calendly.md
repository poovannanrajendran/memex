## Overview
Calendly is a popular online appointment scheduling software that allows users to book meetings easily. It typically operates on a freemium or subscription model.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a paid application for which cal.com is presented as a free, open-source alternative.

## Key facts
- It is a widely used paid tool for scheduling appointments.
