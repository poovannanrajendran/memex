## Overview
The Enviral Plus is an add-on board designed to connect to a Raspberry Pi's GPIO pins, allowing it to measure various air quality parameters such as temperature, humidity, and particulate matter.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used in the second project to build an indoor air quality monitor.

## Key facts
- The Enviral Plus sensor board can also support a particulate matter sensor to measure PM2.5, PM5, or PM10.
