## Overview
Visual Intelligence is an upcoming feature, similar to Google Lens, that uses the iPhone's camera to analyze and understand visual information, allowing users to interact with or get details about objects.

## Role in this knowledge base
A feature accessible via the new Camera Control Button on the iPhone 16.

## Key facts
- Visual Intelligence is described as a way to analyze information using the phone's camera, similar to Google Lens.
