## Overview
The Income Tax Department is a government agency responsible for administering direct taxes in India. It collects income tax, corporate tax, and other direct taxes, and enforces tax laws through assessments, surveys, and investigations.

## Role in this knowledge base
This organisation is the subject of the video, as it is the authority that conducts income tax raids.

## Key facts
- The department operates under the Ministry of Finance, Government of India.
