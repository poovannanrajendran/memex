## Overview
The Kia Niro EV is an electric crossover that shares its platform with the Hyundai Kona Electric, offering similar practicality and range.

## Role in this knowledge base
Presented as a 'closely related cousin' to the Kona Electric, it's recommended for its value and suitability for those wanting an EV with good range.

## Key facts
- A 2022 model with 75,000 miles and 282 miles of range costs around £11,000.
