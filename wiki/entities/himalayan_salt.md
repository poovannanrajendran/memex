## Overview
Himalayan salt is a type of rock salt, typically pinkish in colour, mined from the Punjab region of Pakistan. It is often marketed as a healthier alternative to table salt.

## Role in this knowledge base
An ingredient in the detox drink, contributing to electrolyte balance.

## Key facts
- A pinch of Himalayan salt is included in the detox drink recipe.
