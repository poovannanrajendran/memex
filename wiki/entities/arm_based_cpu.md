## Overview
An Arm-based CPU is a central processing unit that uses the instruction set architecture developed by Arm Holdings. These CPUs are known for their power efficiency and are widely used in mobile devices and increasingly in servers and laptops.

## Role in this knowledge base
The CPU architecture used in the NVIDIA Project Digits Mini PC, featuring a heterogeneous design with performance and efficiency cores.

## Key facts
- The CPU in Project Digits is a 20-core Arm-based design, comprising 10 Cortex-X4 cores for performance and 10 Cortex-A725 cores for efficiency.
