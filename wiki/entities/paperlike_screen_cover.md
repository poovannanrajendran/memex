## Overview
Paperlike is a screen protector for iPads designed to mimic the feel of writing on paper rather than glass. It aims to improve the tactile experience for drawing and note-taking.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a screen cover that enhances the writing and drawing feel on the iPad, reducing glare.

## Key facts
- The Paperlike 2.1 version offers improved screen clarity and reduced reflections.
