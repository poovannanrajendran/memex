## Overview
Deli is an AI tool designed to make a person's knowledge available to their team. It allows team members to ask questions and receive answers without needing direct input from the individual.

## Role in this knowledge base
It serves as a virtual assistant that answers team questions about how to perform tasks, freeing up the individual's time.

## Key facts
- It makes an individual's expertise available to their team by answering questions.
