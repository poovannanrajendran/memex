## Overview
The DJI Mini 2 is a compact and lightweight consumer drone, popular among beginners for its ease of use and advanced features for its size and price point. It is capable of capturing high-quality aerial video and photos.

## Role in this knowledge base
This is the specific drone model used in the tutorial to demonstrate the cinematic moves.

## Key facts
- The DJI Mini 2 weighs less than 249 grams, which in many regions exempts it from registration requirements.
