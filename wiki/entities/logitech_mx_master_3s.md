## Overview
The Logitech MX Master 3S is an advanced wireless mouse known for its ergonomic design, high precision, customizable buttons, and multi-device connectivity. It offers features like gesture control and a MagSpeed electromagnetic scroll wheel.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended as a highly functional mouse that integrates well with macOS, offering gesture controls and customisable scrolling behaviour.

## Key facts
- Its gesture control feature mimics the trackpad's swipe gestures via a button on the mouse.
