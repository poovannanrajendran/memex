## Overview
The Cortex-A725 is a power-efficient CPU core designed by Arm Holdings, often used in conjunction with higher-performance cores to balance power consumption and performance in mobile and other devices.

## Role in this knowledge base
Part of the heterogeneous CPU configuration in the NVIDIA Project Digits Mini PC, focusing on power efficiency.

## Key facts
- Ten Cortex-A725 CPU cores are included in the Project Digits Mini PC's Arm-based CPU, offering better power efficiency.
