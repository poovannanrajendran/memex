## Overview
Samsung is a South Korean multinational conglomerate company that produces a wide range of electronics, including smartphones, televisions, and memory products. It is one of the world's largest technology companies.

## Role in this knowledge base
Manufacturer of the Samsung T7 Shield external SSD recommended for storage expansion.

## Key facts
- The Samsung T7 Shield offers 1,000 megabytes per second read/write speed and is drop-proof up to 3 meters.
