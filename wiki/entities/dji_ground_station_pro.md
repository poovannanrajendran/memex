## Overview
DJI Ground Station Pro (GS Pro) is an application that allows users to plan and execute complex automated flight missions with DJI drones. It offers features like waypoint navigation and area surveying for professional applications.

## Role in this knowledge base
Presented as an example of an autonomous flight app for waypoint missions.
