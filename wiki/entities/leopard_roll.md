## Overview
The leopard roll is a unique dish from Mugli that involves a seekh kebab, enhanced with mango chutney, and then rolled within a freshly made chapati. This is then crisped and brushed with ghee.

## Role in this knowledge base
A specific food item reviewed at Mugli, noted for its unique preparation and flavour profile, but also for its price point.

## Key facts
- Consists of seekh kebab, mango chutney, chapati, and ghee, and is priced at £10.
