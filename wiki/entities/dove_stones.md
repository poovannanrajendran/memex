## Overview
Dove Stones is a popular climbing location and reservoir in Saddleworth, Greater Manchester, England. It is known for its scenic beauty and challenging cycling routes, including a notable 5k climb.

## Role in this knowledge base
Dove Stones became the narrator's local climb and a central focus for their goal of improving speed and achieving 'Queen of the Mountain' status.

## Key facts
- The narrator set a personal goal to become faster on the Dove Stones climb, eventually achieving 'Queen of the Mountain' (QOM) status there.
