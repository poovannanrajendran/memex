## Overview
The AMD Ryzen Threadripper 2990 WX is a flagship HEDT processor with 32 cores and 64 threads, designed for extreme multi-threaded performance. It requires a specific TR4 socket motherboard.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as a high-end comparison point for the Cinebench score achieved by the dual Xeon build.

## Key facts
- The dual Xeon workstation's Cinebench score was very close to that of a Threadripper 2990 WX, which costs significantly more.
