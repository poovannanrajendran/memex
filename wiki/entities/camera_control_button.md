## Overview
A new button on the iPhone 16 designed specifically for camera functions, offering multi-stage press capabilities for zoom, focus, and accessing features like visual intelligence.

## Role in this knowledge base
A new hardware addition to the iPhone 16, enhancing camera interaction.

## Key facts
- The Camera Control Button can detect two stages of press, similar to MacBook trackpads, for various photographic controls.
