## Overview
A historic Anglican cathedral located on Ludgate Hill in the City of London. It is the seat of the Bishop of London and the mother church of the Diocese of London.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary location and subject of the video, housing the discussed architectural features.

## Key facts
- Its Triforium Library contains one of the best-preserved 18th-century interiors in London.
