## Overview
The SanDisk Extreme Pro is a line of rugged, high-speed external solid-state drives (SSDs) offering fast read/write speeds and significant storage capacities.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended for external storage on iPads, particularly for editing video directly from the drive.

## Key facts
- Available in 1, 2, and 4 terabyte versions with fast read and write speeds.
