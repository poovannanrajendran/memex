## Overview
The Apple Pencil is a stylus designed by Apple for use with its iPad line. It offers features like low latency, pressure sensitivity, and tilt support, enabling precise drawing, writing, and navigation.

## Role in this knowledge base
Highlighted as a key accessory that unlocks the iPad's potential for creative tasks like drawing, note-taking, and photo/video editing.

## Key facts
- The second-generation Apple Pencil offers native charging on compatible iPad models and a double-tap feature for quick tool switching.
