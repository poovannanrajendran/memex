## Overview
Neon is a new browser developed by Opera, distinguished as the first "agentic browser." It integrates advanced AI functionalities directly within the browsing experience.

## Role in this knowledge base
The product at the center of the analysis, representing a new paradigm in web browsing by embedding AI capabilities that challenge existing AI startups.

## Key facts
- Neon's AI operator understands the page and can take actions like booking flights or filling forms without user intervention.
