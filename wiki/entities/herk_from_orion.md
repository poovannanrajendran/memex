## Overview
The Herk is a Mini PC offered by Orion, featuring a powerful AMD Ryzen 9 7940HS processor and integrated Radeon 780M RDNA 3 GPU. It is marketed as an AI-capable Mini PC.

## Role in this knowledge base
This Mini PC represents a mid-range consumer-grade option tested for running LLMs locally, specifically highlighting its integrated GPU capabilities and direct Windows installation.
