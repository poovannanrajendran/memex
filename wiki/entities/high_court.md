## Overview
A superior court in the UK legal system. In Scotland, it hears serious criminal cases and is the supreme civil court. In England and Wales, it has civil and criminal divisions.

## Role in this knowledge base
The High Court in Scotland hears serious criminal cases with a judge and jury of 15, and in England, Wales, and Northern Ireland it hears serious civil cases.

## Key facts
- In Scotland, it hears serious criminal cases with a jury of 15 people.
