## Overview
A portable external monitor designed for professionals and creators who need a lightweight, high-quality display on the go. It connects via a single USB-C cable.

## Role in this knowledge base
Featured as part of a planned portable workstation setup for the M4 Mac Mini, showcasing a potential use case for on-the-go productivity.

## Key facts
- The Espresso Display connects to the Mac Mini using just a single USB-C cable.
