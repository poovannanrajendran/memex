## Overview
Sony Pictures International Productions is a division of Sony Pictures Entertainment that focuses on financing and producing films in various international markets. They collaborate with local filmmakers and studios to create content for global audiences.

## Role in this knowledge base
Co-producer of the film 'Amaran'.

## Key facts
- They have co-produced films in multiple languages, including Indian regional cinema.
