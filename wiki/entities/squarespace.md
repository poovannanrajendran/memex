## Overview
Squarespace is a popular website builder that offers hosting and domain registration services. It acquired Google Domains in September 2023. While it offers a domain for free for the first year with some plans, its renewal prices for domains are around $20 per year.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as an example of a service offering a domain for free in the first year but with high renewal costs, and as the new owner of Google Domains.
