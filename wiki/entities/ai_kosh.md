## Overview
AI Kosh is a newly launched AI platform and ecosystem hub by India. It acts as a central repository for the nation's public datasets, machine learning models, toolkits, and real-world AI use cases.

## Role in this knowledge base
The central AI product for India, aiming to foster growth and collaboration within its AI ecosystem.

## Key facts
- It allows users to explore datasets and models only after logging in via Digilocker using Indian government-issued identification.
