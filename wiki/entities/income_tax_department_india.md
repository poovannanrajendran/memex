## Overview
The Income Tax Department is a government agency responsible for administering and enforcing direct tax laws in India. It collects income tax and other direct taxes levied by the Government of India.

## Role in this knowledge base
The entity conducting income tax raids and enforcing tax laws in India, which is the central subject of the video.

## Key facts
- It is a part of the Department of Revenue under the Ministry of Finance.
