## Overview
Domain.com is a domain registrar that offers domain registration, web hosting, and website building services. The video highlights its pricing structure as an example of deceptive practices.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as a specific example of a domain registrar employing misleading pricing tactics, including automatically setting purchases to two years, adding privacy protection fees, and having significantly higher renewal costs.

## Key facts
- Domain.com's renewal price for a .com domain can be $21.99 per year, almost double the initial registration price.
