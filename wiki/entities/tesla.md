## Overview
Tesla, Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company. It is known for its innovative approach to car manufacturing and technology.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as an analogy for starting with a high-value product (Roadster) and gradually moving down market to broader segments (Model S, 3, X, Cybertruck), illustrating a strategy for market penetration and resource deployment.

## Key facts
- Tesla started with the most expensive version of its car, the Roadster, before moving to mass-market models.
