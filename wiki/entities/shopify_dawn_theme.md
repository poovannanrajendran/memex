## Overview
The Dawn theme is a free, highly customisable theme developed by Shopify. It's known for its lightweight design and flexibility, serving as a starting point for creating modern and efficient online stores.

## Role in this knowledge base
A specific theme recommended and demonstrated for building a Shopify store within the tutorial.

## Key facts
- It is Shopify's default theme and is designed to be fast and flexible.
