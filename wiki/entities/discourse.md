## Overview
A free and open-source software platform for building and hosting online forums. It is known for its modern interface and features designed to foster community discussion.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as forum software that, while functional, is not ideal for long-term knowledge capture, with a preference expressed for public forums for better searchability and AI indexing.

## Key facts
- It is popular forum software but criticized for its lack of public discoverability for knowledge capture compared to traditional forums.
