## Overview
Sun Pictures is an Indian film production and distribution company, part of the Sun TV Network. It is one of the leading production houses in Tamil cinema.

## Role in this knowledge base
Sun Pictures is the production company behind the film 'Raayan'.

## Key facts
- They are responsible for producing the movie.
