## Overview
Slate's Life is a wallpaper pack that allows users to synchronise their desktop background across different Apple devices, such as a MacBook and iPhone.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is used in the tutorial to create a consistent aesthetic across the user's devices.

## Key facts
- Enables matching wallpaper on MacBook and iPhone.
