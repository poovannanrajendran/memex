## Overview
Hiyoo AI minimMax is a tool that animates still images into 3D videos using artificial intelligence. It allows users to upload images and provide instructions for animation.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used to transform the static images created by Leonardo AI into dynamic 3D video clips for the story.

## Key facts
- Offers a 3-day unlimited free trial for new users.
