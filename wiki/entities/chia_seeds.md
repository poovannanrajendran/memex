## Overview
Chia seeds are small seeds from the plant Salvia hispanica, native to Mexico and Guatemala. They are known for their nutritional content, including omega-3 fatty acids and fibre, and their ability to absorb liquid.

## Role in this knowledge base
An ingredient in the detox drink, requiring overnight soaking.

## Key facts
- Chia seeds are to be soaked overnight before being added to the drink.
