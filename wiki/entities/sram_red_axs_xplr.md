## Overview
The SRAM Red AXS XPLR is a high-end 1x gravel groupset featuring electronic shifting and a 10-44t cassette. It is designed to bridge the gap between traditional 1x and 2x systems by offering a wide gear range with the simplicity of a single chainring.

## Role in this knowledge base
This specific groupset was tested by the author and served as the catalyst for their change of opinion regarding 1x drivetrains.

## Key facts
- The Red AXS XPLR groupset allows for the left shifter to actuate a dropper post, further simplifying the cockpit.
