## Overview
Zenes is an AI-based customer service tool that handles a large percentage of common customer questions and escalates complex issues to human agents. It can also suggest content to add to its knowledge base.

## Role in this knowledge base
Automates customer service by handling common inquiries, freeing up human agents for complex issues, and improving customer response times.

## Key facts
- Zenes has an AI that suggests content to add to its knowledge base when customers ask questions not covered.
