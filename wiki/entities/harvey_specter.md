## Overview
Harvey Specter is a high-powered lawyer at Pearson Specter Litt, known for his sharp wit and intense work ethic. He is a senior partner at the firm and often takes on challenging cases.

## Role in this knowledge base
Harvey is the primary character reacting to the news of Louis's heart attack and takes charge of Louis's caseload, demonstrating professional responsibility and personal concern.

## Key facts
- Harvey knows Louis is allergic to all flowers, a fact he learned from their time as associates.
