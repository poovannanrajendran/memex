## Overview
Sriracha is a type of hot sauce or chili sauce made from a paste of chili peppers, distilled vinegar, garlic, salt, and sugar.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as a flavour enhancer in the fried eggs to elevate the breakfast sandwich.

## Key facts
- It is added to the fried eggs for an extra level of flavour.
