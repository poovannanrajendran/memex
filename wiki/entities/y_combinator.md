## Overview
Y Combinator is an American seed money startup accelerator. It has invested in hundreds of companies, including Dropbox, Airbnb, and Stripe.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned alongside A16z for its practice of investing in founders with existing experience in the problem domain, reinforcing the value of relatable experience for entrepreneurs.

## Key facts
- They invest in founders who have direct experience with the problem they are trying to solve.
