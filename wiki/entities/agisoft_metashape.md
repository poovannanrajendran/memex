## Overview
Agisoft Metashape is a photogrammetry software that processes digital images taken from drones or other sources to create 3D spatial data, including 3D models and orthomosaic maps.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a tool for 2D and 3D drone modelling.

## Key facts
- It is used for creating 3D models from drone imagery.
