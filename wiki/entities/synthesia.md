## Overview
Synthesia is an AI video generation platform that allows users to create videos featuring realistic AI avatars. These avatars can speak in numerous languages and are designed to help build brands 24/7, even without the user appearing on camera.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is featured as a solution for businesses and creators who want to produce professional video content with AI presenters in multiple languages, ideal for branding and reaching a global audience.

## Key facts
- It can create videos of avatars speaking over 120 languages.
