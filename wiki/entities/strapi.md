## Overview
Strapi is a self-hostable Content Management System (CMS) suitable for small teams. It provides a solution for managing blogs and newsletters and has a free version available for self-hosting, though it lacks single sign-on and dedicated support.

## Role in this knowledge base
Replaces the blog and newsletter publishing function previously handled by Notion and Make.

## Key facts
- It is a perfect solution for small teams looking for a self-hostable CMS, provided they can forgo single sign-on and support.
