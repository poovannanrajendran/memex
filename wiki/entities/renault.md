## Overview
Renault is a French multinational automobile manufacturer. The Dacia Duster shares its engines and chassis with current Renault models like the Captur, indicating a shared platform and technological base.

## Role in this knowledge base
Supplier of engines and chassis technology for the new Dacia Duster.

## Key facts
- The new Duster's engines and chassis are based on the current Renault Captur.
