## Overview
Apple's custom-designed system on a chip (SoC) based on the ARM architecture, used in their Mac computers and iOS devices.

## Role in this knowledge base
The architecture of the MacBook Pro being set up, influencing software compatibility and performance.

## Key facts
- Many software packages offer specific builds or versions optimized for Apple Silicon (ARM64).
