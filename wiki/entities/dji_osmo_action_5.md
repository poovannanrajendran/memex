## Overview
The DJI Osmo Action 5 is the fifth generation of DJI's action camera line, featuring significant internal upgrades such as a new processor and sensor. It boasts improved low-light performance, faster processing speeds, a pressure gauge, and enhanced battery compatibility.

## Role in this knowledge base
The other main action camera being compared, representing a more innovative and rapidly evolving option in the market.

## Key facts
- It features a new 1.3-inch type sensor and a 4nm chip that DJI claims can provide up to 13.5 stops of dynamic range.
