## Overview
Cloudflare is primarily known for its web security and CDN services for businesses. It also operates a domain registrar service that offers domains at cost price, making it a very affordable long-term option, although customer support for beginners is limited.

## Role in this knowledge base
Highlighted as one of the two favourite domain registrars due to its 'at cost' pricing model and included privacy protection, making it the cheapest long-term option.

## Key facts
- Cloudflare offers domain registration at cost price, with no profit margin, for services like .com domains at $9.15 per year for both registration and renewal.
