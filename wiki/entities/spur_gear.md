## Overview
A spur gear is the simplest type of gear with straight teeth cut parallel to the axis of rotation. It is commonly used for transmitting power between parallel shafts.

## Role in this knowledge base
Discussed as a fundamental gear type with specific characteristics regarding efficiency, noise, and speed limitations.

## Key facts
- Has simple straight teeth that are efficient but noisy, used at low speeds.
