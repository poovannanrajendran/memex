## Overview
The Sabrent Rocket is a line of high-performance NVMe SSDs known for their speed and reliability. The 1TB model offers substantial capacity and fast data transfer rates.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as a performance benchmark for the new U.2 SSD, indicating the U.2 drive's random read speeds are comparable.

## Key facts
- The random read speeds of the new U.2 SSD were found to be comparable to the creator's existing Sabrent Rocket 1TB NVMe drive.
