## Overview
IndiTales is a travel and storytelling platform founded by Anuradha Goyal. It focuses on exploring and documenting the rich history, culture, and spiritual heritage of India through travelogues, articles, and other forms of content.

## Role in this knowledge base
Anuradha Goyal's associated venture, underscoring her expertise and passion for documenting India's ancient sites and spiritual narratives.

## Key facts
- IndiTales serves as a primary outlet for Goyal's travel writing and exploration of India's cultural landscape.
