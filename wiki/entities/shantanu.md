## Overview
A Kuru king, father of Bhishma, whose divine marriage to Ganga is a key event in the lineage of the Mahabharata.

## Role in this knowledge base
His divine meeting with Ganga is a crucial part of the Kuru ancestry to be explored in Season 2.

## Key facts
- His meeting with Ganga is a foundational event in the Kuru lineage, leading to the birth of Bhishma.
