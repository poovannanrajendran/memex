## Overview
Apple's premium keyboard accessory for iPads, featuring backlit keys, a built-in trackpad, and a floating cantilever design. It connects magnetically and provides a laptop-like typing experience.

## Role in this knowledge base
Discussed as a high-quality but expensive option for users who spend most of their time in laptop mode on their iPad.

## Key facts
- Includes an extra USB-C port for charging, freeing up the iPad's port.
