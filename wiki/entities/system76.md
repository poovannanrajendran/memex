## Overview
A computer manufacturer that specializes in selling Linux pre-installed laptops, desktops, and servers. They are known for developing their own Linux distribution, Pop!_OS, and the upcoming Cosmic desktop environment.

## Role in this knowledge base
Their development of the Cosmic desktop environment is highlighted as a significant effort to improve the Linux desktop experience, potentially addressing issues like workspace management.

## Key facts
- They are developing the Cosmic desktop environment, aiming to offer a more integrated and refined Linux user experience.
