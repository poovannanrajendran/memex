## Overview
A series of fantasy novels written by British author J. K. Rowling, which have been adapted into a popular film series. The story follows the life of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

## Role in this knowledge base
The Dean's Staircase is recognisable to fans from its appearance in the third Harry Potter film.

## Key facts
