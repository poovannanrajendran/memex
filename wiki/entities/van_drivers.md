## Overview
Individuals who operate vans for commercial purposes, typically involved in the delivery of goods.

## Role in this knowledge base
They represent the essential but often overlooked driving labour in the supply chain that supports daily life, including for those who avoid personal car use.

## Key facts
- Those who take the bus still rely on van drivers for their consumption of goods.
