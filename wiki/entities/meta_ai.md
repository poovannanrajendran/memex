## Overview
Meta AI is the artificial intelligence research division of Meta Platforms (formerly Facebook). They develop and release advanced AI models, including the Llama series of large language models.

## Role in this knowledge base
Meta AI is the developer of the Llama models, which are the subject of the performance tests in the video.
