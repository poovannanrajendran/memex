## Overview
The Tesla Model X is a full-size all-electric luxury SUV produced by Tesla, Inc. It is known for its distinctive falcon-wing rear doors, long-range electric powertrain, and advanced technology features.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a class-leading all-electric SUV that the Kia EV9 is being compared against.

## Key facts
