## Overview
The Opal Tadpoll is a small, clip-on USB-C webcam designed to provide a higher quality video feed than typical built-in iPad cameras. It clips onto the top of the iPad.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended as an alternative to built-in iPad cameras for significantly better video quality during calls and recordings.

## Key facts
- Clips onto the top of the iPad, avoiding the issue of side-facing cameras on some models.
