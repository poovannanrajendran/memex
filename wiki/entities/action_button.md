## Overview
The Action Button is a customizable button introduced on certain iPhone models, allowing users to assign various functions for quick access.

## Role in this knowledge base
A feature now present on the standard iPhone 16, previously exclusive to Pro models.

## Key facts
- The Action Button's placement is noted as potentially uncomfortable for users with smaller hands.
