## Overview
Ganesha, also known as Ganapathi or Pillayar, is a widely revered deity in Hinduism, depicted as an elephant-headed god. He is worshipped as the remover of obstacles and the god of beginnings, wisdom, and intellect.

## Role in this knowledge base
Deity invoked during religious chants and prayers at the engagement ceremony.

## Key facts
