## Overview
Cisco Meraki is a cloud-managed IT company that offers a range of networking products, including wireless access points, switches, security appliances, and mobile device management, all managed from a central cloud dashboard.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is presented as an alternative or complementary platform to Catalyst Center for managing Cisco access points, supporting a unified experience.

## Key facts
- Access points can be managed using Meraki, offering a cloud-based management option.
