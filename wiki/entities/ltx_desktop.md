## Overview
LTX Desktop is a free video editing application that integrates with LTX AI models, allowing users to generate and edit videos locally.

## Role in this knowledge base
It complements the LTX 2.3 model by providing a user-friendly interface for creating and modifying videos offline.

## Key facts
- It allows users to download models, generate videos, and edit everything locally in one app.
