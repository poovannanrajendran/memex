## Overview
The Tata Group is an Indian multinational conglomerate headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. It is one of India's largest and oldest industrial groups, with diverse interests spanning steel, automobiles, IT services, and hospitality.

## Role in this knowledge base
The corporate group associated with Ratan Tata and his legacy.

## Key facts
- The Tata Group is one of India's largest and oldest industrial groups.
