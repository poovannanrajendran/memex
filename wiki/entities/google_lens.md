## Overview
Google Lens is a visual search engine application that allows users to search using images, identify objects, and extract text from photos using AI.

## Role in this knowledge base
Used as a benchmark to describe the functionality of Apple's new Visual Intelligence feature.

## Key facts
- Visual Intelligence on the iPhone 16 is directly compared to Google Lens in terms of its analytical capabilities.
