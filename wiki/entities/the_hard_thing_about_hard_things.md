## Overview
'The Hard Thing About Hard Things: Building a Business When There Are No Easy Answers' is a book by Ben Horowitz. It delves into the difficulties and challenges of leading a startup, offering practical advice from his experiences.

## Role in this knowledge base
This book is recommended by Ritesh Agarwal for its insights into entrepreneurial struggles.

## Key facts
- The book focuses on the struggles of being a CEO and the difficult decisions they face.
