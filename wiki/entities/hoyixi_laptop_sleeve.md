## Overview
The Hoyixi laptop sleeve is designed to protect laptops from scratches and minor impacts. It features a unique foldable design that can be used to elevate the laptop for better ergonomics.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended for protecting the MacBook Air from scratches and for its multi-functional design as a stand.

## Key facts
- It includes a detachable mouse pad on the side.
