## Overview
The Rode Wireless GO II is a wireless microphone system consisting of transmitters and a receiver, designed for high-quality audio capture for video and vlogging.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended for podcasting or high-quality audio on video calls, noting its reliable performance via USB-C connection to iPad or laptop.

## Key facts
