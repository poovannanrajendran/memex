## Overview
The Quest 3S is Meta's budget-friendly virtual reality headset, released in October 2024. It aims to make VR more accessible by simplifying features and reducing cost.

## Role in this knowledge base
The budget-friendly VR headset being compared against the Quest 3.

## Key facts
- The Quest 3S utilizes the optical stack from the previous generation Quest 2.
