## Overview
Solid State Drive (SSD) external storage devices manufactured by Samsung, offering high-speed data transfer and portability.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended for expanding storage capacity on Macs, especially for users dealing with large files or expensive built-in storage upgrades.

## Key facts
- They allow entire applications and project files to be run at normal speeds as if they were on the computer's internal drive.
