## Overview
Puerto Rico is a Caribbean island and unincorporated territory of the United States. It has a rich history and unique cultural identity, with a significant automotive presence.

## Role in this knowledge base
The location where the creator's father owned and used the Toyota 2000GT, and where the creator's childhood memories of the car were formed.

## Key facts
- It was noted that the father's Toyota 2000GT was the only one on the island.
