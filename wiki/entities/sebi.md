## Overview
SEBI stands for the Securities and Exchange Board of India. It is the regulatory body responsible for regulating the securities market in India.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned in the disclaimer to clarify the presenter's status as not being a SEBI-registered investment advisor or research analyst.

## Key facts
- SEBI is the primary regulator of the Indian securities market.
