## Overview
Pushpa: The Rise is a 2021 Indian Telugu-language period action film directed by Sukumar. The film and its soundtrack achieved significant pan-Indian popularity.

## Role in this knowledge base
Its soundtrack, including the song 'Oo Antava Mawa', is likely included or referenced for its broad appeal.

## Key facts
- Allu Arjun's performance and Devi Sri Prasad's music were highly acclaimed.
