## Overview
Yugabharathi is a prominent Tamil poet and lyricist known for his socially conscious and often political songs. He is associated with progressive and Dravidian movements in Tamil Nadu.

## Role in this knowledge base
Speaker in the video, advocating for the understanding of Ambedkar, Periyar, and Marx.
