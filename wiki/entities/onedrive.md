## Overview
OneDrive is a file hosting service and synchronization product operated by Microsoft as part of its web version of Office.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned alongside Google Drive as a cloud storage service that can be connected to ChatGPT.

## Key facts
- Users can connect OneDrive to ChatGPT to enable the AI to access and utilise files from their Microsoft cloud storage.
