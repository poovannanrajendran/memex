## Overview
JL Collins is an author and financial commentator, best known for his 'Stock Series' blog posts and his book 'The Simple Path to Wealth'. He advocates for a straightforward, low-cost approach to investing, primarily in index funds.

## Role in this knowledge base
The source of the argument that a house is a terrible investment, providing his rationale and advocating for alternative investment strategies.

## Key facts
- He advises younger generations to invest in VTSAX and rent rather than buying a house if wealth accumulation is the primary goal.
