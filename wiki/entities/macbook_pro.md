## Overview
The MacBook Pro is a line of high-performance laptop computers manufactured by Apple, typically featuring Apple's in-house developed processors (like the M4 Pro).

The device used to showcase the performance of the Apple M4 Pro chip in direct comparison with the Asus ROG Flow Z13.
