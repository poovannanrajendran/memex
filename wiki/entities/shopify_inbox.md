## Overview
Shopify Inbox is a communication tool integrated within the Shopify platform. It allows store owners to connect with customers, answer questions, and make sales directly through chat, enhancing customer service and engagement.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a tool for customer communication and sales within the Shopify ecosystem.

## Key facts
- It enables direct customer engagement and sales conversion within the Shopify admin.
