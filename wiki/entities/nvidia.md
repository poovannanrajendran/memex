## Overview
NVIDIA is a multinational technology company known for designing graphics processing units (GPUs) and systems-on-a-chip (SoCs). They are a leading player in artificial intelligence, high-performance computing, and gaming.

## Role in this knowledge base
Developer and manufacturer of the Project Digits Mini PC and the Grace Blackwell GB10 superchip.

## Key facts
- NVIDIA announced Project Digits at CES, featuring their new GB10 Grace Blackwell Superchip.
