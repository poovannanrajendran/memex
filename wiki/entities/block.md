## Overview
Block, Inc. (formerly Square, Inc.) is a financial technology company founded by Jack Dorsey. It operates various business segments including Square, Cash App, Spiral, and TBD.

## Role in this knowledge base
Block developed and open-sourced the AI agent Goose, contributing to the advancement of accessible AI tools.

## Key facts
- Block's engineers have reported saving up to 20% of their time using Goose.
