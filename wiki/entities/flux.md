## Overview
A desktop application that adjusts the colour temperature of a computer's display to match the time of day, reducing blue light exposure.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended for indirectly improving productivity by promoting better sleep through reduced blue light emission.

## Key facts
- It shifts the screen colour to a darker red hue at night, significantly more than the built-in Night Shift mode.
