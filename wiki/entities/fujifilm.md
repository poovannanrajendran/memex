## Overview
Fujifilm is a Japanese multinational corporation that produces a wide range of products, including photographic and imaging equipment, chemicals, and medical devices. It is particularly known for its cameras and film products.

## Role in this knowledge base
Manufacturer of the lens being reviewed.

## Key facts
- Fujifilm was originally founded as Fuji Photo Film Co., Ltd. in 1934.
