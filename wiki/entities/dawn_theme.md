## Overview
The Dawn Theme is a free, feature-rich theme offered by Shopify, designed to help businesses create a modern and professional online store. It is known for its speed, flexibility, and ease of customisation, allowing users to tailor the look and feel of their storefront without extensive coding knowledge.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a specific Shopify theme that users can select and customise for their store.
