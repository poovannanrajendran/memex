## Overview
The Charen Pro Magnetic Stand, also known as Mag Flot, is a stand designed for iPads that uses magnetic attachment. It is praised for its aesthetic design and allows for both landscape and portrait viewing.

## Role in this knowledge base
Recommended as an essential iPad accessory for versatile device positioning.

## Key facts
- The 'Mag Flot Pro' variant features an extendable arm to raise the iPad, making it useful alongside laptops or monitors.
