## Overview
A worm gear set comprises a screw-like gear (worm) and a toothed wheel (worm wheel). It is designed for high gear reduction ratios and is often self-locking.

## Role in this knowledge base
Described for its high reduction capability and self-locking feature, preventing back-driving.

## Key facts
- Is a screw-like gear with a high reduction ratio that is self-locking, preventing back driving.
