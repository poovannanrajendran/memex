## Overview
The Antarctic Treaty is a treaty that ensures Antarctica is used for peaceful purposes only. It prohibits military activity, bans nuclear testing and disposal of nuclear waste, and promotes scientific research. It was signed in 1959 and entered into force in 1961.

## Role in this knowledge base
The foundational agreement that makes war illegal in Antarctica.

## Key facts
- It was signed by 12 countries in 1959.
