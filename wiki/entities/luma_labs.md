## Overview
Luma Labs is a company known for its AI research and development, particularly in the field of 3D and video generation. Their Dream Machine tool has gained significant attention for its capabilities.

## Role in this knowledge base
Provider of the Dream Machine AI video tool, noted for its superior image-to-video generation.

## Key facts
- Dream Machine excels at image-to-video generation, especially when using keyframes to animate transitions between specified start and end frames.
