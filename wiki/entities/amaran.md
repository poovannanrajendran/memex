## Overview
'Amaran' is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language biographical drama film directed by Rajkumar Periasamy. The film is based on the life of Indian Army soldier Major Mukund Varadarajan.

## Role in this knowledge base
The film for which the song 'Hey Minnale' was created.

## Key facts
- The film stars Sivakarthikeyan in the lead role, portraying Major Mukund Varadarajan.
