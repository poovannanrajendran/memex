## Overview
EntryPoint AI is a company that provides a platform to simplify the process of fine-tuning large language models. Their platform allows users to focus on use cases and training data without needing to manage complex code or hardware.

## Role in this knowledge base
The organisation founded by the presenter, Mark Hennings, offering solutions for fine-tuning LLMs.

## Key facts
- They have created a fine-tuning platform to make the process easier for users.
