## Overview
Higgsfield AI is a commercial AI platform valued at a billion dollars, primarily known for providing creators with cinematic-level video control without requiring extensive filmmaking expertise. It allows users to adjust camera settings like lens, focal length, and aperture to generate specific video shots.

## Role in this knowledge base
The original commercial product that a new open-source alternative has been modelled after and is now competing with.

## Key facts
- Achieved a billion-dollar valuation by simplifying cinematic video creation for users without a filmmaking background.
