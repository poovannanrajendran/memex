## Overview
Sora is a text-to-video generation model developed by OpenAI. It is designed to create realistic and imaginative videos from textual instructions, capable of generating complex scenes with multiple characters, specific motion types, and accurate details of the subject and background.

## Role in this knowledge base
It is highlighted as a revolutionary tool for video creation, enabling users to generate virtually any video content they can imagine.

## Key facts
- Sora can create any video that a user wants.
