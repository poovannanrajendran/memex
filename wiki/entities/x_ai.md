## Overview
X AI is an artificial intelligence company founded by Elon Musk, focused on developing advanced AI models. Its notable product is the Grok AI.

## Role in this knowledge base
Provider of the Grok AI model, which can be accessed and managed within the Open WebUI and LiteLLM framework.

## Key facts
- Grok AI's API access is mentioned as a model that can be integrated, although availability of the latest versions might vary.
