## Overview
Glide is a no-code platform that allows users to create web and mobile applications from spreadsheets. Similar to other no-code tools, it requires a degree of technical understanding for workflow configuration.

## Role in this knowledge base
A competitor to Base44, serving as another example of a platform that necessitates manual setup and understanding of app logic.

## Key facts
- Glide, like Bubble, requires users to have knowledge of databases and workflows for app creation.
