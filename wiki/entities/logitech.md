## Overview
Logitech is a Swiss company that designs and manufactures computer peripherals and accessories, including mice, keyboards, webcams, and speakers.

## Role in this knowledge base
Manufacturer of the MX Master 3S mouse, recommended for its advanced features and Mac compatibility.

## Key facts
- Logitech offers both Mac-specific and standard versions of their mice.
