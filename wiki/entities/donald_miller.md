## Overview
Donald Miller is an American author and strategist known for his work on storytelling and branding. He is the founder of StoryBrand, a company that helps businesses clarify their message.

## Role in this knowledge base
Author of the book "Building a StoryBrand", which is the central focus of the video's recommendations.

## Key facts
- He developed the SB7 Framework for clarifying brand messaging.
