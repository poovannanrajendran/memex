## Overview
Bold is presented as a tool or platform for building applications, similar to Replate and Lovable, whose functionalities are being integrated into Opera's Neon browser.

## Role in this knowledge base
An example of an AI builder whose capabilities are being subsumed by Neon's feature set.

## Key facts
- Neon's ability to build applications from prompts mirrors the functionality offered by builders like Bold.
