## Overview
Simon Harberg is the founder of a SaaS portfolio comprising four bootstrapped SaaS products focused on social media, AI, customer support, link tracking, and graphic design. He advocates for replacing expensive cloud tools with self-hostable alternatives.

## Role in this knowledge base
The speaker and creator of the video, who shares his personal experience and recommendations for self-hostable tools.

## Key facts
- He is transitioning his remote startup team away from expensive cloud tools to self-hostable alternatives.
