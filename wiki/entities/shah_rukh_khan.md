## Overview
Shah Rukh Khan is a globally renowned Indian actor, often referred to as 'King Khan' or 'SRK'. He is one of the most commercially successful and influential actors in Hindi cinema history, known for his roles in romantic comedies and dramas.

## Role in this knowledge base
He plays the lead character, Rahul, in the film 'Dil To Pagal Hai', who is initially sceptical about love.

## Key facts
- He is one of the most successful actors of Hindi cinema.
