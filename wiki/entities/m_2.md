## Overview
M.2 is a form factor for computer hardware components, typically used for SSDs (Solid State Drives) and Wi-Fi cards. M.2 SSDs are known for their compact size and high performance.

## Role in this knowledge base
Mentioned as a potentially faster but less portable alternative for external storage when used in an external enclosure.

## Key facts
- An M.2 SSD in an external enclosure is suggested as a quicker option for moving large files.
