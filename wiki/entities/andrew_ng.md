## Overview
Andrew Ng is a prominent figure in AI and machine learning, known for his co-founding roles at Coursera and Baidu AI, and as a former director of AI at Tesla and co-founder of OpenAI. He is celebrated for his accessible teaching style in AI.

## Role in this knowledge base
Offers a practical, 'Zero to Hero' learning path for building AI models, including a nano GPT project.
