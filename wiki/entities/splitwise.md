## Overview
Splitwise is an application that simplifies the process of splitting bills and tracking shared expenses among friends or travel companions. It allows users to record who paid for what and calculates who owes whom, supporting multiple currencies.

## Role in this knowledge base
A tool for group travel to easily divide tabs and settle accounts in multiple currencies.

## Key facts
- It allows users to easily divide tabs and settle up accounts in multiple currencies.
