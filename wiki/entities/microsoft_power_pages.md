## Overview
Microsoft Power Pages is a low-code platform for building secure, scalable business websites. It allows users to create websites from templates, customise them with a visual editor, and integrate with other Microsoft services.

## Role in this knowledge base
The primary subject of the tutorial, demonstrating its features and ease of use for website creation.

## Key facts
- It allows users to create both simple websites and fully data-driven web applications.
