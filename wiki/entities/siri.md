## Overview
Siri is Apple's virtual assistant, integrated into its devices to provide voice-activated assistance for tasks like setting reminders, making calls, and answering questions.

## Role in this knowledge base
A key Apple product that will be significantly upgraded by Apple Intelligence.

## Key facts
- The upgraded Siri, powered by Apple Intelligence, is expected to understand context better and rely less on web searches.
