## Overview
Krea is an AI video generation platform that focuses on abstract and morphing animations. It provides a free tier and is distinguished by its unique, trippy visual style suitable for creative projects.

## Role in this knowledge base
An AI video tool offering abstract and experimental animation styles, usable for free with some limitations.

## Key facts
- Krea specialises in creating trippy, morphing animations from keyframes and text prompts, suitable for abstract visualisations and music videos.
