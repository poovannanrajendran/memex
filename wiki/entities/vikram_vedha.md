## Overview
Vikram Vedha is a 2017 Indian Tamil-language neo-noir action thriller film written and directed by Pushkar–Gayathri. Inspired by the folktale Baital Pachisi, it stars R. Madhavan and Vijay Sethupathi and features K. Manikandan as a dialogue writer and actor.

## Role in this knowledge base
A significant film in K. Manikandan's career, for which he wrote the dialogues and played a role.

## Key facts
- The film is inspired by the Indian folktale 'Baital Pachisi'.
