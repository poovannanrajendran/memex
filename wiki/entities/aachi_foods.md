## Overview
Aachi Foods is the company behind the Aachi Masala brand. They are involved in the manufacturing and distribution of a variety of food products, including spice mixes, snacks, and more.

## Role in this knowledge base
The company that produces the featured masala and offers the dish for online ordering.
