## Overview
A double helical gear, also known as aherringbone gear, consists of two sets of helical teeth arranged in a V-shape. This design cancels out axial thrust forces.

## Role in this knowledge base
Highlighted for its advanced design that eliminates axial forces, leading to exceptional quietness and stability.

## Key facts
- Has V-shaped teeth that cancel out axial forces, making it extremely quiet and stable.
