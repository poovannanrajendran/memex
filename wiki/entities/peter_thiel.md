## Overview
Peter Thiel is a German-American billionaire entrepreneur, venture capitalist, and author. He is a co-founder of PayPal and Palantir Technologies, and was an early investor in Facebook.

## Role in this knowledge base
He is the author of the book 'Zero to One', recommended by Ritesh Agarwal.

## Key facts
- He is a co-founder of PayPal.
