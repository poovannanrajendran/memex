## Overview
Karisma Kapoor is an Indian actress who was a prominent star in Hindi films during the 1990s and early 2000s. She is known for her versatility and successful roles in both commercial and critically acclaimed films.

## Role in this knowledge base
She plays Nisha in 'Dil To Pagal Hai', whose belief is that love is akin to friendship.
