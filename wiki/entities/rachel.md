## Overview
Rachel is Morgan's sister and the mother of Jake and Emma. She is portrayed as self-centred and irresponsible.

## Role in this knowledge base
Rachel is the parent who created the dangerous situation by sending her children to the wrong address. Her parenting and judgment are central to the custody case.

## Key facts
- She provided an incomplete and incorrect address ('Oak Street' instead of 'Oak Avenue') when sending her children via taxi.
