## Overview
Costa is the owner of Hepi, a Greek restaurant in London. He is actively involved in the restaurant, making dishes like Taziki by hand at the front.

## Role in this knowledge base
Owner and chef at Hepi, involved in the preparation of key dishes and a notable figure in the restaurant's history.
