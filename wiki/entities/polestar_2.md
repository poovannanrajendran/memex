## Overview
The Polestar 2 is an electric performance fastback with a clean Scandinavian design and an Android Automotive OS-based infotainment system.

## Role in this knowledge base
Praised for its long range, excellent build quality, and refined driving experience, it's highlighted as a strong contender for those seeking a stylish and well-built EV.

## Key facts
- A 2021 model with 12,000 miles and 341 miles of range can be purchased for around £16,300.
