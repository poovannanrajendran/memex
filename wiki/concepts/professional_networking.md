## Definition
The process of building and maintaining relationships with other professionals to share information, advice, and opportunities. It involves connecting with individuals in one's industry or related fields to foster career growth and mutual benefit.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for career advancement, learning, and finding new opportunities. Tools like Happenstance aim to streamline and enhance this process, making it more efficient and effective.

## Key properties or components
- Relationship Building
- Information Exchange
- Opportunity Discovery
- Mutual Benefit
