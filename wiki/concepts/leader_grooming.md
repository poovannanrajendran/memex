## Definition
The process by which experienced leaders intentionally develop the skills, knowledge, and capabilities of potential future leaders within an organisation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source highlights this as a significant time investment for effective leaders, indicating its value in ensuring organisational continuity and growth.

## Key properties or components
- Mentorship
- Skill development
- Succession planning
