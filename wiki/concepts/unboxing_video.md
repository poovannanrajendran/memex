## Definition
An unboxing video is a type of video content where a product is opened and revealed for the first time. Viewers can see the packaging, contents, and sometimes an initial impression of the product.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept describes the format of the source content itself, which is a common way for retailers and tech enthusiasts to showcase products.

## Key properties or components
- Product packaging reveal
- Content enumeration
- First impressions
- Demonstration of included accessories
