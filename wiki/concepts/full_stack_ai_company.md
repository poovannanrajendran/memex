## Definition
A full-stack AI company is a business that uses artificial intelligence agents to automate tasks within an industry, not merely as a product to sell to existing companies, but to build and operate its own entity that competes directly with and aims to surpass incumbents.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept represents a novel and ambitious approach to AI-driven business creation, appealing to venture capitalists like YC who seek disruptive innovation.

## Key properties or components
- Leverages AI agents for core operations
- Directly competes with established industry players
- Aims to automate and improve industry processes fundamentally
- Focuses on disrupting slow-moving incumbents
