## Definition
The M1 Pro is a system on a chip (SoC) developed by Apple. It integrates the CPU, GPU, Neural Engine, and I/O into a single package, offering high performance and power efficiency for professional applications.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is the core component driving the performance of the MacBook Pro M1 Pro, making it suitable for demanding tasks.

## Key properties or components
- Integrated CPU and GPU
- Neural Engine for AI tasks
- High power efficiency
