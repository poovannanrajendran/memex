## Definition
A characteristic of advanced AI models that allows them to process and understand significantly larger amounts of input text or data (context) in a single interaction, improving their ability to perform deep analysis.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This capability is crucial for AI agents to conduct deep analysis of complex risk data within the specialty insurance market, enhancing their utility.

## Key properties or components
- Processes large input data
- Enables deep analysis
- Feature of AI models
