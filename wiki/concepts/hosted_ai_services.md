## Definition
Platforms that provide access to AI models and tools via the cloud, eliminating the need for users to manage infrastructure, setup, or maintenance.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Increases accessibility to powerful AI tools by abstracting away technical complexities and operational burdens, as exemplified by Kiloclaw.

## Key properties or components
- Cloud-based
- No setup required
- 24/7 availability
- Managed infrastructure
