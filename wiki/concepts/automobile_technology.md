## Definition
This refers to the technological advancements and innovations applied to vehicles, including engine design, electronics, safety systems, and connectivity features.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding automobile technology is crucial for consumers to make informed purchasing decisions and for appreciating the evolution of vehicles.

## Key properties or components

- Emission control
- Safety features
- Infotainment systems
- Connectivity
