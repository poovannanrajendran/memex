## Definition
Curated sequences of educational content, such as online courses or tutorials, designed to guide learners progressively through a specific subject or skill set. They build upon previous lessons to reinforce understanding.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provide structured learning experiences that help users effectively acquire new skills, such as digital art or productivity techniques, which can then be applied using iPad accessories.

## Key properties or components
- Structured curriculum
- Progressive learning
- Skill-focused
- Handpicked content
- Beginner to advanced levels
