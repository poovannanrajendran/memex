## Definition
The extent of the scene that is captured by the camera's sensor at any given moment, often expressed in degrees or descriptive terms like 'ultra-wide'.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Determines how much of the environment is included in the shot, crucial for action and vlogging perspectives.

## Key properties or components
- Linear view
- Ultra-wide FOV
- Hyperview mode
- Distortion

## Contradictions or debates
None.
