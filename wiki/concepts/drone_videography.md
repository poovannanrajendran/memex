## Definition
Drone videography is the practice of capturing video footage from the perspective of a drone (an unmanned aerial vehicle). It allows for dynamic aerial shots that were previously difficult or impossible to obtain.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This technique provides unique perspectives of urban environments like Leeds, showcasing the city's architecture and layout in a visually engaging manner.

## Key properties or components
- Aerial perspective
- Dynamic camera movement
- Access to difficult locations
- Requires skilled piloting and filming
