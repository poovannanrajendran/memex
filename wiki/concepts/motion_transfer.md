## Definition
The process by which mechanical motion is transmitted from one part of a system to another, often involving changes in speed, direction, or type of motion.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Core principle in all mechanical engineering, enabling machines to perform specific tasks through coordinated movement.

## Key properties or components
- Speed
- Direction
- Torque
- Type of motion
