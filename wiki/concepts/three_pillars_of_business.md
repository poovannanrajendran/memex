## Definition
The fundamental components of any business: Acquisition (marketing and sales), Delivery (product/service creation), and Operations (supporting functions like finance, HR, legal).

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a high-level structure for understanding business functions and identifying areas for growth, improvement, or external support.

## Key properties or components
- Acquisition
- Delivery
- Operations
- Interdependence
- Scalability
