## Definition
The practice of differentiating product models by offering distinct sets of features, often reserving advanced or 'hero' features for premium versions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Apple uses this strategy to justify the price difference between standard and Pro iPhones, allowing users to wait for features to trickle down.

## Key properties or components
- Standard vs. Pro models
- Premium features for higher cost
- Feature trickle-down over time

## Contradictions or debates
None.
