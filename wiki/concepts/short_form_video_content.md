## Definition
Digital video content that is typically brief in duration, often ranging from a few seconds to a few minutes. Platforms like YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels popularised this format.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding short-form content is crucial given the source is a YouTube Short. This format's characteristics influence content creation, consumption patterns, and engagement strategies, impacting how information or entertainment is delivered.

## Key properties or components
- Brevity
- High engagement potential
- Mobile-first consumption
- Algorithmic discovery
- Quick consumption
