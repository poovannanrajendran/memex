## Definition
A scheme, often run by governments, that allows pre-screened, low-risk travellers to benefit from expedited customs and immigration processing. Membership is typically granted after a background check and often requires biometric data.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a model for streamlining border control and improving the travel experience for vetted individuals, aligning with goals of efficiency and security in international movement.

## Key properties or components
- Pre-screening of travellers
- Background checks
- Expedited border crossing
- Reduced wait times
- Often country-specific or international agreements
