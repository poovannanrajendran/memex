## Definition
Queen of the Mountain (QOM) is a term used in cycling to denote the fastest female rider on a specific climb or segment. It is analogous to the King of the Mountain (KOM) designation for male cyclists.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Achieving QOM status on a local climb was a major personal goal and a significant milestone in the narrator's cycling journey, representing a culmination of their efforts to improve.

## Key properties or components
- Fastest female time
- Specific climb segment
- Competitive cycling achievement
