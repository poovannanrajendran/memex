## Definition
Validation is the act of confirming or supporting the accuracy or truth of something. In a psychological context, it refers to acknowledging and accepting another person's feelings, thoughts, or experiences as legitimate.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The sketch humorously implies that women primarily seek validation and to be heard, touching upon a common theme in interpersonal relationships.

## Key properties or components
- Acknowledgement
- Empathy
- Acceptance
- Understanding
