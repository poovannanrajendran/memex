## Definition
An e-commerce strategy where individuals or businesses leverage Amazon's platform to sell products directly to consumers. This involves sourcing inventory, listing products, managing sales, and utilizing Amazon's fulfillment services.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is presented as a direct and actionable strategy for wealth generation, especially for individuals with a moderate capital sum like ₹1 crore.

## Key properties or components
- Product sourcing
- Inventory management
- Listing optimization
- Customer service
- Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) potential
