## Definition
A throwback is a reference to something in the past, often nostalgic. In media, it refers to re-sharing or highlighting older content.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept frames the content of the video as older material being re-presented, likely for nostalgic appeal or to fill programming slots.

## Key properties or components
- Nostalgia
- Archived content
- Past events/performances
- Re-sharing
