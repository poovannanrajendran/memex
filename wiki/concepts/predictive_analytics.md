## Definition
Predictive analytics uses historical data, statistical algorithms, and machine learning techniques to identify the likelihood of future outcomes. It's used across industries for forecasting trends and behaviours.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the primary application of Miroofish, offering a valuable capability previously only accessible through expensive simulations, relevant to financial markets and potentially insurance risk assessment.

## Key properties or components
- Data Analysis
- Forecasting
- Probability
- Trend Identification
