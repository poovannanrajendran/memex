## Definition
An orchestration layer, in the context of AI agents, is a system that manages, coordinates, and directs the activities of multiple AI agents. It ensures they work together efficiently towards a common goal.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to understanding how Paperclip functions as a platform for organising and utilising AI agents effectively, particularly for enhancing productivity.

## Key properties or components
- Manages multiple AI agents
- Coordinates agent activities
- Directs agent workflows
- Facilitates agent collaboration
