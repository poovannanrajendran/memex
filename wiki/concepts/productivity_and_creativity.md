## Definition
Productivity refers to the efficiency with which tasks are completed, often measured by output per unit of input. Creativity is the ability to generate novel and useful ideas or to produce original work.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
For content creators, the synergy between productivity and creativity is paramount. AI tools aim to enhance both by automating tedious tasks (boosting productivity) and providing new avenues for artistic expression (fostering creativity).

## Key properties or components
- Efficiency
- Novelty
- Originality
- Output optimization
- Ideation support
