## Definition
The ability of a software application or tool to operate without an active internet connection. This is often dependent on the user's local hardware resources.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances accessibility, privacy, and reliability by removing dependency on network connectivity. For LTX Desktop, it allows video generation and editing locally.

## Key properties or components
- Does not require internet connection
- Relies on local hardware
- Can improve performance and reduce latency
- Useful in environments with poor connectivity
