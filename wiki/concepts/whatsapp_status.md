## Definition
A feature on the WhatsApp messaging application that allows users to share short updates, photos, or videos that disappear after 24 hours.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video is explicitly formatted and intended for use on this platform, indicating its utility for personal social media sharing.

## Key properties or components
- Temporary (24-hour lifespan)
- Supports text, photos, videos, and GIFs
- Customisable by users
