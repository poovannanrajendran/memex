## Definition
A metadata table stores information about other data, often referred to as 'data about data'. In a database context, it holds descriptive attributes of data elements, such as their type, format, creation date, or source.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The successful setup of the server was validated by checking the accuracy of records within the Supabase metadata table.
