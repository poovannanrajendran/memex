## Definition
Unboxing is the process of opening a new product package for the first time, often documented on video. It showcases the product's packaging, contents, and initial presentation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The unboxing segment allows viewers to see what comes with the Galaxy S24 Ultra and get a first look at the device's physical appearance.

## Key properties or components

- Product presentation
- Initial impressions
- First reveal
