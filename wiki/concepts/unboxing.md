## Definition
The act of opening a new product, typically a piece of consumer electronics, for the first time and showcasing its contents and initial impressions. It is a popular format for tech reviews and content creation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Unboxing videos generate excitement and provide potential buyers with a visual preview of the product, packaging, and included accessories.

## Key properties or components
- First look at product
- Packaging reveal
- Included accessories
- Initial impressions
