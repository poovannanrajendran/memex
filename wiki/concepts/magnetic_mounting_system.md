## Definition
A mounting mechanism that utilizes magnets for quick and easy attachment and detachment of a camera to accessories or surfaces. This system prioritizes speed and convenience over traditional screw-based mounts.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A feature adopted by GoPro, following trends set by DJI and Insta360, to improve user experience and speed up setup.

## Key properties or components
- Quick attachment/detachment
- Convenient
- Relies on magnets
- Potential alignment issues (criticism)
