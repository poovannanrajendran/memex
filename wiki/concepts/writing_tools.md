## Definition
Features within Apple Intelligence designed to help users refine their written text, offering options to adjust tone, summarise, or condense content.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
One of the most highlighted features of Apple Intelligence, intended to assist with communication tasks.

## Key properties or components
- Tone adjustment (friendly, professional)
- Conciseness
- Proofreading
- Summarisation
- Table generation

## Contradictions or debates
The reviewer found these tools to be of limited practical use and not frequently necessary for their writing tasks.
