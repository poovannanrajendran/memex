## Definition
The use of artificial intelligence tools and technologies to aid in the process of generating, editing, and optimizing digital content.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enables faster and more efficient production of content, particularly for scalable models like faceless YouTube channels, lowering the barrier to entry.

## Key properties or components
- Script generation
- Voiceover synthesis
- Video editing and generation
- Image and music creation
- Content optimization
