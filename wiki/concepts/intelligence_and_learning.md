## Definition
The capacity for logic, understanding, self-awareness, learning, emotional knowledge, reasoning, planning, creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving. It is crucial for acquiring new skills and knowledge, such as those needed for mechanical work.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the comedic dialogue, where the characters debate the nature of intelligence and the learning process required for a technical skill.

## Key properties or components
- Cognitive ability
- Problem-solving
- Adaptability
- Learning capacity
