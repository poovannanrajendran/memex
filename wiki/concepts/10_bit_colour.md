## Definition
A colour depth that allows for over a billion colours, providing smoother gradients and more flexibility for colour grading compared to 8-bit colour's millions of colours.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for professional video production, enabling finer control over colour adjustments in post-production without introducing banding.

## Key properties or components
- Smoother gradients
- Wider colour gamut
- Greater post-production flexibility
