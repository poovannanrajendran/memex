## Definition
A gear mechanism that is similar in appearance to a worm gear but is designed to transmit motion between intersecting axes. It offers a compact design solution.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a compact way to transmit motion between intersecting shafts, complementing the applications of worm gears.

## Key properties or components
- Intersecting axes
- Compact design
- Similar to worm gears
