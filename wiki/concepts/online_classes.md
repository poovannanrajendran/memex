## Definition
Educational sessions conducted over the internet, typically using video conferencing software. These classes allow students and instructors to interact remotely, breaking down geographical barriers.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This video satirises the common student experience during online classes, highlighting the distractions and unique behaviours that arise in this learning environment.

## Key properties or components
- Remote learning
- Video conferencing
- Flexibility
- Potential for distraction

## Contradictions or debates
While offering flexibility, the effectiveness can be hampered by technical issues and student engagement levels.
