## Definition
Curiosity is an intentional drive to explore, experiment, and look beyond the surface to gain a deeper understanding. It involves questioning the status quo and seeking novel perspectives.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for innovative leaders as it enables them to think outside the box, discover new approaches, and drive progress.

## Key properties or components
- Intentional drive
- Exploration
- Experimentation
- Deeper understanding
- Questioning the status quo

## Contradictions or debates
None.
