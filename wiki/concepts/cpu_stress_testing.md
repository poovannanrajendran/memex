## Definition
CPU stress testing involves running demanding software that pushes the central processing unit to its maximum capacity for an extended period. This is done to evaluate thermal performance, stability, and sustained clock speeds under heavy load.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is crucial for understanding the thermal management and sustained performance of the AMD APU versus the M4 Pro, particularly noting that the AMD chip runs cooler despite high performance.

## Key properties or components
- Sustained clock speed
- Thermal throttling
- Power consumption
- System stability
