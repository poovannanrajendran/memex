## Definition
The act of attempting to perform multiple tasks simultaneously, often leading to reduced efficiency and quality in each task. In the context of online classes, this refers to students engaging in non-academic activities while supposedly attending lessons.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video explicitly showcases students multitasking with household chores, which is a common and relatable aspect of the online learning experience, often leading to divided attention.

## Key properties or components
- Simultaneous tasking
- Reduced focus
- Decreased productivity
- Distraction

## Contradictions or debates
Some argue that limited multitasking can improve efficiency by breaking monotony, but excessive multitasking typically degrades performance.
