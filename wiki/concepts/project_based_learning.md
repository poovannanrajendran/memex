## Definition
An educational approach where students learn by actively engaging in real-world and personally meaningful projects. This method emphasizes practical application of knowledge and skills.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlighted as a critical stage in coding education, involving both following tutorials and creating independent projects.

## Key properties or components
- Practical Application
- Skill Development
- Problem-Solving
- Portfolio Building

## Contradictions or debates
