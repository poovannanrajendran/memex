## Definition
Software applications and platforms designed to automate repetitive tasks and workflows, reducing the need for manual human intervention. Examples include Zapier and Make.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for building and delivering services for an AI automation agency, enabling the connection and orchestration of different software.

## Key properties or components
- Connects applications
- Automates workflows
- Reduces manual effort
- Increases efficiency
