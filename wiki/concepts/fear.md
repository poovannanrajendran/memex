## Definition
An unpleasant emotion caused by the belief that someone or something is dangerous, likely to cause pain, or a threat. It is a basic survival mechanism.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Excessive or irrational fear can paralyse individuals, preventing them from taking necessary risks, pursuing opportunities, and achieving their full potential.

## Key properties or components
- Basic emotion
- Survival instinct
- Can be irrational
