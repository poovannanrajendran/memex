## Definition
A neurodevelopmental disorder characterised by inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity. In this context, it is primarily framed as a 'lack of time maturity'.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Explains the root cause of lateness as discussed in the video.

## Key properties or components
- Inattention
- Lack of time maturity
- Disorganization
- Trouble paying attention
- Tendency to lose things
