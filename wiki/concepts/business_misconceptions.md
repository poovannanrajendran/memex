## Definition
Commonly held, but incorrect, beliefs about how businesses operate, their profitability, or the ease with which success can be achieved.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video directly addresses and debunks the misconception that a tea shop is an easy way to earn significant money.

## Key properties or components
- Underestimation of workload
- Overestimation of profit
- Lack of realistic planning
- Idealisation of entrepreneurship
