## Definition
A hardware device that converts an incoming video signal (e.g., from a camera or console) into a format that can be processed and displayed by a computer or, in this case, an iPad.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Transforms an iPad into a high-quality monitor for external sources like cameras or gaming consoles, significantly enhancing its utility for content creators and streamers.

## Key properties or components
- Video input
- Signal conversion
- HDMI/USB-C interface
- Real-time processing
