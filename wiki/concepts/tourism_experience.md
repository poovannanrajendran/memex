## Definition
The overall perception and satisfaction a traveler has with their journey, encompassing attractions, services, interactions, and environmental factors.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The vlog provides a balanced view, detailing both highly enjoyable aspects (cleanliness, safety, culture) and negative points (language, restrooms, vendors), ultimately concluding the trip was thoroughly enjoyed.

## Key properties or components
- Attractions visited
- Local interactions
- Service quality
- Environmental factors
- Overall satisfaction
