## Definition
Dharma is a complex concept in Indian philosophy, often translated as duty, righteousness, moral law, or the "right way of living." It is the cosmic order that sustains the universe.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A central pillar of the [[Mahabharata Moments]] podcast. Understanding dharma is essential to unravelling the moral complexities and character choices in the epic.

## Key properties or components
- **Svadharma:** Individual duty based on one's nature and stage of life.
- **Sadharana Dharma:** Universal moral values (truth, compassion).

## Contradictions or debates
The Mahabharata frequently explores the "subtle nature of dharma," where different duties conflict (e.g., truth vs. loyalty).
