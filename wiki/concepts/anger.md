## Definition
An intense emotion characterized by antagonism toward someone or something perceived to have purposefully done wrong or a threat. It can range from mild irritation to intense rage.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Uncontrolled anger can lead to destructive behaviours, damaged relationships, and poor decision-making, negatively impacting one's life.

## Key properties or components
- Emotional response
- Can be destructive
- Requires management
