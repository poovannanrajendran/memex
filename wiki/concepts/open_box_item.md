## Definition
An open box item refers to a product that has been previously purchased and returned or unpacked, but is still in good condition and often sold at a reduced price. These items are typically inspected and graded based on their physical state.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is relevant as it describes the condition of the iPad Air 5 purchased by Tracy, affecting the perceived value and quality of the product.

## Key properties or components
- Previously opened packaging
- May have minor cosmetic imperfections
- Often sold at a discount
- Inspected for functionality
