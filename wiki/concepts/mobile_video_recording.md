## Definition
The process of capturing moving images using a smartphone. This includes considerations for resolution, frame rates, stabilisation, audio quality, and advanced features like cinematic modes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key area of comparison, highlighting differences in video quality, ultra-wide capabilities, and overall performance between the iPhone and Samsung.

## Key properties or components
- Video Resolution (4K, 8K)
- Frame Rate
- Stabilisation
- Audio Quality
- Ultra-wide Video
- Cinematic Mode
