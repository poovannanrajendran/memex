## Definition
This phrase suggests a new era of progress, development, and opportunity for India. It implies a future characterized by innovation, growth, and a positive outlook.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It sets a visionary tone for discussions about India's potential and future trajectory, aligning with national development goals.

## Key properties or components
- Forward-looking perspective
- Emphasis on progress
- Potential for growth
- National aspiration
