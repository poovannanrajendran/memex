## Definition
Generative Fill is a feature within Adobe Photoshop that uses artificial intelligence to generate new image content based on text prompts or selections made by the user. It allows for the addition, removal, or modification of elements within an image seamlessly.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It significantly streamlines the process of creating or altering product images, reducing the need for manual editing and providing creative options that would be difficult to achieve otherwise.

## Key properties or components
- Text-to-image generation
- Content-aware editing
- Seamless integration with existing images
- Iterative refinement
