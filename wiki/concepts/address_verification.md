## Definition
Address verification is the process of confirming that a given address is accurate and legitimate. This is crucial for ensuring correct delivery of goods, services, or in this case, people.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The failure to perform address verification is the direct cause of the children being sent to the wrong location, leading to the entire crisis. It highlights a basic step that could have prevented the incident.

## Key properties or components
- Accuracy
- Confirmation
- Delivery
- Safety
