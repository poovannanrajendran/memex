## Definition
The use of wit, irony, exaggeration, or absurdity in conversation to evoke amusement. It often relies on unexpected twists, wordplay, or subversion of expectations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The core of the YouTube short is the humorous exchange between two characters, highlighting how comedic dialogue can be used to explore even simple topics like learning and intelligence.

## Key properties or components
- Wordplay
- Exaggeration
- Irony
- Absurdity
