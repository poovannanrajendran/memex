## Definition
A dubbing artist provides voice-overs for films, television shows, or other media, replacing the original dialogue in one language with a translated version in another, or providing voices for animated characters or characters with altered voices.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for international distribution of films and for character creation in animation; K. Manikandan has prior experience in this field.

## Key properties or components
- Voice acting
- Lip-syncing
- Language translation
- Character interpretation
