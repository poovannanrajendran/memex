## Definition
Offline maps are digital maps that can be downloaded and accessed on a mobile device without requiring an active internet connection. They are crucial for navigation in areas with poor or no cellular service.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Ensures travelers can navigate, find points of interest, and access directions even without Wi-Fi or mobile data, saving on roaming charges and providing reliability.

## Key properties or components
- Downloadable Data
- GPS Integration
- No Internet Required
- Offline Navigation
