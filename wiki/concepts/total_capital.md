## Definition
Total capital, in the context of Lloyd's, refers to the aggregate financial resources available, encompassing capital, reserves, and subordinated loan notes. It signifies the overall financial backing.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This metric is vital as it indicates the Lloyd's market's capacity to absorb significant losses, support underwriting activities, and maintain financial stability. It underpins market confidence.

## Key properties or components
- Increased by 5.7% to £49.8bn at 31 December 2025 (from £47.1bn in 2024).
