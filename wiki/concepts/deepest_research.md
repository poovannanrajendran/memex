## Definition
An approach to researching a topic by querying multiple AI models and forums, rather than relying on a single source. This ensures a broader and potentially more accurate understanding.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlights the need for diverse AI inputs for robust research, acknowledging that different models may offer unique perspectives or information.

## Key properties or components
- Querying multiple LLMs (GPT, Grock, Plexity)
- Consulting various forums
- Aim: Comprehensive understanding
