## Definition
Bollywood soundtracks are the musical scores and songs featured in Hindi-language films produced by the Indian film industry. These soundtracks are often released as standalone albums and are a significant commercial component of the films.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Soundtracks are integral to the narrative and commercial success of Bollywood films, often becoming as popular as the films themselves.

## Key properties or components
- Integral to film narrative
- Commercial release as albums
- Diverse musical genres
- Promotional tool for films
