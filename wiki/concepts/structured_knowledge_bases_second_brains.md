## Definition
Organised systems or repositories designed to capture, store, and retrieve personal or organisational knowledge efficiently. The term 'Second Brain' refers to an externalised system for intellectual and informational asset management.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
They are considered essential for effectively retaining valuable market intelligence in the rapidly evolving insurance sector.

## Key properties or components
- Retains market intelligence
- Organised information repository
- Supports recall and synthesis
