## Definition
An intuitive understanding and appreciation for aesthetically pleasing design. It involves recognising good proportions, colour combinations, and visual hierarchies, and avoiding clichés or amateurish choices.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The 'taste skill' attempts to imbue AI with this human-like judgment, moving beyond technical execution to achieve visually appealing and sophisticated outcomes that avoid generic 'AI-looking' results.

## Key properties or components
- Aesthetic judgment
- Recognition of good design
- Avoidance of generic outputs
- Subjective but learnable

## Contradictions or debates
The concept of 'taste' being teachable to an AI is subjective and could be debated, as taste is often considered an intuitive human quality.
