## Definition
A data breach is a security incident where sensitive, protected, or confidential data is copied, transmitted, viewed, stolen, or used by an unauthorised individual.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A data breach is mentioned as the subject of an HR meeting related to Akshata's exit, raising concerns about the mishandling of sensitive information.

- Confidentiality
- Security Incident
- Unauthorized Access
- Sensitive Information
