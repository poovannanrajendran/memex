## Definition
A person's condition of psychological and emotional well-being, typically from the viewpoint of positive or negative attributes and the ability to cope with the normal stresses of life.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The speaker posits that mental health has deteriorated, particularly post-COVID-19, linking it to cognitive changes and reliance on external aids.

## Key properties or components
- Psychological well-being
- Emotional well-being
- Coping mechanisms
