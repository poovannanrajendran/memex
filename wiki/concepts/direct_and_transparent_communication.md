## Definition
This is an approach to communication where information is shared openly, honestly, and without unnecessary intermediaries. In a business context, it means leaders directly address issues with their team members.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for effective leadership in small companies, enabling prompt problem resolution and fostering trust.

## Key properties or components
- Openness
- Honesty
- Timeliness
- Proactiveness

## Contradictions or debates
None.
