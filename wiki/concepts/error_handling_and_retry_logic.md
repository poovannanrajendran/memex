## Definition
This concept involves mechanisms to gracefully manage errors that occur during agent operations and automatically retry failed tasks. This improves the reliability and resilience of AI agents.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlighted as a key feature of Pantic AI that is often missing in other frameworks, enabling agents to recover from transient issues like API rate limits or temporary service outages.

## Key properties or components
- Resilience
- Fault Tolerance
- Automatic Retries
- Error Detection
- Service Outage Mitigation
