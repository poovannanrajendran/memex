## Definition
A principle suggesting that the best way to achieve a desired outcome or maintain stability is to avoid unnecessary actions or reactions that could disrupt the natural course of events. It implies a 'less is more' approach to engagement.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Central theme of the source, advising against 'touching the water' or creating ripples that divert energy from the main goal.

## Key properties or components
- Minimal action
- Observation
- Detachment
- Focus conservation

## Contradictions or debates
None.
