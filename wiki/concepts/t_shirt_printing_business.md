## Definition
A business that customises and prints designs onto t-shirts, often for retail sale or promotional purposes. It involves sourcing blank t-shirts, applying designs using various printing techniques, and marketing the final product.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the core business being discussed in the video, highlighting operational aspects like supplier evaluation and customer orders.

## Key properties or components
- Sourcing blank apparel
- Design customisation
- Printing techniques (e.g., screen printing, DTG)
- Quality control
- Supplier relationship management
