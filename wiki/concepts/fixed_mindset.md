## Definition
A fixed mindset is the belief that one's basic qualities, such as intelligence or talents, are simply fixed traits that cannot be significantly changed.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Individuals with a fixed mindset may avoid challenges and give up easily when faced with difficulties, as they fear failure will expose their perceived limitations.

## Key properties or components
- Belief in innate traits
- Avoiding challenges
- Giving up easily
- Ignoring useful negative feedback
- Threatened by the success of others
