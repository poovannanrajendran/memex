## Definition
The phenomenon where music, despite being in a language not understood by the listener, can still resonate deeply and become popular.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the video's premise, showcasing how compelling music can transcend linguistic barriers and achieve widespread popularity across different regions and cultures.

## Key properties or components
- Universal emotional connection
- Rhythm and melody focus
- Cultural diffusion

## Contradictions or debates
Some argue that lyrics are crucial for a song's full impact, and thus, understanding the language is necessary for true appreciation.
