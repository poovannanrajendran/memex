## Definition
A multibagger stock is an investment that yields returns of more than 100%, effectively more than doubling its price. These are typically stocks of companies that experience significant growth and market appreciation over time.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Identifying potential multibagger stocks is a key objective for many investors seeking substantial capital appreciation, as highlighted in the video's premise.

## Key properties or components
- High growth potential
- Significant stock price appreciation
- Often associated with strong company fundamentals
- Long-term investment horizon
