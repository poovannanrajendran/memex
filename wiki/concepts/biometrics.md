## Definition
The measurement and statistical analysis of people's unique physical and behavioural characteristics. Common examples include fingerprints, facial recognition, and iris scans, used for identification and authentication.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Key technology enabling programmes like FTITP, allowing for secure and rapid identification of individuals, which is crucial for efficient and safe travel processing.

## Key properties or components
- Unique physical characteristics
- Unique behavioural characteristics
- Identification
- Authentication
- Data privacy concerns
