## Definition
This refers to the extent to which a work environment allows for lightheartedness, humour, and non-work-related activities during working hours. While it can boost morale, it can also lead to distractions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Balancing playfulness with productivity is a key challenge in modern workplaces.

## Key properties or components
- Morale Boosting
- Distraction Potential
- Team Bonding

## Contradictions or debates
The skit highlights the potential negative impact of excessive playfulness, suggesting it can detract from work responsibilities.
