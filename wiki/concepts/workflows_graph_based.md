## Definition
A system for orchestrating multiple AI agents and functions in a sequence or graph structure to perform complex, multi-step tasks. Supports features like checkpointing and human-in-the-loop interaction.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enable explicit control and management of complex AI task execution, allowing for robust and adaptable AI applications.

## Key properties or components
- Graph structure
- Type-safe routing
- Checkpointing
- Human-in-the-loop

None.
