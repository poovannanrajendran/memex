## Definition
Goals are specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) objectives that individuals or organisations aim to accomplish.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Goals provide direction and purpose, and their achievement is often intertwined with productivity.

## Key properties or components
- Objectives
- Targets
- Aspirations
