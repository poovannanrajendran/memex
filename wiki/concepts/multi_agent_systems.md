## Definition
AI systems composed of multiple interacting and often specialised AI agents that work collaboratively or cooperatively to achieve complex tasks or manage intricate workflows.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
They enable the distribution of workload, enhance system resilience, and are particularly suited for complex business problems like supply chain management or elaborate customer service scenarios that require diverse capabilities.

## Key properties or components
- Multiple interacting agents
- Collaborative or coordinated
- Achieve complex goals

## Contradictions or debates
None.
