## Definition
Hyperframes is an open-source framework that transforms HTML code into MP4 videos, specifically designed for use with AI agents. It enables rapid video generation from simple prompts and HTML structures.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It significantly lowers the barrier to entry for video production, making it accessible and cost-effective for AI agents and developers, potentially disrupting traditional video creation workflows and enabling new business models.

## Key properties or components
- Renders HTML to MP4
- Open-sourced (Apache 2)
- Designed for AI agents
- Fast video generation
- Low cost
