## Definition
Repositories on GitHub are collections of code, files, and their revision history. They serve as a platform for software development and collaboration, often hosting tools and extensions for various applications, including AI assistants.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
These repositories are crucial for distributing and enhancing AI tools like Claude, enabling rapid development and feature expansion through community contributions.

## Key properties or components
- Code storage
- Version control
- Collaboration platform
- Distribution of tools/extensions
