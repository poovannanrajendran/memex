## Definition
The formal procedures and systems established by law for resolving disputes or enforcing rights. This includes court proceedings, arbitration, and other recognised legal mechanisms.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Mitchell Bond's email explicitly states his intention to pursue matters through 'appropriate legal channels' if an agreement isn't reached, indicating a serious dispute.

## Key properties or components
- Formal procedures
- Legal system
- Dispute resolution
- Enforcement of rights
