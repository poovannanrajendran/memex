## Definition
The practice of carefully crafting input text (prompts) to guide generative AI models to produce desired outputs. Effective prompting is crucial for controlling the AI's behavior and the quality of its results.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A fundamental skill for interacting with and leveraging generative AI models effectively, as required for roles like Generative Application Developer.

## Key properties or components
- Input crafting
- Model guidance
- Output control
