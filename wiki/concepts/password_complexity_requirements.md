## Definition
These are rules set for creating passwords that mandate the use of a mix of character types, such as uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. They are designed to enhance security by making passwords harder to guess or crack.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is relevant as it forms the basis of the humour in the video, highlighting a common user frustration with overly complex password rules.

## Key properties or components
- Requires uppercase letters
- Requires lowercase letters
- Requires numbers
- Requires special characters
