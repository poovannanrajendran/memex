## Definition
The process of selling products or services to large organisations, which typically involves longer sales cycles, multiple stakeholders, and complex decision-making processes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is relevant as one of the founders highlights the initial skepticism they faced regarding their ability to succeed in US enterprise sales.

## Key properties or components
- Long sales cycles
- Multiple stakeholders
- Complex procurement
- High-value contracts

## Contradictions or debates
