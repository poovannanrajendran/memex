## Definition
The process or effect of decreasing ambiguity or unpredictability in a given situation. In service contexts, it often involves providing clear and timely information to manage expectations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is presented as the key driver behind improved passenger satisfaction, more so than actual service speed or efficiency.

## Key properties or components
- Information provision
- Expectation management
- Anxiety reduction

## Contradictions or debates
None.
