## Definition
The process of defining the visual appearance of a website, including colours, fonts, spacing, and overall aesthetic. Theming refers to applying a consistent visual style across the entire site.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Power Pages offers extensive styling options through its 'Styling' tab, allowing users to customise the look and feel of their website to match branding or desired aesthetics.

## Key properties or components
- Colour palettes
- Font customisation
- Button styles
- Layout spacing
- Theme selection
