## Definition
The process of creating the visual layout and interactive elements of a digital product, focusing on aesthetics and usability.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for user adoption and satisfaction; AI tools are increasingly being used to enhance and expedite this process.

## Key properties or components
- Wireframing
- Prototyping
- Visual Design
- User Flows
