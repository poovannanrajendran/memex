## Definition
An individual's internal perception and understanding of time. It is described as a developmental capacity that matures over time and can be affected by environmental factors.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Its underdeveloped state is directly linked to chronic lateness and ADD.

## Key properties or components
- Developmental
- Internal perception
- Susceptible to environmental influence

## Contradictions or debates
None.
