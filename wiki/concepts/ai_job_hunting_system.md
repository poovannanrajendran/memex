## Definition
An automated system that uses artificial intelligence to assist individuals in their job search. It typically includes features like job matching, resume tailoring, and interview preparation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Significantly enhances the efficiency and effectiveness of job searching by automating tedious tasks and providing personalized insights.

## Key properties or components
- Job description analysis
- CV comparison and scoring
- Automated resume generation
- Interview preparation
- Tracking of applications
