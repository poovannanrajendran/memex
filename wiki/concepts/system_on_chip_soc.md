## Definition
A System on a Chip (SoC) is an integrated circuit that integrates most or all components of a computer or other electronic system. This typically includes a processor, memory, and input/output interfaces.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The SoC is the 'brain' of a smartphone, determining its overall performance, efficiency, and capabilities, including AI processing power.

## Key properties or components
- Central Processing Unit (CPU)
- Graphics Processing Unit (GPU)
- Memory controllers
- Connectivity modules
- Image Signal Processor (ISP)
- AI accelerators (e.g., NPU)
