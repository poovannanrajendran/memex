## Definition
The process of creating detailed and specific text instructions (prompts) that guide an AI model to generate a desired video output. This involves specifying elements like style, content, camera angles, lighting, and artistic direction.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for achieving specific and high-quality results from AI video generation tools. The Higgsfield skills aim to automate and optimize this complex prompt engineering task.

## Key properties or components
- Detailed instructions for AI
- Specifies video style and content
- Includes technical production details
- Requires understanding of AI model capabilities
