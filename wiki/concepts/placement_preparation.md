## Definition
The process students undertake to prepare themselves for job interviews and recruitment drives, particularly those conducted by companies on college campuses. It involves skill development, resume building, and interview practice.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for students aiming to secure employment upon graduation, especially in competitive fields like technology.

## Key properties or components
- Skill enhancement
- Resume building
- Interview practice
- Networking
- Understanding company requirements

## Contradictions or debates
None.
