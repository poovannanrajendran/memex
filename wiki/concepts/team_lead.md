## Definition
A team lead is an experienced individual who guides and supports a team in their day-to-day tasks, often acting as a liaison between the team and upper management.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding the role of a team lead is crucial for effective team dynamics and project management.

## Key properties or components
- Guidance
- Support
- Communication
- Task Delegation
