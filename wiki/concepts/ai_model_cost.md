## Definition
The expense associated with accessing and utilising artificial intelligence models, often calculated based on usage, computational resources, or subscription tiers.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A significant barrier to widespread adoption and experimentation with advanced AI technologies, as highlighted in the context of OpenClaw.

## Key properties or components
- Usage-based pricing
- Subscription models
- Credit systems
- Computational resource allocation
