## Definition
The range of terms, principles, and elements associated with design, including aspects like typography, color theory, spacing, and layout. A strong design vocabulary allows for more nuanced and effective visual communication.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Providing Claude with a comprehensive design vocabulary, as done by the 'impeccable' skill, allows it to generate more sophisticated and contextually appropriate design choices.

## Key properties or components
- Typography
- Color
- Spacing
- Layout
