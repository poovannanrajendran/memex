## Definition
A learning methodology where one starts with a broad, foundational concept and progressively branches out to more specific, finer details. It's a hierarchical approach to knowledge acquisition.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the source as it describes Elon Musk's efficient learning hack, relevant to improving personal productivity and skill acquisition in complex domains.

## Key properties or components
- Starts with foundational concepts
- Progresses to finer details
- Hierarchical structure
- Facilitates rapid learning
