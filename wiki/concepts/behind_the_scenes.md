## Definition
Behind-the-scenes refers to content that offers viewers a look at the making of a film, song, or any creative project. It typically includes footage of the production process, interviews with the cast and crew, and glimpses of the challenges and triumphs encountered.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances audience engagement by providing a deeper understanding and appreciation of the effort and artistry that goes into creative works. It demystifies the production process.

## Key properties or components
- Exclusive footage
- Production insights
- Interviews
- Unseen moments
- Fan engagement
