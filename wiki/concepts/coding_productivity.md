## Definition
The measure of efficiency and output achieved by software developers. It involves optimising workflows, utilising effective tools, and minimising time spent on non-coding tasks or hindrances like debugging and searching for information.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhancing coding productivity is vital for companies like Lloyd's, as faster development cycles and more efficient code can lead to quicker innovation, better product delivery, and cost savings. Tools that boost productivity are of significant interest.

## Key properties or components
- Time management
- Tool utilisation
- Workflow optimisation
- Minimising distractions
- Effective problem-solving
