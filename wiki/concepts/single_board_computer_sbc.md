## Definition
A complete computer built on a single circuit board, with a microprocessor, memory, and input/output capabilities. They are typically small, low-power, and affordable, making them suitable for embedded systems and hobbyist projects.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The Raspberry Pi 5 is an example of an SBC, and understanding this category is crucial for appreciating its design and applications.

## Key properties or components
- All components on one board
- Compact size
- Low power consumption
- Affordability
- Versatile I/O
