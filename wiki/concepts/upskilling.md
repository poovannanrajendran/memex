## Definition
The process of learning new skills or improving existing ones to enhance job performance or adapt to changing industry demands.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for career growth and staying relevant in evolving professional landscapes, enabling individuals to tackle new challenges and responsibilities.

## Key properties or components
- Skill acquisition
- Professional development
- Adaptability
- Career advancement
