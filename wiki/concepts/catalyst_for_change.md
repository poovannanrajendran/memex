## Definition
An event or situation that causes or accelerates significant change. It is often a moment of intense pressure or realization that spurs an individual or group to take decisive action.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The speaker identifies the difficult realization of their financial situation and the impact on their loved one as a critical catalyst for their intense period of writing and subsequent success.

## Key properties or components
- Moment of crisis
- Intense emotional impact
- Shift in motivation
- Spurs decisive action
