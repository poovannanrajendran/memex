## Definition
A content format, primarily on YouTube, designed for short-form, vertical videos. It aims to capture viewer attention quickly and is optimised for mobile viewing and rapid consumption.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Indicates the intended format and distribution strategy for this content, aiming for wide reach and virality within the platform's ecosystem.

## Key properties or components
- Short duration
- Vertical video format
- Mobile-first design
- Algorithmically driven discovery
