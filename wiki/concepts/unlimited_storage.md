## Definition
The condition of being able to store an indefinite amount of data without any capacity limits or additional costs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the YouTube video's premise, as it addresses a common user desire to store digital media without worrying about exceeding storage quotas.

## Key properties or components
- No capacity limits
- Cost-effective
- Convenience
