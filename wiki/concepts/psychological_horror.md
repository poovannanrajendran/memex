## Definition
Psychological horror is a subgenre of horror and thriller films that relies on the mental and emotional states of its characters to create suspense, unease, and fear.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is used to categorize one of the types of thriller movies featured in the video.

## Key properties or components
- Focus on mental states
- Atmospheric dread
- Character's psyche
- Subtle scares over gore
