## Definition
Refers to the participation and presence of women in spheres of life outside the private home, such as politics, business, media, and activism. It often involves discussions about barriers they face and their contributions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This was the original topic of the show RJ Balaji was invited to, indicating a focus on gender issues and representation in public discourse.

## Key properties or components
- Gender equality
- Representation
- Societal roles
- Public sphere
