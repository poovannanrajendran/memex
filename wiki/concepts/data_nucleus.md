## Definition
A central, organised repository or core collection of data. In this context, it refers to a structured organisation of all conversational history, images, and documents that an AI can easily access and interpret.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Establishes a comprehensive and organised base for AI interaction, allowing for deeper understanding and recall of information.

## Key properties or components
- Centralised
- Organised
- Comprehensive
- Accessible by AI
