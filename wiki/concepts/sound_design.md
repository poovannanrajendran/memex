## Definition
The art and practice of creating and integrating audio elements into a film, video, or other media to enhance the narrative, atmosphere, and emotional impact.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Integral to the final output, with specific sounds like war ambience, shell impacts, arrow releases, and breathing added to the scene.

## Key properties or components
- Ambience creation
- Foley sounds
- Music composition
- Dialogue integration
- Atmospheric enhancement
