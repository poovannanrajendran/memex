## Definition
A dish for which a restaurant is particularly famous or well-known, often a bestseller and a key part of its identity.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The Perennium Mountain milkfed lamb is presented as Hepi's star dish, known for its popularity and high demand.

## Key properties or components
- Popularity
- High demand
- Limited availability
- Distinctive quality
