## Definition
A business strategy that prioritises the needs and satisfaction of customers. It involves deeply understanding the customer to guide product development, marketing, and service.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for understanding customer needs and driving sales, as highlighted by Ryan Moran's approach to business.

## Key properties or components
- Focus on customer needs
- Understanding customer transformation
- Benefit-driven sales
- Customer journey mapping

## Contradictions or debates
None.
