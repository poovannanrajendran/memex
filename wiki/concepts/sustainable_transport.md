## Definition
Forms of transportation that aim to reduce environmental impact, promote public health, and ensure accessibility.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source acknowledges sustainable options like walking, cycling, and trains but contextualises their limitations compared to cars for many use cases.

## Key properties or components
- Walking
- Cycling (conditional)
- Public Transport (trains, buses)
- Planes (long distance)

## Contradictions or debates
The effectiveness and practicality of sustainable transport options are often overstated when faced with real-world constraints like weather, luggage, children, and distance.
