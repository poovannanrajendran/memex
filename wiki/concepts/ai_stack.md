## Definition
A collection of different AI models and tools chosen and integrated to work together to address a range of specific needs or business objectives.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a more practical and effective approach to leveraging AI, allowing for specialization and optimization across various functions, rather than relying on a single, generalized model.

## Key properties or components
- Integration of multiple models
- Task-specific deployment
- Customization for unique requirements
- Scalability
