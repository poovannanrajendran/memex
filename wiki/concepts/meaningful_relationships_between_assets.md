## Definition
The concept of establishing logical and contextual connections between different pieces of data or assets. Instead of just storing files, this involves understanding how they relate to each other and to broader goals, such as business operations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for AI understanding; Graphify creates these relationships so Claude can better grasp the significance of assets in relation to business outcomes, unlike simple file organisation.

## Key properties or components
- Contextual
- Logical
- Interconnected
- Enables deeper understanding
