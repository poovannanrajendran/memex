## Definition
Chocolate is a preparation of the seeds of the cacao tree, used as food. It may be sweet, dark, or milk chocolate and is often melted for use as a sauce or dip.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The quality and consistency of the chocolate served with churros is highlighted as a key factor in the overall experience and enjoyment.

## Key properties or components
- Made from cacao seeds
- Can be thick and molten
- Rich flavour
- Milk-chocolatey notes
- Served warm
