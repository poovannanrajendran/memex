## Definition
A software application used for recording, editing, and producing audio and MIDI. Popular examples include Logic Pro, Ableton Live, and Pro Tools.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Logic Pro is used as a test case to evaluate the processing power of the Mac Minis, specifically their ability to handle a high number of audio tracks and complex projects without performance degradation.

## Key properties or components
- Audio Recording
- MIDI Sequencing
- Audio Editing
- Mixing
