## Definition
The generally accepted level of minor damage or deterioration that is considered normal for a vehicle of its age and mileage, and which does not incur additional charges when the vehicle is returned at the end of a lease.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding fair wear and tear guidelines (often provided by the BVLA) helps lessees avoid unexpected charges when returning the vehicle.

## Key properties or components
- Normal deterioration
- Excludes accidental damage
- Defined by age and mileage
- Guidelines available from industry bodies
