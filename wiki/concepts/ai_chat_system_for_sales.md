## Definition
An AI chat system for sales is a technology that uses artificial intelligence to interact with potential customers via chat interfaces. It can analyse conversations, provide automated responses, and offer guidance to sales teams to improve outcomes.

Revio exemplifies this concept, showing how AI can enhance sales processes by analysing interactions and providing strategic advice, directly impacting sales performance.

- Sales process analysis
- Real-time recommendations
- Customer interaction
