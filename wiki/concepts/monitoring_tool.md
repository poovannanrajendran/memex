## Definition
A software application designed to observe and report on the status and performance of IT systems, networks, or services, often alerting administrators to issues.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Uptime Kuma is showcased as an open-source example, providing a free way to ensure websites and services are operational.

## Key properties or components
- Status checking
- Performance tracking
- Alerting
- Reporting
