## Definition
Predefined structures or templates for constructing AI prompts, such as 'Role-Task-Format' (RTF) or specific frameworks, designed to guide the AI and improve output quality.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video argues against blindly following these formulas, suggesting they can limit AI's creativity and effectiveness by imposing rigid constraints.

## Key properties or components
- Structured templates
- Predefined steps
- Rigid application

## Contradictions or debates
While structure can aid AI understanding, rigid adherence to formulas can stifle creativity and is often unnecessary for simpler tasks.
