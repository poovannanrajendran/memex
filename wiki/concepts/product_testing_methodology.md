## Definition
The systematic process of evaluating a product's performance, safety, and features through a series of controlled experiments and observations. This involves setting clear criteria, using appropriate tools, and documenting results.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Ensures reliable and objective comparisons between different products, helping consumers make informed decisions based on empirical evidence rather than marketing claims.

## Key properties or components
- Controlled variables
- Objective measurements
- Repeatability
- Data analysis

None.
