## Definition
An AI ecosystem refers to the interconnected network of individuals, organisations, tools, data, and infrastructure that support the development, deployment, and application of artificial intelligence.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
AI Kosh is designed to be the central hub for India's AI ecosystem, fostering collaboration and resource sharing.

## Key properties or components
- Interconnectedness
- Resource Sharing
- Collaboration
- Innovation
