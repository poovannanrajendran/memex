## Definition
Profound dedication and commitment to a cause, person, or belief, often involving deep affection, loyalty, and self-sacrifice.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Exemplified by Nakula's actions to gather vital supplies for loved ones, showing that heroism can manifest through personal commitment and service beyond battlefield achievements.

## Key properties or components
- profound dedication
- commitment
- loyalty
- self-sacrifice
- affection
