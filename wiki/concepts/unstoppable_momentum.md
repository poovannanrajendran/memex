## Definition
A state of progress that is extremely difficult to halt or impede, achieved through sustained and concentrated effort.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents the ultimate goal of sustained effort, where success becomes self-perpetuating and resistant to setbacks.

## Key properties or components
- Requires overcoming initial inertia.
- Builds through consistent application of effort.
- Characterized by rapid and sustained progress.
