## Definition
A Virtual Private Network (VPN) creates a secure, encrypted connection over a public network, like the internet. It masks the user's IP address and encrypts their data, enhancing privacy and security.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for travelers to protect sensitive information on unsecured public Wi-Fi, bypass internet censorship, and access geo-restricted content or services.

## Key properties or components
- Encryption
- IP Address Masking
- Secure Tunneling
- Location Spoofing
