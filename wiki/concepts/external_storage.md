## Definition
The use of storage devices separate from the main computer's internal drive, such as external SSDs or HDDs. This is used to increase available storage capacity, for backups, or for faster data transfer.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for users who work with large files like videos or need more space than their internal drive offers, as highlighted by the presenter's need to upgrade storage for their MacBook.

## Key properties or components
- Portability
- Speed (Read/Write)
- Durability (Drop/Water Resistance)
- Capacity (TB)
