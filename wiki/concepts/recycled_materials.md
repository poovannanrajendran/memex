## Definition
Materials that have been salvaged from waste products and reprocessed for use in new products. Using recycled materials in car manufacturing reduces environmental impact.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The Duster features plastic cladding made from 20% recycled materials, contributing to its sustainability credentials.

## Key properties or components
- Environmental benefit
- Reduced waste
- Potential for cost savings
- Material sourcing
