## Definition
A visual representation of a product or design, often used for presentation or marketing purposes. In this context, it refers to showcasing branded designs within a dynamic video format.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Allows for a more engaging and realistic presentation of branded content compared to static images.

## Key properties or components
- Visual representation
- Branding integration
- Dynamic display
- Marketing tool
