## Definition
The process of comparing the performance of a system, product, or component against a standard or a competitor's offering. In AI, this often involves standardized tests and metrics to evaluate capabilities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Benchmarking provides objective metrics for evaluating the effectiveness and superiority of AI tools like MePalace.

## Key properties or components
- Standardized tests
- Performance metrics
- Comparative analysis
- Objective evaluation
