## Definition
Sympathetic pity and concern for the sufferings or misfortunes of others, often leading to a desire to help or protect them.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Balancing Arjuna's warrior skills, it is exemplified by his lesser-known rescue of a villager from a crocodile, highlighting the multifaceted nature of heroism.

## Key properties or components
- sympathy
- pity
- concern
- kindness
- protection
- empathy
