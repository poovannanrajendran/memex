## Definition
Deepfakes are synthetic media in which a person in an existing image or video is replaced with someone else's likeness. They are created using artificial intelligence, specifically deep learning techniques.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding deepfakes is crucial in an era where AI can generate highly realistic but fabricated content, impacting trust and authenticity in digital media.

## Key properties or components
- AI-generated
- Synthetic media
- Can replace likeness
- Can be indistinguishable from real media
