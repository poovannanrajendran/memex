## Definition
A gear with teeth cut at an angle to the axis of rotation, creating a helical shape. This design allows for smoother and quieter operation than spur gears.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents an advancement in gear design offering improved performance in terms of noise and smoothness, suitable for higher speeds.

## Key properties or components
- Angled teeth
- Quieter operation
- Smoother operation
- Higher speeds
