## Definition
A software tool that uses artificial intelligence to help designers create or refine user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) designs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It represents a significant shift in design workflows, automating complex tasks and potentially democratising design.

## Key properties or components
- Generates designs from prompts
- Automates wireframing
- Creates user flows
- Rapid prototyping
