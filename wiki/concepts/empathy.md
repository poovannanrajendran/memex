## Definition
The ability to understand and share the feelings of another. It involves putting oneself in another's shoes and responding with appropriate care and sensitivity.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Demonstrated by Nakula through his gentle care for horses, highlighting that heroism can manifest through sensitivity and devotion rather than solely through grand battles.

## Key properties or components
- understand feelings
- share feelings
- perspective-taking
- care
- sensitivity

## Contradictions or debates
None.
