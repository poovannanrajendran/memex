## Definition
The use of technology to automate sequences of tasks and processes that typically require human intervention, thereby increasing efficiency and reducing errors.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Streamlines operations, frees up human resources for more complex tasks, and ensures consistency in process execution.

## Key properties or components
- Task sequencing
- Integration
- Automation rules
- Efficiency gains
