## Definition
A defined set of rules, conventions, and procedures that govern how AI agents or tools communicate, interact, and behave within a larger system or environment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Agentic Stack aims to standardise these protocols to ensure seamless integration and consistent operation when different AI tools share a common 'brain' or memory. This consistency is vital for reliable and predictable AI system behaviour.

## Key properties or components
- Rules for communication
- Interaction guidelines
- Shared conventions
- Ensures interoperability
