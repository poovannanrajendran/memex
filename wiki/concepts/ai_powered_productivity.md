## Definition
The concept of using artificial intelligence tools and techniques to enhance efficiency, automate tasks, and improve the quality of work across various professional and personal activities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for individuals and organisations seeking to stay competitive by optimising workflows and achieving more with less effort.

## Key properties or components
- Automation of repetitive tasks
- Enhanced decision-making
- Accelerated content creation
- Personalised assistance

## Contradictions or debates
Over-reliance on AI could potentially lead to a decline in critical thinking skills or human oversight.
