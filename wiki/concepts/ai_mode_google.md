## Definition
A new feature integrated into Google Search that consolidates various AI functionalities, such as conversational search, image recognition, and personalized recommendations, into a single, unified interface.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a significant shift in how users interact with search engines by providing AI-powered, contextualized, and multi-modal responses directly within search.

## Key properties or components
- Integrated AI functionalities
- Conversational search capabilities
- Real-time data utilization
- Personalized suggestions
- Contextual memory for follow-up questions
- Image search integration (via Google Lens)
- Powered by Gemini 2.5
- Free access in India
