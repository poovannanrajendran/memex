## Definition
An artificial intelligence agent that operates entirely on a user's local computer or network, processing data and performing tasks without relying on remote servers or cloud infrastructure.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is crucial as it addresses privacy concerns, reduces reliance on internet connectivity, and can potentially offer faster processing speeds by eliminating network latency, making advanced AI capabilities more accessible and secure for individual users.

## Key properties or components
- Operates offline
- Enhanced privacy
- No cloud dependency
- Direct data processing
