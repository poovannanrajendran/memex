## Definition
A type of investment where the brokerage or platform does not charge any fees or commissions for executing trades.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the source as it highlights a key benefit of using the Trading 212 app, potentially lowering the cost of investing for individuals.

## Key properties or components
- No transaction fees
- Reduced cost of entry for investors
- Increased accessibility
