## Definition
Sleight of hand refers to the manual dexterity and skill used to perform tricks or illusions, particularly in magic. It involves misdirection and precise movements of the hands.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is the fundamental skill set required to execute magical effects like the faro shuffle card trick.

## Key properties or components
- Requires dexterity and practice.
- Often involves misdirection.
- Used in various performance arts.
- Can create illusions of impossibility.
