## Definition
A goal or aspiration that seems highly improbable or outside the realm of current possibility for most observers. It represents a stretch beyond conventional expectations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Such dreams can serve as powerful motivators, pushing individuals to overcome perceived limitations and achieve extraordinary results through sustained belief and effort.

## Key properties or components
- Ambitious
- Challenging
- Perseverance-inducing
- Transformative

## Contradictions or debates
Some might argue that focusing on 'unbelievable' dreams can lead to wasted effort if they are entirely detached from reality or achievable steps.
