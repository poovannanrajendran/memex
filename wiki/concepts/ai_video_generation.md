## Definition
AI video generation is the process of using artificial intelligence algorithms to create or manipulate video content. This can range from generating entirely new scenes to altering existing footage, such as lip-syncing or changing visual styles.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the core capability addressed by both Higgsfield AI and its open-source counterpart, representing a significant area of innovation and competition within the AI and creative industries.

## Key properties or components
- Image to Video
- Text to Video
- Video Editing/Manipulation
- Synthetic Media Creation
