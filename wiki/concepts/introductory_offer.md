## Definition
A promotional price or deal offered to new customers for a limited time or for the first registration period, designed to attract them to a service. These offers often come with much higher prices for subsequent periods.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Can be misleading if customers do not investigate the true long-term cost of the service, particularly concerning domain name renewals.

## Key properties or components
- Limited time/period
- Attracts new customers
- Lower initial cost
- Higher subsequent costs

## Contradictions or debates
None.
