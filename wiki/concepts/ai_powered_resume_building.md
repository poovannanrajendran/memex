## Definition
The use of artificial intelligence to assist in the creation and enhancement of resumes. This can involve automated content generation, template selection, and optimisation for applicant tracking systems (ATS).

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video showcases how AI can drastically reduce the time and effort required to create a professional resume, making job applications more accessible.
