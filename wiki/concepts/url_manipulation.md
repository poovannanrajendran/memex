## Definition
URL manipulation involves altering the characters or structure within a Uniform Resource Locator (URL) to influence the content or behaviour of a web page, sometimes revealing hidden information or functionalities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This technique is the technical basis for the claimed Amazon discount hack, where specific codes are appended to a URL to trigger discount displays.

## Key properties or components
- Modification of URL parameters
- Can affect website output
- May reveal hidden data or features
- Requires understanding of web addresses
