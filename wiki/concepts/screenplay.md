## Definition
A screenplay is the written script for a film or television show, detailing dialogue, character actions, settings, and camera directions. It serves as the blueprint for the production.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The foundational document for filmmaking, guiding all aspects of production; K. Manikandan's expertise in this area is showcased.

## Key properties or components
- Dialogue
- Action lines
- Scene headings
- Character descriptions
- Formatting conventions
