## Definition
Cold emailing is the practice of sending emails to individuals or companies with whom you have no prior relationship, typically for sales, networking, or marketing purposes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is presented as a method to automate outreach to leads generated by tools like Apollo, contributing to sales automation.

## Key properties or components
- Unsolicited contact
- Targeted outreach
- Sales/Marketing objective
- Relationship building (initial)
