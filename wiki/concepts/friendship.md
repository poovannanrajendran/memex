## Definition
Friendship is a relationship of mutual affection between people. It is characterized by trust, loyalty, and support.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the message, emphasizing the critical role of supportive friendships in personal well-being.

## Key properties or components
- Mutual affection
- Trust
- Loyalty
- Support
- Acceptance
