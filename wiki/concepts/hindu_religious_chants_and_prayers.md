## Definition
These are sacred verses, hymns, and prayers from Hindu scriptures and traditions, recited to invoke deities, seek blessings, and create a spiritual atmosphere. They often involve specific mantras and devotional songs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
These chants form a significant part of the engagement ceremony, creating a sacred and auspicious environment.

## Key properties or components
- Mantras
- Devotional songs
- Invocation of deities
- Sacred verses
