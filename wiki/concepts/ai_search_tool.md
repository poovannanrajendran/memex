## Definition
A search engine that uses artificial intelligence to understand queries, retrieve information, and synthesize results, often providing summaries or direct answers.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Offers a more efficient and intelligent way to research information compared to traditional search engines, acting as a powerful research assistant.

## Key properties or components
- Natural language processing
- Information synthesis
- Contextual understanding
- Direct answers
