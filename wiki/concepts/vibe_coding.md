## Definition
A term used to describe a flexible and intuitive approach to coding, often involving AI tools, where the focus is on quickly prototyping and building applications by 'vibing' with the process, rather than strictly adhering to traditional development methodologies.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video uses this term to describe the development process within Firebase Studio, emphasizing its user-friendly and AI-assisted nature.

## Key properties or components
- AI assistance
- Rapid prototyping
- Intuitive workflow
- Less focus on deep technical details initially
