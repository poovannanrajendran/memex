## Definition
Videos that do not feature a visible person speaking or presenting. These are often created using AI-generated visuals, stock footage, or animations, with voiceovers providing the narration.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Relevant to content creators looking for efficient ways to produce videos without appearing on camera, a capability demonstrated by Auto-Shorts.

## Key properties or components
- Automated creation
- No on-camera presenter required
- Can be driven by AI prompts
- Suitable for informational content
