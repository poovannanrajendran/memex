## Definition
A software application powered by artificial intelligence designed to perform tasks typically handled by a human personal assistant, such as scheduling, communication, and information retrieval.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a cost-effective and scalable alternative to human personal assistants, enhancing productivity through automation.

## Key properties or components
- Automates tasks
- Learns user preferences
- Available 24/7
- Scalable
- Cost-effective
