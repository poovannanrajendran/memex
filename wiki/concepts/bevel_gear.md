## Definition
A gear used to transmit power between intersecting shafts. They are typically conical in shape.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Fundamental for changing the direction of rotation between shafts that are not parallel.

## Key properties or components
- Conical shape
- Intersecting axes
- Motion transfer
