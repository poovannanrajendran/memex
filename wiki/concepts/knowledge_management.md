## Definition
The disciplined process of identifying, creating, storing, sharing, and using information and expertise within an organisation or for personal use. Its aim is to improve efficiency, decision-making, and overall understanding.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The entire memex project stems from the author's personal 'classic knowledge problem,' aiming to overcome the limitations of scattered notes and unconnected ideas through a structured, AI-driven knowledge system.

## Key properties or components
- creation
- sharing
- use
- management
- retention
- connection of information
