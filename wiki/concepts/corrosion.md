## Definition
Corrosion is the degradation of a material, typically metal, due to chemical or electrochemical reactions with its environment. In the context of tires and wheels, it can affect metal rims, fasteners, or protective coatings.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding if tire sealants can cause or accelerate corrosion is important for maintaining the structural integrity and appearance of vehicle wheels.

## Key properties or components
- Electrochemical process
- Oxidation
- Material degradation
- Environmental factors
