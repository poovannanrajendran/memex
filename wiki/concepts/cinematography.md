## Definition
The art and science of motion-picture photography. It involves the process of making motion pictures, encompassing everything from lighting and camera choices to lens selection and composition.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source demonstrates how specific lens choices, like the Helios 44-2, can significantly impact the visual aesthetic and creative direction of a film, as seen in Dune 2.

## Key properties or components
- Camera operation
- Lighting techniques
- Lens selection
- Composition
- Color grading
