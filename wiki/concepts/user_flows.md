## Definition
A pathway illustrating the steps a user takes to complete a task within a digital product.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for understanding user journeys and ensuring intuitive navigation; AI can rapidly generate these to guide design.

## Key properties or components
- Sequential steps
- Task completion
- Navigation paths
