## Definition
The process by which the brain matures, involving the growth and organisation of neural pathways. Certain conditions, like environmental stress during sensitive periods, can interfere with this process.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Interference with brain development, particularly in sensitive infants, is posited as the reason for underdeveloped time sense and ADD.

## Key properties or components
- Neural pathway growth
- Maturation
- Sensitive periods
- Susceptible to environmental factors
