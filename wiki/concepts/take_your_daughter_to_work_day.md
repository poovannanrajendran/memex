## Definition
An observance day that encourages parents to bring their daughters to their workplaces. It aims to expose young girls to various career possibilities and empower them.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is the central premise of the video, providing the context for the daughter's experience in her father's restaurant.

## Key properties or components
- Early start
- Observational learning
- Workplace immersion
