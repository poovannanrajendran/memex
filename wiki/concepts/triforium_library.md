## Definition
A hidden library located in the triforium level of St Paul's Cathedral. It is noted for having one of the best-preserved 18th-century interiors in London.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It represents another significant, less-known architectural and historical space within St Paul's Cathedral, showcasing 18th-century design and preservation.

## Key properties or components
- Located in the triforium
- 18th-century interior
- Designed by Wren and Hawksmore
- Well-preserved
