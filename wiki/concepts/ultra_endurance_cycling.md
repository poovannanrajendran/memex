## Definition
Ultra-endurance cycling involves riding for exceptionally long distances or durations, often exceeding 100 miles (160 km) or lasting for 24 hours or more. These events test physical and mental resilience.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The narrator progressed to ultra-endurance events, including attempts at an 'Ultra' and completing a 'second woman back' on an ultra-endurance ride, showcasing their advanced cycling capabilities.

## Key properties or components
- Long distances
- Extended durations
- Mental fortitude
- Physical stamina
