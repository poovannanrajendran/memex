## Definition
The profit or loss earned by an insurer after deducting incurred claims and expenses from earned premiums. It excludes investment income.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A primary performance indicator for his clients at [[lloyds_of_london]]. It measures the pure "technical" success of the underwriting operation.
