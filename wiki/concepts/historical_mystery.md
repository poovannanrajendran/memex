## Definition
A historical event or phenomenon that has not been fully explained or understood due to a lack of evidence or conflicting accounts. It often involves speculation and investigation to uncover the truth.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The carvings in Middleton Passage presented a historical mystery that was eventually solved through investigation.

## Key properties or components
- Lack of clear evidence
- Conflicting theories
- Requires investigation
- Potential for new discoveries
