## Definition
In the context of AI models, tokens are fundamental units of text or data that the model processes. Pricing is often based on the number of tokens consumed for input and output.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a quantifiable measure for the usage of the AI-powered scraping tool, with a generous free tier that makes it practically free for many common use cases.

## Key properties or components
- Unit of processing
- Usage metric
- Cost calculation basis
