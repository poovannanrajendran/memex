## Definition
Deep Work is the ability to focus without distraction on a cognitively demanding task. It is a skill that allows you to quickly master complicated information and produce better results in less time.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the speaker's method for building a consistent and productive coding habit, enabling significant career progress.

## Key properties or components
- Scheduling deep work sessions
- Scheduling distractions
- Evening shutdown ritual
- Cognitive demand
- Focus without distraction
