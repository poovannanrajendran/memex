## Definition
A method used to evaluate a decision, project, or product by listing its advantages (pros) and disadvantages (cons). This systematic approach helps in making a balanced judgment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a structured way to assess different vehicle models, helping potential buyers weigh benefits against drawbacks before making a purchase.

## Key properties or components
- Identification of advantages
- Identification of disadvantages
- Balanced assessment
- Informed decision-making
