## Definition
Daniel Kahneman's dual-process theory describing two modes of thought: System 1 is fast, intuitive, and automatic, while System 2 is slow, deliberate, and effortful.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a framework for understanding the limitations of current LLMs, which primarily operate on System 1, and the need for methods that simulate System 2 thinking.

## Key properties or components
- System 1: Fast, subconscious, automatic, effortless
- System 2: Slow, conscious, deliberate, effortful

## Contradictions or debates
None.
