## Definition
A government-facilitated programme that allows pre-approved, low-risk travellers to bypass traditional immigration queues using expedited processing. This typically involves a one-time registration and biometric verification.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances travel efficiency and reduces stress for frequent travellers by offering a faster route through airport security and immigration, relevant to streamlining processes.

## Key properties or components
- Biometric registration (fingerprints)
- Pre-approval process
- Expedited immigration
- Reduced wait times
- Valid for 5 years
