## Definition
Software applications that leverage artificial intelligence to assist individuals in producing and enhancing digital content, such as videos, images, and text.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
These tools are crucial for modern content creators as they can significantly streamline workflows, overcome creative blocks, and enable the production of higher-quality or more complex content efficiently.

## Key properties or components
- Image generation
- Video generation
- Real-time rendering
- Lip-syncing
- Product creation
- Character creation
- 3D object generation
