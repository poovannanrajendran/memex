## Definition
The use of AI and other technologies to automate the delivery and management of services. This can range from customer support chatbots to automated content creation and video editing.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Presented as a viable business model that can be built and scaled using AI tools.

## Key properties or components
- Efficiency
- Cost reduction
- Scalability
- Potential for AI-driven value creation
