## Definition
Wealth building is the process of increasing one's net worth over time through saving, investing, and strategic financial decisions. It involves accumulating assets and minimizing liabilities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The primary motivation presented in the source for avoiding car payments and leasing. The speaker emphasizes that financial decisions, like car acquisition, should support this goal.

## Key properties or components
- Saving
- Investing
- Financial discipline
- Asset accumulation
- Debt reduction
