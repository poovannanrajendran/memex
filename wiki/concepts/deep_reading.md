## Definition
Deep reading is an approach to consuming written material that involves focused attention, critical analysis, and a thorough understanding of the text. It contrasts with superficial reading or skimming.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Promotes a more profound understanding and retention of information, which is more valuable than passively consuming a large volume of content.

## Key properties or components
- Focused attention
- Critical analysis
- Repeated engagement
- Thorough understanding
