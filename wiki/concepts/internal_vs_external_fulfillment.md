## Definition
The philosophical distinction between finding contentment and satisfaction from within oneself versus seeking it from external sources or achievements.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source argues that true happiness stems from internal states rather than external acquisitions.

## Key properties or components
- Self-awareness
- Inner peace
- Detachment from possessions

## Contradictions or debates
None.
