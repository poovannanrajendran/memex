## Definition
A set of consistent activities performed upon waking up each day. Morning routines are often designed to promote productivity, well-being, and preparedness for the day ahead.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for setting a positive and productive tone for the day, aligning with Poovi's interest in optimizing daily activities and personal effectiveness.

## Key properties or components
- Consistency
- Purposefulness
- Personalisation
- Habit formation

## Contradictions or debates
Some argue that rigid routines can stifle spontaneity and creativity, suggesting a need for flexibility.
