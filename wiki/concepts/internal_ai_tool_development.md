## Definition
Internal AI tool development refers to the process where an organisation builds and deploys artificial intelligence solutions within its own operations. This often aims to improve efficiency, automate tasks, or gain competitive advantages using custom-built AI.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
GumLoop addresses this concept by enabling businesses to create their own AI tools without requiring extensive technical expertise, democratising AI development within organizations.

## Key properties or components
- Customisation
- Internal deployment
- Reduced reliance on external vendors
- Democratisation of AI
