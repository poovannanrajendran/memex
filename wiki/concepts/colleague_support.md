## Definition
The act of providing assistance, understanding, and encouragement to coworkers, especially during times of personal or professional difficulty. This can foster a more positive and productive work environment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Harvey's reaction, despite Louis's initial wish for solitude, shows a form of colleague support, underpinned by a deep understanding of Louis's personality and needs.

## Key properties or components
- Empathy
- Assistance
- Encouragement
- Understanding
