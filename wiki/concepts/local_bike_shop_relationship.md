## Definition
Establishing a trusted relationship with a local bicycle retailer for expert advice, product recommendations, and support.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for beginner cyclists to navigate the complexities of bike choice, upgrades, maintenance, and community engagement, akin to having a reliable advisor.

## Key properties or components
- Expert advice
- Personalised recommendations
- Mechanical support
- Community integration
