## Definition
The ability to understand and interact effectively with others. It involves recognising and responding appropriately to the moods, temperaments, and motivations of other people.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video suggests that reading certain books can enhance this type of intelligence, leading to being perceived as 'the smartest person in the room.'

## Key properties or components
- Understanding emotions
- Social perception
- Effective communication
- Relationship management
