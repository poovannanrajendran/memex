## Definition
Behind-the-scenes (BTS) content provides a glimpse into the making of a film, show, product, or event, showing the process, challenges, and people involved.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video explicitly offers a 'Behind the Reels' perspective, highlighting the often humorous or chaotic nature of content production.

## Key properties or components
- Process documentation
- Human element
- Transparency
- Humour
