## Definition
A YouTube channel is a specific presence on the video-sharing platform YouTube, where a user or organization can upload, share, and manage videos. It serves as a hub for a content creator's output.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source directly references a YouTube channel ('TECHBOSS') as the context for the speaker's decision to quit, highlighting the platform's role in content creation and creator livelihoods.

## Key properties or components
- Video hosting
- Content creation
- Subscribers
- Monetization
