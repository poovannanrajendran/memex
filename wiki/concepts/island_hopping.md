## Definition
Island hopping is a type of leisure travel that involves visiting several islands in sequence. It is a popular activity in archipelagic destinations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlighted as a key activity in the Langkawi itinerary, offering a way to experience multiple locations within the destination.

## Key properties or components
- Sequential travel between islands
- Often involves boat transportation
- Allows exploration of diverse environments
