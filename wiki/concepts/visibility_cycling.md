## Definition
Ensuring that a cyclist is easily seen by other road users, especially motorists, through the use of bright clothing and active lighting.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A critical safety measure to reduce the risk of collisions, as being seen by others is paramount for avoiding accidents, particularly when riding alone or in low-light conditions.

## Key properties or components
- Bright coloured clothing
- Flashing lights (day and night)
- Reflective elements
