## Definition
Recognition refers to acknowledgement or admiration for an achievement, service, or ability. It is the act of being noticed, praised, or rewarded by others for one's accomplishments.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The speech argues against the necessity of recognition, positioning it as secondary or even detrimental to the pursuit of success.

## Key properties or components
- External validation
- Social acknowledgement
- Praise or reward

## Contradictions or debates
While the speech dismisses it, recognition can also serve as a powerful motivator and indicator of impact for some.
