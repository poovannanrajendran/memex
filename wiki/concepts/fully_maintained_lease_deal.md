## Definition
A type of car lease agreement that includes the costs of routine servicing, replacement tires, and sometimes other maintenance items within the monthly payments.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This offers convenience and predictable costs but needs to be evaluated against the cost of the alternative (non-maintained lease) to ensure it provides value.

## Key properties or components
- Includes servicing
- Often includes tires
- Predictable costs
- Can be more expensive than non-maintained deals

## Contradictions or debates
None.
