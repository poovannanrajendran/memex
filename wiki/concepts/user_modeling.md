## Definition
The process by which an AI agent builds and maintains a persistent representation of an individual user's preferences, behaviours, and history across multiple interaction sessions. This model informs the agent's future actions and responses to better align with the user's needs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enables agents to offer more personalised and efficient experiences by remembering user preferences and past decisions, reducing the need for repeated instructions.

## Key properties or components
- Task preferences
- Decision history
- Common task patterns
- Feedback signals
