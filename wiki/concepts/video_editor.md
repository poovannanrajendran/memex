## Definition
A software application used to manipulate and rearrange video clips, add effects, transitions, titles, music, and other elements to create a final video product.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential tool for content creators to refine and assemble raw footage into polished videos. LTX Desktop integrates AI generation with editing capabilities.

## Key properties or components
- Timeline-based editing
- Supports various media formats
- Includes tools for cutting, trimming, and sequencing
- Allows addition of audio, text, and effects
