## Definition
Goals or intentions that individuals set for themselves to accomplish in the upcoming year. These often relate to self-improvement or breaking bad habits.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The comedic treatment of New Year's resolutions, particularly those of managers, highlights common patterns of goal-setting and self-reflection, often satirising their effectiveness or sincerity.

## Key properties or components
- Goal-setting
- Self-improvement
- Annual practice
- Often unfulfilled

## Contradictions or debates
The inherent intention of resolutions is genuine self-improvement, yet they are often treated with cynicism or humour due to high failure rates.
