## Definition
An advanced AI agent capable of autonomously managing and executing complex, multi-step tasks. It can break down large objectives into smaller, manageable sub-tasks, execute them sequentially or in parallel, and synthesise the results into a coherent final output.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Super agents represent a significant advancement in AI's practical application, enabling automation of more sophisticated workflows and complex problem-solving that previously required human oversight and intervention.

## Key properties or components
- Task decomposition
- Autonomous execution
- Multi-step processing
- Output synthesis
- Self-correction (implied)
