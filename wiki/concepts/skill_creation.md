## Definition
In the context of AI agents, this refers to the process of abstracting successful task completion strategies or reasoning patterns into reusable, structured templates or 'skills'. These skills can then be applied by the agent to similar problems in the future.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Forms a foundational part of the learning loop in agent frameworks like Hermes, allowing for the capture and reuse of effective problem-solving methods.

## Key properties or components
- Abstraction of reasoning
- Structured templates
- Reusability
- Context-specific application
