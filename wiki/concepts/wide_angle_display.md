## Definition
A display designed to offer a broader field of view, allowing more content to be seen without scrolling or zooming.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Contributes to a larger perceived screen size and improved user experience for viewing information on the Apple Watch.

## Key properties or components
- Expanded viewing area
- Enhanced usability
- Reduced need for scrolling

## Contradictions or debates
None.
