## Definition
An Apple Intelligence feature that condenses multiple notifications from a single app or conversation into a single, shorter message.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Aims to help users quickly grasp the essence of incoming messages without being overwhelmed by numerous individual alerts.

## Key properties or components
- Condensing multiple alerts
- Summarising chat conversations
- Focusing on key information

## Contradictions or debates
The reviewer found this feature to be largely useless, as their notifications rarely benefited from summarisation, and often cited humorous or poor summarisation examples.
