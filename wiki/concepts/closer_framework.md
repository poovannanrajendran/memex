## Definition
An acronym representing a sales methodology: Clarify why they are there, Label the problem, Overview Past Pain, Sell the Vacation (benefits), Explain Away Concerns, Reinforce the decision.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Offers a structured and effective process for guiding potential customers through the sales funnel and overcoming objections.

## Key properties or components
- Customer-centric approach
- Problem identification
- Benefit-oriented selling
- Objection handling
- Decision reinforcement
