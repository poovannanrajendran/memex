## Definition
A feeling of contentment and pleasure derived from one's own achievements or actions. It is an internal reward that validates one's efforts and choices.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Explains Ratan Tata's motivation and the emotional reward he associates with tackling difficult challenges.

## Key properties or components
- Internal reward
- Linked to achievement
- Subjective experience
- Can be a powerful motivator
