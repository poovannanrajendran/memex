## Definition
A video resolution with a width of approximately 4000 pixels, commonly referring to Ultra High Definition (UHD) at 3840x2160 pixels. It offers significantly more detail than Full HD (1080p).

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a high standard of visual quality, providing a more immersive and detailed viewing experience. LTX 2.3's ability to generate 4K video signifies its advanced capabilities.

## Key properties or components
- High pixel count (approx. 4000 horizontal)
- Superior image detail
- Requires significant processing power and storage
- Industry standard for premium content
