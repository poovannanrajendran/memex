## Definition
The process of soaking foods in a seasoned liquid (marinade) before cooking or for preservation. Marinades add flavour and can tenderise.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for creating the signature flavour of Mayak Eggs, where the eggs absorb the complex tastes of the soy-based mixture.

## Key properties or components
- Soaking in liquid
- Adds flavour
- Infuses ingredients
- Overnight process (for Mayak Eggs)
