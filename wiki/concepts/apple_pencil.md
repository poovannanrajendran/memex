## Definition
A stylus developed by Apple that offers precise input for iPads. It supports features like low latency, pressure sensitivity, and tilt, enabling natural-feeling writing, drawing, and annotation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Key to unlocking the iPad's potential for detailed creative work, note-taking, and precise digital tasks, bridging the gap between physical and digital input for artistic and professional applications.

## Key properties or components
- Low latency
- Pressure sensitivity
- Tilt support
- Magnetic charging (Gen 2)
- Double-tap feature (Gen 2)
