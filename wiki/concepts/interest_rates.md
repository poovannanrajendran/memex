## Definition
Interest rates represent the cost of borrowing money or the return on lending money. In the context of savings, it's the rate at which a bank rewards depositors for keeping their money with the institution.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video title explicitly asks about interest rates on a significant sum (30 lakh rupees), making it a core concept of the content.

## Key properties or components
- Expressed as a percentage
- Can be fixed or variable
- Influences borrowing and saving decisions
