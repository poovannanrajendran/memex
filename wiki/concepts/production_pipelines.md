## Definition
Pre-defined workflows or sequences of operations within a system designed to achieve a specific outcome. In Open Montage, these pipelines are tailored for different types of video content.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Allows the AI system to specialise and efficiently produce diverse video formats by applying appropriate sequences of tools and skills.

## Key properties or components
- Specialised workflows
- Multiple output formats
- Configurable processes
