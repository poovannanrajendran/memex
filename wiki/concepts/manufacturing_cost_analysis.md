## Definition
The process of calculating and evaluating all the expenses involved in producing a product. This includes direct costs like materials and labour, as well as indirect costs such as overhead and tooling.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for determining profitability and pricing strategies in any product-based business, as demonstrated by the cost breakdowns provided in the source.

## Key properties or components
- Cost per unit
- Bulk pricing
- Mould fees
- Material costs
