## Definition
The intensity or purity of colours in an image. Higher saturation means more vivid colours, while lower saturation results in more muted or greyed tones.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A critical element in image aesthetics, directly affecting the vibrancy and 'pop' of colours in photos, and adjustable via features like Photographic Styles.

## Key properties or components
- Intensity of colours
- Colour vividness
- Colour purity
- Adjustable via Photographic Styles

## Contradictions or debates
None.
