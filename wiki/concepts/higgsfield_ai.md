## Definition
Higgsfield AI is a commercial AI platform that allows users, particularly creators, to achieve cinematic-quality video production by translating simple camera settings into complex AI-generated prompts and visuals.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It represents a successful commercial application of AI in the creative video production space, setting a benchmark that has now inspired an open-source competitor.

## Key properties or components
- Billion-dollar valuation
- Focus on cinematic video control
- User-friendly interface for creators
- Proprietary technology
