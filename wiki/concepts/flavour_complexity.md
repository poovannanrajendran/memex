## Definition
Flavour complexity refers to the depth and range of tastes and aromas present in a food or drink. It is often achieved through the interaction of multiple ingredients, prolonged cooking processes, or expert balancing of different flavour profiles.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The perpetual soup's method of preparation is highlighted as the reason for its incredibly deep and complex flavours, which is a key draw for customers.

## Key properties or components
- Depth of taste
- Nuanced aroma
- Multiple interacting notes
- Evolution over time
