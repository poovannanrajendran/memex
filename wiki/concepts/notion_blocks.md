## Definition
Blocks are the fundamental building units within Notion. They can represent various types of content, such as text, headings, to-do lists, databases, media files, and embedded content. Each element added to a Notion page is a block.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding blocks is crucial for building any content or structure within Notion, as they are the basic components that can be manipulated and arranged.

## Key properties or components
- Text
- Headings (H1, H2, H3)
- To-do lists
- Toggle lists
- Bullet lists
- Numbered lists
- Quotes
- Dividers
- Callout boxes
- Databases
- Media files
- Embedded content
