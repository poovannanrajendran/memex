## Definition
SaaS (Software as a Service) startups are companies that offer software-based products delivered over the internet on a subscription basis. They typically focus on recurring revenue models.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The open-source job application tool is positioned as a direct competitor to SaaS startups that charge high fees for similar automation services.

## Key properties or components
- Subscription-based revenue
- Cloud-hosted
- Scalable services
- Recurring costs for users

## Contradictions or debates
None.
