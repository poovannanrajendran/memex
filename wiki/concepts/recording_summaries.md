## Definition
A feature that records audio, transcribes it, and then summarises the content, primarily intended for calls and potentially lectures or meetings.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Offers a way to efficiently capture and digest spoken information.

## Key properties or components
- Audio recording
- Speaker identification
- Transcription
- Content summarisation

## Contradictions or debates
The feature is not integrated into the Voice Memos app, limiting its usefulness for lectures, and the quality of summaries can vary depending on the method of initiation.
