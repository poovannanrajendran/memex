## Definition
Carbon neutrality is a state of net-zero carbon dioxide emissions. This can be achieved by balancing the amount of emitted carbon dioxide with an equivalent amount removed from the atmosphere, or by reducing emissions to absolute zero.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the overarching goal of Formula 1's new initiative, driving their technological and operational changes.

## Key properties or components
- Net-zero emissions
- Balancing emissions and removal
- Reduction strategies
