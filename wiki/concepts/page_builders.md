## Definition
Page builders are tools or software that allow users to create web pages with a drag-and-drop interface, often without needing to write code.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for creating unique, branded, and visually appealing landing pages and overall website designs that stand out from generic templates, leading to better customer engagement and higher conversion rates.

## Key properties or components
- Drag-and-drop interface
- No-code or low-code solutions
- Customizable layouts
- Integration with e-commerce platforms
- Enhances branding and user experience
