## Definition
AI content creation refers to the use of artificial intelligence tools and models to generate various forms of content, such as text, images, audio, and video.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Tools like HeyGen and 11 Labs demonstrate how AI can automate content production, reducing manual effort and time.

## Key properties or components
- Automation
- Generative models
- Reduced human input
- Scalability
