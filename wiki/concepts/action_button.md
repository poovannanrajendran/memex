## Definition
A customizable physical button on iPhones that can be programmed to perform various actions, such as launching the camera, flashlight, or shortcuts.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The presence of the Action Button on the standard iPhone 16 is noted, with criticism regarding its ergonomic positioning.

## Key properties or components
- Customizable
- Physical button
- Quick access to functions
