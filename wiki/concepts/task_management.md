## Definition
The process of coordinating, organising, and tracking the various tasks and sub-tasks required for an AI agent or a multi-agent system to achieve its overall objectives.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Ensures that complex workflows are executed efficiently, in the correct sequence, and that all necessary components contribute effectively to the successful completion of an agent's mission.

## Key properties or components
- Coordinates workflows
- Organises sub-tasks
- Ensures efficient execution
- Tracks progress

## Contradictions or debates
None.
