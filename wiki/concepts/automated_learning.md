## Definition
Automated learning, in this context, refers to the use of AI tools to streamline and enhance the process of acquiring knowledge or skills, potentially by automating aspects of information sharing or analysis.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Google AI Studio is presented as a tool that simplifies and automates the learning process.

## Key properties or components

- AI assistance
- Knowledge acquisition
- Process streamlining
