## Definition
Companies founded with the primary focus on developing and commercializing artificial intelligence technologies, products, or services.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The emergence of integrated AI solutions like Neon poses an existential threat to many AI startups by absorbing their core functionalities into a single platform, potentially devaluing their specialized offerings.

## Key properties or components
- Innovation-focused
- Technology-driven
- Vulnerable to platform integration
