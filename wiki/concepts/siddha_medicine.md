## Definition
Siddha medicine is one of the oldest traditional medicine systems in the world, originating in ancient India. It is a holistic system that emphasizes the use of herbs, minerals, and purification techniques.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video draws upon Siddha medicine to explain the practice of mudras for achieving youthfulness, positioning it as a valid and effective system for health.

## Key properties or components
- Traditional medicine system
- Holistic approach
- Herbal remedies
- Mineral preparations
- Focus on rejuvenation

## Contradictions or debates
While traditional, many of its practices and theories lack rigorous scientific validation according to modern medical standards.
