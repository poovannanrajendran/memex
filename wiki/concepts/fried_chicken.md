## Definition
Chicken that has been coated in a batter or breading and then deep-fried, resulting in a crispy exterior and juicy interior.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlighted by Wingmans as a popular and affordable option, with a specific mention of the 'Buffalo wing' flavour.

## Key properties or components
- Crispy coating
- Juicy meat
- Variety of flavourings
- Often served in pieces or as wings
