## Definition
A development methodology where the project's specification acts as the primary driver for the entire development process.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Ensures that projects built with frameworks like GSD are robust, well-defined, and can automatically handle complex features.

## Key properties or components
- Specification-focused
- Framework-based
- Automation-oriented

## Contradictions or debates
None.
