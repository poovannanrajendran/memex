## Definition
A method of evaluating a proposed explanation for a phenomenon through observation and experimentation. It involves formulating a testable statement and then gathering evidence to support or refute it.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Kunal Shah utilizes ChatGPT as a novel tool to accelerate and enhance the process of refining his hypotheses before or during empirical testing.

## Key properties or components
- Formulation of a testable statement
- Seeking external evaluation
- Identification of potential flaws or weaknesses
- Iterative refinement
