## Definition
A data repository is a centralized location where data is stored, managed, and made accessible. In the context of AI, it typically holds datasets, models, and related resources.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
AI Kosh functions as a data repository, consolidating India's public AI-related data and models in one place.

## Key properties or components
- Centralized Storage
- Data Management
- Accessibility
- Metadata
