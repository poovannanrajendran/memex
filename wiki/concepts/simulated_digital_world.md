## Definition
A virtual environment created through software where agents can interact and behave according to predefined rules or emergent properties. It allows for experimentation and observation of complex systems.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a testbed for observing emergent behaviours of AI agents, such as market dynamics and social interactions, without real-world consequences.

## Key properties or components
- Virtual environment
- Agent interaction
- Rule-based or emergent behaviour
- Data generation
