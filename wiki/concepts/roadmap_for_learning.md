## Definition
A structured, step-by-step plan or guide that outlines the path to acquiring knowledge or mastering a specific subject.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Helps users navigate complex topics efficiently and provides a clear direction for learning, making the process less daunting.

## Key properties or components
- Step-by-step guidance
- Subject-specific
- Learning objectives
- Progress tracking (implied)
