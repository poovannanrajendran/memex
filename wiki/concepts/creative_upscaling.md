## Definition
An AI-powered process that enhances video resolution not just by increasing pixel count, but by intelligently reimagining and refining details, potentially altering the visual style.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Demonstrated by Krea's video upscaler, which can improve video quality and fix issues like warped faces while retaining or subtly reimaging the original content.

## Key properties or components
- Resolution Enhancement
- Detail Refinement
- AI Reimagining
- Style Variation
