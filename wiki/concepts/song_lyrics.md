## Definition
Song lyrics are the words that make up a song. They typically follow a structure of verses and choruses and convey the song's message, narrative, or emotion.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding song lyrics is crucial for appreciating the artistic and thematic content of music, especially in film.

## Key properties or components
- Verse
- Chorus
- Rhyme Scheme
- Rhythm
- Narrative
