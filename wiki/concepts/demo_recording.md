## Definition
A demo recording, or demonstration recording, is an initial recording of a song or musical piece, typically created to showcase its structure, melody, and potential. Demos are often rougher than final studio versions and are used by artists to present their work to producers, labels, or collaborators.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source uses a demo recording of "Beat It" to illustrate Michael Jackson's raw talent and creative process in developing the song's vocal arrangements before its official production.

## Key properties or components
- Early stage of music creation
- Used for demonstration and planning
- Can be less polished than final products
