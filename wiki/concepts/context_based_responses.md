## Definition
Context-based responses in AI refer to answers or actions that are derived from understanding the surrounding information, previous interactions, or environmental factors. This ensures relevance and accuracy.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The AI explicitly states its responses are fact or context-based, highlighting a key characteristic of advanced AI systems that distinguishes them from speculative or hallucinatory outputs.

## Key properties or components
- Relevance
- Accuracy
- Information dependency
- Interaction history utilization
