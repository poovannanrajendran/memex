## Definition
The speed and efficiency with which a smartphone can execute tasks, run applications, and handle multitasking. It is influenced by the processor, RAM, software optimisation, and battery management.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Directly impacts user experience, productivity, and the ability to utilise advanced features and applications, a key factor in choosing a premium device like the iPhone 15 Pro.

## Key properties or components
- Processing speed
- App loading times
- Multitasking capability
- Graphics rendering
- Battery efficiency
