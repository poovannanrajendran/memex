## Definition
Geekbench performance refers to the scores achieved by a processor in the Geekbench benchmarking software. These scores are typically divided into single-core and multi-core metrics, indicating performance on tasks that use one or multiple processing cores, respectively.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is used to quantify and compare the raw CPU processing power of the AMD APU and the Apple M4 Pro, highlighting differences in single-threaded versus multi-threaded capabilities.

## Key properties or components
- Single-core score
- Multi-core score
- Cross-platform comparison
- Raw processing power metric
