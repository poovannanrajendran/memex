## Definition
A situation where the performance of a computer system is limited by the processing power of the Central Processing Unit (CPU). Tasks are slowed down because the CPU cannot keep up with the demands of other components or software.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Explains the creator's primary motivation for building a new workstation, as their previous CPU was consistently limiting their video editing and animation workflow.

## Key properties or components
- 100% CPU utilisation
- Reduced performance in demanding applications
- Sluggish system responsiveness
- Identified through monitoring tools
