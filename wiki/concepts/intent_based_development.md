## Definition
A paradigm in software development where the focus shifts from explicit instructions (coding) to understanding and fulfilling the user's underlying intention or goal. AI interprets the desired outcome and generates the necessary implementation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept underpins Base44's ability to build complex applications from simple natural language descriptions, as recognised by Wix's acquisition.

## Key properties or components
- Focus on user goals
- AI interpretation of needs
- Automated implementation
- Reduced need for detailed technical specifications
