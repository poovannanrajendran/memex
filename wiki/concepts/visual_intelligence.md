## Definition
An AI-powered feature that allows the iPhone's camera to analyze and interpret visual information, providing context-aware data and actions, similar to Google Lens.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This feature, accessed via the Camera Control Button, aims to enhance the phone's ability to understand and interact with the visual world.

## Key properties or components

- Camera-based analysis
- Information extraction
