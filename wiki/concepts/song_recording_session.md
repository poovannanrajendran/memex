## Definition
A song recording session is a period where musicians and audio engineers gather in a studio to capture and produce the audio for a song.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the video's apparent subject matter, illustrating a stage in music production.

## Key properties or components
- Music capture
- Audio engineering
- Studio environment
- Artist performance
