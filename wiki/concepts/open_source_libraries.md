## Definition
Collections of pre-written code, typically designed for a specific purpose, that are made publicly available under a license allowing others to use, modify, and distribute them freely.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Foster collaboration, accelerate development, and provide foundational tools for developers, as exemplified by Sauner and Ball.

## Key properties or components
- Publicly available code
- Permissive licensing
- Community contribution
- Reusability

## Contradictions or debates
None.
