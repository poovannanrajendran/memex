## Definition
A game with a timeless objective, where the goal is not to win but to stay in the game and continue playing indefinitely, often characterized by continuous improvement and adaptation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Reframes the perspective on business and life, shifting from a finite win/loss mentality to a long-term, growth-oriented approach.

## Key properties or components
- Timeless objective
- Continuous play
- Adaptation
- Resilience
- Growth mindset
