## Definition
This concept involves acquiring knowledge and skills by observing, participating in, or reflecting upon events and situations. It emphasizes gaining wisdom from both personal actions and the experiences of others.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video advocates for learning through books, which is a primary method of vicariously learning from the experiences of others, a core tenet for achieving success.

## Key properties or components
- Reflective
- Experiential
- Adaptive
- Cumulative

## Contradictions or debates
Over-reliance on others' experiences without adapting them to one's own context can lead to ineffective strategies. Also, subjective interpretations of experiences can lead to flawed learning.
