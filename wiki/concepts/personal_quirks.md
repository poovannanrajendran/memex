## Definition
Unique, often idiosyncratic habits, preferences, or characteristics specific to an individual. These can range from minor dislikes to deeply ingrained behaviours.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The revelation of Louis's allergy to flowers highlights how well Harvey knows his colleague's personal quirks, even those not immediately relevant to their professional lives.

## Key properties or components
- Individuality
- Habits
- Preferences
- Aversions
