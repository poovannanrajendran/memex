## Definition
Tax compliance refers to the act of adhering to tax laws and regulations, including timely filing of returns and payment of taxes due. It involves accurately reporting income and fulfilling all obligations set by the tax authorities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Maintaining tax compliance is essential for individuals and businesses to avoid penalties, legal actions, and potentially severe consequences such as income tax raids.

## Key properties or components
- Accurate reporting of income
- Timely filing of tax returns
- Payment of taxes due
- Record keeping
- Understanding tax laws
