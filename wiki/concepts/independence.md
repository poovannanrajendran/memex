## Definition
The state of being free from the control, influence, or support of others. In the context of the lyrics, it refers to women's ability to manage their lives and achieve success without relying on men.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The lyrics repeatedly emphasize that women 'can't run this show on your own' and highlight the strength found in independence, suggesting it's a key theme.

## Key properties or components
- Autonomy
- Self-sufficiency
- Freedom
