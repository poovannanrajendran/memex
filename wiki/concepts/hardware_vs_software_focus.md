## Definition
The strategic emphasis a company places on physical product improvements (hardware) versus feature enhancements and capabilities delivered through software and AI.

The reviewer suggests the iPhone 16 generation leans more on software and AI advancements (Apple Intelligence) rather than groundbreaking hardware changes.

- Sensor improvements
- AI algorithms
- User experience enhancements
