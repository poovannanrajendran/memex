## Definition
Errors or flaws within the software that controls a vehicle's entertainment, navigation, and communication systems, leading to malfunctions or unexpected behaviour.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Can significantly impact the user experience, causing frustration and potentially requiring costly repairs or updates. Manufacturers often address these via over-the-air (OTA) updates.

## Key properties or components
- Glitches
- Freezing
- Unresponsiveness
- Requires software updates
