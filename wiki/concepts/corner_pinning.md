## Definition
Corner pinning is a visual effects technique used to warp and align a flat graphic or image to fit a specific perspective within a video frame. It's often used in conjunction with tracking data.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Allows for the precise placement and perspective matching of overlaid elements, such as text or graphics, to the tracked motion in the video.

## Key properties or components
- Warping
- Perspective alignment
- Tracking data integration
