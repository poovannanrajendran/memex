## Definition
A blend of ground spices, commonly used in Indian, Pakistani, and Nepali cuisine. The specific ingredients vary by region and personal preference, but often include cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, and cumin.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is a key spice blend used to flavour the paneer marinade, contributing to the authentic taste of the dish.

## Key properties or components
- Spice blend
- Aromatic
- Common in Indian cooking
