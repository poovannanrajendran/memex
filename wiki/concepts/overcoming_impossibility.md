## Definition
The act of achieving a goal or completing a task that is widely believed or considered impossible by others. This concept often relates to innovation, perseverance, and challenging conventional wisdom.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Central to Ratan Tata's stated source of personal pleasure and a theme in inspirational speaking.

## Key properties or components
- Requires significant determination
- Challenges established norms
- Often leads to groundbreaking results
- Requires a belief in one's own capabilities
