## Definition
A component or stage in a system that limits the overall performance or throughput of the system. It is the point where the system's capacity is most constrained.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding bottlenecks (e.g., GPU, media engine, storage) is crucial for diagnosing performance issues and determining where upgrades or different hardware would be most beneficial in demanding tasks.

## Key properties or components
- Performance Limitation
- Throughput Constraint
- System Capacity
- Weakest Link
