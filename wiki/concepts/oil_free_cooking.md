## Definition
A cooking approach that avoids the use of added oils, fat, or grease in food preparation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a healthier alternative to traditional mayonnaise preparation, which typically relies heavily on oil.

## Key properties or components
- Reduced fat content
- Lower calorie count
- Focus on alternative emulsifiers or bases

## Contradictions or debates
None.
