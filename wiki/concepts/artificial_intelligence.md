## Definition
A branch of computer science that aims to create systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The core technology enabling the AI tool to understand commands and automate complex tasks.

## Key properties or components

- Problem-solving
- Automation
