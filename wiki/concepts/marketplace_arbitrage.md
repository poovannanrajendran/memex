## Definition
A strategy that involves identifying price differences for the same or similar products across different markets or platforms and profiting from these discrepancies. In the context of Amazon, this can involve finding products that are cheaper to source elsewhere and selling them at a higher price on Amazon.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is implied in the strategy of identifying trending products with low competition, suggesting a need to find arbitrage opportunities within the Amazon ecosystem or between external suppliers and Amazon.

## Key properties or components
- Price comparison
- Supply and demand analysis
- Risk assessment
