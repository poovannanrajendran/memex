## Definition
Investigating the podcast landscape, including popular shows, listener preferences, content themes, and audience demographics, to inform the creation or positioning of a new podcast.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This use case highlights how the tools can be used to analyse successful podcasts, identify audience desires, and brainstorm content ideas for aspiring podcast creators.

## Key properties or components
- Identifying top podcasts
- Listener analysis
- Content theme identification
- Competitive positioning
