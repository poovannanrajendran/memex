## Definition
Something that is not valued or appreciated as much as it should be. In the context of products, it refers to items that offer great quality or performance relative to their recognition or price.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Identifying underrated gear, such as the Fujifilm 50mm F/2 lens, can help photographers find excellent value and high-performing equipment that might otherwise be overlooked.

## Key properties or components
- High performance relative to recognition
- Good value for money
- Often overshadowed by more popular alternatives

None.
