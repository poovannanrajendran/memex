## Definition
Relations allow two Notion databases to be linked, establishing a connection between their entries. Rollups then leverage these relations to pull, calculate, and display specific data from one database into another, enabling sophisticated data aggregation and analysis.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
These features are fundamental for building interconnected systems within Notion, allowing for complex project management, data analysis, and the creation of dashboards that provide holistic overviews.

## Key properties or components
- Bi-directional linking between databases
- Data aggregation from related databases
- Calculations (e.g., counts, sums, percentages)
- Displaying related properties
- Enabling progress tracking
