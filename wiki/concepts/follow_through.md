## Definition
The continuation of a movement after the main action has been completed. In cricket batting, it refers to the movement of the bat and body after striking the ball.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for maintaining balance, generating power, and ensuring the shot is played correctly.

## Key properties or components
- Continuation of motion
- Post-impact action
- Aids balance and power

## Contradictions or debates
None.
