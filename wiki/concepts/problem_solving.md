## Definition
The process of identifying complex problems and actively working to find optimal solutions. It involves analyzing the problem, brainstorming potential solutions, evaluating them, and implementing the best course of action.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video showcases how different candidates approach problem-solving in a high-pressure situation, distinguishing between those who focus on blame and those who seek understanding and solutions.

- Analysis
- Critical thinking
- Decision making
- Root cause identification
