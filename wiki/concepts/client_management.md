## Definition
The process of managing relationships and interactions with clients to ensure their needs are met and satisfaction is maintained. This involves communication, understanding expectations, and delivering services effectively.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for business success, as demonstrated by the focus on impressing new clients like Alice and Rajiv to secure opportunities such as international travel.

## Key properties or components
- Communication
- Expectation management
- Relationship building
- Service delivery
