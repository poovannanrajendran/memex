## Definition
A romantic song is a musical composition that focuses on themes of love, affection, and relationships. These songs often evoke emotions such as longing, happiness, or melancholy related to romantic experiences.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Romantic songs are a staple in cinema and popular music, resonating with a wide audience and often serving as a central element in storytelling.

## Key properties or components
- Lyrical themes of love
- Emotional expression
- Melodious composition
- Varied tempo and mood
