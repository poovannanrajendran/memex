## Definition
Independent journalism is reporting that is free from the influence of commercial, political, or special interests. It prioritises accuracy, fairness, and the public interest.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Puthiya Thalaimurai TV's self-proclaimed stance as an independent news channel is highlighted, suggesting a commitment to unbiased reporting relevant to Poovi's interest in reliable information sources.

## Key properties or components
- Objectivity
- Unbiased reporting
- Ethical standards
- Public interest

## Contradictions or debates
The ideal of complete independence is often debated, as news organisations can still face subtle influences.
