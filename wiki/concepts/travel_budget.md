## Definition
The amount of money allocated for a trip, covering expenses like flights, accommodation, food, activities, and tours.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The vlogger shares details about their tour package cost ($800 USD per person for 8 days) and mentions additional costs for certain activities, providing practical financial information for potential travelers.

## Key properties or components
- Land-only package
- Per-person cost
- Inclusions
- Exclusions
- Additional expenses
