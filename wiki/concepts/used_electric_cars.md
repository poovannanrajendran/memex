## Definition
Electric vehicles that have had one or more previous owners, now available for resale at a reduced price compared to their original purchase cost.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Makes EV technology more accessible to a wider range of consumers by lowering the entry price point.

## Key properties or components
- Depreciated value
- Potential for battery degradation
- Wider variety of models available
- Often require careful inspection (e.g., battery health)

## Contradictions or debates
None.
