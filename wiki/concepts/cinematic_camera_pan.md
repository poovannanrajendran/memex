## Definition
A camera movement technique where the camera pivots horizontally on its axis, simulating a panoramic view. In AI video generation, this refers to an AI-driven effect that mimics this smooth, sweeping camera motion.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Adds a professional and visually appealing dynamic to static images, making mockups more engaging.

## Key properties or components
- Horizontal movement
- Smooth motion
- Visual effect
- AI-generated
