## Definition
Instructions or queries given to an AI model to guide its output. In the context of tools like Auto-Shorts, a text prompt serves as the basis for generating video content and its associated script.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A fundamental input method for many AI tools, including Auto-Shorts, enabling users to specify the desired video topic and content.

## Key properties or components
- User-defined input
- Guides AI output
- Can be descriptive or instructional
