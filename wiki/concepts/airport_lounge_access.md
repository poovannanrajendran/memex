## Definition
Airport lounge access grants travelers entry into exclusive airport lounges, which typically offer amenities such as comfortable seating, complimentary food and beverages, Wi-Fi, and sometimes showers.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances the travel experience by providing a more comfortable and less crowded environment before flights, often accessible through premium credit cards or loyalty programs.

## Key properties or components
- Exclusive Access
- Amenities
- Comfortable Seating
- Food and Beverages
