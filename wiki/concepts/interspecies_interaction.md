## Definition
Interspecies interaction refers to the relationship or behaviour between individuals of different species. This can range from predator-prey dynamics to mutualistic or commensalistic relationships, and even social interactions like play.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is relevant as the video reportedly shows an eagle and a dog playing together, which is an uncommon form of interspecies interaction.

## Key properties or components
- Can be social or ecological
- Can be beneficial, harmful, or neutral
- Includes predation, symbiosis, and play

## Contradictions or debates
While common in some contexts (e.g., domestication), complex social play between wild predators and other species is often debated or considered rare.
