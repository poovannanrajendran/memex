## Definition
A thin slice of meat, usually veal or pork, that is breaded and fried. It is a popular dish in many countries, particularly in Central Europe.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlighted as a main dish from "Schnitzel Heaven", emphasizing its large size and hand-pressed preparation as a key feature.

## Key properties or components
- Breaded
- Fried
- Meat cutlet
- Large portion
