## Definition
Business improvement refers to the strategic and systematic efforts undertaken by an organisation to enhance its performance, efficiency, and effectiveness across various operational areas.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for staying competitive, increasing profitability, and achieving long-term sustainability in any market.

## Key properties or components
- Efficiency enhancement
- Cost reduction
- Quality improvement
- Customer satisfaction
- Process optimisation

## Contradictions or debates
None.
