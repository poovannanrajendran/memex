## Definition
A system for Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning responsible for controlling the indoor environmental comfort of a building, including temperature, humidity, and air quality.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for occupant comfort, health, and productivity, especially in office environments. Its failure in the new building highlighted its unfinished state and significantly impacted the IT team's ability to work.

## Key properties or components
- Includes components like compressors, ducts, and refrigerants
- Requires regular maintenance and timely repairs for optimal functioning
- Malfunctions can lead to extreme indoor temperatures and discomfort
