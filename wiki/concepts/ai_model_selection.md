## Definition
The process of choosing the most appropriate Artificial Intelligence model for a particular task or set of tasks.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for maximising the effectiveness and efficiency of AI implementations in various applications, from productivity to complex reasoning.

## Key properties or components
- Task-specific optimization
- Outcome-based evaluation
- Consideration of model strengths and weaknesses
- Alignment with project requirements
