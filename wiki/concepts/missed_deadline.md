## Definition
Failure to complete a task or deliver a product by the scheduled time. This can have significant consequences in professional settings, impacting client satisfaction and project timelines.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the central problem presented in the interview scenarios, testing how candidates handle accountability and problem-solving.

## Key properties or components
- Client impact
- Project delays
- Performance review
- Root cause analysis

## Contradictions or debates
One candidate (Girish) implies the error was a simple technical glitch, while another scenario suggests it might be linked to demotivation, showing different perspectives on the cause.
