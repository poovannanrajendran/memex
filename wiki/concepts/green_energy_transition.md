## Definition
The global shift from fossil-fuel-based energy systems to renewable energy sources like solar, wind, and hydro power.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a significant investment opportunity and a strategic imperative for nations and corporations due to environmental concerns and economic benefits.

## Key properties or components
- Higher profit margins compared to traditional energy sources
- Governmental support and targets (e.g., India's 2030 goal)
- Increasing adoption driven by sustainability and cost-effectiveness

## Contradictions or debates
None.
