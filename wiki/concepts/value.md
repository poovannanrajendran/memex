## Definition
Value refers to the regard that something is held to deserve; the importance, worth, or usefulness of something. In this context, it refers to the ethical and moral principles that guide behaviour.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The anecdote serves to establish loyalty as a supreme value, exceeding the value of comfort or immediate pleasure.

## Key properties or components
- importance
- worth
- principle
- virtue
