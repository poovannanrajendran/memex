## Definition
Constructing a high-performance computer system optimized for demanding professional tasks, such as video editing or 3D rendering, while adhering to a strict cost limitation. This often involves utilizing used or repurposed enterprise-grade hardware.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The core theme of the video, demonstrating that powerful workstations do not necessarily require brand-new, expensive components, making professional capabilities more accessible.

## Key properties or components
- Cost-effectiveness
- Repurposed hardware
- High performance for specific tasks
- Component selection trade-offs
