## Definition
A system engineered to detect the presence of fire or smoke and alert building occupants through audible and visual signals, often linked to emergency services or a central monitoring station.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for providing early warning of fire, enabling timely evacuation and emergency response, which is paramount for saving lives and minimizing property damage.

## Key properties or components
- Includes smoke detectors and other sensing devices
- Must be fully connected and operational
- Essential for building safety and compliance
