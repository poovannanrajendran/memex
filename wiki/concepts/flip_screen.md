## Definition
A display screen on a camera that can be rotated or flipped out, allowing for easier self-recording (vlogging) and creative shooting angles.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Significantly improves the usability of cameras for vloggers and selfie-style content creators.

## Key properties or components
- Articulating display
- Selfie viewing
- Creative angles
