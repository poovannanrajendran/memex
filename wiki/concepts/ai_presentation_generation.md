## Definition
The use of artificial intelligence to automate the creation of presentation slides. This typically involves generating content, selecting visuals, and organising layouts based on a given topic or prompt.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Streamlines the process of creating presentations, making it faster and more efficient for individuals and businesses. It is relevant to productivity and AI tool development.

## Key properties or components
- Topic input
- Automated content generation
- Image generation
- Custom themes
- Smart layouts
- PowerPoint export
