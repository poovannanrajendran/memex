## Definition
A dish where eggplant is a primary ingredient, cooked in a spiced sauce or gravy, often with coconut milk, tomatoes, and various spices. It is a popular dish in many South Asian cuisines.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The central dish being prepared and showcased in the video.

## Key properties or components
- Key ingredient: Eggplant
- Spiced sauce/gravy
- Aromatics (garlic, ginger, chili)
- Spices (curry powder)
- Umami and sweet flavour profiles
