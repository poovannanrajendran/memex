## Definition
A method of producing video content where the creator's face is not shown. This is often achieved using stock footage, animations, AI-generated visuals, or screen recordings.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enables creators to produce content without needing to be on camera, which can lower barriers to entry and streamline production.

## Key properties or components
- No on-camera presence required
- Can utilise AI-generated assets
- Streamlined production process
