## Definition
Cost management involves strategies and techniques used to monitor, control, and reduce expenses associated with services or operations. In AI, this often relates to managing API usage and subscription fees.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key benefit of the Open WebUI and LiteLLM setup is the ability to manage costs by using pay-as-you-go APIs, setting budgets, and avoiding multiple subscription plans.

## Key properties or components
- Budget setting
- Usage monitoring
- Optimisation of resource allocation
- Pay-as-you-go models
