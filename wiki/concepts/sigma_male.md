## Definition
The 'sigma male' is a concept from a loosely defined social hierarchy or typology of men, often discussed in online communities. It describes a man who is introverted, independent, and operates outside the conventional social hierarchy, similar to an 'alpha' or 'beta' male but preferring solitude and self-reliance.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the video's theme, as it aims to compile edits and portray characters embodying these attributed traits. It is a popular trope in online content.

## Key properties or components
- Independent
- Introverted
- Self-reliant
- Operates outside hierarchy
- Successful without seeking validation

## Contradictions or debates
The 'sigma male' concept is not scientifically recognised and is largely a product of internet subcultures, lacking empirical validation. Its definition can be fluid and subjective.
