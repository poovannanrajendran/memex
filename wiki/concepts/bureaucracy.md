## Definition
A system of government or administration characterized by hierarchical structures, official rules, and a division of labor, often perceived as rigid or inefficient.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The character refers to the TVA as 'ridiculous bureaucrats', implying a critical view of their procedural and dictatorial approach to managing individuals and their stories.

## Key properties or components
- Hierarchy
- Rules and regulations
- Official procedures
- Potential for inflexibility
