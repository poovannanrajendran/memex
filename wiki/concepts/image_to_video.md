## Definition
The process of generating a video sequence based on an input still image. The AI animates the image or uses it as a starting point for a new scene.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlighted as a key strength of Luma Labs' Dream Machine, offering a powerful way to bring static visuals to life.

## Key properties or components
- Static Image Animation
- Visual Transformation
- Prompt-based Animation
