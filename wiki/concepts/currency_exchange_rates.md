## Definition
Currency exchange rates represent the value of one nation's currency in relation to another nation's currency. These rates fluctuate based on market forces and economic factors.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding exchange rates is critical for travelers to accurately calculate prices, avoid being overcharged, and manage their budget effectively in foreign countries.
