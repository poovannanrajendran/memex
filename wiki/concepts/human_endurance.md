## Definition
The capacity of the human body and mind to withstand prolonged physical or mental discomfort, stress, or adversity. It involves pushing physiological and psychological limits.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlights the incredible potential of the human spirit and body, inspiring others and expanding our understanding of human capabilities.

## Key properties or components
- Physiological resilience
- Psychological fortitude
- Mental discipline
- Adaptability

## Contradictions or debates
Extreme endurance feats can pose significant health risks and may not be replicable or advisable for most individuals.
