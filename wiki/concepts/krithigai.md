## Definition
Krithigai is an important nakshatra (lunar mansion) in Hindu astrology and a significant festival dedicated to Lord Murugan. It is celebrated on the day the moon is in the Krithigai star, usually once a month, with special emphasis on the full moon Krithigai.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is a period of heightened devotion for followers of Lord Murugan, marked by fasting, prayers, and temple visits, making it relevant to understanding the context of the video.

## Key properties or components
- Monthly observance
- Dedicated to Lord Murugan
- Associated with the Krithigai nakshatra
- Involves prayers and temple rituals
