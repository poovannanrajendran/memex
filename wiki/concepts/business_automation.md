## Definition
Business automation involves using technology to perform repetitive or complex tasks that were previously done manually. This can include customer service, data processing, workflow management, and operational execution.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Automation through tools like OpenClaw can lead to significant efficiency gains, cost reductions, and increased scalability for businesses, allowing human resources to focus on higher-value activities.

## Key properties or components
- Efficiency
- Cost reduction
- Scalability
- Task delegation
- Process optimisation
