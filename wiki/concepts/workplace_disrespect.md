## Definition
Disrespect in the workplace refers to behavior that undermines, demeans, or disregards an individual's feelings, contributions, or dignity. It can manifest through subtle insults, dismissiveness, exclusion, or overt acts of malice.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial in understanding employee motivation, retention, and overall workplace culture. Persistent disrespect can lead to significant mental distress and loss of productivity.

## Key properties or components
- Lack of acknowledgement
- Belittling behaviour
- Exclusion
- Insensitive actions
- Undermining contributions
