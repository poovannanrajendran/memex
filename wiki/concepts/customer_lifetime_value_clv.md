## Definition
The total worth of a customer to a business over the entire period of their relationship, measured by the revenue they generate.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlights the strategic importance of retaining and growing existing customer relationships rather than solely focusing on new customer acquisition.

## Key properties or components
- Revenue generation over time
- Retention strategies
- Upselling and cross-selling
- Profitability maximization

## Contradictions or debates
None.
