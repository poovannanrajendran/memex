## Definition
This refers to the historical progression of who is allowed to vote and at what age in the UK. Key milestones include changes in the voting age for men and women and the expansion of suffrage.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Specific dates related to changes in voting rights and ages are frequently tested in the Life in the UK exam, particularly concerning the enfranchisement of women.

## Key properties or components
- Enfranchisement
- Voting age thresholds
- Historical milestones (1918, 1928, 1969)
