## Definition
The capacity to recover quickly from difficulties, adversity, or shock; toughness. In a narrative context, it refers to a character's ability to bounce back and adapt in the face of hardship.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Underscored through Bhima's poisoning and subsequent rescue, demonstrating his ability to withstand and overcome significant physical and existential threats.

## Key properties or components
- recover from difficulties
- toughness
- adaptability
- strength in adversity
- endurance
