## Definition
A painful feeling of humiliation or distress caused by the consciousness of wrong or foolish behaviour. It involves a negative self-evaluation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Shame leads to feelings of worthlessness, inadequacy, and social withdrawal, making it difficult for individuals to form healthy connections and believe in their own value.

## Key properties or components
- Self-conscious emotion
- Focuses on the self
- Can be deeply damaging
