## Definition
Commitment is the state or quality of being dedicated to a cause, activity, or person. It implies a pledge or undertaking to follow a certain course of action.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is the action that demonstrates loyalty, and in this story, completing the job despite hardship is the act of commitment that leads to the lesson learned.

## Key properties or components
- dedication
- pledge
- undertaking
- perseverance

None.
