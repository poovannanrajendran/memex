## Definition
AI access control refers to the mechanisms and policies used to regulate who can use AI systems, which models they can access, and what actions they can perform.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The described solution allows for granular control over AI access for different users (employees, children), ensuring appropriate usage and preventing misuse, such as for homework cheating.

## Key properties or components
- User authentication
- Role-based permissions
- Model access restrictions
- Usage limitations
- Content filtering (guardrails)
