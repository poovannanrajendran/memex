## Definition
An emotional pain associated with, or characterised by, feelings of disadvantage, loss, despair, grief, helplessness, disappointment and sorrow.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
While a natural response to loss or disappointment, chronic or excessive sadness can lead to depression, a loss of motivation, and a significantly diminished quality of life.

## Key properties or components
- Basic emotion
- Response to loss
- Can become chronic
