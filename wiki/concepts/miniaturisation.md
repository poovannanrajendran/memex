## Definition
The process of making devices or components significantly smaller in size. This often involves advanced engineering and material science.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enables new applications for previously large and complex technologies, such as particle accelerators, moving them closer to practical, widespread use.

## Key properties or components
- Reduction in size
- Increased portability
- Potential for novel applications

## Contradictions or debates
None.
