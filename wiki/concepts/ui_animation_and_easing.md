## Definition
This refers to the process of adding movement and smooth transitions to user interface elements. Easing specifically dictates the rate of change in animation over time, making motion feel more natural.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for creating dynamic and engaging user experiences, making interfaces feel 'alive' and more intuitive.

## Key properties or components
- Motion graphics
- Transition effects
- Perceived performance
- User engagement
