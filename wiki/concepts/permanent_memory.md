## Definition
In the context of AI, permanent memory refers to the ability of a model to retain information across multiple sessions or interactions, allowing it to recall past events, decisions, and context without being reset or having to relearn.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for developing more sophisticated and context-aware AI assistants, reducing the need for repetitive input and improving the continuity of complex tasks.

## Key properties or components
- Persistence across sessions
- Contextual recall
- Reduced need for re-input
