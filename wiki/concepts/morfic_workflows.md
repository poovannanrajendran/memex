## Definition
A feature offered by Morfic that allows users to create pre-built creative pipelines. These pipelines combine multiple AI tools to automate complex creative tasks.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Simplifies the use of AI for creative purposes by offering a no-code solution where users only need to provide inputs.

## Key properties or components
- Pre-built creative pipeline
- Combines multiple AI tools
- Automates creative tasks
- Requires no AI experience
- Handles image generation, video conversion, voiceover
