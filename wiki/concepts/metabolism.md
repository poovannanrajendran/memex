## Definition
The set of life-sustaining chemical reactions in organisms. These reactions allow organisms to grow, reproduce, maintain their structures, and respond to their environments.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key bodily process that the detox drink is claimed to enhance.

- Anabolism
