## Definition
Puncture repair refers to the methods and products used to fix a hole or perforation in a pneumatic tire, typically caused by sharp objects. This can range from temporary sealant applications to more permanent plugging or patching.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for vehicle safety and functionality, as unrepaired punctures can lead to rapid deflation and potential loss of control.

## Key properties or components
- Temporary vs. permanent solutions
- Sealant injection
- Plug insertion
- Patching from inside

## Contradictions or debates
While many products claim permanent fixes, professional tire shops often recommend temporary sealants only for short-term use before a proper repair or replacement.
