## Definition
A workflow diagram visually represents the steps, decisions, and flow of activities within a process. It helps in understanding, analysing, and optimising business or technical processes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Useful for mapping out project processes and data flows, contributing to clearer project management and communication, especially when integrated with documentation.

## Key properties or components
- Start/End points
- Process steps
- Decision points
- Connectors
- Swimlanes
