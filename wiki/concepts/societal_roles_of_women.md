## Definition
The expected behaviors, rights, and obligations associated with women within a given society. These roles are influenced by cultural norms, traditions, and historical contexts, and can vary significantly across different communities and time periods.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The discussion revolves around how women, despite traditional expectations, often assume leadership and provider roles within families and society, challenging conventional notions.

## Key properties or components
- Cultural Norms
- Gender Expectations
- Household Management
- Economic Contribution
- Social Influence
