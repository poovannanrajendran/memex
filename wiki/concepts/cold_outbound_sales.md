## Definition
A sales strategy where a company or individual initiates contact with potential customers who have not previously expressed interest. This often involves reaching out via email, phone calls, or social media.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlighted as a key method to acquire customers for an AI-generated service, with AI assisting in scriptwriting and contact identification.

## Key properties or components
- Proactive outreach
- Lead generation
- Sales script development
- Contact information acquisition
