## Definition
A form of asexual reproduction in which a new individual develops from an unfertilised egg. It occurs by the egg developing on its own, or by the egg being stimulated by something other than a sperm.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a theoretical and now supposedly proven method of human reproduction without male genetic material, challenging traditional understanding of reproduction.

## Key properties or components
- Asexual reproduction
- Development from an unfertilised egg
- Occurs naturally in some species
- Theorized in humans
