## Definition
The duration for which a device can operate on a single battery charge.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Critical for action cameras, which are often used for extended periods outdoors without access to charging.

## Key properties or components
- Continuous recording time
- Overheating resistance
- Battery capacity
