## Definition
The process of designing and providing instructions (prompts) to artificial intelligence models to elicit desired outputs or behaviours. This involves crafting specific text or commands that guide the AI's response.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for controlling and improving the performance, personality, and accuracy of AI agents, as highlighted by the Agent Handbook's features.

## Key properties or components
- Instruction-based
- Output-oriented
- Iterative refinement
