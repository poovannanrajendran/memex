## Definition
A prompting technique where the user instructs the AI to adopt a specific persona or role (e.g., 'Act as an expert consultant'). The aim is to influence the AI's response style and content based on the assigned role.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Commonly suggested as a method to improve AI response quality, but this source questions its consistent effectiveness, especially with advanced models.

## Key properties or components
- Persona assignment
- Contextual influence
- Response style modification

## Contradictions or debates
While widely recommended, studies and experience suggest it does not consistently improve responses and can be unpredictable. It may be most useful for creative or high-accuracy tasks.
