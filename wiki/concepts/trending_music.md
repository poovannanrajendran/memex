## Definition
Songs or audio clips that have gained significant popularity and widespread use on social media platforms within a short period.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source explicitly mentions 'Instagram trending music player', highlighting the desire to leverage popular audio for engagement.

## Key properties or components
- High virality
- Frequent use on social media
- Often associated with challenges or specific content formats

## Contradictions or debates
What is 'trending' can be subjective and change rapidly; what is trending on one platform may not be on another.
