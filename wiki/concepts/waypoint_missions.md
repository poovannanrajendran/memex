## Definition
Waypoint missions are automated flight paths for drones defined by a series of geographical points (waypoints). The drone autonomously flies from one waypoint to the next, performing pre-programmed actions such as hovering, taking photos, or adjusting altitude at each point.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Waypoint missions are fundamental to automated drone operations in AI engineering, enabling precise and repeatable data collection, surveillance, and complex aerial manoeuvres.

## Key properties or components
- Automated flight path
- Pre-defined points (waypoints)
- Programmable actions at waypoints
- Altitude and speed control
- Mission planning software
