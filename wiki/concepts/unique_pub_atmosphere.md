## Definition
Refers to a public house that offers a distinctive or unusual environment or setting, differentiating it from typical establishments. This can include historical significance, architectural features, or unique natural occurrences.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video highlights a pub that floods during high tide, presenting it as an attraction for its unique "people watch" potential and unusual environment.

## Key properties or components
- Distinctive ambiance
- Unusual setting
- Social observation point
- Environmental interaction
