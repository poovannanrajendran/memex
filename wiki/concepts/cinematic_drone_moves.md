## Definition
Specific flight patterns and camera movements executed with a drone that are designed to create visually appealing and story-driven footage. These moves often mimic traditional filmmaking camera techniques but leverage the unique aerial perspective and freedom of drones.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Learning cinematic drone moves is crucial for creating professional-looking video content, adding a dynamic and engaging visual quality that can enhance storytelling.

## Key properties or components
- Smooth and deliberate movements
- Intentional camera framing
- Variety of flight paths (e.g., orbit, dolly, reveal)
- Use of drone's gimbal and camera capabilities
