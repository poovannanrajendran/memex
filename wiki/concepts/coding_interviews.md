## Definition
A process used by companies to assess the technical skills and problem-solving abilities of candidates applying for software development roles. It typically involves one or more technical challenges or coding problems.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)

## Key properties or components

- Problem-solving assessment
- Pressure situations
- Assessing candidate ingenuity
