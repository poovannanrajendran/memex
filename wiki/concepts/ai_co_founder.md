## Definition
An artificial intelligence system that simulates the role of a human co-founder in a startup. It assists with strategic planning, research, and operational tasks.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a novel application of AI in entrepreneurship, potentially lowering barriers to entry and accelerating business development.

## Key properties or components
- Automated market research
- Automated business plan generation
- Automated landing page creation
- No-code requirement
