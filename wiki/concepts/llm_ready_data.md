## Definition
Data that has been processed and structured in a format suitable for ingestion and understanding by Large Language Models (LLMs). This typically involves cleaning, organizing, and sometimes converting data into text or specific formats that LLMs can effectively learn from or process.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for leveraging the power of LLMs, as raw, unstructured data is often not directly usable. Tools like Firecrawl aim to simplify the creation of LLM-ready data from various sources.

## Key properties or components
- Structured
- Cleaned
- Organized
- Text-based
