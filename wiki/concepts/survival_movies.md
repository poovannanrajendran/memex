## Definition
Survival movies are a genre that focuses on characters attempting to stay alive in difficult, often remote, and dangerous environments. These films typically involve themes of resourcefulness, human endurance, and the struggle against nature or hostile elements.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
These movies are popular for their suspenseful narratives, showcasing human resilience and often exploring the psychological impact of extreme situations, which can be engaging for audiences interested in thrilling and thought-provoking content.

## Key properties or components
- Protagonist(s) facing extreme environmental challenges
- Themes of endurance and resourcefulness
- Suspenseful plotlines
- Focus on the struggle for life
