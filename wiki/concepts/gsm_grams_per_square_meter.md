## Definition
A unit of measurement for fabric density and weight. Higher GSM generally indicates a thicker, heavier, and potentially more durable fabric.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Used by the business owner to assess the quality and weight of the t-shirt material supplied.

## Key properties or components
- Measures fabric weight
- Indicates thickness and durability
- Standardised measurement
