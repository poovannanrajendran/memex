## Definition
In a computational context, a system refers to a collection of interconnected components that work together to perform a specific function. This can range from software architectures to integrated hardware and software solutions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source suggests that prompt engineering is a byproduct of understanding and working with systems, highlighting the relationship between AI development and broader system design principles.

## Key properties or components
- Interconnected components
- Defined function
- Interdependencies
- Architecture
