## Definition
Budgeting is the process of creating a plan to spend and save your money. It involves estimating income and expenses over a specific period to manage finances effectively.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Vital for travelers to track spending across different currencies, manage group expenses, and adhere to financial plans, preventing overspending.

## Key properties or components
- Expense Tracking
- Income Projection
- Multi-currency Support
- Bill Splitting
