## Definition
Information or signs that support a claim or belief. This can include documents, testimonies, recordings, or physical objects presented to prove or disprove a point.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video heavily relies on presenting 'evidence' (CCTV footage, audio recordings, witness statements) to refute "Tech Boss's" claims and support their own narrative.

## Key properties or components
- Authenticity
- Relevance
- Sufficiency
- Corroboration
- Proof

## Contradictions or debates
Both sides claim to have evidence, and the interpretation or authenticity of this evidence is a key point of contention. The video specifically aims to 'break' the evidence presented by the opposing side.
