## Definition
The phases a startup goes through from its inception to maturity, often characterised by rapid growth, high risk, and intensive effort.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding the demanding and war-like nature of startups, as described by the founders, is crucial for anyone involved.

## Key properties or components
- High risk
- Intense effort
- Long duration
- Uncertainty

## Contradictions or debates
None.
