## Definition
The principle that individuals are accountable for ensuring their own work, talents, and contributions are noticed and understood, rather than relying on others to identify them.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Challenges a passive approach to career development, promoting proactive self-promotion and clear communication of one's value.

## Key properties or components
- Proactivity
- Self-advocacy
- Visibility
- Accountability

## Contradictions or debates
None.
