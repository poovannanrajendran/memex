## Definition
A film genre that combines elements of horror and comedy. It aims to scare the audience while also making them laugh, often through the use of jump scares, dark humour, or absurd situations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The film "Aranmanai 4", from which this song is taken, belongs to this genre, influencing the tone and context of the song.

## Key properties or components
- Suspense
- Gore
- Humour
- Supernatural elements
- Satire
