## Definition
A process where an AI system automatically chooses the most appropriate AI model to execute a given task, based on criteria such as task complexity, cost, and required performance.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enables cost optimization and performance enhancement by using the right model for the job, crucial in the rapidly evolving AI landscape.

## Key properties or components
- Task analysis
- Model evaluation
- Cost-performance balancing
- Real-time decision making
