## Definition
The principle of ensuring that artificial intelligence technologies and their benefits are available and usable by a wide range of individuals and organisations, regardless of their technical expertise or financial resources.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Goose embodies this concept by being free and open-source, aiming to level the playing field in AI development.

## Key properties or components
- Affordability
- Open access
- Ease of use
- Community support
