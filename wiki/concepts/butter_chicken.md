## Definition
A popular Indian dish consisting of chicken prepared in a rich, spiced tomato and butter sauce. It is known for its creamy texture and mild, aromatic flavour.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Featured as a flavour base for the barbecue chicken chops, which is highlighted as an exceptional dish.

## Key properties or components
- Chicken
- Tomato-based sauce
- Butter
- Creamy
- Mildly spiced
