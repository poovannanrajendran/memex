## Definition
This term refers to attending a movie on its opening day and the very first screening. It is a common practice for highly anticipated films, particularly in certain cultural contexts.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It signifies extreme enthusiasm and dedication to experiencing a movie as soon as it is released, often involving special arrangements like taking time off work.

## Key properties or components
- Opening day attendance
- First screening
- High anticipation
- Fan dedication
