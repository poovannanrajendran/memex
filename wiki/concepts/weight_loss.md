## Definition
Weight loss is the reduction of total body weight. It is typically achieved through a combination of reduced calorie intake and increased physical activity, leading to a decrease in body fat and mass.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Weight loss was a significant outcome of the narrator's cycling journey, with a total loss of 35 kilograms contributing to improved health and self-esteem.

## Key properties or components
- Calorie deficit
- Increased metabolism
- Dietary changes
- Physical activity
