## Definition
An old Arab or non-European city, especially in North Africa. It typically consists of a maze of narrow streets and traditional buildings.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The vlog discusses both the 'old medina' and the 'new medina' (Habous) in Casablanca, highlighting French colonial influence on urban development.

## Key properties or components
- Historic city center
- Narrow streets
- Traditional architecture
- Marketplaces
