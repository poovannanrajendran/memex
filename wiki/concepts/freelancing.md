## Definition
A work arrangement where individuals offer their services to different clients on a project or task basis, rather than being employed by a single company long-term.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Presented as a viable alternative career option for individuals experiencing pressure or seeking flexibility in the IT sector.

## Key properties or components
- Project-based work
- Client-based employment
- Flexibility
- Self-employment

## Contradictions or debates
None.
