## Definition
A visual aesthetic in media that aims to achieve a very high degree of realism, closely mimicking the look and feel of live-action cinema, often with attention to detail in lighting, texture, and character rendering.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This was the desired visual style for the characters (Karna, Arjuna, Krishna) and the overall scene, achieved through specific AI model parameters and reference images.

## Key properties or components
- High detail rendering
- Realistic textures
- Cinematic lighting
- Lifelike character appearance
