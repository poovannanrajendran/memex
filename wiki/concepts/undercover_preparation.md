## Definition
A method of role preparation where an actor or individual assumes a false identity to experience or observe a situation firsthand. This involves blending into a specific environment without revealing their true purpose or identity.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Illustrates an extreme and unconventional approach to actor preparation, highlighting the lengths to which some might go to embody a character.

## Key properties or components
- Assumption of false identity
- Observation of environment
- Secrecy of true purpose
- Immersion in a specific setting
