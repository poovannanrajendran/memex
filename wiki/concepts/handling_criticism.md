## Definition
Handling criticism involves the strategies and techniques individuals use to respond to negative feedback, evaluations, or remarks from others. Effective handling aims to mitigate negative impacts while potentially extracting value or insight from the feedback.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the video's message, providing a framework for how professionals, inspired by Steve Jobs, should engage with criticism constructively.

## Key properties or components
- Acknowledgement of valid points
- Admission of failures
- Apology for mistakes
- Reframing into positives
- Maintaining composure
