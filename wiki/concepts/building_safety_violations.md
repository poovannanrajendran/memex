## Definition
Infractions of established building codes, fire codes, or other regulations designed to ensure the structural integrity, habitability, and overall safety of buildings for their occupants.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Directly impacts the safety and well-being of building occupants. Serious violations, as found in this source, can lead to forced evacuations, fines, legal action, and complete building shutdowns.

## Key properties or components
- Can encompass structural, electrical, plumbing, or fire safety issues
- Identified by authorised inspectors (e.g., city, fire department)
- Often result in immediate restrictions, fines, or mandatory rectification
