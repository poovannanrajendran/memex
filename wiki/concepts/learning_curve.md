## Definition
The rate at which an individual gains skill or knowledge in a new area. A steep learning curve indicates that it takes a significant amount of time and effort to become proficient, while a shallow one means proficiency can be achieved quickly.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The reviewer compares the learning curve of Firebase Studio to its competitors, noting it's higher but manageable.

## Key properties or components
- Ease of use
- Time to proficiency
- Complexity of features
- Required prior knowledge
