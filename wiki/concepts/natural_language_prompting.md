## Definition
The practice of using human language (text or speech) to instruct an AI system on what task to perform. The AI interprets the prompt to understand the desired output or action.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the primary user interface for Director.AI, enabling users to define complex automation tasks simply by describing them in a sentence, making the tool accessible to non-coders.

## Key properties or components
- User-friendly
- Intuitive
- Reduces learning curve
- Requires clear and specific instructions
