## Definition
Female empowerment refers to the process of strengthening women's power and control over their own lives. It involves challenging societal norms and structures that limit women's opportunities and agency.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is present in the lyrical content of the YouTube short, highlighting themes of women's strength and independence.

## Key properties or components
- Self-reliance
- Independence
- Resilience
- Challenging norms
