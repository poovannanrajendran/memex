## Definition
Software applications that leverage artificial intelligence, particularly natural language processing and generation, to assist users in creating written content. These tools can help with various aspects of writing, such as generating ideas, drafting text, improving grammar, and summarising information.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
These tools are increasingly relevant for academic and professional writing, offering efficiency gains and new methods for content creation, though their use in academic settings requires careful consideration of ethical guidelines and detection avoidance.

## Key properties or components
- Automated text generation
- Content structuring
- Tone adjustment
- Summarisation capabilities
