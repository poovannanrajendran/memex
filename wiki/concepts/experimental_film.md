## Definition
An experimental film is a non-traditional approach to filmmaking that explores new artistic or technical possibilities, often deviating from conventional narrative structures, techniques, or subject matter.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
These films push the boundaries of cinema, offering unique artistic visions and potentially influencing mainstream filmmaking.

## Key properties or components
- Unconventional narrative
- Avant-garde techniques
- Artistic expression
- Non-linear structure
- Abstract elements
