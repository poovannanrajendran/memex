## Definition
A request placed with a broker to buy or sell a financial asset, such as a stock, at a specified price or at the prevailing market price.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding investment orders is crucial for executing trades, as described in the simplified process presented by the Trading 212 app.

## Key properties or components
- Buy order
- Sell order
- Market order
- Limit order
