## Definition
A style of street dance that mimics robotic or animated characters. Dancers use techniques like popping, gliding, and waving to create the illusion of being a robot or cartoon.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is one of the core styles Kazuho Monster practices and has gained recognition for, contributing to his 'buzz'.

## Key properties or components
- Robotic movements
- Isolation
- Popping
- Waving
- Gliding

None.
