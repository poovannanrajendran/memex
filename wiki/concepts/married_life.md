## Definition
The state of being married, involving a legal and social union between two people. It encompasses shared responsibilities, emotional intimacy, and the day-to-day realities of living together.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video uses common experiences within married life as a basis for its comedic content, making it relatable to a broad audience.

## Key properties or components
- Shared responsibilities
- Emotional connection
- Daily routines
- Expectations vs. Reality

## Contradictions or debates
The humour arises from the contrast between idealized expectations of marriage and the often mundane or surprising reality.
