## Definition
The rights and control an individual has over media they acquire, including the ability to possess, use, resell, or format-shift it. This contrasts with licensing models where access is granted by a provider.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A growing concern as digital media consumption shifts towards subscriptions and licenses, potentially leading to loss of access to purchased content and highlighting the need for legal protections for consumers.

## Key properties or components
- Digital rights
- Licensing vs. ownership
- Resale rights
- Format shifting
- Consumer protection
