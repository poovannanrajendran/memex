## Definition
Traditional or natural treatments for ailments, often using household ingredients or simple practices, as opposed to conventional medicine.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Presented as a method to manage health issues like headaches and kidney stones, reflecting a cultural tendency towards natural healing alongside or instead of medical intervention.

## Key properties or components
- Natural ingredients (e.g., camphor, cardamom)
- Dietary changes (e.g., water intake, soups)
- Belief in traditional practices
