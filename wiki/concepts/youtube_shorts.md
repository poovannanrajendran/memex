## Definition
YouTube Shorts is a feature on the YouTube platform that allows users to watch and create short-form vertical videos, typically under 60 seconds.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This indicates the format and platform through which the song recording session content is being shared.

## Key properties or components
- Short-form video
- Vertical format
- Under 60 seconds
- Mobile viewing
