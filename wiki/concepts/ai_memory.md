## Definition
The ability of an artificial intelligence system to store, retain, and recall information from past interactions or data processing. Effective AI memory is crucial for context awareness, learning, and task completion.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Addressing the limitations of AI memory is key to developing more capable and persistent AI agents, which is relevant to improving AI tools.

## Key properties or components
- Storage
- Retrieval
- Retention
- Contextual awareness
