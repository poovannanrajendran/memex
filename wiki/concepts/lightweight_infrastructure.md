## Definition
A system or set of resources that are highly efficient, require minimal overhead, and consume fewer computational or network resources, often enabling greater scalability and cost-effectiveness.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Zerodha's adoption of lightweight infrastructure and Kailash Nad's open-sourcing of OUI reflect this philosophy.

## Key properties or components
- Minimal dependencies
- Optimised resource usage
- High performance
- Scalability
