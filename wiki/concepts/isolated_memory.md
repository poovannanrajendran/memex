## Definition
Isolated memory refers to the state where individual AI systems or tools maintain their own distinct and separate knowledge bases. Information learned or rules taught to one system are not automatically shared or accessible by others.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept highlights a key problem that the agentic stack aims to solve, improving AI agent collaboration and knowledge consistency.

## Key properties or components
- Lack of knowledge transfer
- Inconsistent AI behaviour
- Redundant learning
