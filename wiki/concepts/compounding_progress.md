## Definition
A strategy where small, consistent efforts made daily accumulate over time to create significant positive change. It mirrors the concept of compound interest, where growth builds upon previous growth.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the speaker's method for overcoming a major crisis, suggesting it's a powerful tool for personal and professional recovery and growth.

## Key properties or components
- Daily effort
- Consistency
- Accumulation
- Long-term results
- Psychological benefit (avoiding overwhelm)
