## Definition
A unicorn startup is a privately held startup company with a valuation of US$1 billion or more. This term signifies rapid growth and high market potential.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Mentioned as a type of organisation where individuals have successfully landed jobs using cold emails, illustrating the career opportunities enabled by effective outreach.

## Key properties or components
- Privately held
- Valuation over $1 billion
- High growth potential
