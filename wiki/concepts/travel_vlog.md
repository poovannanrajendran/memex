## Definition
A video log that documents a person's travel experiences. Vlogs often provide insights into destinations, activities, and personal journeys.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept describes the format and purpose of the source content, aiming to showcase a travel experience.

## Key properties or components
- Visual storytelling
- Personal narrative
- Destination focus
- Online platform (e.g., YouTube)
