## Definition
This is a work strategy where an individual deliberately commits to delivering less than they are capable of, thereby creating capacity to exceed expectations. It involves careful planning and accurate estimation of task completion.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source advocates this strategy as a method to ensure high-quality output and build a reputation for reliability and exceeding expectations, contributing to one's irreplaceability.

## Key properties or components
- Accurate task analysis
- Strategic planning
- Realistic commitments
- Exceeding expectations
