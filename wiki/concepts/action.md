## Definition
Action is the simplest behavior done in anticipation of a reward. It is the step taken by the user in response to a trigger.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It represents the user's direct engagement with the product, following the trigger and preceding the reward in the Hook Model.

## Key properties or components
- Simple behavior
- Response to trigger
- Precedes reward

## Contradictions or debates
None.
