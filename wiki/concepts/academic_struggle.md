## Definition
The difficulties a student faces in achieving success in their educational pursuits, often leading to poor grades or failure.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is juxtaposed with the student's talent, raising questions about educational support systems and the effectiveness of traditional academic metrics.

## Key properties or components
- Low grades
- Repeated failure
- Lack of engagement
- Learning difficulties

## Contradictions or debates
