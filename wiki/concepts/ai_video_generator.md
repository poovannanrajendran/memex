## Definition
An AI video generator is a software tool that uses artificial intelligence algorithms to create video content. These tools can generate scripts, voiceovers, visuals, and assemble them into a final video, often based on user prompts or data inputs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
These tools are crucial for enabling YouTube automation by handling the bulk of video production tasks, making content creation faster, more cost-effective, and accessible to a wider range of users.

## Key properties or components
- Automated scriptwriting
- AI-generated voiceovers
- Stock footage or AI-generated visuals
- Automated editing
- Template-based creation

## Contradictions or debates
The output from AI video generators may sometimes lack the nuance, creativity, or emotional depth of human-created videos, potentially impacting viewer engagement or brand authenticity.
