## Definition
The quality of being committed to a task or purpose. It involves persistent effort, focus, and devotion to achieving a goal, often over a prolonged period.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Demonstrated by Arjuna's nighttime archery practice, showcasing how sustained commitment and hard work can refine natural talent into mastery.

## Key properties or components
- commitment
- persistent effort
- devotion
- focus
- perseverance
