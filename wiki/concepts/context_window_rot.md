## Definition
A phenomenon in AI models where the effective context or memory of the model degrades over long interactions or complex data inputs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Addressing context window rot is crucial for maintaining AI performance and reliability in long-running or data-intensive tasks, and frameworks can automate its management.

## Key properties or components
- AI Memory Degradation
- Interaction Length Impact
- Performance Issue
