## Definition
Deepavali, also known as Diwali, is a major festival celebrated by Hindus, Jains, Sikhs, and some Buddhists. It is often referred to as the 'Festival of Lights' and symbolizes the victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance.
