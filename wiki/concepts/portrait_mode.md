## Definition
A camera feature that simulates the shallow depth-of-field effect of a DSLR camera, blurring the background to make the subject stand out. It typically uses algorithms to detect the subject and differentiate it from the background.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Both phones offer portrait modes, but Samsung is praised for its refined implementation, especially when using its telephoto lenses, achieving a natural-looking background blur and excellent edge detection.

## Key properties or components
- Background Blur (Bokeh)
- Edge Detection
- Subject Separation
- Depth Simulation
