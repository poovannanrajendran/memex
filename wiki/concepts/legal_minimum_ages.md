## Definition
The specific ages at which individuals are legally permitted to undertake certain activities or be held responsible for particular actions within the UK legal framework.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The test requires knowledge of minimum ages for driving different vehicles, consuming alcohol, voting, and other civic duties, highlighting the legal responsibilities that commence at different ages.

## Key properties or components
- Driving licences
- Alcohol purchase/consumption
- Voting rights
- Jury service eligibility
