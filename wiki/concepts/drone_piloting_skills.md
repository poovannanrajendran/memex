## Definition
The ability to control a drone safely and effectively through various manoeuvres and in different conditions. This includes understanding controls, spatial awareness, and executing precise movements.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for beginners to master before undertaking more complex tasks or flying in challenging environments. Good piloting skills ensure safety and enable creative aerial footage.

## Key properties or components
- Basic flight controls (takeoff, landing, hovering)
- Manoeuvrability (forward, backward, sideways, yaw)
- Altitude control
- Navigation
- Emergency procedures
