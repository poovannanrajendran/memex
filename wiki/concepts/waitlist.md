## Definition
A list of people who are waiting to gain access to a product or service that has limited availability. This is common for highly anticipated new technologies.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A significant barrier to entry for some of the most advanced tools, like Cling, where waitlist numbers can be in the hundreds of thousands or millions.

## Key properties or components
- Limited Access
- Demand Management
- Anticipation Building
