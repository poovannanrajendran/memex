## Definition
Faceless content refers to videos where the creator's face or personal presence is not shown. This is often achieved through screen recordings, animations, stock footage, or AI-generated visuals.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the type of content the described workflow aims to produce, making it suitable for creators who prefer not to appear on camera or want to scale content production rapidly.

## Key properties or components
- No on-camera presence required
- Can be created using various visual elements
- Focus on information or entertainment delivery
- Easier to outsource or automate
