## Definition
The mechanism by which requests are directed to specific Large Language Models (LLMs) within a system, often based on predefined rules or dynamic analysis.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Fundamental to implementing dynamic model selection, allowing an overarching system to intelligently dispatch tasks to suitable LLMs.

## Key properties or components
- Request dispatching
- Rule-based routing
- Dynamic analysis
- Model availability
