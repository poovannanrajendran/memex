## Definition
Crepes are a type of very thin pancake, usually made from wheat flour or buckwheat flour. They are a popular dish in French cuisine and can be served sweet or savoury.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The central culinary item being created in the video. Understanding crepes is key to following the recipe.

## Key properties or components
- Thin batter
- Cooked in a pan
- Versatile (sweet or savoury)
- Golden brown when cooked
