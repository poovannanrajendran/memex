## Definition
The range of complementary products and peripherals designed to work with a primary device, enhancing its functionality and user experience.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for the iPad Pro's transformation into a laptop alternative, with the Magic Keyboard and Apple Pencil Pro being key components of this ecosystem.

## Key properties or components
- Stylus integration (Apple Pencil)
- Keyboard and trackpad attachments (Magic Keyboard)
- Proprietary connectors
- Functionality enhancements
- Increased cost

## Contradictions or debates
None.
