## Definition
The classification of artificial intelligence tools based on their primary function or application area. Common categories include tools for content creation (writing, images, video), development, data analysis, and specialised industries.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Categorisation helps users quickly find AI tools relevant to their specific needs, improving efficiency and productivity. Toolify organises its offerings into categories like writing, images, audio, video, coding, legal, and finance.

## Key properties or components
- Functional grouping
- Application-specific organisation
- Improved user navigation
- Specialised tool identification
