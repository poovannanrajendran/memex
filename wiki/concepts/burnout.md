## Definition
Burnout is a state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by excessive and prolonged stress. It occurs when you feel overwhelmed, emotionally drained, and unable to meet constant demands.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding burnout is crucial in Poovi's context as it challenges the traditional 'hustle culture' often associated with high-achieving environments, suggesting that sustainable productivity is more valuable than sheer hours worked.

## Key properties or components
- Emotional exhaustion
- Cynicism or detachment
- Reduced sense of accomplishment
- Impact on physical health

## Contradictions or debates
Some argue that intense periods of work are necessary for groundbreaking achievements, even if they lead to temporary burnout.
