## Definition
A very high definition television and display resolution with a horizontal resolution of approximately 4,000 pixels. It is a significant step up in picture clarity and detail compared to previous resolutions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Indicates the technical quality and visual fidelity of the YouTube video.

## Key properties or components
- High resolution
- Detail
- Clarity
- Pixel count
