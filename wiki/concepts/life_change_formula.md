## Definition
A structured approach or set of principles designed to help individuals make significant positive transformations in their personal and professional lives.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Central to the video's promise, this concept suggests that life improvements are achievable through a systematic method rather than random chance, aligning with Poovi's interest in actionable strategies.

## Key properties or components
- Actionable steps
- Mindset shifts
- Goal setting
- Consistent application

## Contradictions or debates
The effectiveness of any single 'formula' can be debated, as individual circumstances and the complexity of life changes may require personalised approaches.
