## Definition
The process of synchronizing the movement of an avatar's or character's mouth with the accompanying audio track, making the speech appear natural.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for the believability and professionalism of AI avatar videos; poor lip-sync can significantly detract from the quality and impact of the advertisement.

## Key properties or components
- Audio-visual synchronization
- Facial animation
- Crucial for realism
