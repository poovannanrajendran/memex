## Definition
An advanced form of prompt chaining that involves running multiple prompt chains, often across different models or configurations, and then merging their results using an evaluator function.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enables sophisticated aggregation and evaluation of AI outputs, allowing for robust decision-making and refining results by combining diverse perspectives from multiple model runs.

## Key properties or components
- Parallel processing of chains
- Result merging
- Evaluator function
- Consensus building
