## Definition
The 'new web' refers to the evolving internet landscape increasingly shaped by artificial intelligence, augmented reality, and decentralized technologies, moving beyond the static web pages of the past.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This signifies the technological shift that agentic browsers like Neon are designed to cater to, highlighting a future where AI is deeply embedded in online experiences.

## Key properties or components
- AI-integrated
- Interactive
- Dynamic
- Personalized
