## Definition
3D modeling is the process of creating a digital three-dimensional representation of an object or surface. In the context of drones, this typically involves using photogrammetry techniques to generate detailed 3D models from aerial images.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
3D models created from drone data are valuable for various applications, including architectural visualization, construction progress monitoring, historical preservation, and creating digital twins of real-world environments.

## Key properties or components
- Digital representation
- Geometric data
- Textured surfaces
- Photogrammetry input
