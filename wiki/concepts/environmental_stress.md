## Definition
Adverse conditions or pressures within an individual's surroundings that can negatively impact development. In this context, parental stress during infancy or pregnancy is highlighted.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is presented as a primary factor that can impede the brain development necessary for a healthy time sense.

## Key properties or components
- External origin
- Impacts sensitive individuals
- Affects brain development
- Can occur in utero or infancy
