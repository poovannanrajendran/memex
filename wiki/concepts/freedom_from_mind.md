## Definition
This concept suggests that true mental freedom is not about controlling one's thoughts, but about detaching from them and reducing internal mental noise and struggle. It implies a state of peace achieved by quieting the ego and desires.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is presented as a more profound form of freedom than simply having a free mind, offering a path to reduced stress and greater contentment.

## Key properties or components
- Mental detachment
- Reduced internal conflict
- Mindfulness/Meditation adjacent

## Contradictions or debates
None.
