## Definition
Theta waves are a type of brainwave associated with states of relaxation, creativity, and light sleep. They have a frequency range of 4-7 Hz.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source claims these waves are used in the recommended music to relax the brain and improve memory, suggesting a direct application for learning.

## Key properties or components
- Frequency range of 4-7 Hz
- Associated with relaxation and creativity
- Can be influenced by external auditory stimuli
