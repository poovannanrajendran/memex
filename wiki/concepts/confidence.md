## Definition
A feeling of self-assurance or certainty arising from an appreciation of one's own abilities or qualities. It is often a key factor in achieving goals.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Identified by Georgie's father as a significant trait that, combined with another quality, could lead to unstoppable success.

## Key properties or components
- Self-belief
- Resilience
- Positive outlook
