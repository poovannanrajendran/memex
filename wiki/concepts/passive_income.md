## Definition
Earnings derived from a rental property, limited partnership, or other enterprise in which a person is not actively involved. It is income that requires little to no effort to earn and maintain.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enables financial independence and reduces reliance on traditional employment, aligning with goals of increased personal productivity and freedom.

## Key properties or components
- Scalability
- Automation
- Minimal ongoing effort
- Initial investment (time/money)
