## Definition
Synced blocks in Notion allow a specific block of content to be replicated across multiple pages. Any edits made to the synced block on one page will automatically update all other instances of that block, ensuring consistency.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This feature is vital for maintaining consistent information across different parts of a workspace, such as headers, footers, or frequently used snippets of text or code.

## Key properties or components
- Content replication
- Real-time synchronization
- Single source of truth
- Option to unsync specific instances
