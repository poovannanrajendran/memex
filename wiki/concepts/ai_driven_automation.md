## Definition
The use of artificial intelligence technologies to perform tasks that were previously done by humans, particularly those that are repetitive, time-consuming, or prone to error.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Allows businesses and individuals to increase efficiency, reduce costs, and free up human resources for more complex or creative work, aligning with Poovi's interest in optimising processes.

## Key properties or components
- Task delegation
- Efficiency gains
- Error reduction
- Scalability

## Contradictions or debates
