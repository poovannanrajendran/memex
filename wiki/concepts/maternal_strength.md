## Definition
The profound physical, emotional, and mental fortitude demonstrated by mothers, often in the face of significant personal challenges. This strength is typically manifested through sacrifice, perseverance, and unwavering dedication to their children's well-being.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video emphasizes maternal strength as a cornerstone of family stability and societal contribution, highlighting the often-unseen sacrifices mothers make.

## Key properties or components
- Sacrifice
- Resilience
- Nurturing
- Perseverance
- Selflessness
