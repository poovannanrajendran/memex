## Definition
The study and application of materials used in construction, focusing on their properties, performance, and how they can be optimised for specific structural requirements.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Explains the shift to lighter, stronger materials that allow for taller and more efficient skyscraper designs.

## Key properties or components
- Steel alloys
- Concrete formulations
- Composite materials
- Strength-to-weight ratio
