## Definition
A Sport Utility Vehicle (SUV) that offers high-end features, premium materials, advanced technology, and a comfortable ride, often at a higher price point than standard SUVs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The core classification of the Range Rover, defining its market position and target audience.

## Key properties or components
- Premium materials
- Advanced technology
- Comfortable ride
- Off-road capability (often)
- High price point
