## Definition
A traditional yogurt-based beverage from the Indian subcontinent. It can be sweet or savoury and is often blended with fruits or spices.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Mentioned as a perfect accompaniment to the naan and roti, enhancing the overall dining experience.

## Key properties or components
- Yogurt-based
- Drinkable
- Sweet or savoury
- Refreshing
