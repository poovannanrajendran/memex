## Definition
The process by which an electronic device, such as an audio accessory, is assessed and approved by regulatory bodies to function as a medical-grade hearing aid.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The AirPods Pro 2 achieving clinical-grade hearing aid certification is a significant development for accessibility, expanding their utility beyond audio playback.

## Key properties or components
- Regulatory approval
- Medical device functionality
- Enhanced accessibility
