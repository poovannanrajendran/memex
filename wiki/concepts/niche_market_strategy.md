## Definition
Focusing on a small, specific segment of a larger market, often starting with higher-value offerings to a select group before expanding.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Allows for efficient resource allocation and deeper customer understanding, mirroring Tesla's approach of starting with a premium product.

## Key properties or components
- Targeted approach
- High-value offerings
- Limited customer base initially
- Scalability potential

## Contradictions or debates
