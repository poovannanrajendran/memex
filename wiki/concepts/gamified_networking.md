## Definition
The application of game-design elements and game principles in non-game contexts, such as professional networking. This can involve using points, badges, leaderboards, and challenges to make networking activities more engaging and motivating.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Introduces an element of fun and competition into networking, potentially increasing user engagement and the frequency of networking activities. Happenstance is presented as a platform that uses gamification.

## Key properties or components
- Engagement
- Motivation
- Fun
- Interactivity
- Rewards

## Contradictions or debates
Some may find gamification trivialising or distracting from the core purpose of building genuine professional relationships.
