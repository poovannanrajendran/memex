## Definition
A lamb shank that has been slow-cooked with Indian spices (masala) for an extended period, then finished on a grill, and served as part of a roast dinner.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key dish featured in the video, showcasing the fusion of Indian flavours with the traditional roast dinner concept.

## Key properties or components
- Lamb shank
- Slow-cooked
- Indian spices
- Grilled finish
- Rich gravy
