## Definition
A method of investing that involves identifying and investing in specific industries or sectors of the economy that are expected to perform well.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for identifying growth opportunities and making informed investment decisions by focusing on future trends.

## Key properties or components
- Identification of inevitable sectors (e.g., 5G, 6G, EVs, AI, data centers)
- Analysis of major players within sectors
- Consideration of industry consolidation trends
