## Definition
Compensation refers to the payment or remuneration provided to an individual for their work or services. This can include salary, wages, bonuses, and other benefits.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Compensation is a critical factor in employee satisfaction and retention, and dissatisfaction with it can lead to individuals leaving their roles or projects, as alluded to in the source.

## Key properties or components
- Payment
- Remuneration
- Salary
- Wages
- Bonuses
