## Definition
A road constructed through a desert region, often facing unique challenges such as extreme temperatures, lack of water, and shifting sands.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a significant feat of engineering and logistics to connect regions and facilitate transport through arid environments.

## Key properties or components
- Length (565 km)
- Location (Taklamakan Desert)
- Construction on shifting sand
- Protective measures (forest, water pipes)
