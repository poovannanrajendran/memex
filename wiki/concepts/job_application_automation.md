## Definition
Job application automation refers to the use of software and tools to streamline and expedite the process of applying for jobs online. This can include tasks like form filling, resume customization, and submission.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the video, demonstrating how technology can drastically reduce the time and effort involved in job searching.

## Key properties or components
- Time-saving
- Efficiency enhancement
- Scalability (applying to many jobs)
- Customization of applications
