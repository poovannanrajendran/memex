## Definition
Specific hardware components chosen for or found in high-performance computer workstations, designed for reliability, speed, and capacity beyond typical consumer PCs. Examples include ECC RAM, server-grade CPUs, and professional GPUs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlights the specialized nature of hardware needed for professional tasks and how repurposed workstation components can offer significant value and performance.

## Key properties or components
- ECC RAM
- Server CPUs (Xeon, EPYC)
- Professional GPUs (Quadro, Radeon Pro)
- High-speed storage (NVMe, U.2)
- Robust chassis and power supplies
