## Definition
The practice of developing both the client-side (front-end) and server-side (back-end) of a web application. This involves working with databases, server logic, APIs, and user interfaces.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key selling point of Firebase Studio is its potential to facilitate full-stack development through its integration with the Firebase backend.

## Key properties or components
- Front-end development (UI/UX)
- Back-end development (server, database, API)
- Database management
- User authentication
