## Definition
Advanced artificial intelligence models trained on vast quantities of text data, enabling them to understand, generate, and process human-like language for a wide range of tasks.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
They serve as the core intelligence within many AI agent frameworks and applications, facilitating natural language interaction, content creation, and complex reasoning capabilities for businesses.

## Key properties or components
- Understand human language
- Generate human language
- Process human language
- Trained on vast text data
