## Definition
A dish where eggs (usually hard-boiled) are simmered in a gravy or sauce, typically made with a base of onions, tomatoes, and spices. Variations are found across many cuisines.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Egg curry is a popular and versatile dish, often representing comfort food or a staple in home cooking. This specific variant is South Indian.

## Key properties or components
- Contains eggs
- Served with gravy
- Spiced flavour profile
- South Indian origin
