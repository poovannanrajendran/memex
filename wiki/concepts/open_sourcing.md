## Definition
Open sourcing involves making source code publicly available, allowing anyone to view, use, modify, and distribute it. This fosters collaboration and rapid development.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The decision to open source Miroofish led to its rapid growth in popularity and community engagement on GitHub.

## Key properties or components
- Public Code Access
- Collaboration
- Community Development
- Transparency
