## Definition
A vast desert in the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region of China, known for its extreme temperatures and shifting sands, earning it the nickname 'Sea of Death'.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The challenging environment where the world's longest desert highway was built, requiring innovative solutions for construction and protection.

## Key properties or components
- Location: China
- Nickname: Sea of Death
- Terrain: Shifting sand
- Climate: Extreme temperatures
