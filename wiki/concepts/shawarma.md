## Definition
A Middle Eastern meat preparation where lamb, chicken, beef, or veal is placed on a vertical rotisserie, then slowly roasted and shaved off.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Cited as one of the cheapest options in London available at Fosdakb.

## Key properties or components
- vertical rotisserie
- shaved meat
- marinated
