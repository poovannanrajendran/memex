## Definition
Photorealism in AI image generation refers to the ability of a model to create images that are indistinguishable from actual photographs in terms of detail, lighting, texture, and overall appearance.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the core metric by which MidJourney and Leonardo AI are evaluated in the source content, determining the effectiveness of their respective image generation capabilities for realistic human depiction.

## Key properties or components
- Accurate lighting
- Realistic textures
- Natural composition
- Detailed features
- Correct anatomy (including hands)
