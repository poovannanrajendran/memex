## Definition
The use of artificial intelligence algorithms to identify and reduce unwanted noise or grain in digital images or video footage, particularly in low-light conditions, to improve clarity.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A technology employed by the DJI Osmo Action 5 in its 'Super Night Mode' to enhance video quality in dimly lit environments.

## Key properties or components
- Utilizes AI algorithms
- Reduces noise
- Improves low-light footage
- Can increase processing time
