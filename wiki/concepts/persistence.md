## Definition
The quality of continuing firmly or obstinately in a course of action despite difficulty or opposition. In a business context, it refers to the unwavering commitment to achieving goals.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for overcoming inevitable challenges and setbacks in business, leading to long-term success and resilience.

## Key properties or components
- Tenacity
- Resilience
- Grit
- Commitment
