## Definition
An autobiography is a self-written account of the author's own life.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Recommended as a source for learning from the experiences and potentially identifying patterns of successful individuals.

## Key properties or components
- Personal narrative
- First-person perspective
- Life experiences
