## Definition
The idea that individuals with exceptional mathematical or strategic abilities can also excel in complex video games. This suggests a transferable skill set between analytical tasks and high-level gaming.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the video's narrative, linking the character's success in a game to inherent genius.

## Key properties or components
- High strategic thinking
- Rapid decision-making
- Pattern recognition
- Transferable skills
