## Definition
The development and distribution of AI models, tools, and platforms under open-source licenses, allowing for free use, modification, and distribution. This fosters collaboration and innovation within the AI community.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Open-source solutions like Open Web UI and local models (via Ollama) offer cost-effective and customisable alternatives to proprietary services, enabling greater control and accessibility for users.

## Key properties or components
- Free accessibility
- Community contribution
- Customisation
- Transparency
- Local deployment
