## Definition
The sequence of steps and processes involved in creating a finished video product, from capturing footage to final export. This includes editing, colour grading, adding effects, and audio mixing.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video contrasts the creator's previous inefficient workflow (due to hardware limitations) with the vastly improved and smooth workflow enabled by the new workstation.

## Key properties or components
- Footage ingestion
- Proxy editing
- Timeline scrubbing
- Effects application
- Rendering and export
