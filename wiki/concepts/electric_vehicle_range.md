## Definition
The maximum distance an electric vehicle can travel on a single charge of its battery. This is a critical factor for consumers considering an EV purchase.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A primary concern for potential EV buyers, influencing practicality and usability for different driving needs.

## Key properties or components
- Measured in miles or kilometres
- Affected by battery size, driving style, and external conditions
- Key differentiator between EV models
