## Definition
A complex emotion characterized by feelings of insecurity, fear, and concern over a relative lack of possessions or safety. It often involves envy and suspicion.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Jealousy erodes personal happiness, fosters resentment towards others, and can damage relationships, leading to a negative outlook on life.

## Key properties or components
- Social emotion
- Insecurity-driven
- Can be destructive to relationships
