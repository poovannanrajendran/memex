## Definition
A Tamil slang term that translates to 'next level', 'superb', or 'outstanding'. It is used to describe something of exceptionally high quality or impressive.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It's used in the title to signify the high quality and wide appeal of the songs featured, implying they are 'next level' hits.

## Key properties or components
- Informal
- Positive
- Expressive of high quality

None.
