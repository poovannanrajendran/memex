## Definition
The practice of dynamically selecting and using different AI models based on the specific requirements of a task, aiming to balance performance, cost, and efficiency.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Strategic model switching allows developers to utilise the most cost-effective model for each task, significantly reducing overall AI expenses without sacrificing quality or effectiveness.

## Key properties or components
- Task-specific optimisation
- Cost-performance trade-offs
- Model capabilities assessment
- Dynamic selection
