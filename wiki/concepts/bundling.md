## Definition
Bundling is a sales strategy where multiple products are offered together as a single package, often at a discounted price compared to purchasing them individually.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Effective for increasing average order value and selling more units, particularly for products that are consumables or naturally go together.

## Key properties or components
- Combines multiple products
- Offers a package deal
- Increases Average Order Value (AOV)
- Best for consumables or related items

## Contradictions or debates
None.
