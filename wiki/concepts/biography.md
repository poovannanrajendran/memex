## Definition
A biography is an account of another person's life written by somebody else. It typically covers the subject's life story, including significant events, achievements, and personal experiences.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding the "unknown side" of KV Anand involves exploring biographical details that go beyond his professional achievements, offering a more complete picture of the individual.

- Life story
- Personal experiences
- Achievements
- Public vs. Private persona
