## Definition
The welfare or well-being of the community at large. In policy-making, regulations are often intended to serve the public good.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Musk states he objects to regulations that do not serve the public good, framing his dissent as an obligation to uphold societal well-being.

## Key properties or components
- Community welfare
- Societal benefit
- Basis for regulation
- Can be subject to interpretation
