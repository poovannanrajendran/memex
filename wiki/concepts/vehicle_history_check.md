## Definition
A report that provides comprehensive information about a used vehicle's past. It typically includes details on previous owners, accident history, mileage records, and any outstanding finance or legal issues.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for buyers of used cars to avoid purchasing vehicles with hidden problems, assess their true value, and prevent potential financial loss or legal complications.

## Key properties or components
- Covers accident history
- Mileage verification
- Theft status
- Finance checks
- Ownership changes
