## Definition
An approach that prioritises objective mathematical outcomes over subjective emotional states. It suggests that focusing on the measurable impact of actions is more productive than being swayed by feelings.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept underpins the video's motivational aspect, encouraging viewers to trust the process and the numbers rather than succumbing to discouragement or complacency.

## Key properties or components
- Objectivity
- Data-driven
- Emotional detachment (from results)
- Focus on process

None.
