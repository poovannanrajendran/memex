## Definition
In-stock deals refer to vehicles that are readily available for immediate or very quick delivery, as opposed to factory orders which can involve long waiting times. These are often part of promotional offers or available due to existing dealership inventory.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Appeals to consumers who need a vehicle urgently or wish to take advantage of limited-time offers, potentially securing a vehicle sooner than through custom orders.

## Key properties or components
- Quick availability
- Limited selection (specific models/trims)
- Often promotional pricing
- Reduced waiting times

## Contradictions or debates
The selection of vehicles and specifications may be restricted, and popular in-stock deals can be quickly depleted.
