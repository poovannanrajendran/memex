## Definition
Refers to the unique operational characteristics and challenges faced by small businesses. Unlike larger organisations, there is less scope for individuals to remain unnoticed or for problems to remain hidden.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlights why certain leadership styles, like direct communication, are particularly effective and necessary in small business environments.

## Key properties or components
- Limited scope for hiding issues
- Direct team interaction
- High visibility of actions

## Contradictions or debates
None.
