## Definition
A period of time off work granted to an employee for rest, recreation, or personal reasons. It is a standard employee benefit designed to prevent burnout and promote well-being.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A character explicitly states they are going on vacation leave, highlighting a common aspect of employment and work-life balance.

- Employee Benefit
- Rest and Recreation
