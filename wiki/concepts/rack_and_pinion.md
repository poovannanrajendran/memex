## Definition
A mechanical system composed of a toothed wheel (pinion) that meshes with a linear gear bar (rack). It converts rotational motion into linear motion, or vice versa.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A fundamental mechanism for linear actuation and motion conversion, widely used in steering systems and other mechanical devices.

## Key properties or components
- Rotational to linear motion conversion
- Pinion gear
- Rack bar
- Steering systems
