## Definition
Side income refers to earnings generated from sources other than one's primary employment. It often involves leveraging personal skills, hobbies, or investments to create additional revenue streams.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Pursuing side income can provide financial flexibility, reduce reliance on a single job, and allow individuals to explore entrepreneurial interests.

## Key properties or components
- Supplemental earnings
- Often pursued part-time
- Can be from various sources
- May require additional skills or time investment

## Contradictions or debates
Depending on the nature of the side income, it can lead to burnout if not managed effectively alongside primary responsibilities.
