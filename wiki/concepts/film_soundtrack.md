## Definition
A film soundtrack is the recorded music accompanying a film. It includes the score (instrumental music composed specifically for the film) and songs that are featured within the movie.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Soundtracks significantly contribute to the mood, emotion, and overall impact of a film, enhancing the audience's viewing experience.

## Key properties or components
- Original compositions
- Licensed songs
- Score
- Sound design
