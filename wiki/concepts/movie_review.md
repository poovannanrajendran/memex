## Definition
A movie review is an evaluation of a film. It typically includes a summary of the plot, an analysis of the acting, directing, writing, and technical aspects, and a recommendation or critique.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Helps potential viewers decide whether to watch a film and provides insights into the film's quality and themes.

## Key properties or components
- Summary
- Analysis
- Critique
- Recommendation
