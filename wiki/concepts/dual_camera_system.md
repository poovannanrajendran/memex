## Definition
A dual camera system consists of two camera lenses on a single device, typically offering wider field of view, enhanced zoom, or depth sensing capabilities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Improves the photographic versatility and quality of the iPhone 16, offering multiple focal lengths and advanced features.

## Key properties or components
- Multiple lenses
- High resolution sensors (e.g., 48MP)
- Autofocus
- 2x crop mode
- Macro capabilities
