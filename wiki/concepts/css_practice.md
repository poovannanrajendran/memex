## Definition
The process of actively applying and reinforcing Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) skills through hands-on coding exercises and real-world projects, rather than solely focusing on theoretical learning.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for developing proficiency and mastery in CSS, moving beyond basic understanding to advanced application.

## Key properties or components
- Hands-on coding
- Repetitive application
- Problem-solving
- Skill reinforcement
