## Definition
Blood donation is a voluntary process where a person gives blood to be transfused to another person. It is a critical medical procedure that can save lives.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The inability to donate blood, as depicted in the video, highlights a severe consequence of alcoholism, underscoring the health risks and incapacitation associated with the disease.

## Key properties or components
- Voluntary process
- Life-saving medical procedure
- Requires donor health
