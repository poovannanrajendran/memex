## Definition
The use of artificial intelligence technologies, particularly generative AI and LLMs, to assist in or fully create various forms of content, including text, images, and video.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a paradigm shift in content production, offering faster, potentially cheaper, and more scalable solutions. This video showcases how AI is making sophisticated video content creation more accessible.

## Key properties or components
- Utilizes AI models (LLMs, generative AI)
- Automates creative processes
- Scalable content production
- Potential for cost reduction
