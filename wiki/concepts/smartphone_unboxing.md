## Definition
Smartphone unboxing is the process of opening a new smartphone's packaging for the first time, often recorded on video. It typically showcases the contents of the box, the initial look and feel of the device, and sometimes a brief first impression.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides potential buyers with a visual and tactile introduction to the product, highlighting what is included and the initial presentation.

## Key properties or components
- Packaging reveal
- In-box contents
- Initial device appearance
- First impressions
