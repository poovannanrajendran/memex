## Definition
A concept and system represented by a portable ".agent" folder that centralises and standardises an AI's memory, custom skills, and communication protocols across multiple AI coding tools and environments.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is crucial for creating a more cohesive and efficient AI-assisted development workflow by addressing the fragmentation of AI tool memory. It enables a single 'brain' to power various AI 'harnesses', improving consistency and knowledge retention.

## Key properties or components
- Portable ".agent" folder
- Standardises memory
- Incorporates custom skills
- Defines protocols
- Plugs into various AI tools
