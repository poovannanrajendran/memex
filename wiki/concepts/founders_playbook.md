## Definition
A conceptual guide or set of strategies and best practices specifically for founders navigating the unique challenges of building and scaling a startup.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The idea of a 'Founder's Playbook' is proposed as a valuable resource, distinct from other business playbooks, underscoring the unique skillset and journey of founders.

## Key properties or components
- Founder-centric advice
- Startup strategy
- Growth tactics
- Resilience building
