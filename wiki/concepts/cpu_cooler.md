## Definition
A CPU cooler is a device designed to dissipate heat generated by a computer's central processing unit (CPU). Effective cooling is crucial for maintaining processor performance and longevity.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential component for managing thermal output of CPUs, directly impacting system stability and performance, relevant to building and optimising computer systems.

## Key properties or components
- Heat dissipation
- Thermal conductivity
- Fan speed
- TDP rating
