## Definition
The capital available to a business for its day-to-day trading operations. It is calculated as current assets minus current liabilities and represents the liquidity available to meet short-term obligations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source highlights that an Amazon Marketplace business can be started with very little working capital, making it an attractive option for those with limited initial funds.

## Key properties or components
- Liquidity
- Short-term financial health
- Operational efficiency
