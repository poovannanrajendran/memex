## Definition
A rule or directive made and maintained by an authority. In the context of business, regulations are rules that govern how an industry or company operates.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Central to the discussion, as Elon Musk is defending his compliance with various regulations across his companies.

## Key properties or components
- Mandatory
- Extensive
- Enforced by authorities
- Can impact product sales and operations
- Can be challenged if not serving public good
