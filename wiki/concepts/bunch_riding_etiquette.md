## Definition
The unwritten rules and social norms that govern safe and respectful behaviour when cycling in a group or peloton.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for the safety and enjoyment of all participants in group rides, preventing accidents caused by unpredictable actions or lack of awareness within the pack.

## Key properties or components
- Rider progression
- Awareness of surroundings
- Predictable movements
- Communication
- Respect for others
