## Definition
AI-powered systems designed to understand and respond to spoken language, enabling conversational interactions. They can be used for various applications, including customer service and information retrieval.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The focus of the Retail AI update, with the Agent Handbook aiming to make them '10 times smarter' and more trustworthy.

## Key properties or components
- Speech recognition
- Natural language understanding
- Speech synthesis
- Conversational AI
