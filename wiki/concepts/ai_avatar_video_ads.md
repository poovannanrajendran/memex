## Definition
Video advertisements created using artificial intelligence where a digital avatar, rather than a real person, is featured as the presenter.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Allows for highly scalable, cost-effective, and consistent content creation for marketing purposes, especially when combined with efficient AI tools.

## Key properties or components
- Digitally generated presenter
- AI-driven lip-sync
- Potential for rapid production
- Lower cost compared to traditional video production
