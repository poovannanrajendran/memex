## Definition
The idea that the pursuit and attainment of desires can paradoxically lead to dissatisfaction, while the absence of desire can preserve a sense of anticipation and fulfillment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept explains why not getting what you want can sometimes be better than getting it, according to the source.

## Key properties or components
- Anticipation
- Loss of excitement upon attainment
- Value in unattained goals

## Contradictions or debates
This notion can be debated, as some philosophies advocate for the pursuit of goals and the satisfaction of desires as essential for growth and happiness.
