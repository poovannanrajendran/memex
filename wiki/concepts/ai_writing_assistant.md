## Definition
A software tool that leverages artificial intelligence, particularly natural language processing, to help users generate, edit, and improve written content. These assistants can range from grammar checkers to full content generators.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a growing application of AI in productivity, offering efficiency gains for content creators and professionals across various fields.

## Key properties or components
- Content generation
- Text tailoring
- Instantaneous output
- Versatility in application (essays, blogs, ads, etc.)
