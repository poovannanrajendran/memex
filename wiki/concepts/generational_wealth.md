## Definition
Assets passed down from one generation of a family to the next. This can include money, property, or investments, and it often comes with expectations or responsibilities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The skit humorously touches upon the expectations associated with generational wealth, particularly a parent's feelings about living off their child's earnings.

## Key properties or components
- Inherited assets
- Intergenerational transfer
- Family expectations
- Financial legacy
