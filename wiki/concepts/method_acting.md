## Definition
An acting technique where performers seek to 'become' the character they are portraying, drawing on their own experiences and emotions to create a realistic performance. This can sometimes involve extreme or immersive preparation methods.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides context for why an actor like Tom Holland might consider such an intense preparation method, even if the specific instance described was initially a joke.

## Key properties or components
- Emotional recall
- Character immersion
- Psychological identification
- Immersive preparation
