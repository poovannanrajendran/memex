## Definition
The quality of acting or speaking without premeditation or rehearsal. It often involves reacting in real-time to a situation or prompt.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
RJ Balaji's ability to speak spontaneously on Jallikattu when the topic was abruptly changed led to the viral success of his speech.

## Key properties or components
- Unplanned
- Impromptu
- Real-time reaction
- Authenticity
