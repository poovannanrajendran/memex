## Definition
The process of synchronising a character's lip movements with spoken dialogue. AI lip-syncing tools can generate this automatically from audio and a character model.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A rapidly improving area in AI video, with tools like Runway and Hendra offering new ways to create realistic talking avatars and characters.

## Key properties or components
- Audio-to-Visual Synthesis
- Facial Animation
- Avatar Animation
