## Definition
Widgets are small, focused applications or visual elements that can be added to a desktop interface, providing quick access to information or controls without needing to open the full application.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Widgets enhance MacBook productivity by offering at-a-glance information and controls for frequently used functions like clocks, calendars, and music.

## Key properties or components
- Customisable
- Information at a glance
- Control of applications
