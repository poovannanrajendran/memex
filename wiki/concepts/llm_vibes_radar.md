## Definition
A proposed startup idea that visualizes the results of queries to Large Language Models (LLMs). It aims to present information in a way similar to Google Trends, serving entertainment, review, and AI bias detection purposes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Demonstrates a novel application of LLMs beyond simple question-answering, focusing on visualization and potential bias analysis relevant to marketing and data analysis.

## Key properties or components
- Visualisation of LLM responses
- Query types: 'best dosa in Bangalore', 'top Delhi startups'
- Functionality: Entertainment, wire cutter-like reviews, AI bias detection
