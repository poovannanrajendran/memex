## Definition
A set of paradoxical but effective business strategies. 'Deliver early' suggests meeting or exceeding expectations on timelines. 'Learn fast' emphasizes rapid iteration and knowledge acquisition. 'Trust slow' advises caution in forming opinions or commitments. 'Bet big' advocates for significant investment after 'test small' validates an idea.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
These principles guide decision-making, risk management, and operational efficiency, aiming to maximise successful outcomes in business ventures.

## Key properties or components
- Strategic sequencing
- Risk mitigation
- Iterative approach
- Performance optimisation
