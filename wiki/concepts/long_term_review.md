## Definition
A review of a product or service conducted after an extended period of use, rather than immediately after purchase. This allows for a more comprehensive assessment of durability, performance over time, and long-term user satisfaction.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a more realistic and trustworthy assessment of a product's value and reliability beyond initial impressions, crucial for tech products like smartphones.

## Key properties or components
- Extended usage period
- Focus on durability
- Real-world performance
- Long-term satisfaction

## Contradictions or debates
None.
