## Definition
The process of designing and creating digital forms used to collect specific information from users. This typically involves defining fields, data types, and validation rules.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for building data-driven applications in Power Pages, allowing users to collect data like names and investment amounts, which is then stored in tables.

## Key properties or components
- Field creation
- Data type selection
- Required fields
- Form submission actions
