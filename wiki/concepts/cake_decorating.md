## Definition
The art of applying frosting, icing, fondant, and other edible or non-edible decorations to a cake to enhance its appearance. It can range from simple designs to elaborate artistic creations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for presentation and aesthetic appeal, transforming a basic cake into a centrepiece for celebrations and events. It adds value and perceived quality to baked goods.

## Key properties or components
- Use of various mediums (frosting, fondant, ganache, fruit, edible paints).
- Application of diverse techniques (piping, sculpting, airbrushing, stencilling).
- Focus on visual design and theme.
- Can be a skill that enhances baking businesses.
