## Definition
Refers to the quality and quantity of information a person consumes, with the goal of making it as healthy and beneficial as possible by filtering out noise and irrelevant content.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video emphasizes the importance of a clean information diet in the age of AI and presents 'Zero Noise' as a tool to achieve this.

## Key properties or components
- Content filtering
- Relevance assessment
- Mindful consumption
