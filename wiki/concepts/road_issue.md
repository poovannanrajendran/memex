## Definition
A 'road issue' refers to any problem or deficiency concerning the condition, usage, or management of roads. This can encompass potholes, traffic congestion, lack of proper signage, or inadequate construction.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding road issues is crucial for urban planning, public safety, and economic activity. It's a common concern for citizens and a key area for government intervention.

## Key properties or components
- Potholes
- Traffic management
- Road maintenance
- Infrastructure quality
