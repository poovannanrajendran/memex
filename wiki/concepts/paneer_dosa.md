## Definition
A popular South Indian dish where a thin, crepe-like pancake (dosa) is filled with a spiced mixture, in this case, paneer (Indian cottage cheese). This variation includes cheese for added flavour and texture.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A well-known and loved dish in Indian street food and home cooking, adaptable with various fillings.

## Key properties or components
- Crispy or soft dosa base
- Spiced paneer filling
- Melted cheese
- Street food style
