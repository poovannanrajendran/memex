## Definition
A visual workflow management tool used to help visualise work, limit work-in-progress, and maximise efficiency. Tasks are represented by cards that move through columns representing stages of a workflow.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Offers a clear, visual overview of project status and task progression, facilitating better understanding and coordination within teams.

## Key properties or components
- Visualisation
- Workflow stages
- Task cards
- Work-in-progress limits
- Status tracking

## Contradictions or debates
None.
