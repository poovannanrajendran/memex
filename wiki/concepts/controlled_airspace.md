## Definition
Controlled airspace is a volume of airspace of defined dimensions within which Air Traffic Control (ATC) services are provided. Access to and operation within this airspace is subject to specific regulations and ATC clearance.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding and navigating controlled airspace is crucial for drone pilots to ensure flight safety and regulatory compliance, preventing potential conflicts with manned aircraft.

## Key properties or components
- ATC services provided
- Regulatory oversight
- Flight clearances required
- Defined dimensions
