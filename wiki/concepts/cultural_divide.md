## Definition
A divergence in beliefs, values, customs, and practices between different cultural groups or regions. This can lead to misunderstandings, friction, and distinct social identities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This was the unexpected topic introduced for the show RJ Balaji was participating in, highlighting societal differences that can be a point of discussion and contention.

## Key properties or components
- Regional differences
- Value system variations
- Customary practices
- Social identity
