## Definition
The manner and approach of providing direction, implementing plans, and motivating people. It encompasses a leader's personality, behaviour, and the way they interact with their team.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlights Steve Jobs's autocratic and demanding approach to leadership, contrasting with more collaborative styles.

## Key properties or components
- Autocratic
- Demanding
- Visionary
- Uncompromising
- Results-oriented

## Contradictions or debates
While presented as 'not a nice person,' his methods were undeniably successful in driving innovation and business growth.
