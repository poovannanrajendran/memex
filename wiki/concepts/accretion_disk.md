## Definition
A structure formed by diffuse material in orbital motion around a massive central body. The central body will not have a solid surface; instead, it will be a vacuum, its surface being the event horizon. Material from an external source is falling toward the central body for inclusion in the body.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Explains the brightness of the black hole by detailing how infalling matter generates energy.

## Key properties or components
- Swirling matter
- Orbital motion
- Emits energy
- Visible as light
