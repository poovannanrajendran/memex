## Definition
A category of tasks where the user fully understands both the desired outcome (the goal) and the specific steps required to achieve it manually, using AI primarily for execution.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding this task type allows for tailored prompting, focusing on efficient execution rather than complex problem-solving guidance from the AI.

## Key properties or components
- Known outcome
- Defined process
- AI for execution
- Efficiency focus

## Contradictions or debates
None.
