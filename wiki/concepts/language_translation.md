## Definition
Language translation is the process of converting text or speech from one language into another. Modern tools leverage AI for increasingly accurate and nuanced translations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for travelers to overcome communication barriers, understand signs, menus, and engage in conversations with locals.

## Key properties or components
- Real-time Translation
- Text-to-Speech
- Speech-to-Text
- Image Translation
- Offline Translation
