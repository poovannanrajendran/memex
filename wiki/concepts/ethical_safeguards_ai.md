## Definition
Ethical safeguards in AI refer to the built-in restrictions and guidelines designed to prevent AI models from generating harmful, biased, or inappropriate content and from being used for malicious purposes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video touches upon the potential for users to bypass these safeguards using specific prompting tactics, highlighting a tension between AI capabilities and responsible use.

## Key properties or components
- Content Filtering
- Bias Mitigation
- Safety Protocols
- Potential for Circumvention
