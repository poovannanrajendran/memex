## Definition
Different iterations or configurations of an AI model, such as GPT-4o and GPT-1, each with distinct capabilities, performance characteristics, and use cases.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Selecting the appropriate model version is key to achieving optimal results, with some models being better suited for complex reasoning (GPT-1) and others for speed and efficiency (GPT-4o).
