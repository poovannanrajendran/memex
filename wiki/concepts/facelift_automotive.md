## Definition
A mid-cycle refresh of a vehicle model, typically involving minor updates to styling, features, and technology, rather than a complete redesign.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Contextualizes that the 2024 models being reviewed are updated versions, not entirely new generations.

## Key properties or components
- Updates exterior and interior elements
- Introduces new technology or features
- Extends the model's lifespan
