## Definition
Persistent memory, in the context of AI, refers to the ability of a system to retain information and learnings over extended periods, even beyond the duration of a single interaction or session.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for developing AI assistants that can learn from user interactions, remember past workflows, and provide more consistent and personalised support.

## Key properties or components
- Information retention across sessions
- Learning from past interactions
- Enhanced user support consistency
