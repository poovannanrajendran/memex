## Definition
Business research is a systematic process of acquiring information on all the areas of business and using that information to identify business issues and opportunities. It encompasses market research but can also include internal operational analysis, financial studies, and strategic planning.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides the foundational knowledge needed for strategic decision-making, risk management, and identifying avenues for growth and improvement within a business context.

## Key properties or components
- Systematic Inquiry
- Information Acquisition
- Problem Identification
- Opportunity Assessment
- Decision Support
