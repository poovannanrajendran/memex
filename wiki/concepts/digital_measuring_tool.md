## Definition
A measuring device that uses electronic components to determine length, angles, or other dimensions, often displaying the results digitally. These tools typically offer greater precision and additional features compared to traditional tools.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Demonstrates the evolution of everyday tools through technology, enhancing precision and ease of use for tasks requiring measurement and design.

## Key properties or components
- Digital display
- Angle calculation
- Line drawing capability
- High precision
