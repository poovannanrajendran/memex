## Definition
A pre-designed layout and style for a website that can be customized by users. Templates provide a starting structure, saving time and effort in the design process.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The initial building block for creating a website quickly and efficiently, as demonstrated in Step 1.

## Key properties or components
- Pre-designed layout
- Customizable elements
- Time-saving
- Structural foundation
