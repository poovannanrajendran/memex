## Definition
Learning activities that require active user input and provide immediate feedback, allowing for hands-on practice and reinforcement of concepts.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Presented as an effective method for learning the fundamentals of a programming language like Java.

## Key properties or components
- Immediate Feedback
- Hands-on Practice
- Self-paced Learning
