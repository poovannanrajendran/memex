## Definition
The current condition and capacity of an electric vehicle's battery pack relative to its original manufactured state, affecting its range and overall performance.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial factor when buying a used EV, as battery degradation directly impacts the car's usability and residual value.

## Key properties or components
- Measured as a percentage of original capacity
- Affected by charging habits, climate, and age
- Key indicator for long-term ownership costs
