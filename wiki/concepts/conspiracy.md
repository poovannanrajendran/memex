## Definition
A conspiracy is a secret plan by a group to do something unlawful or harmful. In a broader sense, it can refer to any secret, often suspicious, plan or plot.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is used by the woman and her mother to describe the man's actions, indicating suspicion and a belief that his acquisition of her details was a pre-meditated, potentially manipulative, act.

## Key properties or components
- Secrecy
- Planning
- Malicious intent (implied)
- Suspicion
