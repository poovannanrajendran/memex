## Definition
The act of devising and carrying out a plan or idea in an unplanned, on-the-spot manner, often in response to a situation. It emphasizes adaptability and quick thinking.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlights the creative and agile nature of the content creators, suggesting their ability to generate engaging material without extensive pre-planning.

## Key properties or components
- Improvisation
- Adaptability
- Quick thinking
- Unplanned creativity
