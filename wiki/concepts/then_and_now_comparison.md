## Definition
A 'then and now' comparison is a visual or narrative format that shows how something or someone has changed over a period of time. It is often used for celebrities, historical sites, or product evolution.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This format is the primary structure of the YouTube short, showcasing cast members from 'The Mummy' franchise from their earlier appearances to their current state.

## Key properties or components
- Time progression
- Visual contrast
- Evolutionary depiction
