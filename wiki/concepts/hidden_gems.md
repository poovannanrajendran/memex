## Definition
Undiscovered or lesser-known places, attractions, or experiences that offer unique value or interest to travellers. These are often off the beaten path and provide a more authentic or memorable experience.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances the travel experience by offering unique discoveries beyond typical tourist spots, making trips more personal and memorable.

## Key properties or components
- Uniqueness
- Discovery
- Authenticity
- Local flavour
