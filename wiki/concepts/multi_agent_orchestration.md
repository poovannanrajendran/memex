## Definition
The process of coordinating and managing the interactions between multiple autonomous AI agents to achieve a common goal or complete a complex task.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for building sophisticated AI systems that can tackle problems requiring diverse capabilities and collaborative intelligence, as facilitated by the Agent Framework's workflows.

## Key properties or components
- Coordination
- Communication
- Task delegation
- Goal alignment
