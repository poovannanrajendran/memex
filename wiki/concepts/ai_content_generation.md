## Definition
AI content generation refers to the use of artificial intelligence technologies to create written or other forms of content automatically.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Helps e-commerce businesses overcome creative blocks, generate marketing copy, product descriptions, and brand messaging efficiently, allowing for faster testing of different angles and content strategies.

## Key properties or components
- Automated content creation
- Utilizes machine learning
- Can generate text, images, and more
- Optimized for specific outputs (e.g., e-commerce)
