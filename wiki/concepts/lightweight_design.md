## Definition
Refers to a product that has been engineered to be as light as possible without significantly compromising its functionality or durability.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for wearable tech like microphones, enhancing user comfort and reducing perceived bulk during use.

## Key properties or components
- Reduced mass
- Portability
- User comfort
- Ergonomics

## Contradictions or debates
None.
