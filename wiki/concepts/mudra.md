## Definition
A mudra is a symbolic or ritualistic gesture or pose in Hinduism and Buddhism. In the context of this video, it refers to specific hand gestures used in Siddha medicine.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video claims that performing a specific mudra can lead to regaining youthfulness and has positive effects on the human body.

## Key properties or components
- Hand gestures
- Symbolic
- Ritualistic
- Health benefits

## Contradictions or debates
The efficacy of mudras for significant rejuvenation is not scientifically proven in mainstream Western medicine.
