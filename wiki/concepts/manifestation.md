## Definition
Manifestation is the concept of bringing something into your physical reality (or your life) through thought, belief, and focused energy. It is the belief that positive or negative thoughts can lead to a tangible outcome.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Central to Jim Carrey's personal story of achieving success, as described in the source.

## Key properties or components
- Requires focused thought
- Relies on belief
- Involves visualization
- Aims to bring ideas into reality

None.
