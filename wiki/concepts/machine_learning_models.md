## Definition
Machine learning models are computational algorithms trained on data to identify patterns, make predictions, or perform tasks without being explicitly programmed. They are the core components for building AI applications.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
AI Kosh hosts various machine learning models, making them available for exploration and potential use within India's AI initiatives.

- Training Data
- Predictions
- Pattern Recognition
