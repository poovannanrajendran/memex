## Definition
Context compression is the process of reducing the amount of information needed to represent the essential details of a given context. This is often achieved using AI techniques to summarise or extract the most relevant data points.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enables AI systems to handle and recall larger amounts of information within their processing limits, thereby enhancing their ability to maintain context over longer interactions and reducing resource consumption.

## Key properties or components
- Information reduction
- AI-powered summarisation
- Relevance extraction
