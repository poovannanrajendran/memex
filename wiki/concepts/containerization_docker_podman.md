## Definition
The process of packaging software applications, along with their dependencies, into isolated units called containers. This ensures consistency across different environments, simplifying deployment and management.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A standard method for packaging and deploying applications, made easier with AI assistance.

## Key properties or components
- Isolation
- Portability
- Consistency
- Dependency management
- Scalability
