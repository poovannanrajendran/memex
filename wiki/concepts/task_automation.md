## Definition
The use of technology to perform tasks with minimal human intervention. In the context of AI, it involves intelligent systems handling complex or repetitive processes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A core function of the AI tools discussed, enabling significant time savings and allowing users to focus on more strategic work.

## Key properties or components
- Reduces manual effort
- Increases efficiency
- Minimizes errors
- Frees up human resources
