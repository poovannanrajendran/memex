## Definition
A person who is asked to consider an idea or proposal and argue against it, for the sake of considering the opposing viewpoint. This role helps to identify potential weaknesses or flaws in the original idea.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The 'flip' prompt directly invokes this role within Claude, encouraging a more rigorous and balanced evaluation of concepts by presenting counterarguments.

## Key properties or components
- Argues against a proposal
- Seeks weaknesses
- Promotes thoroughness
- Challenges consensus
