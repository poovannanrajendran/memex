## Definition
Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) is the systematic process of increasing the percentage of website visitors who take a desired action, such as making a purchase, signing up for a newsletter, or filling out a form.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Directly impacts the profitability of an e-commerce store by ensuring that more of the traffic generated is turned into paying customers or leads, maximizing the return on marketing investments.

## Key properties or components
- Focuses on user behaviour
- Employs A/B testing
- Utilizes analytics data
- Aims to improve user experience
- Increases ROI from traffic
