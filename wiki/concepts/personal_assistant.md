## Definition
A human assistant who helps with administrative, organizational, and personal tasks for an individual, typically a business executive or high-profile person.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Serves as a baseline for comparison for the AI tools discussed, highlighting the cost and functional differences.

## Key properties or components
- Handles scheduling
- Manages communication
- Performs administrative tasks
- Requires salary and benefits
