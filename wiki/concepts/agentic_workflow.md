## Definition
A system where AI agents autonomously perform a sequence of tasks to achieve a goal, often involving planning, execution, and adaptation based on feedback.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The 'Zero Noise' application demonstrates an agentic workflow powered by GPT-4o mini, showcasing how these workflows can be made highly affordable.

## Key properties or components
- Autonomy
- Task sequencing
- Goal-oriented
- Feedback loops
