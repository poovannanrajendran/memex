## Definition
A Tamil term that translates to 'divine play' or 'miraculous deeds.' It refers to the supernatural acts and divine interventions performed by gods or saints, often depicted in Hindu mythology and religious narratives.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It frames the events in the video as miraculous and divinely orchestrated, enhancing the spiritual significance of the procession to Srirangam.

## Key properties or components
- Divine intervention
- Miraculous events
- Mythological narratives
- Supernatural acts
