## Definition
Pre-designed website structures and layouts that users can select as a starting point for their own websites. They typically include placeholder content and design elements.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Templates in Power Pages significantly speed up the website creation process by providing a foundation, making it easier for users to achieve a professional look and feel quickly.

## Key properties or components
- Pre-defined layouts
- Placeholder content
- Design consistency
- Customisable elements
