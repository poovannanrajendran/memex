## Definition
This concept posits that significant personal or professional challenges are often a necessary precursor to achieving notable success. It highlights the internal battles and efforts that are not visible to the outside world.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Central theme of the video, suggesting that understanding and embracing struggles is key to appreciating and achieving success.

## Key properties or components
- Hidden effort
- Resilience building
- Overcoming adversity
- Internal fortitude
