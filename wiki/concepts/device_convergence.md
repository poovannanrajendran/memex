## Definition
The trend where different types of electronic devices (smartphones, tablets, laptops) become capable of performing similar tasks, leading to overlap in functionality and the potential for one device to replace others.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Central to the creator's decision-making process, as they evaluate whether the iPad Pro can consolidate the roles of their existing laptop and tablet.

## Key properties or components
- Increased performance in mobile devices
- Advanced software capabilities on tablets
- Development of versatile accessories
- Potential for a simplified tech setup
