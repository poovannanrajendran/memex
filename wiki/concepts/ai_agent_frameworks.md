## Definition
Structured environments or toolkits that provide the necessary building blocks and methodologies for developing, deploying, and managing AI agents efficiently and scalably.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for businesses to implement agentic AI, as they streamline the creation of autonomous programmes by offering predefined architectures, communication protocols, and integration tools.

## Key properties or components
- Predefined Architecture
- Communication Protocols
- Task Management
- Integration Tools
- Monitoring Tools
