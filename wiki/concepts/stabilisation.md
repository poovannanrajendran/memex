## Definition
Technology used in cameras to counteract shake and vibration, producing smooth footage even when the camera is moving or handheld.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Key for creating professional-looking action footage, especially when moving at speed or without a gimbal.

## Key properties or components
- In-camera stabilisation
- Gimbal-like smoothness
- High stabilization modes
