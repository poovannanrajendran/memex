## Definition
These are specialized sets of instructions or modules designed to enhance the capabilities of Large Language Models (LLMs) like Claude. They enable the LLM to perform specific, complex tasks, such as generating detailed video production prompts.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
These skills allow users to leverage Claude's power for sophisticated content creation, specifically in generating production-ready video prompts, automating a complex part of the creative workflow.

## Key properties or components
- Generate full production-ready video prompts
- Cover various styles (brand ads, product shots, cinematic, anime)
- Consider production elements (camera control, lighting, consistency)
- Integrate with LLMs like Claude Opus 4.6
