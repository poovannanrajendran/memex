## Definition
These are platforms or websites that compile and list a wide variety of AI tools, often categorising them by function or industry. They serve as a central hub for users to discover new AI applications and stay updated on the latest developments.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Helps users navigate the rapidly expanding landscape of AI tools, making it easier to find relevant solutions for their specific needs.

## Key properties or components
- Curated lists
- Categorisation
- Regular updates
- Search functionality
