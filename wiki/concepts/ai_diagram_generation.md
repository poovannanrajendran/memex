## Definition
The process of using artificial intelligence algorithms to automatically create diagrams, such as flowcharts or ERDs, based on textual input or existing data. This automates a often manual and time-consuming design task.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Significantly enhances productivity for developers and engineers by quickly generating complex visual representations from requirements, reducing manual effort and potential errors.

## Key properties or components
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Code generation
- Automated layout
- Iterative refinement
