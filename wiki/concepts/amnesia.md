## Definition
A medical condition characterized by the loss of memory. It can result from injury, illness, or psychological trauma, affecting a person's ability to recall past events or form new memories.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A central plot element in one of the recommended films ('Sleeping Dogs'), driving the protagonist's journey and the unfolding mystery.

## Key properties or components
- Memory loss
- Inability to recall past
- Potential for new memory formation issues
- Can be psychological or physical
