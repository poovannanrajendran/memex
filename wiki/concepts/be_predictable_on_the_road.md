## Definition
Acting in a manner that is consistent and easily understood by other road users, avoiding sudden movements or unexpected actions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A core principle of road safety that enhances predictability for motorists and other cyclists, thereby reducing the likelihood of accidents caused by misinterpretation or surprise.

## Key properties or components
- Consistent speed
- Clear signalling
- Smooth manoeuvres
- Awareness of traffic flow
