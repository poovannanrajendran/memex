## Definition
The practices, policies, and technologies implemented to protect sensitive information from unauthorised access, use, disclosure, modification, or destruction, and to ensure compliance with relevant regulations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Paramount for AI agent frameworks, especially when handling sensitive business data, as it ensures trust, prevents breaches, and maintains compliance through features like encryption and access controls.

## Key properties or components
- Encryption (at rest and in transit)
- Access controls
- Sensitive data handling
- Regulatory compliance
