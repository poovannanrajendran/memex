## Definition
Gross Written Premium (GWP) is the total amount of premium charged by an insurer for insurance policies issued during a specific period, before any deductions for reinsurance or commissions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key growth metric for his clients at [[lloyds_of_london]]. It reflects the scale and market share of the syndicates he manages.
