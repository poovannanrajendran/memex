## Definition
A consumption tax placed on a product or service by the government. In the context of vehicle leasing, it affects the final price, particularly for commercial vehicles.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Noted specifically for the VW ID Buzz Cargo, clarifying that the quoted price includes VAT and how it differs for VAT-registered businesses.

## Key properties or components
- Consumption tax
- Government levy
- Applies to goods and services
- Different rates exist
