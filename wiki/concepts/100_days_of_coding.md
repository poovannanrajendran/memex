## Definition
A self-directed challenge or course that encourages individuals to code for at least 100 consecutive days, typically for a set duration each day.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Aids in developing a consistent habit of coding, which is crucial for skill improvement and retention.

## Key properties or components
- Daily coding practice
- Habit formation
- Consistency
- Can be slightly outdated
