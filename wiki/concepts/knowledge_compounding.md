## Definition
The exponential growth of understanding and insight that occurs when newly acquired knowledge is systematically integrated with, and builds upon, existing interconnected knowledge, rather than being stored in isolation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is a desired outcome of the memex system, addressing a significant limitation of traditional tools that fail to allow knowledge to naturally 'compound' and generate new value.

## Key properties or components
- exponential growth of understanding
- systematic integration of new knowledge
- interconnectedness of information
- generation of new insights
