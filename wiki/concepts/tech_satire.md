## Definition
A genre of commentary that uses humour, irony, exaggeration, or ridicule to expose and criticize foolishness and corruption in technology and its associated industries or users.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a critical and often humorous lens through which to view the rapid advancements and potential impacts of AI, questioning the hype surrounding new technologies.

## Key properties or components
- Humour
- Irony
- Exaggeration
- Criticism
