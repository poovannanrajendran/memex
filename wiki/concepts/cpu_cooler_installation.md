## Definition
The process of physically attaching a heat dissipating device (cooler) to a computer's central processing unit (CPU). This involves securing mounting hardware to the motherboard and ensuring proper contact with the CPU surface using thermal paste.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for maintaining optimal CPU temperatures, preventing thermal throttling, and ensuring the longevity and performance of the processor. Proper installation is key to effective heat dissipation.

## Key properties or components
- Mounting brackets
- Screws
- Thermal paste
- Fan attachment
