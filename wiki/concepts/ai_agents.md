## Definition
Autonomous computer programmes capable of devising plans, utilising function calling to interact with external tools (APIs, data sources, web searches), and learning from feedback to achieve specific goals.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
They form the core components of agentic AI systems, enabling businesses to automate complex processes, make intelligent decisions, and interact with various digital environments without constant human oversight.

## Key properties or components
- Autonomous
- Devise plans
- Use function calling
- Learn from feedback
