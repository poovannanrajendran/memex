## Definition
Refers to a product engineered to be as small and lightweight as possible without significantly compromising its functionality or performance.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances portability, ease of use, and aesthetic appeal, particularly important for mobile devices and accessories used by creators.

## Key properties or components
- Small dimensions
- Low weight
- Ergonomics
- Portability

## Contradictions or debates
None.
