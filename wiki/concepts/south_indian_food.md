## Definition
South Indian food refers to the cuisine originating from the southern states of India, characterised by its diverse use of rice, lentils, coconut, spices, and vegetables.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It provides the culinary context for the Mutton Urulai dish featured in the video.

## Key properties or components
- Use of rice and lentils
- Emphasis on spices like mustard seeds, curry leaves, tamarind
- Variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes
- Use of coconut
