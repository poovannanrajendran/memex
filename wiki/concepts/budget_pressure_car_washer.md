## Definition
A budget pressure car washer refers to a high-pressure cleaning device specifically marketed and priced to be affordable for consumers, particularly for the task of washing cars. The emphasis is on providing essential functionality at a lower cost point.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the video, suggesting that advanced cleaning tools are becoming accessible to a wider market in India, catering to cost-conscious consumers.

## Key properties or components
- Lower price point
- Focus on core functionality
- Suitability for car cleaning
- Accessibility for Indian market

## Contradictions or debates
The term 'budget' can be subjective, and a 'budget' model might compromise on durability, power, or features compared to premium models, potentially leading to a shorter lifespan or less effective cleaning for very stubborn dirt.
