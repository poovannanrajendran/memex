## Definition
VTSAX is a total stock market index mutual fund offered by Vanguard. It aims to track the performance of the entire U.S. stock market, providing broad diversification.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Collins explicitly recommends VTSAX as a superior investment vehicle for wealth accumulation compared to owning a primary residence.

## Key properties or components
- Low expense ratio
- Broad diversification
- Long-term growth potential
- Index fund
