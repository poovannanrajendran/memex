## Definition
Sunset is the daily disappearance of the Sun below the horizon in the west. It is often associated with beautiful visual phenomena and is a popular time for photography and relaxation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The vlog captures the experience of watching and photographing the sunset at the beach.

## Key properties or components
- Daily event
- Horizon phenomenon
- Visual beauty
- Photography opportunity
