## Definition
In the context of AI, hallucinations refer to instances where a model generates false or nonsensical information that is presented as factual, often due to gaps in its training data or misinterpretations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video directly addresses reducing AI hallucinations as a key benefit of specific prompting techniques.

## Key properties or components
- Fabricated Information
- Confidence in Falsehoods
- Lack of Verifiability
- Can be reduced by citing sources
