## Definition
An approach to web design that makes web pages render well on a variety of devices and window or screen sizes. Elements adjust fluidly to the viewing environment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Ensured the landing page functions correctly and looks good across all devices, a critical aspect of modern web development.

## Key properties or components
- Fluid grids
- Flexible images
- Media queries

## Contradictions or debates
None.
