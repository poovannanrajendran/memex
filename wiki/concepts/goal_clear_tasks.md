## Definition
A category of tasks where the user has a clear understanding of the desired outcome but lacks clarity on the specific steps or process to achieve it, requiring AI assistance for guidance and problem-solving.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Recognizing this task type enables prompts that encourage exploration, brainstorming, and strategy generation, leveraging AI as a problem-solving partner.

## Key properties or components
- Known outcome
- Undefined process
- AI for guidance
- Exploration focus
