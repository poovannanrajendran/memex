## Definition
Content aggregation involves collecting and organising content from various sources into a single platform or format. This can include news articles, videos, social media posts, and other digital media.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It allows for efficient access to diverse information and entertainment. For creators and distributors like UIE, it helps in managing and promoting content across multiple channels and languages.

## Key properties or components
- Collection of diverse sources
- Organised presentation
- Centralised access
- Often involves curation
- Can be platform-specific or cross-platform

## Contradictions or debates
Can sometimes lead to copyright issues if not managed properly, or a perceived lack of originality.
