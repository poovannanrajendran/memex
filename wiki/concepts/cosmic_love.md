## Definition
A form of love that is perceived as vast, boundless, and possibly divinely connected, extending beyond earthly limitations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key theme incorporated into the English lyrics of "Aasa Kooda" to convey deep emotional connection.

## Key properties or components
- Boundless
- Vast
- Transcendental
- Profound connection
