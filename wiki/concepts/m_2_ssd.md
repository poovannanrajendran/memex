## Definition
A small form factor expansion card and connector standard used for solid-state drives (SSDs) and other devices. M.2 SSDs offer high performance and are commonly used in modern laptops and desktops for storage.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The support for M.2 SSDs is presented as a key advantage of the Ooid 8 series, offering high-speed storage capabilities.

## Key properties or components
- Small form factor
- High read/write speeds
- NVMe or SATA interface
