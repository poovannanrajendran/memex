## Definition
A one-time charge associated with the creation of a mould, which is a custom tool used in manufacturing to shape materials. This fee is often incurred when a new product design requires a unique mould.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a significant upfront investment in product development and customisation, directly influencing the feasibility and cost of design changes.

## Key properties or components
- Upfront cost
- Design-specific
- Tooling investment
- Shape-dependent
