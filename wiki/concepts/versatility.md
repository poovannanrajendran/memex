## Definition
The capability of a device or accessory to be used for many different purposes or in many different ways. For an iPad, versatility is increased through accessories that allow it to function as a laptop replacement, drawing tablet, media center, or portable workstation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A primary goal for iPad users seeking to justify the device's cost and utility, enabling it to adapt to various tasks and environments.

## Key properties or components
- Adaptability
- Multi-functionality
- Broad application range
- Flexibility
