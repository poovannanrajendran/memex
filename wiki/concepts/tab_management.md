## Definition
Refers to the methods and features within a web browser that allow users to organise, view, and interact with multiple open web pages (tabs). Advanced tab management can include features like smart sorting, grouping, and intuitive closing mechanisms.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlighted as a key feature of Arc Search, demonstrating its advanced approach to handling multiple browsing sessions efficiently.

## Key properties or components
- Swipe to close
- Smart sorting
- iOS multitasking integration
