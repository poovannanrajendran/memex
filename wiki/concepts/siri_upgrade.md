## Definition
Enhancements to Apple's virtual assistant, Siri, leveraging AI to improve its understanding of natural language, context, and ability to perform complex tasks directly.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Siri has been a perceived weak point in iOS, and its integration with Apple Intelligence promises a significant improvement in conversational ability and task execution.

## Key properties or components
- Improved contextual understanding
- Reduced reliance on web searches
- Direct task execution
