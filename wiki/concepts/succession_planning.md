## Definition
Succession planning is a process for identifying and developing potential future leaders or key employees within an organisation to fill critical positions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The lack of growth opportunities for Akshata is attributed to strong succession planning, illustrating its impact on individual career progression.

## Key properties or components
- Talent Management
- Leadership Development
- Career Pathing
- Employee Retention
