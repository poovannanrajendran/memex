## Definition
An AI-powered tool in the Photos app that allows users to remove unwanted objects or people from the background of an image, filling the space with generated content.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A highly practical feature for photo editing, offering a powerful and convenient way to clean up images.

## Key properties or components
- Generative fill
- Object detection
- User-guided selection
- Works offline
