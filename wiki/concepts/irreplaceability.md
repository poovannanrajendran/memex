## Definition
Irreplaceability refers to the state of being unique or essential in a role, making it difficult or impossible for someone else or a system to take over one's responsibilities. It is achieved through developing specialized skills, consistent high-quality output, and unique contributions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The concept is central to professional success and job security, as highlighted in the source, suggesting that focusing on becoming irreplaceable is key to career advancement and avoiding displacement by automation.

## Key properties or components
- High-quality work
- Unique skills
- Strategic planning
- Undercommitting and overdelivering
