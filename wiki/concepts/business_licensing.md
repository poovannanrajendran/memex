## Definition
A business license is a permit to operate a business within a specific jurisdiction. It ensures that the business complies with local, state, and federal regulations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for legal operation and avoiding penalties, particularly in industries like food service which often have strict health and safety regulations.

## Key properties or components
- Legal compliance
- Regulatory adherence
- Operational permission
- Industry-specific requirements
