## Definition
An electronic book that can be read on a computer, tablet, or dedicated e-reader device. It is a digital version of a printed book.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The DEAR BIKE e-book is central to the promotion, as purchasing it grants customers entry into the giveaway.

## Key properties or components
- Digital format
- Accessible on electronic devices
- Portable
- Environmentally friendly (no paper)

## Contradictions or debates
None.
