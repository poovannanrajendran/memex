## Definition
These are artificial intelligence models designed to understand and generate human language, but with a significantly smaller parameter count and computational requirement compared to Large Language Models (LLMs).

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a more efficient and accessible approach to AI language processing, enabling wider adoption and diverse applications.

## Key properties or components
- Lower computational requirements
- Reduced power consumption
- Trained on curated synthetic data
- High performance for specific tasks
