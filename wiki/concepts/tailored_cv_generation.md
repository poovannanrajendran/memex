## Definition
The process of creating a curriculum vitae (CV) or resume that is specifically customised for a particular job application. This involves highlighting the most relevant skills and experiences for that specific role.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Increases the chances of a job application being successful by demonstrating a strong fit between the candidate and the job requirements.

## Key properties or components
- Keyword optimisation
- Skill matching
- Experience highlighting
- Role-specific content
