## Definition
A system where an AI acts autonomously to perform a series of tasks towards a goal. In this context, it means the AI handles the entire video production process from start to finish based on user instructions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a significant advancement in AI automation, moving beyond single-task generation to complex, multi-step workflows like video production.

## Key properties or components
- Autonomous operation
- Goal-oriented task execution
- Multi-stage process handling (research, scripting, editing, etc.)
