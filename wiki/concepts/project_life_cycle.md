## Definition
The sequence of phases that a project goes through from its initiation to its closure. Key phases typically include planning, execution, monitoring, and closure.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video illustrates how AI can be applied across multiple phases of the project life cycle, not just the coding phase, to maximise benefits.

## Key properties or components
- Initiation
- Planning
- Execution
- Monitoring & Control
- Closure
