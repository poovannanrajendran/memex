## Definition
A technique that enhances the capabilities of large language models by augmenting their knowledge with external data retrieved from a specific corpus or knowledge base.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Allows AI agents to access and utilize up-to-date or domain-specific information, improving the accuracy and relevance of their responses.

## Key properties or components
- Information retrieval
- Knowledge base integration
- Contextual grounding
- Query processing
