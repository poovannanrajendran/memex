## Definition
The act of achieving a performance level that surpasses all previous known achievements within a specific context, such as a game, sport, or competition.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Setting a record is a key plot point, showcasing the exceptional ability of the character El Guapo.

## Key properties or components
- Benchmark performance
- Surpassing limits
- Public recognition
