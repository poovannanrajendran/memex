## Definition
The development of a new individual from a female gamete without fertilization by a male gamete. In a religious context, it refers to the miraculous conception of Jesus Christ.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Used colloquially in the source to describe the outcome of parthenogenesis in humans, emphasizing the absence of a father.

## Key properties or components
- Absence of male fertilization
- Development from a single parent
- Can be natural (parthenogenesis) or supernatural/miraculous

## Contradictions or debates
None.
