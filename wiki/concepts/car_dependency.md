## Definition
The extent to which modern societies rely on personal vehicles for transportation, impacting urban planning, economy, and individual lifestyles.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source argues that car dependency is far more pervasive and economically critical than often acknowledged by anti-car advocates.

## Key properties or components
- Economic engine (70% of economy)
- Supply chain facilitator
- Versatile for various journey types
- Reliable in diverse conditions
