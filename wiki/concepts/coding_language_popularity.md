## Definition
The relative adoption and usage of different programming languages within the developer community, often indicated by surveys, job postings, and repository activity.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Choosing popular languages can lead to more extensive training data for AI models, resulting in better code generation, and a larger pool of resources and community support.

## Key properties or components
- Based on usage statistics
- Influences AI training data
- Impacts available examples and libraries
- Affects community support
