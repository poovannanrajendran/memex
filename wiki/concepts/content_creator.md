## Definition
A content creator is an individual who produces digital content, such as videos, blog posts, social media updates, or podcasts, typically for online platforms.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video is about the process and humour involved in content creation, specifically for platforms like YouTube and Instagram Reels.

## Key properties or components
- Digital content production
- Online platform focus
- Audience engagement
