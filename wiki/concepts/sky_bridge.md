## Definition
A sky bridge is a type of walkway or bridge that is elevated, often connecting two high points or offering panoramic views. The Langkawi Sky Bridge is a notable tourist attraction.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Identified as a popular attraction in Langkawi, offering unique scenic views and a distinctive tourist experience.

## Key properties or components
- Elevated walkway
- Scenic views
- Tourist attraction
