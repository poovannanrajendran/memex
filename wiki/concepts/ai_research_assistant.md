## Definition
An AI research assistant is a software tool that leverages artificial intelligence to help users gather, analyse, and synthesise information from various sources. It automates research tasks and provides insights to support decision-making.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is relevant as NotebookLM is positioned as a primary example of such a tool, directly impacting business productivity by streamlining research efforts.

## Key properties or components
- Information processing
- Real-time operation
- Data synthesis
- Automated research
