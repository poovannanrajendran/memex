## Definition
A form of marketing where a brand pays an influencer or creator to promote their product or service to their audience. This is often disclosed to viewers.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video discusses the extent to which "Tech Boss" relies on paid promotions, contrasting it with "Adada Creators'" approach and alleging it's a primary driver for their content strategy.

## Key properties or components
- Sponsored Content
- Influencer Marketing
- Advertising
- Monetisation
- Brand Partnerships

## Contradictions or debates
While paid promotions are a legitimate business practice, the video suggests an over-reliance on them by "Tech Boss" at the expense of organic content, potentially misleading viewers about genuine endorsements.
