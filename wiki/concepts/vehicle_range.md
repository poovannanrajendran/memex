## Definition
Vehicle range refers to the maximum distance a vehicle can travel on a single charge (for electric vehicles) or a single tank of fuel (for conventional vehicles). For EVs, it is a critical factor for consumer adoption and practicality.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key performance metric for electric vehicles like the Kia EV9, influencing consumer purchasing decisions and perceived usability.

## Key properties or components
- Distance on a full charge/tank
- Affected by battery size/fuel capacity
- Influenced by driving style and conditions
- A major consideration for EV buyers
