## Definition
A mechanical system designed to cool, dehumidify, and often filter the air within an enclosed space, providing thermal comfort. It typically comprises components like a compressor, condenser, evaporator, and a circulating refrigerant.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
While primarily related to comfort rather than direct safety, its prolonged malfunction was an early and persistent indicator of the building's unfinished state and the landlord's neglect of essential amenities.

## Key properties or components
- Compressor
- Refrigerant
- Duct system
- Cool air
- Ventilation
