## Definition
A prompting technique that encourages the AI to generate intermediate reasoning steps before providing a final answer. This mimics human thought processes and is often used for complex reasoning tasks.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Presented as a more effective technique than simple role prompting for certain tasks, but still potentially unnecessary for simpler queries.

## Key properties or components
- Intermediate reasoning steps
- Step-by-step generation
- Complex task suitability

None.
