## Definition
In animation and video editing, keyframes define the starting and ending points of a transition or animation. AI tools use these to interpolate the motion or changes between them.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for tools like Luma Labs' Dream Machine and Krea, allowing users to define specific start and end states for video generation and transitions.

## Key properties or components
- Start Frame
- End Frame
- Interpolation
- Transition Control
