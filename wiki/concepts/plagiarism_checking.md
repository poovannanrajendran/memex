## Definition
The process of verifying that content is original and has not been copied from existing sources without proper attribution. This is particularly relevant when using AI-generated text for academic or professional purposes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Ensures academic integrity and ethical use of AI by preventing the submission of unoriginal work, as AI models can inadvertently reproduce training data.

## Key properties or components
- Originality verification
- Source comparison
- Academic integrity
- Ethical AI use
