## Definition
Edge AI refers to the implementation of artificial intelligence algorithms directly on edge devices, such as IoT devices, smartphones, or single-board computers, rather than relying on cloud-based processing.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The Nvidia Jetson Orin Nano SUPER is marketed as an Edge AI development kit, showcasing the trend towards localised AI processing for lower latency, increased privacy, and reduced bandwidth requirements.

## Key properties or components
- On-device processing
- Low latency
- Reduced bandwidth usage
- Enhanced privacy
