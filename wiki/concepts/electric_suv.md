## Definition
An electric SUV (Sport Utility Vehicle) is a vehicle that combines the features of an SUV, such as higher ground clearance and the option of four-wheel drive, with an all-electric powertrain. These vehicles typically offer increased space and practicality compared to traditional sedans.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a growing segment of the automotive market, focusing on sustainability and advanced technology, which the Kia EV9 aims to capture.

## Key properties or components
- All-electric powertrain
- SUV body style
- Increased cargo and passenger space
- Often feature advanced technology
