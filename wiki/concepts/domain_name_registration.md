## Definition
The process of acquiring the right to use a specific internet address (domain name) for a set period, typically on an annual basis. This involves registering the name with a domain registrar accredited by ICANN.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for establishing an online presence; understanding the nuances of registration, renewal, and associated services is crucial for cost-effective website management.

## Key properties or components
- Annual rental, not purchase
- Requires a registrar
- Includes renewal fees
- Can have associated services (privacy, SSL)

## Contradictions or debates
None.
