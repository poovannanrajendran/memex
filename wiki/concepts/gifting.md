## Definition
Gifting is the act of giving something to someone without expecting anything in return, often as a gesture of kindness, appreciation, or celebration. It can create positive social connections.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Although initiated by the creator's offer, the rug-making becomes a gift for the car owner, demonstrating a thoughtful gesture.

## Key properties or components
- Altruism
- Reciprocity (social)
- Gesture of Goodwill
- Relationship Building
