## Definition
The rivalry between different companies and entities developing and deploying artificial intelligence technologies. This competition often focuses on innovation, market share, and the development of superior AI models.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding the competitive landscape is crucial for assessing the impact of new AI tools like Perplexity AI on established giants like Google and ChatGPT.

## Key properties or components
- Innovation Race
- Market Disruption
- Talent Acquisition
- Technological Superiority
