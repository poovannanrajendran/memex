## Definition
A detailed, step-by-step strategy that clearly outlines the tasks required to achieve a specific goal. It focuses on execution and provides concrete steps that can be followed by an individual or team.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Emphasised in the video as crucial for successfully building a business, ensuring that efforts translate into tangible results.

## Key properties or components
- Detailed steps
- Focus on execution
- Clear objectives
- Measurable outcomes
