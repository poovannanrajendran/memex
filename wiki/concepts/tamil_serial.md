## Definition
A Tamil serial is a television drama series produced and broadcast in the Tamil language. These serials often focus on family relationships, social issues, and cultural themes, and are a very popular form of entertainment in Tamil Nadu and among Tamil-speaking communities worldwide.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
These are a core part of Sun TV's broadcast content and are heavily promoted via their YouTube channel and SUN NXT platform.

## Key properties or components
- Narrative-driven episodes
- Often long-running
- Focus on family and social themes
- Cultural relevance
