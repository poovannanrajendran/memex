## Definition
Sibling dynamics refer to the complex patterns of interaction, communication, and relationships between brothers and sisters. These dynamics can range from supportive and affectionate to rivalrous and conflict-ridden.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video explores a common aspect of sibling dynamics, where one sibling confides in another about personal issues and seeks support or advice.

## Key properties or components
- Communication patterns
- Emotional support
- Conflict resolution
- Confidante roles
