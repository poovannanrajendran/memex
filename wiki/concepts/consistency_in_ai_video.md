## Definition
The ability of an AI model to maintain stable and coherent characteristics of elements within a video, such as the appearance of characters, objects, and backgrounds across different scenes or frames.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key technical challenge and a differentiator for advanced AI video models like SeaArt 2.0, enabling more realistic and professional-looking outputs.

## Key properties or components
- Character consistency
- Object permanence
- Background stability
- Temporal coherence
