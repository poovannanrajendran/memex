## Definition
The mental or emotional strain experienced by an individual due to demands or expectations in their work environment. This can stem from deadlines, workload, or challenging tasks.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Central to the discussion about the realities of working in the IT industry, as highlighted by Mahesh.

## Key properties or components
- Demanding workload
- Tight deadlines
- Emotional strain
- Unpredictability
