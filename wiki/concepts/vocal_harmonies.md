## Definition
Vocal harmonies occur when two or more singers sing different notes simultaneously, creating a richer and fuller sound. This involves layering distinct melodic lines that complement each other.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source emphasizes Michael Jackson's skill in creating and performing complex vocal harmonies for "Beat It" on the demo, showcasing his exceptional musical ear and arranging abilities.

## Key properties or components
- Multiple independent melodic lines sung simultaneously
- Creates a richer texture and fuller sound
- Requires skilled vocalists and arrangers
