## Definition
The process by which individuals acquire understanding and information related to medical practices, procedures, and terminology. This can occur through formal education, practical experience, or simulated environments.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is relevant as it explores the blurred lines between simulated medical knowledge gained through acting and actual medical expertise, highlighting potential misconceptions.

## Key properties or components
- Formal training
- On-the-job experience
- Simulated learning
- Potential for misinformation
