## Definition
The difference between the information an AI model has been trained on or is provided in a prompt, and the complete information required to fully understand and execute a task accurately.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Identifying and minimizing information gaps, by asking the AI what it needs or having it identify contradictions, is key to improving prompt quality and AI performance.

## Key properties or components
- Missing information
- AI comprehension limits
- Prompt refinement
- Contradiction detection
