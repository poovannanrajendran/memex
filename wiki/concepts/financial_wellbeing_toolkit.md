## Definition
A financial wellbeing toolkit is a collection of resources, strategies, and tools aimed at improving an individual's overall financial health and resilience. It typically covers aspects like budgeting, saving, investing, debt management, and financial planning.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It provides a structured approach for individuals to gain control over their finances, enabling them to make informed decisions, achieve financial goals, and navigate economic uncertainties, such as the cost of a new vehicle.

## Key properties or components
- Comprehensive financial planning
- Resource collection
- Skill development
- Goal achievement support

## Contradictions or debates
Effectiveness relies heavily on the user's commitment and ability to implement the strategies provided.
