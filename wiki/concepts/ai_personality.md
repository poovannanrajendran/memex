## Definition
The distinct characteristics and behavioural traits exhibited by an AI system, designed to make interactions more engaging, relatable, or aligned with specific branding or user needs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
An explicit goal of the Retail AI Agent Handbook, aiming to improve the user experience by refining the AI's persona.

## Key properties or components
- Tone of voice
- Response style
- Empathy simulation
- Character consistency
