## Definition
These are external hardware devices or add-ons designed to enhance the functionality, usability, or aesthetic appeal of an Apple iPad. They can range from input devices like keyboards and styluses to protective cases, stands, and external storage solutions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for users looking to maximize their iPad's capabilities beyond its basic functions, transforming it into a more versatile tool for productivity, creativity, and entertainment.

## Key properties or components
- Enhance functionality
- Improve user experience
- Increase versatility
- Offer protection
- Aid productivity
- Support creativity
