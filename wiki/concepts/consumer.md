## Definition
In Kafka, a consumer is an application or service that subscribes to one or more Kafka topics and processes the events published to them.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Consumers are responsible for reacting to events and performing actions, such as updating databases, sending notifications, or triggering further processes.

## Key properties or components
- Subscribes to topics
- Reads events
- Can be part of a consumer group
