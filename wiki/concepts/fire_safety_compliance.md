## Definition
The adherence to local and national fire safety regulations, ensuring that buildings have functional alarms, sprinklers, and escape routes to protect occupants.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A critical operational risk identified in the [[building_inspection_violations]] case. It highlights the importance of due diligence in physical infrastructure, even for IT-focused projects.

## Key properties or components
- **Functional Alarms:** Smoke and heat detectors must be connected and monitored.
- **Sprinkler Pressure:** Main water lines must maintain adequate pressure.
- **Permits:** Fire system modifications require specific regulatory approval.
