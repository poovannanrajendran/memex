## Definition
A crime thriller is a genre of fiction and film that combines elements of crime fiction and suspenseful thrillers. It typically involves a protagonist pursuing a criminal or uncovering a criminal conspiracy, with a focus on suspense, tension, and often unexpected plot twists.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This genre is popular for its engaging narratives and suspenseful plots, often appealing to audiences seeking intellectually stimulating and exciting content.

## Key properties or components
- Mystery
- Suspense
- Investigation
- Plot twists
- Anticipation
