## Definition
Visualization is the practice of forming a mental image or scene of something desired. It is often used as a technique to achieve goals by mentally rehearsing success or imagining desired outcomes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Jim Carrey identifies himself as a 'huge visualizer,' indicating its importance in his personal success strategy.

## Key properties or components
- Mental imagery
- Focus on desired outcomes
- Can be practiced regularly
- Aims to influence reality
