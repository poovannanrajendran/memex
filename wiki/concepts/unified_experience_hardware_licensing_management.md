## Definition
A unified experience in IT infrastructure means that hardware, software licensing, and management tools are integrated and consistent, providing a seamless and simplified operational environment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Cisco is promoting this approach to make managing network infrastructure, particularly their new access points, more efficient and less complex for IT professionals.

## Key properties or components
- Integrated hardware and software
- Simplified licensing models
- Centralised management dashboards
