## Definition
Smartphone design encompasses the physical and aesthetic aspects of a mobile device, including its form factor, materials, button placement, screen bezels, and overall ergonomics. It aims to balance visual appeal with user comfort and functionality.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Design is a critical factor in consumer purchasing decisions, influencing brand perception and user interaction with the device. Changes in design, such as flatter rails, can signify industry trends or brand shifts.

## Key properties or components
- Form factor
- Materials
- Ergonomics
- Aesthetics
- Durability
