## Definition
The basic physical and organizational structures and facilities needed for the operation of a society or enterprise, such as transportation, water, and sanitation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The availability and cleanliness of public restrooms are noted as inadequate compared to developed countries, representing a drawback in public services.

- Infrastructure development
