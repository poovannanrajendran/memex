## Definition
The act of altering, falsifying, or suppressing evidence to mislead an investigation or legal proceeding. In this context, it refers to manipulating video footage, audio recordings, or other forms of proof.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A central claim is that Adada Creators tampered with evidence, such as CCTV footage and audio recordings, to support their false allegations against the Tech Boss team.

## Key properties or components
- Falsification
- Fabrication
- Suppression
- Misleading Proof
- Perjury (in legal contexts)

## Contradictions or debates
Adada Creators present their 'evidence' as factual, while the Tech Boss creators claim this evidence is fabricated and manipulated.
