## Definition
An Islamic place of worship, characterized by specific architectural elements like minarets and domes, and adhering to Islamic design principles.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The Hassan II Mosque is a major focus, discussed for its scale, location on the coast, and its status as one of the largest mosques globally and the largest in Africa.

## Key properties or components
- Minaret
- Prayer hall
- Oceanfront location
- Large capacity
- Architectural grandeur
