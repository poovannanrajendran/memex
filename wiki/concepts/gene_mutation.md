## Definition
A permanent alteration in the DNA sequence that makes up a gene, such that the sequence differs from what is found in most people. Mutations can be spontaneous or caused by external factors.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Mentioned as a possible biological mechanism that could contribute to or enable parthenogenesis.

## Key properties or components
- Alteration in DNA
- Can be spontaneous
- Can occur naturally
