## Definition
The style and method of designing and constructing religious buildings, particularly temples. Indian temple architecture is diverse, often featuring intricate carvings, monumental structures, and symbolic representations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The Dwarkadhish Temple exemplifies traditional Indian temple architecture, showcasing the craftsmanship and religious symbolism prevalent in such structures.

## Key properties or components
- Religious symbolism
- Intricate carvings
- Monumental scale
- Regional styles
