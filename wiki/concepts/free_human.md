## Definition
A 'free human' is conceptualised as an individual liberated from all forms of oppression, exploitation, and subservience. This freedom encompasses not only political and economic independence but also intellectual and social autonomy.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding the 'free human' is central to comprehending the goals of liberation movements and ideologies that strive for complete human emancipation and self-determination.

## Key properties or components
- Autonomy
- Independence
- Absence of Exploitation
- Self-Determination
- Intellectual Freedom
