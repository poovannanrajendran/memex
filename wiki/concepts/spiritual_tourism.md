## Definition
Spiritual tourism involves travelling to places of religious or spiritual significance, such as temples, monasteries, or pilgrimage sites, with the primary aim of experiencing a connection to the divine or seeking spiritual enlightenment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept frames Anuradha Goyal's travels and her exploration of ancient Indian temples, including the Ram Janmabhoomi Temple, as a form of purposeful journeying for spiritual growth.

## Key properties or components
- Travel to sacred sites
- Spiritual exploration
- Cultural immersion
- Personal reflection
