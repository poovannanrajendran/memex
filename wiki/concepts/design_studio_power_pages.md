## Definition
The visual editor interface within Microsoft Power Pages that allows users to design, build, and customise their websites using a what-you-see-is-what-you-get (WYSIWYG) approach.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is the primary tool for editing websites in Power Pages, offering an intuitive experience for modifying pages, adding components, and adjusting styling without deep technical knowledge.

## Key properties or components
- WYSIWYG editor
- Component-based editing
- Styling controls
- Page and section management
