## Definition
The act of evaluating and commenting on the quality or style of creative works, such as music or acting. This can range from constructive feedback to harsh judgment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video highlights instances where public criticism of artistic output (singing, acting) by fellow artists has generated controversy and discussion.

- Evaluation of creative work
- Subjective judgment
- Potential for controversy
- Impact on artist reputation
