## Definition
A document that outlines the purpose, features, functionality, and behaviour of a product to be developed. It serves as a guide for design and development teams.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for 'vibe coding' as it defines the goals and specifications of a project, ensuring alignment and reducing ambiguity.

## Key properties or components
- Clear Objectives
- Feature Descriptions
- User Stories
- Technical Specifications
- Success Metrics

## Contradictions or debates
None.
