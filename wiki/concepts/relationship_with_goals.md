## Definition
The personal connection and commitment an individual has with their goals, measured by their willingness to make sacrifices and persevere through challenges to achieve them.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Emphasizes that achieving significant goals requires a deep, personal investment and a willingness to prioritize them above other aspects of life.

## Key properties or components
- Willingness to sacrifice
- Perseverance
- Personal commitment
- Goal alignment

## Contradictions or debates
None.
