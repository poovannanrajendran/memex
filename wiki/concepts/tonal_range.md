## Definition
The range between the lightest and darkest parts of an image, representing the overall contrast and detail captured in highlights and shadows.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key parameter adjusted by photographic styles, influencing the dynamic range and mood of a photograph.

## Key properties or components
- Lightest parts of an image
- Darkest parts of an image
- Contrast adjustment
- Detail in highlights and shadows
