## Definition
Loyalty is a strong feeling of support or allegiance to a person, group, or cause. It involves faithfulness, steadfastness, and a commitment to uphold one's word and obligations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Central to the narrative, this concept is presented as the highest virtue, outweighing personal discomfort or desire for immediate gratification.

## Key properties or components
- faithfulness
- allegiance
- commitment
- steadfastness

## Contradictions or debates
None.
