## Definition
Condensed milk is cow's milk from which water has been removed by evaporation. It is often sweetened with sugar.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key ingredient in this specific crepe recipe, providing sweetness and body to the batter.

## Key properties or components
- Sweetened
- Thickened milk
- Used as a base ingredient
