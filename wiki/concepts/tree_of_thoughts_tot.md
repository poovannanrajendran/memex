## Definition
A prompting technique for LLMs that encourages them to explore multiple reasoning paths ('thoughts') systematically, evaluating each step before proceeding, thus simulating more complex problem-solving.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It's presented as one method to overcome the inherent 'System 1' limitations of LLMs by forcing a more deliberate, multi-perspective approach.

## Key properties or components
- Explores multiple reasoning paths
- Evaluates intermediate steps
- Simulates deliberation
