## Definition
The process of gathering and compiling information from various disparate sources into a single, unified location or format. In the context of a browser, this means collecting data from multiple websites to answer a specific query.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Central to Arc Search's 'browser for me' feature, showcasing its ability to provide comprehensive answers by consolidating web content.

## Key properties or components
- Pulls from multiple sources
- Creates custom websites
- Provides direct links
