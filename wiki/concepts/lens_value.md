## Definition
Lens value refers to the perceived worth of a lens in relation to its cost, considering its performance, features, build quality, and longevity.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding lens value helps photographers make informed purchasing decisions, balancing budget with desired photographic outcomes. The presenter specifically claims the 50mm f2 offers better value than more expensive alternatives.

## Key properties or components
- Price
- Optical performance
- Build quality
- Features
- Durability
- Resale value
