## Definition
Pongal is a traditional Tamil harvest festival celebrated primarily in Tamil Nadu, India, and by Tamil communities worldwide. It is a four-day festival dedicated to the sun god, Surya, and marks the end of the winter solstice.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It's a significant cultural and religious event for the Tamil diaspora, fostering community spirit and cultural identity abroad, as seen in its celebration in America.

## Key properties or components
- Harvest festival
- Celebrated in Tamil Nadu and diaspora
- Dedicated to Surya (Sun God)
- Involves cooking Pongal dish
- Four days of festivities
