## Definition
Frontend design refers to the visual and interactive aspects of a digital product that users directly engage with. This includes the layout, user interface (UI), and user experience (UX) of websites, applications, or other digital interfaces.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Effective frontend design is critical for user engagement and the success of digital products, influencing how users perceive and interact with an application or website.

## Key properties or components
- User Interface (UI)
- User Experience (UX)
- Visual Layout
- Interactivity
