## Definition
Programming code that allows for fine-grained control and extensive modification of a system's underlying functionalities, providing maximum flexibility at the cost of requiring significant technical expertise.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Offers unparalleled customisation and optimisation potential for highly specific or complex AI agent requirements, catering to skilled developers who need deep control over system behaviour.

## Key properties or components
- Fine-grained control
- Extensive modification
- Requires technical expertise
- High flexibility

None.
