## Definition
An interview preparation guide is a resource that provides potential questions, insights, and recommended answers based on the specific job role and company. It aims to help candidates prepare effectively for interviews.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The tool generates tailored interview prep guides for each application, offering a significant advantage in the interview stage.

## Key properties or components
- Role-specific questions
- Company-specific insights
- Suggested answers
- Confidence building
