## Definition
An annual automobile endurance race held near Le Mans, France, considered one of the most prestigious motorsport events in the world.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The primary target race for the Ford GT40, representing the pinnacle of endurance racing where Ford aimed to defeat Ferrari.

## Key properties or components
- Endurance
- 24-hour duration
- Teamwork
- Reliability
- Speed
