## Definition
AI plugins are add-on software components that extend the functionality of an artificial intelligence model. They allow the AI to interact with external tools, services, or data sources, thereby expanding its capabilities beyond its core programming.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Plugins are crucial for making AI models like Claude more versatile and powerful, enabling them to perform a wider range of tasks and access real-time information or specialised functions.

## Key properties or components
- Extend AI capabilities
- Enable integration with external tools
- Enhance task performance
- Provide specialised functions
