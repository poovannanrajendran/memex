## Definition
Leveraging artificial intelligence to produce supplementary video footage (B-roll) that can be used to enhance a primary video narrative. This AI-generated footage can consist of cinematic clips or relevant visuals.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a quick and easy way to source high-quality visual assets for videos, improving production value without extensive filming.

## Key properties or components
- Cinematic clips
- Automated footage creation
- Content enhancement
- Direct upload capability
