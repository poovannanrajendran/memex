## Definition
These are software applications and platforms that leverage artificial intelligence technologies to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as understanding language, recognising patterns, making decisions, and generating content.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
AI tools are crucial for enhancing efficiency, automating repetitive tasks, and unlocking new creative possibilities in various professional fields, including marketing, content creation, and general workplace productivity.

## Key properties or components
- Automation
- Data analysis
- Pattern recognition
- Natural Language Processing
- Machine Learning
- Content generation
