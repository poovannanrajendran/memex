## Definition
The reduction, limitation, or abolition of armed forces and weapons in a particular area or country. In Antarctica's context, it means the prohibition of any military measures.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is a core principle of the Antarctic Treaty System, ensuring the continent is free from conflict and militarisation, allowing for scientific pursuits.

## Key properties or components
- Prohibition of military bases
- Prohibition of military manoeuvres
- Ban on weapons testing
- Focus on peaceful activities

## Contradictions or debates
None.
