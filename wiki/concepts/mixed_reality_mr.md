## Definition
Mixed Reality (MR) blends the physical and digital worlds to produce new environments and visualizations where physical and digital objects co-exist and interact in real time. It is distinct from Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR).

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A feature supported by both headsets, with the Quest 3's improved capabilities in this area due to its optical stack and depth sensor.

## Key properties or components
- Integration of digital and physical
- Real-time interaction
- Pass-through cameras
- Spatial mapping
