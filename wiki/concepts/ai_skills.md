## Definition
AI skills are pre-packaged functionalities or expertise that can be added to an AI model, transforming it into a specialist for specific tasks. They act as shortcuts, enabling the AI to perform complex actions without extensive prompt engineering.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Skills enhance AI capabilities by providing specialized knowledge and automation, increasing productivity and efficiency in AI-assisted workflows.

## Key properties or components
- Installable
- Task-specific
- Reusable
- Enhance AI expertise
