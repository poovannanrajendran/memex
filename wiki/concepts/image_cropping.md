## Definition
Image cropping is the process of removing the outer sections of an image to improve framing, composition, or to remove unwanted areas. It effectively changes the aspect ratio and size of the image.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source mentions the photo was cropped to better highlight the bullet, demonstrating a post-capture editing technique to emphasize the key subject.

## Key properties or components
- Resizing
- Composition adjustment
- Aspect ratio modification
- Focus enhancement
