## Definition
Cottage rental refers to the temporary accommodation in a small house or dwelling, typically used for holidays or short stays. These rentals offer a more private and home-like experience compared to hotels.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A significant portion of the vlog is dedicated to showcasing a rented cottage and the associated lifestyle.

## Key properties or components
- Temporary stay
- Private dwelling
- Holiday accommodation
- Self-catering
