## Definition
The ability of a system to understand and interpret human language, specifically in this context, to parse natural language phrases indicating dates and times for scheduling.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances user experience by allowing for more intuitive input of deadlines and scheduling information, reducing the need for manual date selection.

## Key properties or components
- Date parsing
- Time interpretation
- Ambiguity resolution
- User-friendly input
