## Definition
A dance move that creates the illusion of the dancer being pulled backward while they are actually moving forward. It is a form of illusionary movement.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is a signature move for Kazuho Monster, who won a world championship in it, highlighting his mastery of dance illusion.

## Key properties or components
- Illusionary
- Backward motion
- Footwork intensive
- Popping/Animation element
