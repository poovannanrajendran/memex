## Definition
Wildlife photography is a genre of photography focused on capturing images of animals, birds, and other wildlife in their natural habitat. It requires patience, skill, and often specialized equipment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The concept is central to the video's title and hashtags, indicating the primary theme and visual style.

## Key properties or components
- Capturing animals in natural environments
- Requires patience and knowledge of animal behavior
- Often uses telephoto lenses
- Focus on ethical practices
