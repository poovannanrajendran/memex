## Definition
Docker is a platform for developing, shipping, and running applications in containers. Containers package software with everything it needs to run, ensuring consistency across different computing environments.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The central concept of the video, demonstrating its wide-ranging applications from simple utilities to complex development environments and security labs.

## Key properties or components
- Containerization
- Image-based deployment
- Isolation
- Portability
- Reproducibility
