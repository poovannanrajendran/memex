## Definition
A heart attack, or myocardial infarction, occurs when blood flow to a part of the heart muscle is blocked, causing tissue damage.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This medical event is the central crisis in the short, prompting immediate reactions and professional considerations.

## Key properties or components
- Sudden onset
- Requires immediate medical attention
- Can be triggered by stress
- Varying recovery times
