## Definition
A specific type of tracker that analyses the motion of a plane or surface within a video. It accounts for perspective changes and zoom, making it ideal for tracking flat areas or objects with consistent planar movement.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for accurately attaching elements to surfaces that move and change perspective in video, as demonstrated in the tutorial for seamless integration.

## Key properties or components
- Surface analysis
- Perspective tracking
- Corner pinning
- Motion and zoom compensation
