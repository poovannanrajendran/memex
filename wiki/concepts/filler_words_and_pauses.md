## Definition
Filler words (e.g., 'um', 'uh', 'like') and unintentional pauses are common in spoken language that can detract from the clarity and professionalism of a recorded video. Automated tools can identify and remove these elements.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Removing filler words and excessive pauses improves the conciseness and flow of video content, making it more engaging and professional for the viewer.

## Key properties or components
- Speech analysis
- Automated trimming
- Improved pacing
- Reduced video length
