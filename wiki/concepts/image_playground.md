## Definition
A tool within Apple Intelligence that generates cartoon-style images based on text descriptions, with options to incorporate recognisable people from photos.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a creative outlet for generating personalised cartoon images for various uses.

## Key properties or components
- Text-to-image generation
- Cartoon style
- Personalisation with photos
- On-device generation
