## Definition
An individual who performs at a superior level in a specific sport or physical activity, often requiring years of dedicated training, exceptional physical attributes, and strong mental fortitude.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Helps to frame the extreme physical and mental requirements of an F1 driver, positioning them as top-tier athletes.

## Key properties or components
- Exceptional physical conditioning (strength, stamina, endurance)
- Advanced cognitive abilities (reaction time, decision making)
- High level of skill and technique
- Mental resilience and focus
