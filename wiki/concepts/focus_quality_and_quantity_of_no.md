## Definition
The act of concentrating intensely on a specific goal or objective, defined by the ability to strategically decline other opportunities and distractions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Identified as a critical factor for entrepreneurial success, enabling deep work and the avoidance of 'agents in disguise' that can derail progress.

## Key properties or components
- Strategic refusal
- Prioritisation
- Elimination of distractions
- Deep work enablement
