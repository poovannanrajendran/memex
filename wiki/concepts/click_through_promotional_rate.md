## Definition
The click-through promotional rate (CTR) measures the percentage of people who receive an email or see an advertisement and click on a specific link within it. It is a key metric for evaluating the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A high CTR indicates that marketing content is engaging and relevant to the target audience, which is crucial for driving conversions and achieving business objectives like booking sales calls.

## Key properties or components
- Percentage calculation
- Measures engagement
- Key marketing metric
