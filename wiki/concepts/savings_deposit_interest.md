## Definition
Savings deposit interest is the amount of money a bank pays to a customer for holding their funds in a savings account. It is typically calculated as a percentage of the deposited amount over a specific period.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is directly mentioned in the transcript and is central to the financial discussion implied by the video title and context.

## Key properties or components
- Percentage rate
- Compounded over time
- Varies by bank and account type
- Paid to the depositor
