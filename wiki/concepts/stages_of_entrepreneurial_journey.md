## Definition
A progression through distinct phases entrepreneurs experience: Uninformed Optimism, Informed Pessimism, Valley of Despair, Informed Optimism, and Goal Achievement.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides context for the emotional and psychological challenges faced by entrepreneurs, normalising the difficulties and guiding them through to success.

## Key properties or components
- Optimism (informed/uninformed)
- Pessimism
- Despair
- Resilience
- Goal attainment
