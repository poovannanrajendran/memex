## Definition
AI models whose source code, architecture, and often trained weights are made publicly available, allowing others to use, modify, and distribute them freely.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
They foster community-driven development and innovation, enabling rapid improvements and wider accessibility, as seen with the LTX series of video models.

## Key properties or components
- Publicly available code and architecture
- Permits modification and redistribution
- Community-driven development potential
- Often free to use

## Contradictions or debates
None.
