## Definition
A research tool is any software or service designed to assist in the process of gathering, analysing, and synthesising information on a particular subject. These tools can range from simple search engines to sophisticated AI-powered platforms.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Key component of the AI agent, enabling it to perform deep dives into topics and provide comprehensive reports, as exemplified by Perplexity.

## Key properties or components
- Information gathering
- Data analysis
- Source citation
- Report generation
- AI-powered capabilities
