## Definition
Small business strategy involves the plans and actions undertaken by smaller enterprises to achieve their objectives, often focusing on agility, personalised customer interactions, and building strong relationships.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source highlights how a small business used exceptional customer service and flexibility to manage a high-value transaction, turning a potential loss into a gain and fostering customer loyalty.

## Key properties or components
- Agility
- Customer focus
- Relationship building
- Value proposition
