## Definition
A collection of beliefs, attitudes, and mental frames that influence how individuals approach challenges, opportunities, and goals. A 'growth mindset' focuses on learning and improvement, while a 'fixed mindset' believes abilities are innate.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for navigating the complexities of AI engineering and insurance innovation. A positive and growth-oriented mindset fosters persistence, problem-solving, and adaptability in the face of technical difficulties and market changes.

## Key properties or components
- Belief in potential
- Focus on learning
- Resilience to setbacks
- Proactive approach to challenges

## Contradictions or debates
Overly optimistic mindsets might lead to underestimating risks or challenges. It can also be difficult to cultivate and maintain consistently, especially under pressure.
