## Definition
A trigger is a cue that prompts a user to take a specific action. Triggers can be external (like notifications) or internal (like emotions or thoughts).

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is the first step in the Hook Model, initiating the user's engagement with a product or service.

## Key properties or components
- External cues
- Internal cues
- Prompt for action

## Contradictions or debates
None.
