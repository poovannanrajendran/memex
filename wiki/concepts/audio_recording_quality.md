## Definition
The fidelity and clarity of sound captured by a device's microphone(s). For smartphones, this includes factors like the number of microphones, their sensitivity, and software processing for noise reduction.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Identified as a significant improvement area for the iPhone 16 Pro Max, which now features four studio-grade microphones and better wind noise reduction.

## Key properties or components
- Number of Microphones
- Microphone Quality
- Wind Noise Reduction
- 360° Sound Recording
