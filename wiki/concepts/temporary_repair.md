## Definition
A short-term fix or patch applied to a system or equipment to restore partial functionality, often as an interim measure before a permanent solution can be implemented. It may not fully address the root cause of the problem.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlights potential deceit or negligence when presented as a permanent fix. In this context, the suspicious nature of the repair and refusal to explain raised red flags about the landlord's intentions and the building's true condition.

## Key properties or components
- Short-term solution
- May not fix root cause
- Often quick and superficial
- Can be misleading
