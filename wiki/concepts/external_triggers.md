## Definition
Events or signals from outside a system that initiate an action or process within that system. For Mindpal, this could mean starting a workflow based on an external event.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances the automation capabilities of tools by allowing them to react to real-world events or data changes.

## Key properties or components
- Event-driven
- System integration
- Initiation of processes
