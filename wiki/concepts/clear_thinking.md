## Definition
The process of defining a problem or goal precisely, understanding one's own knowledge and uncertainties, and planning a logical approach before communicating it.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Identified as a fundamental component of effective AI prompting, enabling the user to articulate their needs clearly to the AI.

## Key properties or components
- Problem definition
- Goal clarity
- Logical planning
- Self-awareness of knowledge
