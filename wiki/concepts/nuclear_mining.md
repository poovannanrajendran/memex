## Definition
Nuclear mining refers to the extraction of radioactive ores, often to obtain elements used in nuclear energy or other specialized applications. This process can carry significant risks related to radiation exposure and environmental contamination.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding nuclear mining is crucial for assessing potential environmental and health risks, which could have insurance implications related to liability and damages.

## Key properties or components
- Extraction of radioactive ores
- Potential for radiation exposure
- Environmental contamination risks
- Government regulation and approval
