## Definition
Time blocking is a time management method where you divide your day into specific blocks of time dedicated to particular tasks or activities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This technique is used by the speaker to schedule and commit to their daily coding sessions, ensuring dedicated focus time.

## Key properties or components
- Calendar scheduling
- Daily events
- Task allocation
