## Definition
A detailed plan for a journey, including scheduled activities, routes, and accommodations. It serves as a guide to ensure a smooth and organised travel experience.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Helps travellers stay organised, maximise their time, and ensure they visit desired locations and attractions.

## Key properties or components
- Schedule
- Route details
- Activity list
- Accommodation information
