## Definition
Ready-to-eat meals or drinks sold by vendors in streets and other public places. Street food is often a significant part of a region's culture and economy.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents accessible, often traditional, and flavourful culinary experiences that are integral to many cultures, including Indian cuisine.

## Key properties or components
- Mobile vendors
- Affordable
- Quick preparation
- Local flavours
