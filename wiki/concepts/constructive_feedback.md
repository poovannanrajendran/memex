## Definition
Feedback that is helpful, specific, and actionable, aimed at improvement rather than criticism. It focuses on identifying areas for development and suggesting ways to achieve better outcomes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for productive collaboration and problem-solving, whether with human colleagues or AI tools.

## Key properties or components
- Actionable
- Specific
- Goal-oriented
- Improvement-focused

## Contradictions or debates
None.
