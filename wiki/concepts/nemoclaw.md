## Definition
NemoClaw is an open-source security and privacy layer developed by NVIDIA. It is designed to be integrated with or installed on top of the OpenClaw framework to enhance the protection of AI agents and their data.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It addresses the growing need for robust security and privacy measures in AI systems, particularly as they become more integrated into critical applications.

## Key properties or components
- Open-source
- Security layer
- Privacy layer
- Built on OpenClaw
- Developed by NVIDIA
