## Definition
The intrinsic value and importance of an individual. It is the belief in one's own dignity and worthiness, independent of external achievements or the opinions of others.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for personal well-being, resilience, and the ability to set healthy boundaries. In Poovi's context, understanding self-worth can influence career progression, negotiation effectiveness, and overall confidence in pursuing AI engineering goals.

## Key properties or components
- Internal belief
- Resilience
- Boundary setting
- Independence from external validation

## Contradictions or debates
Some may confuse self-worth with arrogance or entitlement, leading to interpersonal conflicts. It can also be challenging to maintain during periods of failure or criticism.
