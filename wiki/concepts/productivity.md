## Definition
Productivity measures the efficiency of converting inputs into outputs. In a personal or organisational context, it represents the ability to achieve more tasks, goals, or valuable outcomes with the same or fewer resources, often through improved methods or tools.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The ultimate benefit of innovations like token optimisation and AI agents is often an enhancement of productivity, allowing individuals and organisations to accomplish more effectively and efficiently.

## Key properties or components
- Efficiency
- Output quality
- Resource utilisation
- Goal attainment
