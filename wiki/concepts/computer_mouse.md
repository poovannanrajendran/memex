## Definition
A pointing device that detects two-dimensional motion relative to a surface. Modern computer mice offer features like programmable buttons, scroll wheels, and gesture support.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A well-chosen mouse can significantly enhance productivity and user experience, especially for tasks requiring precision or extensive navigation, as demonstrated by the Logitech MX Master 3S.

## Key properties or components
- Connectivity (Bluetooth, USB)
- Buttons (Programmable)
- Scroll Wheel (MagSpeed)
- Gesture Control
- Customisation
