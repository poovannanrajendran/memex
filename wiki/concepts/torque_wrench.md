## Definition
A tool used to apply a specific, predetermined rotational force (torque) to a fastener. It ensures that fasteners are tightened to the correct specification, preventing over-tightening or under-tightening.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Used in the lug bolt removal process to apply the necessary force to loosen the extractor tool without damaging other components or exceeding the tool's capability.

## Key properties or components
- Measures and controls torque
- Often set to specific values (e.g., 110 foot-pounds)
- Prevents over-tightening
