## Definition
Free tools are software applications or services that can be used without any charge. They offer access to functionalities that might otherwise be behind a paywall.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The availability of a powerful AI image generation tool for free is a significant point, making advanced technology accessible to a wider audience.

## Key properties or components
- No cost
- Accessible
- Potentially limited features compared to paid versions
