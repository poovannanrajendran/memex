## Definition
A marketing framework developed by Donald Miller that helps businesses clarify their message by positioning the customer as the hero of the story and the brand as the guide. It follows a seven-step process to ensure clear communication.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This framework is important for Poovi's Second Brain as it provides a structured method for understanding and refining brand messaging, which is crucial for effective communication in any business context, including insurance and AI.

## Key properties or components
- Customer as the Hero
- Brand as the Guide
- Clear Call to Action
- Problem, Solution, Success, Avoidance Stakes
- SB7 Framework (Summarize)
- Seven Steps: Characters, Plot, Resolution, Theme, Stakes, Call to Action, Success
