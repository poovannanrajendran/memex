## Definition
A strategy where tasks are divided among different individuals or systems based on their unique capabilities, allowing for greater efficiency and expertise in each area.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlights how delegating tasks to AI (the 'boring stuff') and focusing human effort on strategic tasks (the 'boss stuff') leads to a more effective and fulfilling work environment.

## Key properties or components
- Division of labour
- Skill utilisation
- Efficiency
- Focus

## Contradictions or debates
None.
