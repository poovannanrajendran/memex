## Definition
The ability to remember and recall information that has been learned or experienced.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for learning and skill development; using multi-modal input (like reading and listening simultaneously) can improve how well information is stored and recalled.

## Key properties or components
- Memory recall
- Learning effectiveness
- Multi-modal learning
- Active engagement
