## Definition
Incumbents are established companies that hold a dominant position in their market or industry. They are often characterised by their size, legacy systems, and slower pace of innovation compared to newer entrants.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The concept of full-stack AI companies is presented as a strategy to directly challenge and outperform these established, slow-moving players.

## Key properties or components
- Dominant market position
- Often slow to adapt to new technologies
- Large and established operations
