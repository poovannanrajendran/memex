## Definition
A business model, often associated with video games, where players can earn real-world rewards, such as cryptocurrency or NFTs, through gameplay.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the game's monetization strategy, aiming to attract players by offering the prospect of financial gain alongside entertainment.

## Key properties or components
- In-game rewards
- Real-world value
- Player-driven economy
- Monetization strategy
