## Definition
Methods and accessories used to safeguard a laptop from physical damage, such as scratches, drops, and spills. This includes sleeves, cases, and protective films.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for maintaining the aesthetic and functional integrity of a laptop, especially when transported frequently, as discussed with the laptop sleeve.

## Key properties or components
- Scratch Resistance
- Padding
- Water Resistance
- Portability
