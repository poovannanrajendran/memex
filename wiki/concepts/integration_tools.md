## Definition
Utilities, connectors, and functionalities embedded within a framework that simplify the process of linking AI agents to external resources such as APIs, databases, or other software systems.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for expanding the operational reach and data access of AI agents, enabling them to leverage external services and information through function calling, thereby enhancing their overall utility.

## Key properties or components
- Simplifies connections
- Connects to external resources (APIs, databases)
- Enables function calling
- Extends agent capabilities

## Contradictions or debates
None.
