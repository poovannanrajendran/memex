## Definition
A technique that uses AI to apply the artistic style of one image or video to another. This allows for consistent visual aesthetics across generated content.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key feature in tools like LTX Studio, enabling users to apply custom styles, such as from Midjourney images, to entire generated scenes.

## Key properties or components
- Artistic Emulation
- Content Adaptation
- Visual Consistency
