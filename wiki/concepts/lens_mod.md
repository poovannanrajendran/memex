## Definition
An interchangeable accessory that attaches to a camera to alter its lens characteristics, such as focal length, field of view, or focusing capabilities. These mods can enable specialized shooting styles.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key differentiator for the GoPro Hero 13 Black, allowing for enhanced creative control and different visual aesthetics.

## Key properties or components
- Interchangeable
- Alters optical properties
- Enhances creative potential
- Automatic detection (in GoPro Hero 13)
