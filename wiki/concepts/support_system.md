## Definition
A support system refers to a network of people who provide emotional, practical, or financial assistance. It is crucial for navigating life's challenges.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source explicitly identifies good friends as the 'best support system in the world,' underscoring their vital role in personal resilience.

## Key properties or components
- Emotional support
- Practical assistance
- Reliability
- Trustworthiness
