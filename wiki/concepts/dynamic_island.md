## Definition
The Dynamic Island is a feature introduced by Apple that repurposes the notch on iPhones and potentially MacBooks into an interactive area that displays alerts, notifications, and ongoing activities in a fluid and integrated manner.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is relevant as it shows how hardware features (like the MacBook notch) can be given new life and functionality through software, enhancing user interaction and providing a more dynamic user experience.

## Key properties or components
- Interactive display area
- Integrates alerts and notifications
- Fluid animation
