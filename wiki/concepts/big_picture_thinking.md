## Definition
The ability to understand the overall situation, strategy, or goal, rather than focusing solely on small details. It involves foresight and a comprehensive perspective.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is identified as a crucial quality for top-level leaders like Adani and Ambani, enabling them to navigate complex challenges and set strategic direction.

## Key properties or components
- Strategic foresight
- Holistic view
- Long-term planning
