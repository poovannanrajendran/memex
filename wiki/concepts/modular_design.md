## Definition
A design approach where a system is composed of independent, interchangeable components or modules. This allows for flexibility, easier upgrades, and customisation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It highlights the flexibility and customisation potential of the Ooid 8 series, suggesting it can be tailored to specific user needs.

## Key properties or components
- Independent components
- Interchangeability
- Flexibility
- Customisation
