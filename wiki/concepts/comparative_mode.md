## Definition
A feature within an AI tool that allows for the direct comparison of two or more subjects or entities. This mode typically presents information side-by-side to highlight similarities, differences, and relative performance.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances analytical capabilities by facilitating direct comparisons, which can lead to quicker understanding and decision-making, as demonstrated with the Cursor vs. Windsurf example.

## Key properties or components
- Side-by-side presentation
- Parallel research
- Feature comparison
- Divergence analysis
