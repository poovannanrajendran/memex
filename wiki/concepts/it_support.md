## Definition
The service provided by an organisation to assist users with technical issues related to computer hardware, software, or networks. This can involve troubleshooting, maintenance, and guidance.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The dialogue includes a scenario where one person takes control of another's system via a Teams call to resolve an issue, indicative of IT support functions.

## Key properties or components
- Troubleshooting
- Remote Access
- Screen Sharing
- Problem Resolution
