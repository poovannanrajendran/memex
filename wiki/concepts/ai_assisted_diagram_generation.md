## Definition
The process of using artificial intelligence to create visual diagrams, such as flowcharts, mind maps, or mockups, based on textual descriptions or other inputs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Significantly accelerates the creation and editing of diagrams, making complex visualisations more accessible and efficient for users.

## Key properties or components
- Text-to-diagram conversion
- Editable output
- Automation of design elements
- Integration with existing tools
