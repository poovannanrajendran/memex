## Definition
The practice of offering multiple services together as a package deal, often at an attractive introductory price. In domain registration, this can include hosting, website builders, or privacy protection.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Can be a deceptive sales tactic, where the introductory price is low but the renewal costs for individual components, or the bundle as a whole, are significantly inflated.

## Key properties or components
- Package deal
- Attractive initial price
- Potentially high renewal costs
- Common with hosting/website builders

## Contradictions or debates
None.
