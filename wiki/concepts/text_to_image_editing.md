## Definition
A specific application of AI where textual descriptions are translated into visual modifications within an existing image.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This technology allows for intuitive and rapid image alteration, moving beyond traditional manual editing tools.

## Key properties or components
- User provides text instructions
- AI interprets and applies changes
- Can be used for adding, removing, or modifying image content
