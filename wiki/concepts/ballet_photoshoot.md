## Definition
A specialised type of photography focused on capturing dancers, particularly ballet dancers, in motion and in poses. It requires an understanding of ballet technique and aesthetics.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Demonstrates the creative and technical skills involved in capturing artistic movement and expression through photography.

## Key properties or components
- Focus on movement and form
- Understanding of ballet terminology and poses
- Specific lighting techniques
- Artistic composition
