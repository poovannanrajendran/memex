## Definition
The process of articulating and communicating a compelling future state or goal. It involves creating a clear picture of what an organization or individual aspires to achieve.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for motivating individuals and teams towards a common objective, especially when the objective is ambitious and requires significant effort.

## Key properties or components
- Inspirational
- Future-oriented
- Communicable
- Ambitious

## Contradictions or debates
None.
