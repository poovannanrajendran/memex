## Definition
Autonomous flight refers to the capability of a drone to fly and perform tasks without direct human intervention. This is achieved through pre-programmed flight paths, artificial intelligence, and onboard sensors.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Autonomous flight allows for precise and repeatable missions, such as aerial mapping and data collection, increasing efficiency and reducing human error in complex drone operations.

## Key properties or components
- Pre-programmed flight paths
- Automated decision-making
- Sensor integration
- Mission repeatability
