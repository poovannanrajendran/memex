## Definition
A cloud-based service model where a third-party provider manages the backend infrastructure for applications, including databases, user authentication, and cloud storage. Developers can focus on the front-end development.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Firebase Studio leverages Google's Firebase BaaS, which is a key reason for its full-stack capabilities and is contrasted with other AI app builders that may lack robust backend support.

## Key properties or components
- Managed databases
- Authentication services
- Cloud functions
- Scalability
