## Definition
Writing produced for academic contexts, such as essays, research papers, theses, and dissertations. It typically requires a formal tone, structured arguments, evidence-based reasoning, and proper citation of sources.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding academic writing conventions is essential for students and researchers. The emergence of AI tools presents new challenges and opportunities for how academic work is produced and assessed.

## Key properties or components
- Formal tone
- Structured arguments
- Evidence-based reasoning
- Proper citation
- Objectivity
