## Definition
A traditional British breakfast meal consisting of fried bacon, fried eggs, fried mushrooms, fried tomatoes, fried bread or toast, and sausages. It may also include baked beans and black pudding.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Mentioned as a component of the "Restless n breakfast wrap" from Dishoom, indicating a fusion of traditional breakfast elements into a portable format.
