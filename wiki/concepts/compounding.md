## Definition
The process where growth (e.g., in investments, skills, or business) generates further growth over time, leading to exponential increases.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Underpins long-term wealth creation and personal development, highlighting the power of consistent, uninterrupted progress.

## Key properties or components
- Exponential growth
- Time-dependent
- Requires consistency
- Avoids interruption
