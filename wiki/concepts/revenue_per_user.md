## Definition
Revenue Per User (ARPU - Average Revenue Per User) is a metric that calculates the total revenue generated by a company divided by the total number of users over a specific period.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Figma's strategy focuses on increasing revenue per user by encouraging adoption of multiple products, rather than solely focusing on acquiring new users.

## Key properties or components
- Total Revenue divided by Number of Users
- Measures monetization efficiency
- Can be increased by upselling/cross-selling
