## Definition
Utilizing AI and automated systems to handle customer inquiries, support requests, and provide information, thereby reducing the need for direct human intervention for common issues.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
AI can significantly improve response times and reduce the workload on customer support teams, leading to increased customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

## Key properties or components
- Chatbots
- Knowledge base integration
- Automated responses
- Escalation to human agents
