## Definition
A virtual team assistant is an AI-powered tool that acts as a resource for team members. It can answer questions, provide information, and assist with tasks, thereby improving team efficiency and knowledge sharing.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Deli represents this concept by allowing team members to access information and task guidance on demand, enhancing overall team productivity and reducing reliance on specific individuals.

## Key properties or components
- Knowledge retrieval
- Task assistance
- Team support
- Availability
