## Definition
Emoji reactions are a feature that allows users to respond to messages or content by selecting a specific emoji, rather than typing a full text response. This provides a quick and visually expressive way to acknowledge or comment on something.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances user engagement and communication efficiency by offering a faster and more nuanced way to interact with content on platforms like WhatsApp.

## Key properties or components
- Quick response mechanism
- Visual communication
- Predefined set of emojis
- Reduces text input
