## Definition
Specific functionalities, actions, or capabilities that an AI agent is programmed or taught to perform, often tailored to user-defined requirements or particular domain contexts, beyond its default or general-purpose abilities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Within the context of Agentic Stack, the standardisation of custom skills means these tailored capabilities can be made portable and accessible across various AI tools, greatly increasing the adaptability and utility of AI assistance in diverse development scenarios.

## Key properties or components
- User-defined functionality
- Portable across tools
- Enhances AI capabilities
- Context-specific
