## Definition
The characteristic of a vehicle to return to its intended course after being subjected to a disturbance.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A critical factor in high-speed racing, highlighted by the GT40 being described as 'terrifyingly unstable' on its first outing, posing a significant challenge for the design and engineering team.

## Key properties or components
- Handling
- Traction
- Suspension
- Aerodynamic forces
- Driver control
