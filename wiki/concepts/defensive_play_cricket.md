## Definition
A batting strategy in cricket focused on protecting the wicket and preventing dismissals, often by blocking or nudging the ball. It prioritizes safety over scoring runs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Defensive play is fundamental for survival and building a foundation, similar to how a strong defensive strategy in insurance protects against catastrophic losses.

## Key properties or components
- Prioritises safety
- Prevents dismissals
- Often involves blocking or nudging the ball
- Played close to the body
