## Definition
The ability to effectively communicate information and ideas to an audience through visual aids and verbal delivery. This includes structuring content, using graphics, and engaging the audience.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for impressing clients, securing business, and career advancement, as highlighted by the opportunity to go to Germany.

## Key properties or components
- Content delivery
- Visual aids (animations, graphics)
- Audience engagement
- Clarity and structure
