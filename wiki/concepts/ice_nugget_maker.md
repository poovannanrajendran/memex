## Definition
A kitchen appliance designed to rapidly produce small, soft, and chewable ice pieces, often referred to as 'nuggets' or 'pebbles'. These machines are typically faster than traditional ice cube trays.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlights a specific product that offers convenience and speed for ice production, differentiating it from standard ice-making methods.

## Key properties or components
- Fast freezing time (as little as 6 minutes)
- Produces 9 ice nuggets per cycle
- Offers 3 ice size options
- Includes a scoop
- Can be operated from a phone
