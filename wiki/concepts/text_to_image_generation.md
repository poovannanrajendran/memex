## Definition
A type of generative AI that creates images based on textual descriptions (prompts) provided by the user.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Key step in the video creation process, enabling the visual storytelling from abstract ideas to concrete images.

## Key properties or components
- Prompt-based input
- Algorithmic image synthesis
- Customizable styles
- Iterative refinement
