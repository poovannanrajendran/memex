## Definition
A pre-established structural design or blueprint that outlines the organisation, components, and inherent capabilities of an AI agent or an AI agent framework.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a ready-to-use and tested foundation for building AI agents, accelerating development, ensuring structural consistency, and offering a robust starting point for implementation.

## Key properties or components
- Pre-established design
- Outlines components
- Defines capabilities
- Accelerates development

## Contradictions or debates
None.
