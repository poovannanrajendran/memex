## Definition
A company accredited by ICANN to manage the reservation of internet domain names. They act as intermediaries between domain registrants and the central domain registry system.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The choice of registrar significantly impacts domain management, pricing, and the availability of essential services like privacy protection.

## Key properties or components
- Accredited by ICANN
- Manages domain reservations
- Offers related services
- Varying pricing structures

## Contradictions or debates
None.
