## Definition
The process of physically laying, connecting, and configuring cables (e.g., Ethernet) and associated hardware to establish a functional computer network within a building or area.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for establishing the foundational IT infrastructure required for modern business operations. The landlord's refusal to provide this was an early red flag regarding their commitment to preparing the space.

## Key properties or components
- Cables
- Network lines
- Tools
- Computer network setup
