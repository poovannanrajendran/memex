## Definition
A vehicle designed to accommodate up to seven occupants, typically with three rows of seating. This offers increased passenger-carrying capacity.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A significant differentiator for the EQB, offering greater versatility for larger families or groups compared to the standard five-seater EQA.

## Key properties or components
- Includes a third row of seats
- Often offers flexible seating arrangements
- May compromise boot space when in use
