## Definition
A fully digital screen behind the steering wheel that replaces traditional analogue instrument clusters, providing the driver with information such as speed, RPM, navigation, and media.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances driver information delivery and customisation within the vehicle's cockpit, contributing to a modern and technologically advanced user experience.

## Key properties or components
- Replaces analogue dials
- Customisable information
- Integrated into dashboard
- High resolution
