## Definition
Negligence is the failure to exercise the care that a reasonably prudent person would exercise in a like situation. In legal contexts, it can lead to liability for harm caused to others.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Rachel's actions are repeatedly described as negligent, particularly her failure to verify the address before sending her children alone in a taxi. This negligence is the key factor in the judge's decision.

## Key properties or components
- Duty of care
- Breach of duty
- Causation of harm
- Damages
