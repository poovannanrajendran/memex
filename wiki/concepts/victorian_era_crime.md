## Definition
Refers to the period of criminal activity and law enforcement during the reign of Queen Victoria (1837-1901). This era saw the development of modern policing methods but also high rates of crime in urban areas.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The high crime rate in the area led to policemen being stationed there, which in turn led to the carvings.

## Key properties or components
- Urbanisation
- Poverty
- Development of policing
- Secluded areas for criminal activity
