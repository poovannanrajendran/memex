## Definition
A feature in AI tools that allows users to interact with generated audio content, such as pausing, asking questions, and receiving contextual responses.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This feature enhances the learning experience by enabling users to engage in a conversational manner with the synthesized information, deepening understanding and retention.

## Key properties or components
- Conversational learning
- Real-time questioning
- Audio synthesis
- Interactive feedback
- On-the-go learning
