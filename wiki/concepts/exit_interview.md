## Definition
An exit interview is a process conducted by an organisation when an employee leaves the company. It aims to gather feedback on the employee's experience, reasons for leaving, and suggestions for improvement.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The exit interview is a central element of the narrative, where critical feedback is sought, and potential issues like data mishandling are discussed.

## Key properties or components
- Employee Feedback
- Retention Improvement
- Onboarding Analysis
- Departure Process
