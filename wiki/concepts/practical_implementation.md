## Definition
The act of applying theoretical knowledge or concepts through hands-on work and experimentation. It emphasizes learning by doing rather than just understanding abstract principles.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Stressed as the most effective way to internalize and master skills, especially in complex technical domains like AI and coding.

## Key properties or components
- Hands-on experience
- Experimentation
- Skill retention
- Problem-solving
