## Definition
A problem-solving and information-gathering technique that involves asking six key questions: Who? What? When? Where? Why? and How?

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Recommended as a tool to help users clarify their goals and the 'What' and 'Why' of a task when developing prompts, providing essential context for the AI.

## Key properties or components
- Question-based inquiry
- Information gathering
- Problem definition
- Contextualization
