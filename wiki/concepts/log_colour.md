## Definition
A flat, desaturated colour profile recorded by cameras that preserves the maximum amount of dynamic range and colour information, designed for extensive colour grading in post-production.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for professionals who need to achieve a specific colour look for their footage, offering the most flexibility in grading.

## Key properties or components
- Flat image profile
- Maximised dynamic range
- Requires colour grading

## Contradictions or debates
None.
