## Definition
A series of structured exercises designed to build and refine specific skills through repetition. In the context of drone piloting, these drills focus on fundamental flight techniques.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for developing muscle memory and confidence in drone operation. They provide a systematic way to progress from basic controls to more advanced flying.

## Key properties or components
- Repetitive tasks
- Skill-focused
- Progressive difficulty
- Measurable improvement
