## Definition
Workflow skills, in this context, refer to pre-defined sets of actions or capabilities within the 'Everything Claude Code' system that enable users to perform specific development tasks more efficiently. They are designed to be integrated into the overall development process.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The system includes over 12 such workflow skills, contributing to its utility as a comprehensive development tool.

## Key properties or components
- Pre-defined capabilities
- Task-specific
- Efficiency enhancement
- Integration into workflows
