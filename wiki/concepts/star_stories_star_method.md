## Definition
A common interview technique where candidates structure their answers to behavioural questions using the STAR method: Situation, Task, Action, and Result. The term 'star stories' likely refers to well-crafted examples using this method.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a structured and compelling way for candidates to showcase their skills and experiences during job interviews, leading to more effective communication.

## Key properties or components
- Situation
- Task
- Action
- Result
