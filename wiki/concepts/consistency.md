## Definition
The quality of achieving a certain level of performance or behaviour repeatedly and dependably over time.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Presented as a more robust driver of success than motivation, as it involves enduring challenges.

## Key properties or components
- Requires sustained action.
- Is distinct from fluctuating motivation.
- Involves outlasting resistance.

## Contradictions or debates
None.
