## Definition
The phenomenon of a water-filled balloon rupturing, causing the water and balloon fragments to disperse rapidly. This event involves fluid dynamics and material stress.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It serves as a visually striking subject for slow-motion capture, demonstrating the complex patterns of water dispersal and the fragmentation of the balloon material.

## Key properties or components
- Sudden rupture
- Water dispersion
- Material fragmentation
- Acoustic event
