## Definition
An individual characterized by profound knowledge, significant life experiences, and a substantial inner caliber. They are often associated with thoughtful inquiry and a consistent approach to self-improvement.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents an ideal of personal development and substantive engagement with the world, contrasting with superficiality.

## Key properties or components
- Extensive reading
- Life lessons
- Inner caliber
- Consistent effort

## Contradictions or debates
None.
