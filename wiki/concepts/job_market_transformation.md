## Definition
Job market transformation refers to significant shifts in the types of jobs available, the skills required, and the overall structure of employment, often driven by technological advancements or economic changes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The core theme of the discussion, illustrating how AI is reshaping employment landscapes globally.

- Job creation
- Skill evolution
- Economic impact
