## Definition
The use of algorithms and AI to generate or modify design elements, layouts, and even entire products or interfaces with minimal human intervention.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source directly showcases automated design by claiming Claude Code can replace a human website designer, highlighting the disruptive potential of this concept in creative industries.

## Key properties or components
- Algorithmic generation
- Rule-based systems
- Machine learning models
- Template-based creation

## Contradictions or debates
The quality and nuance of AI-generated designs compared to human-crafted ones are often debated, with concerns about originality and creative depth.
