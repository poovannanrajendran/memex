## Definition
Custom skills refer to specific functionalities or capabilities that an AI agent can perform, often tailored to particular tasks. Protocols define the rules or standards for interaction and operation between different AI components.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The agentic stack standardises these elements, ensuring that custom skills and interaction protocols are consistent across all integrated AI tools.

## Key properties or components
- Task-specific functionalities
- Interaction standards
- Operational rules
