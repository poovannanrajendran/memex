## Definition
Virtual Reality (VR) is a simulated experience that can be similar to or completely different from the real world. VR systems typically aim to immerse the user by activating sight and hearing with specialized equipment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The core technology domain of the products being discussed (Quest 3 and Quest 3S).

## Key properties or components
- Immersive experience
- Simulated environment
- Specialized hardware (headsets, controllers)
