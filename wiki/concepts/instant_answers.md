## Definition
The ability to receive immediate and accurate responses to inquiries or problems. This concept is central to tools designed for efficiency and quick information retrieval.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a significant shift in how information is accessed and processed, enhancing user productivity and reducing the time spent on research or problem-solving.

## Key properties or components
- Real-time response
- High accuracy
- User-friendly interface
- Broad applicability
