## Definition
A practice session designed to simulate a real job interview. It allows candidates to rehearse their responses, receive feedback, and become more comfortable with the interview process.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The entire video is structured around mock interviews to demonstrate effective and less effective candidate responses to challenging scenarios.

## Key properties or components
- Scenario-based questions
- Performance evaluation
- Feedback mechanism
- Skill assessment
