## Definition
An action film is a genre of film that emphasizes exciting action sequences, such as fighting, chases, and stunts. These films often feature a clear protagonist and antagonist, with a focus on physical conflict and high stakes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a popular genre that often explores themes of heroism, conflict, and resolution through physical prowess and dramatic sequences.

## Key properties or components
- Stunts
- Chases
- Combat
- High stakes
- Fast-paced narrative
