## Definition
The use of artificial intelligence technologies to automate and enhance the process of designing, developing, and delivering educational courses.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Significantly speeds up the course creation process, personalises learning experiences, and can reduce the cost and effort involved.

## Key properties or components
- Automated Content Generation
- Personalised Learning Paths
- Intelligent Feedback
- Predictive Analytics

## Contradictions or debates
AI-generated content may sometimes lack the nuanced understanding or creativity of human experts, and ethical considerations around data privacy are paramount.
