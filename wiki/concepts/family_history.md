## Definition
The study of the lineage and origins of a family and its members. It involves tracing ancestry, understanding family relationships, and preserving stories and traditions across generations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the video's message, highlighting the importance of knowing one's roots and the generational disconnect where this knowledge is often lost.

## Key properties or components
- Lineage
- Ancestry
- Generational Knowledge
- Family Stories
- Traditions
