## Definition
A cricket batting shot played off the back foot, typically against a short or good length delivery. It involves driving the ball powerfully through the off-side.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is a classic and effective shot for scoring runs, particularly against pace bowling, and demonstrates good technique and timing.

## Key properties or components
- Played off the back foot
- Against good length or short balls
- Aims for power and timing
- Can be played through covers, point, or middle
