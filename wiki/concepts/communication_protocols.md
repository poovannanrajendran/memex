## Definition
Standardised sets of rules and formats that govern how AI agents, human users, and other systems exchange information and interact with one another within an AI ecosystem.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for establishing clear, consistent, and reliable interaction within and between agentic systems, allowing for effective understanding, command, and data exchange.

## Key properties or components
- Standardised rules
- Governs information exchange
- Facilitates interaction
- Ensures consistency
