## Definition
Software utilities or features within an AI agent framework designed to track, observe, and report on the performance, behaviour, and operational status of AI agents and the entire system.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for ensuring the reliability, efficiency, and correct functioning of AI agent applications by allowing developers to identify issues, debug effectively, and optimise system performance over time.

## Key properties or components
- Tracks performance
- Monitors behaviour
- Reports operational status
- Supports debugging
