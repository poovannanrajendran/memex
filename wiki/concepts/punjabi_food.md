## Definition
A cuisine originating from the Punjab region of the Indian subcontinent, known for its rich and often spicy flavours, extensive use of dairy products like ghee and butter, and popular cooking methods such as tandoor grilling.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The central theme of the video, focusing on identifying and reviewing high-quality Punjabi cuisine in London.

## Key properties or components
- Rich flavours
- Spicy
- Use of dairy
- Tandoor cooking
- Meat and seafood dishes
