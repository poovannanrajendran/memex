## Definition
AI website generation refers to the use of artificial intelligence, particularly machine learning models, to automatically design and build websites based on user inputs such as text prompts, style preferences, or data.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It significantly speeds up the website creation process and can lower the barrier to entry for creating professional-looking websites, potentially disrupting traditional web design agencies.

## Key properties or components
- Natural language processing (NLP) for prompts
- Generative design capabilities
- Automated layout and content placement
- Personalisation and optimisation

## Contradictions or debates
The quality and uniqueness of AI-generated websites can vary, and they may sometimes lack the nuanced creativity or specific strategic insight a human designer can provide.
