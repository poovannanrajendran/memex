## Definition
A large database of interconnected entities (people, places, things, concepts) and their relationships, used by Google Search to understand the meaning behind search queries and provide more relevant results.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It serves as a crucial data source for Google's AI mode, enabling it to provide structured and informed answers by leveraging real-time information about the world.

## Key properties or components
- Interconnected data
- Entity recognition
- Relationship mapping
- Real-time information
- Underpins search result relevance
