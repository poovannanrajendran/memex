## Definition
Ollama is an application that simplifies the process of running large language models locally on personal computers (Mac, Windows, Linux). It handles model management and inference, allowing for private and offline AI usage.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides the foundational local infrastructure for running LLMs that AnythingLLM then enhances with agent capabilities.

## Key properties or components
- Local execution
- Cross-platform support
- Model management
- Privacy-focused
