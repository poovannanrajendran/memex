## Definition
A classification system used in the UK insurance industry to rate the risk associated with a particular vehicle model. Lower groups generally indicate lower insurance premiums.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A factor mentioned for each car, providing context for potential insurance costs.

## Key properties or components
- Risk assessment
- Categorisation (1-50)
- Impacts premium
