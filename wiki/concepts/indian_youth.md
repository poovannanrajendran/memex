## Definition
Refers to the young population of India, typically considered a demographic group that will drive the nation's future. This segment is often seen as dynamic, innovative, and crucial for societal and economic advancement.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The speech highlights the critical role of Indian youth in achieving the envisioned 'new horizon' for the country, emphasizing their potential and responsibility.

## Key properties or components
- Demographic segment
- Potential for change
- Future leaders
- Innovation drivers
