## Definition
The process of creating and configuring an online store using the Shopify platform. This includes choosing a theme, setting up product listings, and configuring payment and shipping options.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for small businesses looking to establish an online sales channel, enabling them to reach a wider customer base.

## Key properties or components
- Account creation
- Theme selection
- Product listing
- Payment gateway setup
- Shipping configuration
