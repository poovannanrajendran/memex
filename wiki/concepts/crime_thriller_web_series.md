## Definition
A crime thriller web series is a serialised television programme, typically released online, that focuses on suspenseful plots involving criminal activity, investigations, and intense psychological elements.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
These series are popular forms of entertainment, and recommendations for high-quality ones cater to a specific audience segment looking for engaging content.

## Key properties or components
- Suspenseful plotlines
- Criminal activities
- Investigation elements
- Psychological tension
- Serialised format
