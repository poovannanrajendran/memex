## Definition
An engagement ceremony is a formal event where two people publicly announce their intention to marry. It typically involves rituals, blessings from family and friends, and often the exchange of rings.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the central event documented in the video, marking a significant step towards the couple's marriage.

## Key properties or components
- Rituals
- Blessings
- Ring exchange
- Family and friends
- Public announcement of marriage intent
