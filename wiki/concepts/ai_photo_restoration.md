## Definition
The use of artificial intelligence algorithms to repair, enhance, and restore old, damaged, or low-quality photographs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enables the preservation and revitalisation of historical images and personal memories, making them accessible and visually appealing for future generations.

## Key properties or components
- Automated enhancement
- Damage repair (scratches, tears)
- Colourisation
- Noise reduction
- Detail restoration
