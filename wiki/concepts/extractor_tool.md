## Definition
A tool designed to remove broken, stripped, or otherwise difficult-to-remove fasteners. In this context, it's a specialized bit with hardened ridges that grip into a drilled hole.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key component of the locking lug bolt removal toolkit, enabling the actual extraction of the compromised fastener.

## Key properties or components
- Features hardened ridges
- Designed to be hammered into a drilled hole
- Engages with the material of the fastener
