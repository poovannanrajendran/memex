## Definition
The process of setting the correct vertical position of a bicycle saddle relative to the pedals to optimise comfort, efficiency, and injury prevention.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A fundamental aspect of bike fit that significantly impacts pedalling biomechanics, power output, and the prevention of discomfort or injury.

## Key properties or components
- Heel-to-pedal method
- Incremental adjustments
- Rider feedback
- Compensatory movement detection
