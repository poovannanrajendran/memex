## Definition
The duty of professionals to act in the best interests of their clients and to maintain the integrity of their practice. This includes ensuring continuity of service and upholding client confidentiality.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Harvey and Jessica demonstrate professional responsibility by stepping in to manage Louis's cases, ensuring clients are not negatively impacted by his absence.

## Key properties or components
- Client focus
- Maintaining service levels
- Confidentiality
- Ethical conduct
