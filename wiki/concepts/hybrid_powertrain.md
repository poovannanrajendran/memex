## Definition
A hybrid powertrain combines a conventional internal combustion engine with an electric motor, allowing the vehicle to operate on electric power alone, or in conjunction with the engine, to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a key technological advancement in the new Dacia Duster, offering improved efficiency and a smoother driving experience compared to previous diesel engines.

## Key properties or components
- Electric motor assist
- Improved fuel economy
- Smoother low-speed operation
- Automatic gearbox compatibility
