## Definition
A gear that combines features of helical and bevel gears, having curved teeth that transmit motion between intersecting shafts smoothly and quietly.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Offers the advantages of helical gears (smoothness, quietness) in applications involving intersecting shafts.

## Key properties or components
- Angled teeth
- Conical shape
- Intersecting axes
- Smooth power transfer
