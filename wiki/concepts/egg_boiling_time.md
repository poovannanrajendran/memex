## Definition
The specific duration an egg is boiled, which determines the consistency of the yolk and white. Optimal boiling times vary based on desired texture, from fully hard to soft and jammy.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source claims that 7 minutes is the 'objectively best' time for boiling eggs, crucial for achieving the desired jammy yolk for Mayak Eggs.

## Key properties or components
- Time-dependent
- Affects yolk consistency
- Affects white consistency
