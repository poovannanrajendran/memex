## Definition
The capability of an AI system to tailor its responses and interactions based on an individual user's data, history, and preferences.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Explains how ChatGPT can provide such specific and impactful advice using the 'god prompt', showcasing AI's growing ability to understand and adapt to individual users.

## Key properties or components
- Learns from user history
- Tailors output
- Increases relevance
- Enhances user experience
