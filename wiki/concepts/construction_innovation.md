## Definition
Refers to the introduction of new ideas, methods, or products in the field of building and infrastructure development. This can encompass new materials, technologies, design approaches, or construction techniques aimed at improving efficiency, sustainability, safety, or cost-effectiveness.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
