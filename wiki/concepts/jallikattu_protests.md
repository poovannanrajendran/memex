## Definition
A series of protests that took place in Tamil Nadu, India, in January 2017. The protests were against the ban on the traditional bull-taming sport of Jallikattu, which is deeply ingrained in Tamil culture.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The protests form the contextual backdrop for RJ Balaji's viral speech and subsequent involvement, illustrating a significant cultural and social movement.

## Key properties or components
- Cultural significance
- Protest against bans
- Student and public mobilization
- Tamil Nadu origin
