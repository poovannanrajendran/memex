## Definition
The speed at which a film's plot unfolds. Good pacing keeps the audience engaged without rushing or dragging.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The reviewer explicitly discusses the pacing of 'DNA', noting that it starts well but is hindered by unrelated songs and fights, leading to audience fatigue.

## Key properties or components
- Speed of plot progression
- Audience engagement
- Rhythm of scenes
- Transitions
