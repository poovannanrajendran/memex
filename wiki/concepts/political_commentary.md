## Definition
The act of analysing, interpreting, and offering opinions on political events, policies, and figures. It often involves expressing viewpoints and engaging with the partisan aspects of governance and public affairs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source advises UPSC aspirants to minimise their engagement with political commentary as it can be a distraction from the factual and analytical requirements of the exam.

## Key properties or components
- Opinion-based
- Partisan
- Interpretive
- Subjective

## Contradictions or debates
None.
