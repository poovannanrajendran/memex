## Definition
A marketing strategy where a company offers prizes to a select group of participants, often to increase brand awareness, customer engagement, or sales.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept explains the nature of the event described in the video, where BIKECARE360 is using a giveaway to promote their DEAR BIKE e-book and reward customers.

## Key properties or components
- Prize incentives
- Participant eligibility criteria
- Promotional goals
- Random selection or contest mechanics
