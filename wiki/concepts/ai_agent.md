## Definition
An autonomous software programme that can perceive its environment, reason, make decisions, and execute actions to achieve specific goals, often integrating large language models for advanced cognitive capabilities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
In the memex system, the AI agent is envisioned as the 'librarian' responsible for the active ingestion, cross-linking, and synthesis of knowledge, making the system 'AI-maintained' and intelligent.

## Key properties or components
- autonomy
- goal-oriented behaviour
- perception and action
- reasoning capabilities
- decision-making
