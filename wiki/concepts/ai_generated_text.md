## Definition
Content, such as articles, essays, or responses, that is created autonomously by an artificial intelligence system rather than by a human author.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding AI-generated text is crucial for academic integrity, content authenticity, and the development of AI detection technologies.

## Key properties or components
- Can mimic human writing styles
- May be flagged by AI detectors
- Can be edited by users
- Can be used to bypass detection
