## Definition
Emotional control refers to the ability to manage and regulate one's emotional responses. It involves understanding emotions, accepting them without being overwhelmed, and responding in a considered manner rather than impulsively.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for maintaining focus on goals and avoiding unnecessary distractions or energy expenditure, as discussed in the source.

## Key properties or components
- Self-awareness
- Regulation
- Mindfulness
- Non-reactivity
