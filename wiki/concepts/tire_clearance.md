## Definition
The maximum width of tire that a bicycle frame and fork can accommodate. This is influenced by the frame's design, the bottom bracket, and the presence of components like front derailleurs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The author emphasizes that 1x drivetrains can significantly increase tire clearance, which is crucial for gravel riding as wider tires offer better traction and comfort on varied terrain.

## Key properties or components
- Frame and fork design limitation
- Influenced by drivetrain components
- Allows for wider tires
- Impacts ride comfort and traction
- Measured in millimeters (mm)
