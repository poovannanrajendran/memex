## Definition
An individual who focuses on superficial aspects of life, such as external validation, social media presence, and fleeting trends, rather than on deep knowledge or personal growth.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Serves as a counterpoint to the 'deep person', highlighting the pitfalls of prioritizing outward appearances over substantive development.

## Key properties or components
- Superficial marketing
- Focus on followers/likes
- Inconsistent effort
- Lack of depth

## Contradictions or debates
None.
