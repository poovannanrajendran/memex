## Definition
A range of accolades given in the UK to recognise achievement in various fields such as art, literature, music, theatre, and film.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Familiarity with prominent awards like the Turner Prize (art), Man Booker Prize (literature), Mercury Prize (music), and Olivier Awards (theatre) is expected for the Life in the UK test.

## Key properties or components
- Field of recognition (Art, Music, etc.)
- Prestigious institutions
- Annual ceremonies
