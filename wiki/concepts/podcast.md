## Definition
A podcast is a digital audio file or series of files made available on the internet for downloading or streaming, typically available as a series, new installments of which can be received by subscribers automatically.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This source is an example of a podcast, a popular medium for information dissemination and entertainment.

## Key properties or components
- Digital audio
- Internet-based
- Episodic
- Downloadable/Streamable
