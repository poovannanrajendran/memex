## Definition
These are AI tools and models that are hosted on remote servers and accessed by users over the internet, typically through a subscription model.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
They represent the traditional method of accessing powerful AI capabilities but come with drawbacks such as recurring costs, potential usage restrictions, and less user control over data and processing.

## Key properties or components
- Requires internet connection
- Users rent computational power
- Often subscription-based
- Potential for throttling and usage limits
- Provider controls infrastructure and updates
