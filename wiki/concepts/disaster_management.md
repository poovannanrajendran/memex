## Definition
Disaster management is the organisation and management of resources and responsibilities for dealing with all aspects of emergencies, in particular preparedness, response and recovery. It aims to minimise the impact of disasters on communities and the environment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Effective disaster management, as exemplified by the railway staff's actions, is vital for public safety and the continuity of essential services, preventing loss of life and property during emergencies.

## Key properties or components
- Preparedness
- Response
- Recovery
- Mitigation
- Risk Assessment
