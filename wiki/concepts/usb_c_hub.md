## Definition
A device that expands the connectivity options of a USB-C port on a computer or tablet. It typically includes multiple ports such as USB-A, HDMI, SD card readers, and power delivery.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for modern devices with limited ports, like iPads, allowing users to connect peripherals, external displays, and storage devices, thus increasing the iPad's capability as a workstation.

## Key properties or components
- Multiple ports
- Power Delivery (PD)
- Data transfer
- Video output (HDMI)
- Portability
