## Definition
A digital library is a collection of information resources made available in electronic form. These resources can include books, journals, audio, video, and other media, accessible via computers or the internet.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Digital libraries offer wider access to information, preserve resources, and can cater to diverse learning needs, aligning with government initiatives to improve education and information access for students in Tamil Nadu.

## Key properties or components
- Electronic resources
- Online accessibility
- Searchable databases
- Digital preservation
