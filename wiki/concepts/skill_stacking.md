## Definition
The process of acquiring and combining multiple distinct skills to create a unique and more valuable professional profile.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for building expertise and increasing earning potential, as demonstrated by the progression from sales to marketing.

## Key properties or components
- Acquisition of multiple skills
- Synergistic value
- Career advancement
- Increased earning potential
