## Definition
A productivity strategy that focuses on identifying and completing the most critical tasks within a very short timeframe, such as the next 18 hours, rather than long-term planning.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Explains Steve Jobs's operational philosophy, emphasizing urgent, high-impact actions over broader strategic planning for daily execution.

## Key properties or components
- Short-term focus (e.g., 18 hours)
- Identification of critical tasks (top 3-5)
- Elimination of distractions
- Urgency and decisiveness
