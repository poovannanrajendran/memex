## Definition
Visual Effects (VFX) are the processes by which imagery is created or manipulated outside the context of a live-action shoot. In modern filmmaking, VFX involves the integration of computer-generated imagery (CGI) with live-action footage.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
VFX are crucial for creating visually stunning and engaging content, particularly in marketing and entertainment, and OMagic AI aims to democratise access to high-quality VFX.
