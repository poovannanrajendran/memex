## Definition
Apple's integrated AI system that enhances device capabilities with personalized intelligence, context awareness, and generative models, designed to be private and secure.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is presented as the central theme for the iPhone 16 generation, aiming to redefine user interaction with Apple devices, though its initial release is delayed.

## Key properties or components
- Personalized
- Context-aware
- Generative AI
- Privacy-focused
