## Definition
Samsaram Adhu Minsaram (Family is Electricity) is a popular 1986 Tamil film directed by Visu. It explores the complexities and conflicts within a joint family, often with comedic undertones.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It represents a significant work in Tamil cinema, known for its relatable portrayal of family dynamics and its lasting impact on audiences.
