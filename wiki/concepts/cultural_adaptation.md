## Definition
Cultural adaptation is the process of modifying or adjusting elements from one culture to fit the norms, values, and context of another. In music, this can involve translating lyrics, altering instrumentation, or adjusting the performance style to resonate with a new audience.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to understanding the creation of a Tamil version of an internationally known song, aiming to make it accessible and relatable to a Tamil-speaking audience.

## Key properties or components
- Translation
- Localization
- Audience reception
- Cultural resonance
