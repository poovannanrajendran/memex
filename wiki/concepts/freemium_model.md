## Definition
A business model where a product or service is offered free of charge, but with limitations, and users can pay for premium features, additional capacity, or an ad-free experience.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The tutorial heavily relies on the free tiers and trial periods of various AI tools, making the creation process accessible without cost.

## Key properties or components
- Free basic service
- Paid premium features
- Usage limits on free tier
- Trial periods
