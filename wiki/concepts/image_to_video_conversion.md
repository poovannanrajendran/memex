## Definition
A specific application of AI video generation where a static image is used as the primary input to create a video sequence. The AI interprets the image and associated textual prompts to animate and generate motion.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for applications seeking to quickly visualise concepts or create dynamic content from existing visual assets, particularly in marketing and social media.

## Key properties or components
- Input: Image + Prompt
- Output: Video
- AI interpretation
- Animation from static data
