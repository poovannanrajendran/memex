## Definition
YouTube Shorts are short-form vertical videos, typically under 60 seconds, designed for mobile viewing. They are a direct competitor to platforms like TikTok and Instagram Reels.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This format is crucial for reaching a wider audience quickly and efficiently, often going viral due to its bite-sized and easily shareable nature. For content creators, it's a way to increase visibility.

## Key properties or components
- Vertical video format
- Under 60 seconds
- Mobile-first design
- Designed for quick consumption and sharing
