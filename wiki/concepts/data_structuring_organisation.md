## Definition
The process of arranging and categorizing data into a defined format or hierarchy to improve its usability, searchability, and retrieval. This applies to both human and AI information management.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source highlights that effective data structuring significantly boosts AI retrieval efficiency, a key principle in personal knowledge management and AI development.

## Key properties or components
- Hierarchy
- Categorisation
- Searchability
- Retrieval efficiency
