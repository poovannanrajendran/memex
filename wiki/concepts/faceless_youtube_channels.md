## Definition
YouTube channels where the creator does not appear on camera. Content is typically produced using stock footage, animation, AI-generated visuals, or screen recordings, often with a voiceover.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a scalable and potentially lucrative side hustle or income stream that leverages AI for content creation efficiency.

## Key properties or components
- No on-camera presence required
- AI-assisted content creation
- Focus on niche topics or broad appeal content
- Monetization through ads, sponsorships, affiliate marketing
