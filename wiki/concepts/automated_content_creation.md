## Definition
The process of using artificial intelligence systems to generate content, such as text, images, or videos, with minimal human intervention. This aims to increase efficiency and scale of content production.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Demonstrates the power of AI in streamlining creative processes, as seen with Auto-Shorts, which automates video generation.

## Key properties or components
- AI-driven
- Efficiency-focused
- Scalable production
- Reduced manual effort
