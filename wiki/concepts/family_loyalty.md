## Definition
Family loyalty refers to the strong bond and commitment among family members, often involving mutual support, protection, and adherence to shared values. It can sometimes create pressure or conflict when individual needs clash with perceived familial obligations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Morgan's mother invokes the concept of family loyalty to defend Rachel, creating a conflict for Morgan who prioritizes the safety of her niece and nephew over unconditional familial support.

## Key properties or components
- Commitment
- Support
- Obligation
- Interpersonal dynamics
