## Definition
A dish typically consisting of a selection of grilled or roasted meats, often including lamb, chicken, and kebabs. It is served sizzling and is designed for sharing or as a substantial meal.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlighted as a sensational dish at Lahore Kebab House, showcasing the quality of their meat preparation.

## Key properties or components
- Variety of meats
- Grilling/Tandoori preparation
- Sizzling presentation
- Often served with accompaniments
