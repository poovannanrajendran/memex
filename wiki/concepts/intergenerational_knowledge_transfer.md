## Definition
The process by which knowledge, skills, values, and traditions are passed down from one generation to the next. This can occur formally or informally within families and communities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video implicitly discusses the breakdown of this transfer, as younger generations are shown to be less aware of their family history.

## Key properties or components
- Oral Tradition
- Mentorship
- Cultural Transmission
- Family Lore

## Contradictions or debates
While the video laments the loss of family history, it also presents a counterpoint that traditional knowledge is not the only valuable form of learning.
