## Definition
The practice of gathering and presenting various artificial intelligence tools and resources in a centralised platform or directory. This simplifies discovery and access for users.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Toolify exemplifies this concept by consolidating numerous AI tools, making them more accessible and manageable for individuals and businesses interested in leveraging AI.

## Key properties or components
- Centralised access
- Categorisation of tools
- Discovery and search functionality
- Information on tool availability (e.g., free vs. paid)
