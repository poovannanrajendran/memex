## Definition
The practice of training oneself to obey rules or a code of behaviour, often through self-control and rigorous routine. It leads to controlled behaviour and consistent improvement.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Coupled with dedication, it is shown through Arjuna's archery practice as a path to refining talent and achieving mastery in a chosen skill.

## Key properties or components
- self-control
- training
- adherence to rules
- controlled behaviour
- routine
