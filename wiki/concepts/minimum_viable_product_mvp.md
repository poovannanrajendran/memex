## Definition
A version of a new product which allows teams to collect the maximum amount of validated learning about customers with the least effort. It focuses on core functionality to test market viability.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The project discussed in the video is framed as an MVP, influencing the scope of the initial requirements, project plan, and user stories generated by AI.

## Key properties or components
- Core functionality
- Market validation
- Iterative development
- Reduced development time
