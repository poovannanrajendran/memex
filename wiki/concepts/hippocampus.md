## Definition
A complex brain structure embedded deep into the temporal lobe. It has a major role in learning and memory, such as in the formation of new memories.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Mentioned in the context of cognitive decline, suggesting that the inability to perform simple calculations without aid indicates a potential dysfunction of this brain region.

## Key properties or components
- Brain structure
- Memory formation
- Learning
