## Definition
Car leasing is a long-term rental agreement where a customer pays to use a vehicle for a fixed period, typically 2-4 years, in exchange for regular monthly payments. At the end of the term, the vehicle is usually returned to the leasing company.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides an alternative to purchasing a vehicle outright or taking out a traditional car loan, often offering lower monthly payments and the ability to drive a new car every few years. This is relevant for individuals and businesses seeking vehicle access without full ownership.

## Key properties or components
- Fixed monthly payments
- Fixed contract duration
- Mileage restrictions
- No ownership at end of term
- Lower initial outlay than purchasing

## Contradictions or debates
Leasing agreements can involve penalties for exceeding mileage limits or for excessive wear and tear, and the customer does not build equity in the vehicle.
