## Definition
A simplified three-step framework for building a successful online business. It involves attracting an audience (traffic), building credibility with them (trust), and converting them into paying customers (monetization).

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This represents the overarching funnel structure described in the video, guiding the user from initial engagement to revenue generation.

## Key properties or components
- Attract audience
- Build credibility
- Generate revenue
