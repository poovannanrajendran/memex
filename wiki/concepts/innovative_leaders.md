## Definition
Leaders who demonstrate exceptional creativity and forward-thinking, consistently producing novel solutions and driving significant change within their fields.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Their curiosity and willingness to challenge norms are key drivers of progress and differentiation in any sector.

## Key properties or components
- Curiosity
- Vision
- Problem-solving
- Adaptability
- Forward-thinking
