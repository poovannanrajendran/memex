## Definition
The process of requesting a modification in one's role, team, or responsibilities within an organisation. It involves strategic communication and understanding organisational dynamics.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for personal career growth and development, allowing individuals to seek better opportunities or align their roles with their skills and aspirations.

## Key properties or components
- Requires clear communication
- Involves understanding timing
- Needs a strategic approach
- Benefits from skill and attitude
