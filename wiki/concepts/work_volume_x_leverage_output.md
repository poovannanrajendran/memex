## Definition
A formula that defines work not by the effort expended, but by the output achieved, which is a product of the volume of activity and the level of leverage applied.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Shifts the focus from 'working hard' to 'working smart' and effectively, emphasizing the strategic use of leverage to maximize results.

## Key properties or components
- Focus on output
- Importance of volume for skill
- Skill as a form of leverage
- Efficiency
