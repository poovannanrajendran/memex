## Definition
Artificial intelligence tools that can be accessed and used by individuals or organisations without incurring any direct costs. These often have limitations compared to premium versions but provide significant value.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The availability of free AI tools lowers the barrier to entry for experimentation and adoption of AI technologies. Toolify highlights that many of the AI tools it lists are accessible free of charge.

## Key properties or components
- Cost-effectiveness
- Accessibility
- Potential limitations (features, usage)
