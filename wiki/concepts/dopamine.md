## Definition
A neurotransmitter that plays a role in the brain's reward and pleasure centers. It is associated with motivation, addiction, and feelings of enjoyment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The pitch leverages the understanding of dopamine to argue that the game will be highly engaging and addictive, driving player retention and spending.

## Key properties or components
- Neurotransmitter
- Reward system
- Pleasure response
- Motivation
