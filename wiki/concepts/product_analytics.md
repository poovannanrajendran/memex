## Definition
Product analytics involves collecting and analyzing data on how users interact with a product. The goal is to understand user behavior, identify trends, measure performance, and inform product development decisions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for SaaS businesses to understand user engagement and product success. The source highlights the difficulty and cost associated with traditional product analytics tools, advocating for custom, self-hosted solutions using databases and visualization tools.

## Key properties or components
- User behavior tracking
- Performance metrics
- Data-driven insights
- Product improvement
- Funnel analysis
