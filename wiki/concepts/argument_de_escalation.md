## Definition
The process of reducing the intensity or severity of a conflict or argument. This often involves communication techniques aimed at calming emotions and finding common ground.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the video's theme, as one partner attempts to "get out of an argument" by offering help and a beverage.

## Key properties or components
- Active listening
- Empathy
- Offering solutions
- Humour
