## Definition
A period in an entrepreneur's journey where they must deliberately isolate themselves from social distractions and non-goal-oriented activities to focus intensely on learning and building.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Emphasizes the necessity of sacrifice and focused effort during critical growth phases, particularly when starting out.

## Key properties or components
- Intentional isolation
- Sacrifice of social life
- Intense focus on goals
- Prioritisation of learning and action

## Contradictions or debates
None.
