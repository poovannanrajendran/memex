## Definition
Electric vehicles (EVs) specifically designed or offering a battery capacity that allows for an extended driving distance on a single charge, typically considered to be 300 miles or more.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Addresses 'range anxiety' and makes EVs a more practical option for longer journeys or for users who cannot charge frequently.

## Key properties or components
- High battery capacity
- Optimised energy efficiency
- Typically higher purchase price (new)
- Reduced charging frequency

## Contradictions or debates
None.
