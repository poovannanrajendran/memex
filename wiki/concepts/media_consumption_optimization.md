## Definition
Designing devices and operating systems to provide the best possible experience for watching videos, listening to music, and engaging with other media content, often including features like offline downloads.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The creator suggests iPads, due to their mobile OS roots, are better optimized for media consumption (especially offline downloads) than traditional computer OSs.

## Key properties or components
- Offline download capabilities
- Optimized app experiences (streaming services)
- Screen quality (OLED, HDR)
- Audio quality
- Portable form factor
