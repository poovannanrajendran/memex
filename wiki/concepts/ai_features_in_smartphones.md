## Definition
Artificial Intelligence features in smartphones refer to software capabilities that leverage machine learning and AI algorithms to enhance user experience. This can include features like intelligent assistants, real-time translation, advanced photo processing, and predictive text.

AI features are increasingly becoming a key differentiator in the smartphone market, offering advanced functionalities that go beyond traditional hardware capabilities and provide intelligent assistance to users.
