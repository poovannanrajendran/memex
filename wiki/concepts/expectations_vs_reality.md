## Definition
A common trope where the imagined or desired outcome of a situation differs significantly from what actually occurs. This gap often leads to humour, disappointment, or learning.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the humour in the video, as the comedy is derived from the unexpected turns that married life takes compared to what might have been anticipated.

## Key properties or components
- Anticipation
- Outcome
- Discrepancy
- Surprise

## Contradictions or debates
This concept inherently involves a contradiction between what is hoped for and what is experienced.
