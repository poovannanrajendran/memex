## Definition
Protective coverings placed on the floor of a vehicle's interior, designed to protect the original carpet from dirt, wear, and spills.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances vehicle cleanliness, protects the interior, and can add a touch of style or luxury, as highlighted by the sponsor's product.

## Key properties or components
- Material (rubber, carpet)
- Fit (custom vs. universal)
- Durability
- Ease of cleaning
