## Definition
The practice of trying out new features, techniques, or approaches in coding, often in a playful or challenging context, to deepen understanding and discover innovative solutions. This can involve drawing, animating, or tackling specific coding problems.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Keeps the learning process fun and engaging, leading to a more profound grasp of concepts and the development of unique skills.

## Key properties or components
- Trial and error
- Creative application
- Exploration of features
- Fun learning environment
