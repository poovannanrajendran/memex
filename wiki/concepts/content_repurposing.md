## Definition
The process of adapting existing content into different formats or for different platforms to extend its reach and engagement. For example, turning a video script into a blog post.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
AI tools can automate this time-consuming process, allowing creators to maximize the value of their content across multiple channels.

## Key properties or components
- Adapts existing material
- Extends content reach
- Saves time and resources
- Increases engagement

## Contradictions or debates
