## Definition
A growth mindset is the belief that one's abilities and intelligence can be developed through dedication, hard work, and learning from mistakes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This mindset is associated with greater achievement and resilience, as individuals are more likely to embrace challenges and persist in the face of setbacks.

## Key properties or components
- Belief in development
- Embracing challenges
- Persistence
- Learning from criticism
- Effort is key
