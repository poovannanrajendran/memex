## Definition
The process of tailoring a resume to specific job requirements and personal preferences. This involves adjusting content, formatting, and keywords to best present an individual's qualifications.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
While the tool offers instant generation, the ability to customise ensures the resume is unique and effectively communicates the applicant's suitability for a role.

## Key properties or components
- Personalisation
- Targeted content
- Formatting adjustments
- Keyword optimisation
