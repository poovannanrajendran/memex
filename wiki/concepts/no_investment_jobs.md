## Definition
These are job opportunities or income streams that do not require the individual to invest any capital upfront to start earning. The focus is on utilising skills or time rather than financial resources.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Appeals to individuals with limited financial means, offering a path to income generation without the risk of financial loss.

## Key properties or components
- Zero initial capital
- Skill-based or time-based
- Lower barrier to entry

## Contradictions or debates
Often associated with lower pay or pyramid schemes if not legitimate. The 'no investment' claim can sometimes mask hidden costs or unrealistic earning potentials.
