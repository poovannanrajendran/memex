## Definition
An intentional attempt to damage the reputation or credibility of a person or entity through slander, false accusations, or the spread of damaging information.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The Tech Boss creators accuse Adada Creators of engaging in character assassination by bringing up personal issues and fabricating stories to discredit them, rather than addressing the core business disputes.

## Key properties or components
- Slander
- Libel
- Smear Campaign
- Personal Attacks
- Reputational Harm

## Contradictions or debates
Adada Creators are accused of resorting to personal attacks instead of focusing on business matters, which contradicts the idea of a professional dispute resolution.
