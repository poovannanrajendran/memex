## Definition
The concept that time is a finite and irretrievable resource, making it inherently precious and more valuable than material wealth or money. It suggests that experiences, life, and moments cannot be purchased or regained once lost.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the quote, highlighting a fundamental life lesson that transcends financial status and underscores the importance of how time is spent.

## Key properties or components
- Finite
- Irretrievable
- Non-monetary
- Precious
