## Definition
The overall visual fidelity of a camera's output, encompassing factors like colour accuracy, sharpness, dynamic range, and low-light performance.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A primary consideration for selecting any camera, directly impacting the final look of recorded footage.

## Key properties or components
- Colour vibrancy
- Lighting balance
- Sharpness
- Noise levels
- Detail retention
