## Definition
The practice where experienced professionals in the film industry guide and advise less experienced individuals. This can involve sharing knowledge, offering support, and setting positive examples.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for the development of new talent and the preservation of industry standards and values, such as professional conduct and respect.

## Key properties or components
- Knowledge transfer
- Guidance
- Role modelling
- Support system

## Contradictions or debates
None.
