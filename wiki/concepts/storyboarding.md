## Definition
The process of visually planning a sequence of shots in a film or animation, often depicted as a series of drawings or images, to outline the narrative flow and identify potential pacing or continuity issues.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Used as a crucial step in the AI filmmaking process to review and ensure continuity and pacing of the generated clips before final editing.

## Key properties or components
- Visual planning
- Sequence arrangement
- Continuity check
- Pacing assessment
