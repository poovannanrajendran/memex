## Definition
The process of quickly creating a functional model or sample of a product or system to test concepts and gather feedback. It allows for iterative development and early validation of ideas.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The platform demonstrated in the video enables rapid prototyping, allowing users to quickly build and test ideas for websites and applications, a valuable skill for innovation and efficiency.

## Key properties or components
- Speed of creation
- Iterative design
- Early feedback integration
- Cost-effectiveness
- Risk reduction
