## Definition
A moped is a low-powered motorcycle that can be pedaled like a bicycle. The term 'moped' is a portmanteau of 'motor' and 'pedal'.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The TVS 50 discussed in the video is a type of moped, highlighting its specific design and function within this category.

## Key properties or components
- Low-powered engine
- Pedal-assist or pedal-start functionality
- Lightweight frame
- Lower top speeds compared to motorcycles
