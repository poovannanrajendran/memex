## Definition
An online platform that aggregates car leasing deals from multiple brokers and finance companies, allowing users to compare options based on various criteria.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Recommended as the best way to find competitive lease deals by enabling users to view a wide range of offers and terms.

## Key properties or components
- Aggregates deals from multiple brokers
- Facilitates comparison
- Helps find best prices and terms
