## Definition
The use of artificial intelligence algorithms to perform or assist in the editing of images and videos. This can include tasks like object removal, background generation, style transfer, and automatic enhancements.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlighted as a strong suit for Samsung, with features like generative edit and sketch-to-image, while Apple is noted as being behind in this area.

## Key properties or components
- Generative Edit
- Sketch to Image
- Object Removal
- Automatic Enhancement
- Content-Aware Fill
