## Definition
In the context of AI services, API keys are credentials that grant access to a service, while usage-based pricing means customers are charged based on the volume of their consumption (e.g., processing time, data used).

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This model is crucial for the sustainability of the open-source Higgsfield AI, as it allows the free software to leverage powerful, paid AI processing infrastructure without bearing the upfront cost, passing the direct cost to the end-user.

## Key properties or components
- Authentication (API Key)
- Variable Cost
- Scalability
- Resource Consumption Tracking
