## Definition
A web series is a series of scripted digital videos, usually available on the internet, released episodically. They can be found on platforms like YouTube, Netflix, or dedicated streaming services.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source content is identified as a mini web series, placing it within this media format.

## Key properties or components
- Episodic release
- Internet distribution
- Digital video format
