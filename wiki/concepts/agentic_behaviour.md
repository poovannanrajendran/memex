## Definition
Refers to the capability of an AI system to act autonomously, make decisions, and take actions towards achieving a goal, often by interacting with its environment or tools.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Distinguishes more advanced AI coding tools (like Windsurf/Cursor) from simpler chatbots, as agentic tools can perform more complex, iterative tasks within a development context.

## Key properties or components
- Autonomy
- Goal-oriented action
- Tool usage
- Planning and execution
- Iteration
