## Definition
Homeownership is the state of owning a house or other dwelling. It is often seen as a significant financial milestone and a form of investment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Collins challenges the conventional view of homeownership as a sound financial investment, arguing it should be treated as a lifestyle choice with significant associated costs.

## Key properties or components
- Ownership of property
- Mortgage payments
- Property taxes
- Maintenance and repairs
- Potential for equity building
- Lifestyle benefits

## Contradictions or debates
While traditionally viewed as a cornerstone of wealth building, Collins posits it can be a financial drain if not approached with the correct expectations.
