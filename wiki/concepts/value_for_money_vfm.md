## Definition
A measure of the benefits received from a product or service relative to its cost. It implies that the quality, features, or performance justify the price paid.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The core selling point of the gadget being reviewed, emphasizing its affordability and extensive functionality.

## Key properties or components
- Cost-effectiveness
- Feature set
- Performance
- Durability
