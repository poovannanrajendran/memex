## Definition
ProRes is a video compression format developed by Apple for use in post-production workflows. It offers higher quality and more flexibility in editing than standard compressed formats, but requires significantly more storage space.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A professional video format that the gadget enables recording in, indicating a higher-end use case for the storage expansion.

## Key properties or components
- High quality video
- Editing flexibility
- Large file sizes
- Requires significant storage
