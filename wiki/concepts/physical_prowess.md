## Definition
The combination of strength, stamina, agility, and endurance that allows an individual to perform demanding physical tasks.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for Formula 1 drivers to withstand the G-forces, maintain control of the car, and perform consistently throughout a race.

## Key properties or components
- Strength
- Stamina
- Endurance
- Reaction Time
