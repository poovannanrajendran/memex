## Definition
A flagship smartphone is the top-of-the-line model offered by a mobile device manufacturer. These devices typically feature the latest technology, best performance, most advanced cameras, and premium build quality, representing the company's most ambitious product.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Flagship devices like the iPhone and Samsung Ultra models are often benchmarks for technological advancement and consumer trends. Their performance and features are key considerations for anyone interested in high-end mobile technology.

## Key properties or components
- Latest technology
- Highest performance
- Premium features
- Top-tier pricing
- Manufacturer's best offering
