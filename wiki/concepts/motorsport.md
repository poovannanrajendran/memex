## Definition
The competitive arena of motor racing. It encompasses various forms of racing with motor-powered vehicles.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The core domain driving the development of the Ford GT40, which was designed specifically for racing against competitors like Ferrari.

## Key properties or components
- Competition
- Speed
- Reliability
- Engineering
- Endurance
