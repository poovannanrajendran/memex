## Definition
An event organised for a team, typically outside the regular work environment, intended to foster team bonding, improve morale, and enhance collaboration.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The primary subject of the dialogue, with discussions focusing on its planning, inclusivity, and potential outcomes.

## Key properties or components
- Team building
- Morale boosting
- Social interaction
- Event planning

## Contradictions or debates
Initial plans for an 'outing' evolved into a 'dinner' due to various concerns, indicating a shift in the nature of the event.
