## Definition
A skin lubricant applied to the chamois pad of cycling shorts or directly to the skin to reduce friction and prevent chafing during long rides.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Helps to increase comfort and prevent soreness, particularly on extended cycling sessions, by minimising skin irritation.

## Key properties or components
- Friction reduction
- Chafing prevention
- Skin lubrication
