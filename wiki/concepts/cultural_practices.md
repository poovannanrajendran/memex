## Definition
The customary behaviors, traditions, and social norms of a particular group or society.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The vlog points out the practice of mothers carrying children on their backs using a blanket or cloth, observed both in Canada and widely in Morocco, as a notable cultural practice.

## Key properties or components
- Child-carrying methods
- Traditional attire
- Daily life observations
- Respect for heritage
