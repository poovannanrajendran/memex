## Definition
A denim finishing process that uses a chemical (typically a mild form of bleach or acid) to create a faded, worn-in look on fabric. It results in a marbled or streaky appearance.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A specific type of finishing applied to the t-shirts in question, which the business owner evaluated for its aesthetic quality.

## Key properties or components
- Faded appearance
- Marbled or streaky texture
- Chemical treatment
- Applied to denim and cotton fabrics

## Contradictions or debates
The owner found the acid wash on the sample t-shirts to be 'too intense' or 'too dipped', suggesting a potential issue with the execution of this finishing technique by the supplier.
