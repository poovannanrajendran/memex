## Definition
A short film or video that accompanies a musical recording, typically featuring the performing artist(s) singing the song and often including narrative or conceptual elements. Music videos are a primary promotional tool for songs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source is a music video for a film song, showcasing the visual aspect intended to complement the audio.

## Key properties or components
- Visual narrative
- Performance footage
- Editing
- Cinematography
- Art direction
