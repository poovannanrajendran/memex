## Definition
The 'meta' in this context refers to the current trend or most effective strategy in CPU cooling. It signifies what is considered the best approach or type of cooler that provides the most value or performance at a given time.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding the current 'meta' helps in making informed purchasing decisions for hardware that offers the best performance for the price or meets specific cooling needs.

## Key properties or components
- Current trends
- Best value proposition
- Optimal performance-to-price ratio
