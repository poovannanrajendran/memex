## Definition
The process of computationally identifying and categorising opinions expressed in text, especially to determine whether the writer's attitude towards a particular topic, product, etc., is positive, negative, or neutral.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It's a key function of one of the AI agents, contributing to the overall decision-making process for stock trading by gauging market mood from news.

## Key properties or components
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Opinion mining
- Polarity detection (positive, negative, neutral)
