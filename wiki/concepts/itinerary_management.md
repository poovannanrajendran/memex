## Definition
Itinerary management involves organizing and planning all aspects of a trip, including flights, accommodation, activities, and transportation, into a cohesive schedule.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Streamlines travel by consolidating booking details, providing a clear overview of the trip, and offering easy access to essential information, often with offline capabilities.

## Key properties or components
- Consolidation of Bookings
- Scheduling
- Information Hub
- Offline Access
