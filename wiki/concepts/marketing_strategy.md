## Definition
A marketing strategy is a long-term plan designed to promote and sell a product or service. It involves identifying target audiences and developing approaches to reach and engage them.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for reaching potential customers and differentiating a business in a competitive market.

## Key properties or components
- Target audience identification
- Promotional activities
- Sales objectives
- Brand positioning

## Contradictions or debates
