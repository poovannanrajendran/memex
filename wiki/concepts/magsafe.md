## Definition
MagSafe is a technology used by Apple for magnetic alignment and wireless charging of its devices, ensuring optimal connection and power delivery.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
MagSafe compatibility is mentioned in relation to new charging accessories and improved wireless charging speeds for the new iPhones.

## Key properties or components
- Magnetic alignment
- Wireless charging
- Accessory ecosystem
