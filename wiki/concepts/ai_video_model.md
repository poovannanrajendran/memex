## Definition
An artificial intelligence model that generates video content, typically from textual prompts, images, or other inputs. These models use deep learning techniques to create sequences of images that form a video.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a significant advancement in content creation, enabling automated and stylized video production. LTX 2.3 is an example of this technology, made freely available.

## Key properties or components
- Generates video from various inputs
- Utilizes deep learning
- Can produce high-resolution output (e.g., 4K)
- May include audio and effects synchronization
