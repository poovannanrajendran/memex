## Definition
The specific methods and skills employed by a batsman to hit the ball in cricket. This includes techniques for both attacking and defensive play.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Mastering batting techniques, like those for playing spin, can significantly improve performance and efficiency, akin to optimising processes in a professional setting.

## Key properties or components
- Involves bat-and-ball coordination
- Includes defensive and attacking strokes
- Requires understanding of the ball's trajectory
- Involves body positioning
