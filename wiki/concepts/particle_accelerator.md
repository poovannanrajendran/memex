## Definition
A device that uses electromagnetic fields to propel charged particles to very high speeds and energies. They are crucial tools for scientific research, particularly in physics and medicine.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a significant advancement in miniaturisation for scientific tools, with potential applications in medicine.

## Key properties or components

- Uses electromagnetic fields
- Can be large-scale (LHC) or miniature (new device)
