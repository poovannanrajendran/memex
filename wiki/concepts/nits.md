## Definition
A nit is a unit of luminance, used to measure the brightness of a display. One nit is equivalent to one candela per square meter (cd/m²). Higher nit values indicate a brighter screen.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Important for display clarity in various lighting conditions. The video recommends a minimum of 400 nits, preferably 500, for laptop screens used in data science.

## Key properties or components
- Luminance measurement
- cd/m²
- Display brightness
- Outdoor visibility
