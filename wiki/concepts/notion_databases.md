## Definition
Databases in Notion are a powerful way to organize and manage structured information. They consist of entries (each being a page itself) with properties (columns) that can hold various types of data, and can be viewed in multiple formats like tables, boards, calendars, and timelines.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Databases are central to Notion's power, enabling sophisticated data management, project tracking, and custom system creation beyond simple notes.

## Key properties or components
- Multiple views (Table, Board, Calendar, Timeline, List, Gallery)
- Properties (columns) for data types (text, number, date, select, relation, etc.)
- Entries (rows) which are themselves pages
- Filtering and Sorting options
- Grouping options
- Calculations
- Import/Export capabilities
