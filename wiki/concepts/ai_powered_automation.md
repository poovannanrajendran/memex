## Definition
Automation that utilizes artificial intelligence to perform tasks, offering capabilities beyond traditional rule-based systems. AI allows for adaptation, learning, and handling of complex, unstructured data.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This represents the core technological advancement of Director AI, enabling it to handle dynamic web content and adapt to changes.

## Key properties or components
- Adaptability
- Dynamic content handling
- Problem-solving (e.g., CAPTCHAs)
- Real-time adjustments
