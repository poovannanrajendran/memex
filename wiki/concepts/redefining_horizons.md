## Definition
This concept refers to the continuous process of expanding one's personal boundaries, aspirations, and understanding of what is possible. It involves challenging existing perspectives and setting new goals.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It encourages personal growth and prevents stagnation by prompting individuals to seek new opportunities and knowledge.

## Key properties or components
- Self-reflection
- Goal setting
- Continuous learning
- Adaptability
