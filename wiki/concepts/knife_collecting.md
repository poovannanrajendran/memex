## Definition
The hobby or practice of acquiring, curating, and appreciating knives. This can encompass a wide range of types, including culinary, tactical, historical, and decorative blades.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a personal passion and area of interest for the presenter, showcasing an appreciation for design, craftsmanship, and the utility of blades.

## Key properties or components
- Acquisition of diverse knife types
- Appreciation for design and craftsmanship
- Curating a collection
- Understanding of different knife purposes
