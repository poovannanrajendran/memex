## Definition
Data privacy refers to the measures taken to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. It encompasses the rights of individuals regarding their data.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A significant benefit of self-hosting, as it allows businesses to keep sensitive customer and operational data on their own servers, reducing risks associated with third-party data breaches and enhancing user trust.

## Key properties or components
- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability
- Compliance
- User consent
