## Definition
The process of creating text captions that appear on a video screen, displaying dialogue or other audio information. This can be done manually or through automated tools using speech recognition.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for accessibility, search engine optimization (SEO), and reaching a broader audience by providing content in multiple languages or for viewers who prefer or require captions.

## Key properties or components
- Automated speech recognition
- Multi-language support
- Accuracy and editing
- Integration with video platforms
