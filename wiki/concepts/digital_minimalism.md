## Definition
A philosophy that proponents argue is the antidote to the attention economy; it is about intentionally and mindfully using technology to support your values, rather than letting it dictate your attention.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Helps in reducing digital clutter, minimising distractions, and maintaining focus on essential tasks, leading to a more organised and productive digital environment.

## Key properties or components
- Intentional technology use
- Focus on value
- Reduced distractions
- Decluttering digital spaces

## Contradictions or debates
None.
