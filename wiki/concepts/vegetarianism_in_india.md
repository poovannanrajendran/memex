## Definition
A dietary practice followed by a significant portion of the Indian population, where individuals abstain from consuming meat, poultry, and fish. This is often rooted in religious, cultural, and ethical beliefs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It explains the prevalence and importance of dishes like paneer curry, which cater to a large vegetarian demographic in India.

## Key properties or components
- High prevalence
- Cultural influence
- Religious undertones
