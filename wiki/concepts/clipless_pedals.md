## Definition
Pedals that require special cycling shoes with cleats that lock into the pedal mechanism, allowing the rider to pull up as well as push down.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances pedalling efficiency and power transfer but requires a learning curve for safe engagement and disengagement, necessitating practice in a controlled environment.

## Key properties or components
- Requires cleated shoes
- Improved power transfer
- Learning curve
- Requires practice
