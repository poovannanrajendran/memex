## Definition
Official authorisations issued by a government or other regulatory body that allow the construction or renovation of a building.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The absence of permits was the primary legal lever used to terminate an unsafe lease in the [[building_inspection_violations]] source. It represents a foundational "red flag" in facility management.
