## Definition
App switching refers to the process of moving between different open applications on a computer or mobile device.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Efficient app switching is crucial for productivity, allowing users to multitask seamlessly and reduce time spent navigating between tasks.

## Key properties or components
- Multitasking
- Workflow efficiency
- Keyboard shortcuts
- Third-party enhancers
