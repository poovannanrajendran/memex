## Definition
The ability of an AI agent framework to smoothly connect and operate harmoniously with an organisation's existing technology stack, data sources, and infrastructure (e.g., on-premises or cloud environments).

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Facilitates the smooth adoption and deployment of AI agents within existing enterprise ecosystems, reducing friction, ensuring compatibility, and maximising the utility of current IT investments.

## Key properties or components
- Compatibility with existing tech stack
- Compatibility with data sources
- Compatibility with infrastructure
- Minimal friction in deployment

## Contradictions or debates
None.
