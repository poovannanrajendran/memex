## Definition
A design philosophy where applications (apps) are the primary means of interaction and service delivery, often optimized for touchscreens and mobile devices, contrasting with traditional website or desktop application design.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key reason the creator finds the iPad Pro appealing, as many modern services are better experienced through their dedicated apps.

## Key properties or components
- Optimization for touch interfaces
- Intuitive user experience
- Cross-platform consistency (mobile vs. web)
- Offline capabilities
- Preference for apps over websites

## Contradictions or debates
None.
