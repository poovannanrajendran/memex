## Definition
A legal document or status indicating that a building is safe and fit for human habitation or use, often granted after a final inspection confirming compliance with all codes and regulations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for the legal and safe operation of a building. Its loss in this case meant the building could not be legally or safely used, rendering the lease voidable.

## Key properties or components
- Granted by city or local authorities
- Requires compliance with building codes and safety standards
- Can be revoked if safety standards are not met

## Contradictions or debates
None.
