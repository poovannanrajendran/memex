## Definition
Humour derived from situations, interactions, and peculiarities found in a typical office or workplace environment. It often includes jokes about colleagues, bosses, meetings, and the general absurdity of corporate life.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Relatable content that fosters a sense of community and shared experience among viewers who work in office settings.

## Key properties or components
- Situational comedy
- Observational humour
- Relatability
- Character-based jokes
