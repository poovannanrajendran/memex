## Definition
The obligation of a citizen to participate in the life of a community and nation, including fulfilling responsibilities such as paying taxes for the maintenance of public services and collective goods.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video contrasts the framing of tax as a 'burden' with the idea of tax as a 'civic duty', advocating for a re-framing that emphasizes collective responsibility and benefit.

## Key properties or components
- Responsibility towards society
- Contribution to public services
- Maintenance of collective goods
- Participation in community welfare

## Contradictions or debates
The common perception of tax as a 'burden' from which one seeks 'relief' conflicts with the idea of tax as a positive civic duty.
