## Definition
A curated collection or display of the most successful examples of cold emails that achieved their intended outcome, such as securing a response from a prominent individual.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Serves as a proof of concept and a learning resource, demonstrating what works in cold emailing by showcasing successful examples.

## Key properties or components
- Collection of successful emails
- Features responses from notable figures
- Acts as a learning tool
