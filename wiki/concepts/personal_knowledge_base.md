## Definition
A structured collection of an individual's knowledge, notes, and insights, designed for easy access, retrieval, and synthesis. It is often built using digital tools.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the underlying principle of a 'second brain', representing the organised accumulation of information that fuels its utility.

## Key properties or components
- Digital storage
- Organised data
- Personalised content
- Searchable
