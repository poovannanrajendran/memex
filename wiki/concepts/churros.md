## Definition
Churros are a type of fried dough confection, popular in Spain, Portugal, France, and Latin America. They are typically elongated and ridged, often served with hot chocolate for dipping.

## Key properties or components
- Fried dough
- Often ridged
- Can be dipped in chocolate
- Can be sugared and spiced
- Varying textures (crunchy exterior, soft/pillowy interior)
