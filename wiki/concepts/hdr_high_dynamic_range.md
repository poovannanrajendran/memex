## Definition
A technology that captures a greater range of luminance between the darkest and brightest parts of an image, resulting in more detailed shadows and highlights.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances the realism and detail in video footage, especially in scenes with high contrast.

## Key properties or components
- HLG (Hybrid Log Gamma)
- Standard HDR
- Post-production grading

## Contradictions or debates
The HDR effect can sometimes appear heavy-handed and unnatural if not properly controlled or dialled down.
