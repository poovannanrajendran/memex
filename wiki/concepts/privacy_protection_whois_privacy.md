## Definition
A service offered by domain registrars that replaces the registrant's personal contact information (name, address, email, phone number) in the public WHOIS database with the registrar's details. This safeguards the registrant's privacy.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for individuals and businesses who wish to protect their personal information from public exposure and potential spam or phishing attempts.

## Key properties or components
- Masks registrant data
- Replaces with registrar's data
- Available for free or paid
- Protects against spam/phishing

## Contradictions or debates
None.
