## Definition
Relationship dynamics refer to the interplay of emotions, behaviors, and interactions between individuals in a relationship. These dynamics shape the communication patterns, power structures, and overall health of the connection.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The sketch uses a comedic scenario to highlight unequal power dynamics within a marriage, where one partner may feel like a 'junior partner' or 'class citizen'.

## Key properties or components
- Communication patterns
- Power balance
- Interpersonal interactions
- Emotional exchange
