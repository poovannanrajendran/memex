## Definition
The process of minimizing the non-display areas (bezels) around a smartphone screen to maximize the screen-to-body ratio and enhance immersion.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key visual upgrade for the iPhone 16 Pro models, contributing to their larger screen sizes within similar external dimensions.

## Key properties or components
- Slimmer borders
- Increased screen-to-body ratio
- Modern aesthetic

## Contradictions or debates
None.
