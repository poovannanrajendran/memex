## Definition
This Claude code skill allows the AI to access and utilise real-world design references and examples, moving beyond generic templates.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Helps to generate more unique, relevant, and high-quality design outputs by grounding the AI's creativity in actual design trends and aesthetics.

## Key properties or components

- Generation of specific examples
- Reduction of generic outputs
- Improved design quality
