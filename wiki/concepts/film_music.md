## Definition
Music composed or specifically chosen to accompany a film. It is used to enhance the narrative, create atmosphere, and evoke emotions in the audience.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Film music is crucial for the emotional impact and overall artistic success of a movie, as demonstrated by the track 'Makkamishi' from 'Brother'.

## Key properties or components
- Score
- Soundtrack
- Diegetic
- Non-diegetic
