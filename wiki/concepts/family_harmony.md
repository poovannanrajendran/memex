## Definition
Family harmony refers to a state of peaceful and cooperative relationships among family members. It involves mutual respect, understanding, and a willingness to resolve conflicts constructively.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is a fundamental aspect of a healthy family unit, contributing to the emotional well-being of its members and the overall stability of the household.

## Key properties or components
- Mutual respect
- Understanding
- Effective communication
- Conflict resolution
- Cooperation

## Contradictions or debates
Some may argue that excessive focus on harmony can suppress individual needs or dissent, leading to underlying tensions.
