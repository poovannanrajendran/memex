## Definition
The strategic use of narratives to connect a brand with its audience on an emotional level. It involves crafting stories that communicate a brand's values, mission, and impact, making it more relatable and memorable.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Brand storytelling is key to building trust and recognition for any initiative or entity, including those within Poovi's Second Brain that aim to communicate complex ideas like AI in insurance.

## Key properties or components
- Emotional Connection
- Relatability
- Memorable Narrative
- Brand Values Communication
- Customer Journey Integration
