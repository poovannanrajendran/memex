## Definition
A gathering of individuals who share common interests, such as technology or a specific brand. These events facilitate networking, knowledge sharing, and direct engagement with products or creators.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Such meetups foster a sense of community among technology enthusiasts and provide direct engagement opportunities with new products and influencers.

## Key properties or components
- Shared interests
- Networking opportunities
- Product demonstrations
- Community building
