## Definition
AI Game Development refers to the use of artificial intelligence technologies to automate or assist in the creation of video games. This can range from generating game assets and levels to building entire game mechanics or even entire games from user prompts.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the video, demonstrating a novel application of AI that democratises game development by lowering the technical barrier to entry.

## Key properties or components
- Prompt-based generation
- Automated game creation
- Iterative modification
- No-code development
