## Definition
A solid-state drive that connects externally to a computer or tablet, typically via USB-C or Thunderbolt. SSDs offer significantly faster data read/write speeds compared to traditional HDDs.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for managing large files and improving performance for tasks like video editing directly on an iPad, offering faster access and more storage than the device's internal capacity.

## Key properties or components
- High speed
- Durability
- Compact size
- Large capacities
- Portability
