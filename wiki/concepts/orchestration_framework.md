## Definition
A type of framework specifically designed to manage, coordinate, and sequence complex workflows and interactions, often involving multiple AI agents or system components.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Critical for ensuring that tasks are executed in a logical order, inter-agent communication is managed, and the overall AI system operates cohesively and efficiently to achieve its objectives.

## Key properties or components
- Coordinates complex workflows
- Manages agent interactions
- Sequences tasks
