## Definition
This refers to the practice of running artificial intelligence models directly on a user's personal hardware, such as a computer or mobile device, rather than accessing them through a remote server or cloud service.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is crucial for reducing costs, enhancing privacy, and providing users with greater control over AI tools like video generation, as opposed to relying on subscription-based online platforms.

## Key properties or components
- No internet dependency for operation
- User owns computational power
- No usage limits or throttling
- Enhanced privacy and data control
- Requires capable local hardware
