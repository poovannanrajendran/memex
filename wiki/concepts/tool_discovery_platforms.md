## Definition
These are platforms or systems designed to help users find, compare, and select appropriate tools for their needs. They often aggregate information, provide search functionalities, and categorise offerings.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Addresses the challenge of information overload and simplifies the selection process for users, particularly in rapidly evolving fields like AI.

## Key properties or components
- Search functionality
- Categorisation
- Information aggregation
- Filtering by features or pricing
