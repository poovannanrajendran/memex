## Definition
Honoring a legacy involves recognizing and continuing the values, principles, or achievements of a person who has passed away. It's about integrating their positive influence into one's own life and actions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a framework for personal growth and ethical decision-making. In this context, it motivates the narrator to leave a negative situation and live according to his father's example of integrity.

## Key properties or components
- Emulating positive values
- Continuing positive influence
- Living with integrity
- Making value-aligned choices
