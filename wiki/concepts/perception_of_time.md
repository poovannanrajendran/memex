## Definition
How individuals subjectively experience and interpret the passage of time. This perception can be significantly altered by factors like engagement, emotional state, and the presence or absence of information.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source illustrates that the perception of waiting time is more critical to satisfaction than the objective duration of the wait.

## Key properties or components
- Subjective
- Malleable
- Influenced by context
