## Definition
A distinctive dialect and vocabulary associated with the Tirunelveli region of Tamil Nadu, India. It is often characterised by unique pronunciations and word choices.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Adds local flavour and authenticity to the comedy, making it more relatable to audiences familiar with the dialect and potentially offering a unique comedic texture.

## Key properties or components
- Regional dialect
- Unique vocabulary
- Specific pronunciation
- Cultural identity
