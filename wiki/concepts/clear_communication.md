## Definition
Expressing ideas, requests, or information in a direct, unambiguous, and easily understandable manner, ensuring the recipient can accurately interpret the message.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A core skill for effective AI prompting, ensuring that the AI receives precise instructions and context, leading to better prediction and output.

## Key properties or components
- Direct language
- Unambiguous instructions
- Contextual relevance
- Certainty/uncertainty expression
