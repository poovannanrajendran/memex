## Definition
Notion templates are pre-designed page structures or databases that users can utilise as a starting point for their own content. They offer a quick way to set up common pages or systems, saving time and providing inspiration.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Templates significantly speed up the process of setting up new pages or databases, offering best-practice examples and reducing the learning curve for new users.

## Key properties or components
- Pre-defined layouts
- Example content
- Database structures
- Customizable
- Template Gallery
- Template Buttons
