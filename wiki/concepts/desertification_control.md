## Definition
The process of preventing or reversing the degradation of land in arid, semi-arid, and dry sub-humid areas, often through afforestation and water management.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for maintaining ecological balance, protecting infrastructure like roads, and supporting human settlements in desert regions.

## Key properties or components
- Afforestation
- Water management (pipes, wellhouses)
- Sand dune stabilisation
- Long-term maintenance
