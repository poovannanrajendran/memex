## Definition
An incremental update refers to a minor enhancement or revision to a product, typically involving small changes in features, performance, or design, rather than a complete overhaul.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The iPhone 16 is described as an incremental update, which raises questions about its value proposition compared to its predecessors and the rapid advancements in AI.

## Key properties or components
- Small changes
- Minor performance boosts
- Refined design
- Cost-effective for manufacturers

## Contradictions or debates
While the hardware is an incremental update, the potential for future software features (like Apple Intelligence) is presented as a significant, non-incremental development.
