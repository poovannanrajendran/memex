## Definition
A common shot in cricket where the batter hits the ball along the ground towards the off-side. It is typically played when the ball is pitched on or outside the off stump.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A foundational shot in cricket batting, crucial for scoring runs and demonstrating technique.

## Key properties or components
- Hitting the ball along the ground
- Targeting the off-side
- Requires specific footwork
- Involves a follow-through
