## Definition
An evening shutdown is a daily ritual where one reviews unfinished tasks and plans specific actions for the next day before ending their workday.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This practice helps the speaker to psychologically detach from work and organize their thoughts, promoting better rest and preparation for subsequent deep work sessions.

## Key properties or components
- Reviewing unfinished tasks
- Planning next day's actions
- Daily ritual
