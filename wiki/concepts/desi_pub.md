## Definition
A pub that serves Indian cuisine or offers an Indian-inspired twist on traditional pub fare. These establishments blend British pub culture with South Asian flavours.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a fusion of culinary traditions, creating unique dining experiences like the roast dinner with Indian elements.

## Key properties or components
- Indian-inspired menu
- Pub atmosphere
- Fusion cuisine
