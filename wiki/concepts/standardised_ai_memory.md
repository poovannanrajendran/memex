## Definition
A unified and consistent approach to storing, accessing, and sharing an AI's learned information, rules, and contextual knowledge across different AI tools and operational environments.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is a key benefit and objective of Agentic Stack. It is essential for enabling different AI agents to collaborate effectively, share knowledge, and avoid redundant learning, significantly enhancing the overall efficiency and intelligence of AI systems.

## Key properties or components
- Shared context
- Consistent data structure
- Accessible by multiple agents
- Enables knowledge transfer
