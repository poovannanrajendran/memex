## Definition
AI-powered learning utilises artificial intelligence to enhance and personalise the educational experience. This can involve AI tutors, intelligent content summarisation, adaptive learning paths, and tools that help users comprehend complex information more effectively.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Makes learning more efficient and accessible by providing tools that can summarise, explain, and interact with educational materials in new ways.

## Key properties or components
- Content summarisation
- Interactive Q&A
- Personalised learning paths
- Multi-modal understanding
