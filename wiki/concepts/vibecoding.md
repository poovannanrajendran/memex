## Definition
A colloquial term for the process of intuitively or collaboratively building AI applications, especially within a conversational AI interface, often implying a low-code or no-code approach.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a user-friendly paradigm for AI app development, making it accessible to a broader audience.

## Key properties or components
- Intuitive interface
- Low-code/no-code
- Rapid development
- AI-powered functionality

## Contradictions or debates
None.
