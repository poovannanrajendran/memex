## Definition
Structured prompts involve organising input for an AI model in a specific format, often using tags or delimiters, to clearly delineate different types of information or instructions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This technique is presented as a method to improve an LLM's understanding and focus, leading to better results.

## Key properties or components
- Use of Tags
- Clear Input Organisation
- Improved Model Comprehension
- Separation of Data and Instructions
