## Definition
An 'OS of Agents' suggests an integrated system where various specialised AI agents can be deployed, managed, and orchestrated, similar to how an operating system manages applications on a computer.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This conceptualisation aims to make advanced AI capabilities more accessible and manageable, allowing users to leverage a suite of AI tools for diverse tasks without needing deep technical expertise in each.

## Key properties or components
- Agent management
- Task orchestration
- Unified interface
- Scalability of AI functions
