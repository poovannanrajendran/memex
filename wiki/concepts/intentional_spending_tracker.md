## Definition
An intentional spending tracker is a tool designed to help individuals consciously monitor and manage their expenditure. It encourages users to align their spending with their financial goals and values, rather than making impulsive purchases.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
In the context of financial well-being, such trackers promote mindful consumption and help identify areas where savings can be made, supporting broader financial objectives like purchasing expensive items like electric cars.

## Key properties or components
- Budgeting tool
- Expense tracking
- Goal alignment
- Mindful consumption

## Contradictions or debates
Requires consistent effort and discipline from the user to be effective; can feel restrictive for some.
