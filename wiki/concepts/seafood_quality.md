## Definition
The standard of freshness, size, and taste of seafood, often influenced by sourcing and preparation methods.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Emphasized through the description of unusually large, handpicked scallops and butterfied prawns.

## Key properties or components
- Freshness
- Size
- Sourcing method (e.g., diver-picked)
- Preparation (e.g., butterfied)

## Contradictions or debates
