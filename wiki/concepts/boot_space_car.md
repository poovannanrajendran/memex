## Definition
The storage compartment at the rear of a vehicle, commonly referred to as the trunk or boot. It is used for carrying luggage and other items.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key practical consideration for car buyers, particularly families or those needing to transport goods.

## Key properties or components
- Measured in litres
- Can be expanded by folding rear seats
- May include underfloor storage or be limited by battery placement
