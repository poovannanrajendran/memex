## Definition
The quality of having experience, knowledge, and good judgment; the ability to apply understanding to make sound decisions and guide one's actions, often accompanied by foresight and prudence.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Embodied by Sahadeva, who possesses foresight but is bound by cosmic designs, highlighting the burdens, responsibilities, and sometimes the necessary restraint associated with great knowledge.

## Key properties or components
- experience
- knowledge
- good judgment
- foresight
- understanding
- prudence

## Contradictions or debates
None.
