## Definition
A cooking pan made of cast iron, known for its durability, heat retention, and ability to develop a natural non-stick surface over time with proper seasoning.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The primary cooking vessel used by Gordon Ramsay for searing and cooking the breakfast sandwich ingredients.

## Key properties or components
- High heat retention
- Even heat distribution
- Durability
- Requires seasoning
