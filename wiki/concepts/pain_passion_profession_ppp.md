## Definition
A framework for identifying target customer segments based on their problems (pain), interests (passion), or existing professional skills.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a systematic approach for entrepreneurs to choose the 'who' they will serve, ensuring relevance and potential for high value exchange.

## Key properties or components
- Problem-solving focus
- Alignment with interests
- Leveraging existing expertise
- Customer identification
