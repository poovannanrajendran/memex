## Definition
A principle advocating for basing decisions and understanding on verifiable data and evidence rather than personal beliefs, assumptions, or the views of others.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for objective analysis and sound decision-making in business, minimising the impact of bias and misinformation.

## Key properties or components
- Evidence-based
- Objective analysis
- Data-driven
