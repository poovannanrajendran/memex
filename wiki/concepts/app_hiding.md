## Definition
App hiding is a feature or technique that allows users to conceal applications from their device's main interface, such as the home screen. This can be done for aesthetic reasons, to declutter the interface, or for privacy.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Allows users to manage their home screen appearance and potentially enhance privacy by making certain apps less visible.

## Key properties or components
- Concealment of applications
- User interface management
- Potential privacy enhancement

## Contradictions or debates
While apps can be hidden from the home screen, they are still installed on the device and accessible through search functions or the App Library.
