## Definition
A set of persistent, high-level instructions or system prompts configured within an AI IDE or assistant. These rules dictate the AI's general behaviour, preferences, and adherence to best practices across all interactions or specific projects.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Automates recurring instructions, ensuring consistency and reducing the need for repeated prompting.

## Key properties or components
- System prompts
- Persistent instructions
- Workspace-specific or global
- Behavioural guidelines
- Constraint enforcement
