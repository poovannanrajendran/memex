## Definition
A musical composition created specifically for a motion picture, often integrated into the narrative or presented as a performance piece within the film. These songs can be crucial for setting the mood, developing characters, or advancing the plot.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source content is entirely dedicated to a film song, highlighting its role in a movie's promotion and artistic presentation.

- Rhythm
- Vocals
- Instrumentation
