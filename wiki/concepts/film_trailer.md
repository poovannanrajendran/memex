## Definition
A film trailer is a short promotional video that showcases snippets of an upcoming motion picture. It is designed to generate interest and excitement among potential viewers.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central as the source content is an official film trailer.

## Key properties or components
- Short duration
- Promotional purpose
- Highlights key scenes/characters
- Aims to attract audience

## Contradictions or debates
