## Definition
The process of extracting structured information from unstructured resume documents using AI and natural language processing techniques. This allows for easier analysis and matching against job requirements.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A fundamental capability of Claude AI that enables the job hack by understanding the user's qualifications and experience.

## Key properties or components
- Information extraction
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Structured data output
- Compatibility with job matching
