## Definition
A power-saving or heat-reducing measure that automatically lowers the processing speed of a CPU or GPU when it overheats. This prevents damage but can lead to performance degradation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding thermal throttling is important for maintaining optimal performance of the Raspberry Pi 5, especially under heavy loads, necessitating the use of heatsinks and fans.

## Key properties or components
- Performance reduction
- Heat management
- CPU/GPU speed adjustment
- Protection against overheating
