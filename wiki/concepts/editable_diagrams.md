## Definition
Diagrams that can be easily modified after their initial creation, allowing users to update content, change layouts, or adjust visual elements without starting from scratch.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances the utility of diagrams by enabling iterative refinement and adaptation to changing requirements, crucial for project planning and documentation.

## Key properties or components
- Modifiable elements
- Vector-based or structured data
- User-friendly editing interface
- Supports updates and revisions
