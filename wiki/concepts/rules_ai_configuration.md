## Definition
Customizable directives or instructions, often written in natural language, that guide the behaviour and output of AI coding assistants. These can be global or specific to a project.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Allow developers to enforce coding standards, best practices, and specific workflows, ensuring the AI's output aligns with project requirements and preferences.

## Key properties or components
- Natural language configuration
- Global and workspace scope
- Behavioral guidance
- Enforcement of best practices
- Customizable workflows
