## Definition
User Interface design is the process of creating the visual elements and interactive components of a digital product, focusing on how users interact with a system or application.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The mention of Canva suggests that Pinnacle AI prioritises a user-friendly and visually appealing interface, making it accessible for users without extensive design training.

- Usability
- Aesthetics
- Interactivity
- Accessibility
