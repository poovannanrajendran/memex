## Definition
Shopify themes are pre-designed website templates that control the look and feel of an online store built on the Shopify platform. They offer various layouts, styles, and functionalities that can be customised to match a brand's identity.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Choosing and customising the right theme is crucial for creating an appealing and user-friendly online store, as demonstrated in the tutorial.

## Key properties or components
- Customisable design
- Responsive layout
- Brand representation
- User experience enhancement
- Free and premium options
