Advice and techniques tailored for individuals new to piloting drones. These tips often cover basic flight controls, safety procedures, understanding drone limitations, and how to achieve better footage.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for new drone pilots to build confidence, avoid accidents, and quickly improve their flying and filming skills.

## Key properties or components

- Emphasis on safety and regulations
- Recommendations for equipment
- Guidance on achieving specific shots
