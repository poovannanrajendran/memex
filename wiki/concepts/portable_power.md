## Definition
The availability of electrical power for devices when away from mains outlets. This is typically achieved using portable chargers or power banks with sufficient capacity.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Ensures continuous device operation when working remotely or travelling, preventing interruptions due to depleted battery levels.

## Key properties or components
- Capacity (Wh, mAh)
- Output Ports (USB-C, USB-A)
- Portability
- Rechargeability
