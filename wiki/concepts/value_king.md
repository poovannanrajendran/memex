## Definition
A colloquial term used to describe a product or service that offers exceptionally high value for its cost. It implies that the benefits received far outweigh the price paid.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Identifying 'value kings' like Gemini Flash 2.0 allows developers to prioritise cost-effective solutions that still meet their performance needs, crucial for budget-conscious projects.

## Key properties or components
- High benefit-to-cost ratio
- Exceptional performance for price
- Preferred choice for budget-conscious users
