## Definition
This concept contrasts two distinct parenting styles: 'support' involves empowering a child and offering help through difficulties, while 'control' means dictating a child's actions and choices to prevent mistakes or deviation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding this distinction is crucial for developing effective parenting strategies that foster healthy child development and independence, aligning with personal growth objectives.

## Key properties or components
- Empowerment
- Guidance
- Autonomy
- Boundaries
- Consequences
