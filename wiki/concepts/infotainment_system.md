## Definition
An infotainment system in a car integrates audio, visual, and information services, typically controlled via a touchscreen display. It often includes features like navigation, Bluetooth connectivity, and smartphone integration.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The infotainment system in the new Duster has been updated, but experienced glitches during testing, highlighting potential reliability issues.

## Key properties or components
- Touchscreen interface
- Smartphone integration (Apple CarPlay, Android Auto)
- Bluetooth connectivity
- Navigation (potential)

## Contradictions or debates
The reviewer encountered significant glitches with the infotainment system, describing it as being in 'weird limbo' and requiring a reset, but ultimately managed to fix it by pulling a fuse.
