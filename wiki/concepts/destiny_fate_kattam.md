## Definition
In some cultural contexts, this refers to a preordained path or a destined time for achieving certain life events, such as financial success or good fortune.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The skit references 'kattam' as a factor influencing when one is meant to become wealthy, adding a layer of comedic fatalism to the characters' financial aspirations.

## Key properties or components
- Predetermined path
- Favorable timing
- Life's circumstances
- Astrological or spiritual influence

## Contradictions or debates
This concept can sometimes be seen as conflicting with the idea of pure meritocracy, where success is solely based on hard work and individual effort.
