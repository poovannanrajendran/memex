## Definition
Agent swarm is a feature in AI systems where multiple individual AI agents can coordinate and work together autonomously to accomplish complex tasks.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This capability, as seen in Kimmy K2.5, enables parallel processing and sophisticated task management, potentially increasing efficiency and tackling more intricate problems than a single AI agent.

## Key properties or components
- Multi-agent coordination
- Parallel task execution
- Autonomous operation
