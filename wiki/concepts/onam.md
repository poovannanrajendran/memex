## Definition
Onam is a major festival celebrated in the Indian state of Kerala. It is a harvest festival and is celebrated with various traditions, including feasts, boat races, and cultural performances.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Onam is a significant cultural and religious festival for Malayalis, and its mention in the skit highlights a cultural context for the humour.

## Key properties or components
- Harvest festival
- Celebrated in Kerala
- Feasts
- Cultural performances
