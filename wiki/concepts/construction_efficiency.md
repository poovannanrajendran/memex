## Definition
The measure of how effectively resources (labour, materials, time, capital) are utilised in the process of constructing buildings or infrastructure. Higher efficiency typically means completing projects faster, with less waste, and at a lower cost.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding efficiency in construction is key to addressing housing shortages and making properties more affordable, aligning with interests in optimized resource allocation.

## Key properties or components
- Reduced project timelines
- Optimised material usage
- Lower labour costs
- Minimised waste
- Improved productivity
