## Definition
A specific, highly effective prompt designed to elicit profound, personalized, and often critical self-reflective advice from AI language models like ChatGPT, based on the user's past interactions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Demonstrates the potential for advanced AI prompts to act as powerful tools for self-reflection and personal strategy refinement, highlighting the evolving capabilities of AI.

## Key properties or components
- Viral
- Therapeutic in function
- Personalised
- Based on chat history
- Direct and truthful
