## Definition
A principle in research emphasizing the importance of using reliable, accurate, and relevant sources, even if fewer in number, over a large volume of potentially lower-quality information.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This principle is advised when importing sources into NotebookLM, ensuring that the analysis is based on the most valuable and trustworthy data available.

## Key properties or components
- Reliability
- Relevance
- Accuracy
- Trustworthiness
- Impact on analysis
