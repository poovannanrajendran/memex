## Definition
A learning technique involving identifying common elements or patterns within successful instances (e.g., top-performing content, customers, or ads) to understand what drives success.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enables data-driven skill development and strategic decision-making by learning from past performance and identifying key success drivers.

## Key properties or components
- Data analysis
- Pattern recognition
- Identification of success factors
- Continuous improvement loop
