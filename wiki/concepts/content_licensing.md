## Definition
Content licensing is the process of granting permission for a third party to use copyrighted material under specific terms and conditions. This typically involves payment and outlines the scope, duration, and territory of use.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The explicit mention of licensing requirements is critical for anyone wishing to use this footage, highlighting the legal and commercial aspects of content creation and distribution.

## Key properties or components
- Copyright protection
- Permission to use
- Defined terms and conditions
- Commercial and non-commercial use
