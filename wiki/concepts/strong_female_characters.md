## Definition
Fictional characters, typically in literature or film, who display significant strength, independence, resilience, and agency. They are often portrayed as capable of overcoming adversity and influencing their environment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Selva Raghavan frequently creates strong female characters in his films, inspired by real-life women, which resonates with audiences and explores themes of female empowerment.

## Key properties or components
- Agency
- Resilience
- Independence
- Leadership
- Sacrifice
