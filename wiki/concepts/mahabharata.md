## Definition
A major ancient Indian epic, one of the two most important Sanskrit epics of ancient India, narrating the struggle between two groups of cousins (the Pandavas and Kauravas) for the throne of Hastinapura.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is the central subject of the 'Mahabharata Moments' podcast, providing a rich source of stories, moral lessons, and philosophical insights relevant to human existence.

## Key properties or components
- ancient Indian text
- epic narrative
- dharma
- duty
- destiny and free will
- moral complexity
