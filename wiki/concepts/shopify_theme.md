## Definition
A Shopify theme is a pre-designed template that dictates the visual appearance and layout of an online store built on the Shopify platform. Themes can be customised to match a brand's identity and improve user experience.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Choosing and customising a theme is a crucial step in setting up a Shopify store, directly impacting its aesthetic appeal and usability.

## Key properties or components
- Pre-designed templates
- Customisable layouts
- Brand alignment
- User experience optimisation
