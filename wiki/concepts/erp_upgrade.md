## Definition
Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems are software suites that businesses use to manage day-to-day business activities such as accounting, procurement, project management, risk management and compliance, and supply chain operations. An upgrade refers to updating this system to a newer version.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is presented as the specific project for which a new project manager is being sought, adding a layer of business context to the skit.

- Data management
