## Definition
The process of creating computational models that replicate or predict how humans might act or react in certain situations or environments. This can range from individual decision-making to group dynamics.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Allows for the forecasting of market reactions and social trends, as demonstrated by Mirofish's ability to predict responses to news events.

## Key properties or components
- Predictive capability
- Modelling of decision-making
- Group dynamics
- Emotional responses
