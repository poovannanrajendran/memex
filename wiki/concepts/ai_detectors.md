## Definition
AI detectors are tools or algorithms designed to identify text that has been generated by artificial intelligence, as opposed to human writing. They analyse various linguistic patterns to make this determination.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Relevant as the video claims a specific 'code' can make Claude's output indistinguishable from human writing, potentially bypassing these detection systems.

## Key properties or components
- Pattern recognition
- Linguistic analysis
- Probability scoring
- False positives/negatives

## Contradictions or debates
AI detection technology is constantly evolving, and AI-generated text can often evade detection, especially if specific techniques like those mentioned in the video are employed. Conversely, AI detectors can sometimes misclassify human text as AI-generated.
