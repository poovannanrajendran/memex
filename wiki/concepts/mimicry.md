## Definition
Mimicry is the act of imitating the voice or actions of another person or of a particular type of person. It is a form of performance art often used for entertainment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is the central theme of the content featured in the video, presented as a 'marathon'.

## Key properties or components
- Imitation of voices
- Imitation of mannerisms
- Performance art
- Entertainment
