## Definition
A type of bevel gear where the teeth are straight and tapered along the length of the cone. They are used for transmitting motion between intersecting shafts.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A basic implementation for transmitting rotation between intersecting shafts.

## Key properties or components
- Straight teeth
- Conical shape
- Intersecting axes
