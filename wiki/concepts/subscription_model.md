## Definition
A sales method where customers pay a recurring fee (e.g., monthly or yearly) for access to a product or service.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the proposed primary revenue stream for the game, ensuring predictable income and allowing for tiered access to features.

## Key properties or components
- Recurring revenue
- Customer loyalty
- Tiered access
- Predictable income

## Contradictions or debates
None.
