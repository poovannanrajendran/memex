## Definition
Tikka refers to small pieces of meat or vegetables that have been marinated in spices and yogurt, then traditionally cooked in a tandoor (clay oven). It is a foundational element in many popular Indian dishes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The term 'Tikka' appears in several dishes reviewed, such as goat tikka buns and chicken tikka butter masala, indicating a common preparation method.

## Key properties or components
- Marination
- Spices
- Yogurt base
- Often tandoor-cooked
