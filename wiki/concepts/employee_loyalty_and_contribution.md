## Definition
Employee loyalty signifies a commitment and dedication to an organisation, often demonstrated through sustained effort and a willingness to go above and beyond. Significant contributions refer to the valuable impact an employee has on the company's success, such as client acquisition or growth.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding the balance between employee contribution and organisational recognition is key to employee satisfaction and retention. This source highlights a severe imbalance where loyalty and contribution were not adequately valued.

## Key properties or components
- Long tenure
- Client acquisition
- Company growth
- Dedicated effort (long hours)
- Dedication to the company's success
