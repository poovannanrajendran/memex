## Definition
The expense ratio measures an insurer's operational efficiency by comparing its total operating expenses (including acquisition costs and administrative expenses) to its earned premiums. It indicates cost management effectiveness.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This ratio is a critical component of the combined ratio and directly influences underwriting profitability. Managing the expense ratio efficiently is key to competitive advantage and financial health.

## Key properties or components
- Rose to 35.6% in 2025 (from 34.4% in 2024).
- Factors include profitability-driven commissions, mix-driven acquisition costs, and foreign exchange impacts.
