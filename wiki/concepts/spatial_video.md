## Definition
Spatial video is a type of video recording that captures depth information, allowing for a more immersive viewing experience, particularly on devices like VR/AR headsets.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is a key new feature enabled by the redesigned cameras on the standard iPhone 16, targeting users with Apple Vision Pro.

## Key properties or components
- Captures depth information
- Viewable on Apple Vision Pro
- Requires specific camera setup
