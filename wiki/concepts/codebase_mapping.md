## Definition
The process of creating a structured representation that visualises and details the relationships between different parts of a software project's codebase. This can include files, functions, classes, and their dependencies.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Graphify utilises this concept to provide AI with a complete understanding of a project, overcoming the limitations of fragmented analysis.

## Key properties or components
- Dependency visualisation
- Structural representation
- Interconnectivity analysis
