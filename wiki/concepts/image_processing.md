## Definition
The manipulation and enhancement of digital images using algorithms. This can involve adjusting colours, contrast, sharpness, noise reduction, and applying effects to improve the overall appearance of a photo or video.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial in determining the final output of smartphone cameras, with differences in processing styles (e.g., Samsung's aggressive sharpening vs. Apple's more balanced approach) significantly impacting perceived quality.

## Key properties or components
- Noise Reduction
- Sharpening
- Colour Correction
- White Balance
- Tone Mapping
