## Definition
A type of animation that mimics the physical behavior of a spring, characterized by overshoot and oscillation before settling into its final state. It provides a dynamic and lively feel.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Adds a realistic and engaging feel to UI elements, making interactions more polished and visually appealing than linear animations.

## Key properties or components
- Overshoot and bounce
- Configurable tension and friction
- Mimics physical properties
- Dynamic settling
