## Definition
A group of individuals who share an interest in investing and often collaborate, share information, or discuss investment strategies. This can be formal or informal.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a network for sharing financial information and insights, which could indirectly influence investment decisions and market trends.

## Key properties or components
- Shared interest
- Information exchange
- Community platforms (e.g., Patreon)
