## Definition
A journey or search of moral or spiritual significance. Typically, it is a journey to a place considered sacred by people of a certain religion or belief.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The Dwarkadhish Temple is a major pilgrimage site, attracting devotees seeking spiritual merit and connection with the divine.

## Key properties or components
- Spiritual purpose
- Journey to a sacred site
- Devotion and faith
- Cultural significance
