## Definition
A way of life in which people are caught up in a fiercely competitive struggle for wealth and power. It typically refers to the cycle of working long hours in a job to earn money, often to pay for a lifestyle that requires the job.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding and escaping the 'rat race' is a key theme for personal freedom and financial well-being, relevant to productivity and life choices.

## Key properties or components
- Endless competition
- Emphasis on money
- Cycle of work and spending
- Lack of personal fulfillment
