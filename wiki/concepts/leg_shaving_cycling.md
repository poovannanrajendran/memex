## Definition
The practice of removing hair from cyclists' legs, often for perceived benefits in aerodynamics, comfort with chamois cream, and easier wound cleaning after falls.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
While often seen as a cosmetic or performance-enhancing choice, it's presented as a practical consideration for comfort and potential medical reasons when skin is exposed.

## Key properties or components
- Aesthetics
- Comfort
- Easier wound care
- Aerodynamic considerations (minor)

## Contradictions or debates
None.
