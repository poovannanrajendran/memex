## Definition
The profession focused on creating the visual appearance, user interface, and user experience of websites. This includes aspects like layout, colour schemes, typography, and interactivity.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source directly addresses the potential displacement of this role by AI, prompting reflection on the future of creative professions and the skills that will remain valuable.

## Key properties or components
- Visual design
- User experience (UX)
- User interface (UI) design
- Client communication
- Problem-solving
