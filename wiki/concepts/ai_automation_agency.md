## Definition
A business that specialises in using artificial intelligence and automation tools to streamline processes and improve efficiency for other businesses. This can involve setting up automated workflows, integrating AI solutions, and providing consulting services.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The core business model discussed in the video for achieving rapid financial success through AI implementation.

## Key properties or components
- Leverages AI tools
- Focuses on process automation
- Provides services to clients
- Aims for efficiency and productivity gains
