## Definition
Professional boundaries are guidelines that define the limits of appropriate interaction and information sharing in a workplace setting. They help maintain a healthy and productive work environment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source advises limiting personal information sharing to maintain professional boundaries, which is crucial for workplace harmony, privacy, and effective management of personal commitments like leave.

## Key properties or components
- Limited personal disclosure
- Focus on work-related interactions
- Privacy management
- Respect for personal space
