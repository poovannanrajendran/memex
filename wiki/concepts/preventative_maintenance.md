## Definition
Scheduled servicing and checks performed on a vehicle to prevent potential problems and extend its lifespan. It involves proactive measures like regular oil changes, fluid checks, and timely replacement of wear-and-tear items.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for maintaining a vehicle's reliability and value, especially for high-mileage luxury cars like the Evoque. The presenter emphasizes that neglecting preventative maintenance can lead to more costly repairs down the line.

## Key properties or components
- Scheduled servicing
- Proactive repairs
- Extends vehicle life
- Reduces risk of major failures
