## Definition
The ability to work hard and energetically towards a goal. In a business context, it often refers to the dedication and intense effort required to build and sustain a venture.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The daughter reflects on the 'hustle' her parents put into their restaurants, highlighting the immense effort involved in entrepreneurship.

## Key properties or components
- Hard work
- Dedication
- Long hours
- Passion
