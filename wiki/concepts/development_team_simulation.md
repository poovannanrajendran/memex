## Definition
Simulating the functions and roles of a human development team (e.g., CEO, engineer, designer, reviewer, QA) using AI tools and automation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Significantly speeds up the development cycle and reduces the need for a large, multidisciplinary human team for initial stages.

## Key properties or components
- Automation of roles
- AI-driven task execution
- Single terminal interface
- Rapid deployment

## Contradictions or debates
None.
