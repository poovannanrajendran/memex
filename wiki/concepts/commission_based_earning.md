## Definition
A compensation structure where an individual earns a percentage of the value of the sales or services they facilitate. This often incentivizes performance and can lead to variable income.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Mentioned in the context of a business transaction within the skit, highlighting a practical aspect of earning money through customer interactions and sales.

## Key properties or components
- Performance-based
- Percentage of sale
- Variable income
- Sales incentive
