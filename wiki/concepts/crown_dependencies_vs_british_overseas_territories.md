## Definition
Crown Dependencies (like the Channel Islands and Isle of Man) are self-governing possessions of the Crown. British Overseas Territories (like St. Helena) are territories under the jurisdiction and sovereignty of the UK but are not part of it.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Distinguishing between these two types of territories, their names, and their general locations is a specific, often tricky, question in the Life in the UK test.

## Key properties or components
- Sovereignty
- Self-governance
- International representation (UK handles defence/foreign affairs)
- Geographical location
