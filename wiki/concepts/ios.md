## Definition
iOS is a mobile operating system created and developed by Apple Inc. exclusively for its hardware. It is the operating system that powers many of its mobile devices, including the iPhone.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is the foundational software that enables all functionalities on the iPhone, including the method shown for hiding apps.

## Key properties or components
- Mobile operating system
- Exclusive to Apple devices
- User-friendly interface
