## Definition
User-defined directives or rules given to Perplexity to guide its search behaviour and output format, allowing for more tailored and semi-automated research.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Custom instructions enable users to direct Perplexity to search for specific types of information from reputable sources, streamlining the initial data gathering phase.

## Key properties or components
- Task delegation
- Source filtering
- Output formatting
- Semi-automation
- Personalized search queries
