## Definition
A structured approach to prompt engineering where instructions and context are organized into distinct layers. The video outlines three key layers: system, input, and action.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Provides a systematic way to build comprehensive and effective prompts for AI agents, ensuring all necessary components are considered.

## Key properties or components
- System Layer: Role, instructions, rules, context, examples.
- Input Layer: User query or data.
- Action Layer: Tool descriptions and parameters.
