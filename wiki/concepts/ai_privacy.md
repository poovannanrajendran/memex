## Definition
AI privacy concerns the responsible collection, use, and protection of personal data within artificial intelligence systems, ensuring compliance with regulations and respecting individuals' rights to data confidentiality.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Maintaining user trust and adhering to data protection regulations are crucial for the ethical and widespread adoption of AI technologies.

## Key properties or components
- Data confidentiality
- User consent
- Anonymisation
- Regulatory compliance
