## Definition
The consistency and trustworthiness of a service provider in fulfilling orders as promised. This includes ensuring products are delivered as ordered and on time, without arbitrary cancellations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key factor in the speaker's purchasing decisions. The speaker highlights 'Forests' as more reliable than 'Baskets', where orders are often cancelled at the provider's discretion.

## Key properties or components
- Predictable fulfillment
- Minimal arbitrary cancellations
- Customer trust

## Contradictions or debates
None.
