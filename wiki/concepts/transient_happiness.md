## Definition
Happiness that is temporary and short-lived, often derived from external factors like material possessions or fleeting experiences.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the source's message, suggesting that such happiness is ultimately unfulfilling.

## Key properties or components
- External origin
- Short duration
- Materialistic basis

## Contradictions or debates
None.
