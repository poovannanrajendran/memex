## Definition
Custom CV generation involves tailoring a curriculum vitae (CV) or resume to specifically match the requirements and keywords of a particular job application. This increases the relevance and effectiveness of the application.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The tool automatically generates unique CVs for each application, significantly improving the chances of success by aligning with specific job descriptions.

## Key properties or components
- Job-specific tailoring
- Keyword optimization
- Targeted content
- Increased application relevance
