## Definition
The pervasive challenge individuals face in effectively retaining, connecting, and leveraging the vast amounts of information they consume, often resulting in fragmented notes, isolated ideas, and redundant discovery of insights.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the core issue that the memex project seeks to solve, highlighting the inadequacies of existing tools and methods for modern information consumption.

## Key properties or components
- information overload
- poor retention
- scattered notes
- disconnected ideas
- redundant discovery of insights
