## Definition
Musical talent refers to an innate aptitude or exceptional ability in understanding, creating, or performing music. This can manifest in various forms, such as instrumental proficiency, vocal ability, composition, or a keen sense of rhythm and melody.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source specifically highlights Michael Jackson's extraordinary musical talent, particularly his ability to conceive and execute complex vocal parts for a song without instrumental aid.

## Key properties or components
- Innate aptitude
- Versatile in different musical aspects
- Can be developed through practice
