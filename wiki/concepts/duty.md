## Definition
A moral or legal obligation; a responsibility. In the context of the Mahabharata, it often refers to an individual's specific role, responsibilities, and obligations within their family, society, and the cosmic order.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlighted as a central theme in the Mahabharata, influencing characters like Bhima's duty to protect the vulnerable and Nakula's devotion to loved ones.

## Key properties or components
- moral obligation
- responsibility
- social role
- cosmic obligation
- allegiance
