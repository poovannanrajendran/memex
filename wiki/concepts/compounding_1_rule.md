## Definition
The principle that small, consistent improvements or declines amplify significantly over time due to repeated application. A 1% daily gain leads to exponential growth, while a 1% daily loss leads to exponential decline.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the video's message, demonstrating how seemingly minor daily actions can lead to massive long-term outcomes, applicable to personal development, business, and financial growth.

## Key properties or components
- Daily improvement/decline
- Exponential growth/decay
- Time horizon (one year)
- Mathematical certainty

None.
