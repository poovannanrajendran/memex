## Definition
The ability of an AI model to grasp the background information, nuances, and relationships within the input provided. Unlike humans, AI relies on pattern matching and probability, not true comprehension.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlights why specific and relevant context is crucial in prompts, as AI does not possess inherent world knowledge or understanding like humans.

## Key properties or components
- Pattern matching
- Probability calculation
- Input-dependent
- Lack of true comprehension
