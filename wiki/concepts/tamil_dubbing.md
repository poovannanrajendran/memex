## Definition
Tamil dubbing refers to the process of replacing the original audio track of a film or show with a new one in the Tamil language, featuring voice actors who synchronise their speech with the on-screen characters.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It makes content from other languages accessible to a wider Tamil-speaking audience, increasing viewership and cultural exchange.

## Key properties or components
- Voice acting
- Lip-sync synchronisation
- Translation
- Cultural adaptation
