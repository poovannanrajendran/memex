## Definition
A unique helical staircase located within St Paul's Cathedral. It features a design where each step is partially embedded in the wall and supported by the step below, creating an illusion of floating.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is highlighted as a hidden architectural treasure within St Paul's Cathedral, noted for its ingenious design and appearance in popular culture.

## Key properties or components
- Helical design
- Appears to float
- Steps supported by those below
- Located within St Paul's Cathedral
- Featured in Harry Potter film
