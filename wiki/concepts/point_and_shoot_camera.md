## Definition
A type of camera, or a mode on a digital camera or smartphone, designed for simplicity and ease of use, requiring minimal user input to capture a photograph.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The reviewer questions whether Apple's focus on advanced features for professionals is detracting from the core 'point and shoot' simplicity that many users prefer.

## Key properties or components
- Simple operation
- Minimal settings
- Automatic adjustments
