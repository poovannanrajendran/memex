## Definition
Work from Home (WFH) refers to a work arrangement that allows employees to perform their job duties from their place of residence, rather than commuting to a central office.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It offers flexibility and can reduce commuting costs, making it an attractive option for many individuals seeking supplementary income or alternative employment.

## Key properties or components
- Flexibility
- Reduced commute
- Location independence

## Contradictions or debates
None.
