## Definition
A formal assessment of an employee's job performance over a specific period. It is used to evaluate an individual's contributions, identify areas for development, and inform decisions about promotions, compensation, and training.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The consequences of a missed deadline, even if due to a technical error, are shown to potentially impact an employee's appraisal, highlighting the importance of diligent work.

## Key properties or components
- Employee evaluation
- Goal setting
- Feedback
- Career progression
