## Definition
A feature in ChatGPT that allows users to provide persistent background information and specific instructions about how they want the AI to respond. This personalizes the AI's behaviour and output.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enables ChatGPT to tailor responses more accurately to the user's specific needs, preferences, and context without requiring repetition in each prompt.

## Key properties or components
- User background
- Response preferences
- Behavioral guidelines
- Personalization
