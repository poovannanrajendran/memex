## Definition
A battery endurance test is a method used to evaluate how long a device, typically a smartphone or laptop, can operate on a single charge under specific usage conditions. This often involves simulating real-world tasks or running continuous demanding applications.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding battery endurance is crucial for consumers choosing a mobile device, as it directly impacts usability and convenience. For Poovi's Second Brain, it relates to evaluating the practical performance of cutting-edge technology.

## Key properties or components
- Simulated usage
- Continuous operation
- Battery drain measurement
- Comparison across devices
