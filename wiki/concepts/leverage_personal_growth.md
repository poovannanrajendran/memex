## Definition
The concept that personal growth and business growth are intrinsically linked; as a business expands, the individual must also grow to lead it effectively.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Connects quantitative business metrics (revenue) with qualitative personal development, suggesting that business success is an indicator of personal evolution.

## Key properties or components
- Business growth as a proxy for personal growth
- Overcoming challenges
- Developing new capabilities
- Mindset evolution

## Contradictions or debates
None.
