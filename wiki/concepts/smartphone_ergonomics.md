## Definition
Ergonomics in the context of smartphones refers to how comfortably and efficiently a user can interact with the device. This includes factors like size, weight, grip, button placement, and ease of one-handed operation.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for daily usability and user satisfaction, influencing how long a user can comfortably hold and operate their phone.

## Key properties or components
- Size and weight
- Grip comfort
- One-handed usability
- Reachability of controls
