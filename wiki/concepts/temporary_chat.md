## Definition
A mode within ChatGPT that does not save the conversation history or use it for future training. It functions similarly to 'incognito' or 'private browsing' modes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for handling sensitive information or conducting brainstorming sessions where privacy and a clean conversational history are desired.

## Key properties or components
- Privacy
- No history saving
- No training data usage
