## Definition
Agent frameworks provide developers with tools, libraries, and structures to simplify the creation and management of AI agents. They often handle complexities like LLM interactions, tool integration, and state management.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video critiques existing frameworks and proposes Pantic AI as a more robust solution, emphasizing the critical role of frameworks in production-ready agent development.

## Key properties or components
- Modularity
- Abstraction
- Tool Integration
- LLM Orchestration
- State Management

## Contradictions or debates
The video suggests that current frameworks like LangChain and CrewAI are not 'good enough' for mature production-grade agents, implying a gap in their capabilities or ease of use.
