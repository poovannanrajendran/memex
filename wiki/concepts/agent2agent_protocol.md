## Definition
A specific communication standard or set of rules that defines how different AI agents interact and exchange information with each other in a structured and understandable manner.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Facilitates seamless and reliable communication within multi-agent systems, ensuring that agents can understand, interpret, and respond effectively to messages from other agents.

## Key properties or components
- Standardised communication
- Facilitates agent interaction
- Ensures data exchange
