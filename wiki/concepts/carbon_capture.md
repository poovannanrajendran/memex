## Definition
Carbon capture refers to a range of technologies that are designed to capture carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions from sources like industrial facilities or directly from the atmosphere.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Captured carbon dioxide is a crucial feedstock for Formula 1's sustainable fuel production, helping to create a closed-loop system.

## Key properties or components
- CO2 removal
- Industrial sources
- Direct air capture
