## Definition
The phenomenon where the collective feelings, sentiments, and psychological biases of a large group of market participants significantly influence asset prices.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding crowd emotion is vital for recognising market bubbles, crashes, and opportunities driven by sentiment rather than fundamental value.

## Key properties or components
- Fear
- Greed
- FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)
- Herd mentality
- Hype

## Contradictions or debates
Some believe efficient market hypotheses suggest that crowd emotion is quickly priced in and difficult to exploit consistently.
