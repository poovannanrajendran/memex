## Definition
A mode of operation for laptops where the lid is closed, but the computer remains active and can be used with external peripherals like a monitor, keyboard, and mouse. This is typically achieved by connecting a power source and external display.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Allows a laptop to function as a desktop replacement, providing a larger screen experience while maintaining the portability of the laptop itself.

## Key properties or components
- External Monitor
- External Keyboard/Mouse
- Power Source
- Closed Lid Operation
