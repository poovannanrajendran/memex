## Definition
This phrase signifies a positive and meaningful impact on others or on the world. It implies actions or contributions that lead to betterment or significant change.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the specific and singular aspiration Ratan Tata has for his legacy, as stated in the source.

## Key properties or components
- Positive impact
- Meaningful contribution
- Tangible change
- Altruism

## Contradictions or debates
None.
