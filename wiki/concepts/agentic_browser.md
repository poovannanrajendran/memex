## Definition
An agentic browser is a web browser equipped with artificial intelligence capabilities that allow it to understand context, take actions, and generate content autonomously or semi-autonomously on behalf of the user.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Represents a significant shift in web interaction, moving beyond passive browsing to active assistance and task automation directly within the browser, impacting how users interact with online services and potentially disrupting existing AI-focused companies.

## Key properties or components
- AI-powered
- Contextual understanding
- Autonomous actions
- Content generation
- Integrated features
