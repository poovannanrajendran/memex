## Definition
Technology that synthesizes human-like speech from written text. It is used for applications like audiobooks, virtual assistants, and voiceovers.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for adding narration to the animated videos, making them more engaging and accessible.

## Key properties or components
- Input: Text
- Output: Audio
- Voice customization
- Natural-sounding speech
