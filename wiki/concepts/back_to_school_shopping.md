## Definition
The practice of purchasing necessary supplies, clothing, and equipment for students before the commencement of a new academic year.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept provides the immediate context and reason for the customer's laptop purchase, highlighting a common consumer behaviour pattern.

## Key properties or components
- Seasonal purchasing
- Student needs
- Educational preparedness
