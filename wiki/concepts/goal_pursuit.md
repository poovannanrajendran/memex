## Definition
The process of actively working towards achieving a specific objective or desired outcome. It typically involves planning, execution, and overcoming obstacles.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source provides advice on how to pursue goals more effectively by minimising distractions and emotional reactions.

## Key properties or components

- Action planning
- Execution
- Adaptation
