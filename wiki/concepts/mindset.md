## Definition
A mindset refers to a person's established set of attitudes, beliefs, and assumptions that shape how they perceive and respond to situations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding one's mindset is crucial for personal development and achieving potential, as it influences ambition and resilience.

## Key properties or components
- Attitudes
- Beliefs
- Assumptions
- Perception
- Response patterns
