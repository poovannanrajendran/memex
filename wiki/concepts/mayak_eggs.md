## Definition
Mayak Eggs (or 'drug eggs') are a popular Korean dish featuring soft-boiled eggs marinated in a savoury and slightly sweet soy-based sauce. They are called 'drug eggs' due to their highly addictive nature.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the central food item discussed in the source, notable for its unique preparation and flavour profile.

## Key properties or components
- Boiled eggs
- Marinated
- Soy sauce based marinade
- Jammy yolk
- Savoury flavour
- Addictive
