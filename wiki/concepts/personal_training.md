## Definition
A fitness service where a qualified trainer works one-on-one with a client to help them achieve their specific health and fitness goals.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Personal training is crucial for tailored fitness plans, motivation, and proper technique, which is the core offering of "Train with Sethu."

## Key properties or components
- Individualised plans
- Goal-oriented
- Expert guidance
- Accountability
