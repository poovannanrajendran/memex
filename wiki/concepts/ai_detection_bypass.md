## Definition
The process or capability of using AI tools or techniques to circumvent systems designed to identify AI-generated content.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is relevant to maintaining academic standards and understanding the evolving capabilities of AI in content creation and detection.

## Key properties or components
- Utilises AI for content generation
- Aims for human-like output
- Challenges the accuracy of AI detectors
- Involves user editing
