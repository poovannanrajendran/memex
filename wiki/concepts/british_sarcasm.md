## Definition
A form of ironic or satirical humour, often delivered with a dry or understated tone, that is characteristic of British culture. It can sometimes be misinterpreted by those unfamiliar with the cultural nuances.

## Key properties or components
- Understated delivery
- Irony
- Cultural specificity
- Potential for misinterpretation
