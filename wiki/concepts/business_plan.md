## Definition
A formal document outlining a company's objectives, strategies, market analysis, and financial projections. It serves as a roadmap for the business and is often required for funding.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Critical for structuring and validating business ideas, particularly in regulated industries like insurance, to ensure feasibility and attract investment.

## Key properties or components
- Executive summary
- Market analysis
- Marketing and sales strategy
- Financial projections
- Management team
