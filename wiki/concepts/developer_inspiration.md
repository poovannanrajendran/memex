## Definition
The act of seeking and drawing creative ideas, techniques, and motivation from the work produced by other developers. This involves observing, analysing, and adapting successful approaches.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Helps in discovering new methods, understanding best practices, and fostering creativity in one's own development work.

## Key properties or components
- Observing code
- Analysing techniques
- Seeking novel solutions
- Learning from peers

## Contradictions or debates
None.
