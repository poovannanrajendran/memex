## Definition
The process by which organisations successfully integrate and utilise artificial intelligence solutions into their operations.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key challenge identified is the difficulty customers face in adopting AI solutions they have recently purchased, indicating a gap between acquisition and effective implementation.

## Key properties or components
- User training
- Change management
- Integration challenges
- Demonstrating ROI
