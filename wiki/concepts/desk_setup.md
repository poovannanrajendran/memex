## Definition
The arrangement of a workspace, including furniture, hardware (monitors, keyboards, mice), and accessories, designed to optimize efficiency, comfort, and aesthetics for work or study.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Enhances productivity and user experience by creating an ergonomic and functional environment, particularly relevant when adapting an iPad for more intensive work tasks.

## Key properties or components
- Ergonomics
- Hardware integration
- Cable management
- Aesthetics
- Workflow optimization
