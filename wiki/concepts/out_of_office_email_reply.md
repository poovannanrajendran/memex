## Definition
An out-of-office (OOO) email reply is an automated response sent to people who email you when you are unavailable to respond immediately, typically due to leave or travel.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The misinterpretation of an out-of-office reply is central to the humorous misunderstanding in the video.

## Key properties or components
- Automated response
- Indicates unavailability
- Can be set for holidays or leave
