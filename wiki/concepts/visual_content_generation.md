## Definition
This concept involves using AI tools to create visual assets such as images, graphics, and animations from textual prompts or data. The aim is to automate and accelerate the design process, making visual creation more accessible.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Significantly reduces the time and effort required to produce visual materials for presentations, marketing, and content creation, enabling faster iteration and a higher output.

## Key properties or components
- Text-to-image
- Image editing
- Infographic generation
- Rapid prototyping
