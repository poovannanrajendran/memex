## Definition
A basic visual guide that represents the skeletal framework of a website or application, focusing on content structure and functionality without visual design elements.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Serves as a foundational blueprint for UI design, allowing for early testing of layout and functionality; AI can generate these quickly.

## Key properties or components
- Structural blueprint
- Content hierarchy
- Functionality focus
- Low-fidelity

## Contradictions or debates
None.
