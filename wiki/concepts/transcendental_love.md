## Definition
A type of love that is not limited by physical or societal boundaries, suggesting a spiritual or destined connection that overcomes all obstacles to find its object.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is a core theme conveyed through the lyrics, highlighting the profound and borderless nature of the love described in the song.

## Key properties or components
- Borderless
- Breaches order
- Destined connection
- Transcends limitations
