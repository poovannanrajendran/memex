## Definition
The process of determining the most efficient or desirable path between two or more points. This can involve considering factors like distance, time, cost, scenery, and points of interest.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for efficient and enjoyable travel, especially road trips, by optimising the journey and discovering new places.

## Key properties or components
- Destination setting
- Path optimisation
- Time/distance calculation
- Point of interest integration
