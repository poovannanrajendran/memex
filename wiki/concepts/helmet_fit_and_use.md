## Definition
The process of ensuring a cycling helmet is correctly sized for the wearer's head and adjusted properly for maximum protection.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Essential for safety in case of an accident; an improperly fitted or adjusted helmet significantly reduces its effectiveness, impacting potential injury mitigation.

## Key properties or components
- Proper sizing
- Retention system adjustment
- Strap positioning (V's below ears)
- Chin strap tension (two-finger rule or yawn test)
