## Definition
The screen door effect (SDE) in VR headsets refers to the visible grid or mesh pattern created by the gaps between pixels on the display. It can detract from the visual immersion and clarity of the virtual environment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A visual artifact present in both headsets, though slightly more pronounced in the Quest 3S, that can affect image quality.

## Key properties or components
- Visible pixel grid
- Reduced immersion
- Affects sharpness
- Mitigated by higher resolution and advanced optics
