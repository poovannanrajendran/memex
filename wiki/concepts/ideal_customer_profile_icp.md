## Definition
An Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) is a detailed description of the type of customer or client that a business aims to attract and serve. It outlines demographics, firmographics, needs, and behaviours.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Understanding the ICP is crucial for targeted sales and marketing efforts, enabling tools like Apollo to generate effective lead lists.

## Key properties or components
- Demographics
- Firmographics
- Needs/Pain Points
- Behavioural patterns
- Company size/industry
