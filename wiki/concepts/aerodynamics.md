## Definition
The study of how air flows around solid objects and the forces such as drag and lift that result.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for designing race cars like the GT40 to achieve high speeds efficiently, as indicated by the mention of its sleek shape helping it 'cut through the air like a missile'.

## Key properties or components
- Drag reduction
- Lift generation
- Airflow
- Vehicle shape
- Stability
