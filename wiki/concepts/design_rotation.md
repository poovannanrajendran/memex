## Definition
The concept of cyclically returning to previous design elements or aesthetics in product development, rather than strictly progressing forward.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The reviewer uses this to describe the iPhone's camera design evolution, suggesting a lack of definitive design progression.

## Key properties or components
- Cyclical
- Return to past aesthetics
- Can feel like stagnation

## Contradictions or debates
None.
