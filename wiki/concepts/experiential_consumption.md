## Definition
Experiential consumption refers to the act of purchasing experiences rather than physical goods. These experiences can include travel, dining, entertainment, or learning new skills, and are often valued for the memories and personal growth they provide.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is central to the argument that Americans should travel more and spend on experiences, as it is suggested to be more beneficial for both individual well-being and the economy.

## Key properties or components
- Focus on memories
- Personal growth
- Higher long-term satisfaction
- Economic stimulus
