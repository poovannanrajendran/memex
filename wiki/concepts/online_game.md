## Definition
A video game played over a computer network, typically the internet. It allows multiple players to interact with each other in a shared virtual environment.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the core product being pitched, representing a significant market opportunity with potential for direct revenue generation and high user engagement.

## Key properties or components
- Multiplayer interaction
- Internet-based
- Virtual environment
- Direct customer payment
