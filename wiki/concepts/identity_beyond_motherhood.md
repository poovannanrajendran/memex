## Definition
This concept refers to an individual's sense of self that extends beyond their role as a parent. It encompasses personal aspirations, career, hobbies, and self-worth independent of familial responsibilities.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
One character's struggle to prove she is 'more than just a mom' highlights a common societal and personal challenge for parents seeking to maintain their individual identity.

## Key properties or components
- Self-worth
- Personal aspirations
- Individual identity
- Roles outside of parenting
