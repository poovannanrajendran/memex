## Definition
The ratio of the angular velocities or the number of teeth of two meshing gears. It determines the change in speed and torque between the input and output shafts.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A fundamental parameter in mechanical design that dictates speed reduction or increase and corresponding torque multiplication or reduction.

## Key properties or components
- Speed change
- Torque change
- Number of teeth
- Input/Output shafts
