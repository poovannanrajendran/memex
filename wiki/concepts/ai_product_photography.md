## Definition
The use of artificial intelligence, particularly generative AI models, to create or enhance images of products for marketing and e-commerce purposes. This can involve generating entirely new product photos or placing existing product images into various AI-created scenes.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Offers a potentially cost-effective and rapid alternative to traditional product photography, making professional-looking visuals accessible to a wider range of businesses, especially small enterprises.

## Key properties or components
- Automation
- Cost-effectiveness
- Speed
- Customisation
- Scalability

## Contradictions or debates
The quality and realism of AI-generated images can still vary, and they may lack the nuanced artistic touch or specific brand alignment that a professional photographer can achieve. There's also debate about the originality and ethical implications of AI-generated art.
