## Definition
Running artificial intelligence models, particularly large language models (LLMs), on a user's own hardware rather than relying on cloud-based services. This approach prioritizes privacy, control, and offline access.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Crucial for users concerned about data privacy, security, and the desire to avoid reliance on third-party cloud providers for AI tasks.

## Key properties or components
- Enhanced privacy
- Data control
- Offline capability
- Hardware requirements
- Potential for lower long-term cost

## Contradictions or debates
None.
