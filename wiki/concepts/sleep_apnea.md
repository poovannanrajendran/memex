## Definition
A serious sleep disorder in which breathing repeatedly stops and starts during sleep. If untreated, sleep apnea can lead to other health problems.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is a significant new health detection feature for the Apple Watch, potentially improving early diagnosis and management of a serious condition.

## Key properties or components
- Breathing cessation during sleep
- Requires monitoring of breathing patterns
- Can be detected by accelerometers
- Can be diagnosed with complex equipment
- Early detection can lead to better health outcomes
