## Definition
Matured parenting is an approach where parents focus on providing support and guidance rather than strict control. It involves allowing children to navigate challenges and learn from their experiences, offering assistance when needed.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This concept is relevant to personal growth and development, highlighting effective strategies for raising resilient and independent individuals.

## Key properties or components
- Supportive
- Non-controlling
- Empathetic
- Guidance-oriented
- Encourages learning from mistakes

## Contradictions or debates
None.
