## Definition
The developed ability to accurately perceive, manage, and adhere to time. A lack of time maturity is presented as a core issue in ADD, leading to lateness.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is identified as the central deficit causing chronic lateness.

## Key properties or components
- Accurate time perception
- Time management
- Punctuality
- Developmental aspect

## Contradictions or debates
None.
