## Definition
Achieving high-quality results or significant output using minimal financial resources. This often involves creative problem-solving and leveraging affordable or unconventional tools.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The video highlights how an inexpensive lens ($49) can be used effectively in a blockbuster film ($165 million budget), suggesting that creative vision and tool selection can outweigh raw budget in certain aspects of production.

- Resourcefulness
- Budget optimization
- Innovative tool utilization
