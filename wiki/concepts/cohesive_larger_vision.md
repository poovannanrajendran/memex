## Definition
A cohesive larger vision refers to a unified, overarching strategy or goal that integrates various smaller parts or initiatives into a coherent whole. It provides direction and ensures that individual efforts align with the ultimate objective.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Steve Jobs' response highlights the challenge of fitting specific product decisions (like potentially dropping OpenDoc) into a broader strategic vision that drives significant revenue.

## Key properties or components
- Integrative
- Strategic
- Goal-oriented
- Directional
