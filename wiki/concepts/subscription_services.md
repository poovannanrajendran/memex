## Definition
Subscription services require users to pay a recurring fee, typically monthly or annually, to access a product or service. In the context of digital content, this often grants unlimited access to a library of books, music, or videos.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source contrasts the free access model with the common subscription model, highlighting a potentially disruptive alternative.

## Key properties or components
- Recurring payment
- Access to content
- Often curated libraries
