## Definition
A retail establishment that primarily sells tea and related products, often including light food items. It can range from a simple takeaway counter to a more elaborate sit-down cafe experience.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The source material uses this as a vehicle to explore common misconceptions about small business profitability and operational demands.

## Key properties or components

- Customer service
- Operations management
- Marketing
- Staffing
