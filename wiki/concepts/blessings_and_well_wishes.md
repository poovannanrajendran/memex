## Definition
Blessings and well wishes are expressions of goodwill, prosperity, and happiness offered to individuals, especially during significant life events like engagements and weddings. They can be conveyed verbally, through gestures, or written messages.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A major part of the video consists of various individuals offering their blessings and congratulations to the engaged couple.

## Key properties or components
- Goodwill
- Prosperity wishes
- Verbal expressions
- Written messages
- Virtual and in-person delivery
