## Definition
A high-performance computing chip designed by NVIDIA that integrates advanced CPU and GPU technologies for AI and data-intensive workloads. It is part of NVIDIA's strategy to offer unified solutions for complex computational tasks.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
It is the core processing unit of the new NVIDIA Project Digits Mini PC, enabling its substantial AI capabilities.

## Key properties or components
- GB10 processor
- Grace Blackwell architecture
- Petaflop AI computing performance
- Integration of CPU and GPU
- NVLink interconnect
