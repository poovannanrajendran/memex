## Definition
The concept that one's surroundings and external conditions can either hinder (friction) or facilitate (lubricant) the achievement of goals.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
Highlights the power of environment design in influencing behavior and making desired actions easier to perform.

## Key properties or components
- Influence on behavior
- Facilitation of goals
- Obstruction of goals
- Strategic manipulation
