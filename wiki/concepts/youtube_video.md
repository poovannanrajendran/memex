## Definition
A video file uploaded and hosted on the YouTube platform, accessible via a URL and playable through a web browser or the YouTube application.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the medium through which the content is delivered and accessed.

## Key properties or components
- Hosted online
- Streamable
- Shareable URL
- User-generated or professional content
