## Definition
Stock market analysis involves evaluating securities for investment. It encompasses fundamental analysis (examining financial health and economic factors) and technical analysis (studying price charts and trading volumes to predict future movements).

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
This is the core methodology discussed in the video, as the presenter uses it to identify stocks with potential for significant growth.
