## Definition
A radical change in the expected direction or outcome of the plot of a work of fiction. It is a narrative device that surprises the audience by revealing information that contradicts prior assumptions.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
A key characteristic highlighted for all the recommended crime thriller movies, suggesting it's a significant draw for viewers.

## Key properties or components
- Unexpected revelation
- Audience surprise
- Reversal of expectation
- Narrative device
