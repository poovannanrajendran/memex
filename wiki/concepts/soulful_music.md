## Definition
Soulful music is characterised by its deep emotional expression and heartfelt delivery. It often conveys strong feelings and can create a profound connection with the listener.

## Why it matters (in Poovi's context)
The description emphasizes the 'soulful voice' and the 'goosebumps' evoked by the performance, highlighting the emotional impact of the music.

## Key properties or components
- Emotional expression
- Heartfelt delivery
- Connects with listener's emotions
- Often associated with blues, gospel, and R&B, but applicable to other genres
