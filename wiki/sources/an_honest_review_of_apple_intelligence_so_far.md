## Summary
This review of Apple Intelligence, released in stages starting in late 2024 and expected to be fully available by March 2025, assesses its features as they become available. The reviewer finds many features, such as writing tools, notification summaries, Genmoji, and image playground, to be of limited practical use or not targeted at their demographic. However, the background object removal in the Photos app and visual intelligence show promise, with the latter being compared to older technologies. The integration with ChatGPT, while functional, is noted as not yet a significant upgrade to Siri, with more substantial improvements anticipated in future software updates.

## Key claims
- Apple Intelligence features are rolling out gradually, with full availability expected by March 2025.
- Many of the initial Apple Intelligence features, such as writing tools and notification summaries, offer limited practical value to the reviewer.
- The background object removal tool in the Photos app is considered a strong feature, potentially surpassing competitors.
- Visual Intelligence, while not entirely novel, has the potential to be very useful, especially with its ChatGPT integration.
- The most significant benefit of Apple Intelligence so far is the increase in base RAM and memory across Apple devices.
