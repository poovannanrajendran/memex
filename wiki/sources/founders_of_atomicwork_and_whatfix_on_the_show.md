## Summary
This YouTube discussion features founders from Atomicwork and Whatfix, sharing insights on the challenges and realities of building a startup, particularly in enterprise sales. They highlight the intense nature of startups, comparing them to war, and the difficulty of driving AI solution adoption. The conversation also touches on the need for founders to push their teams and the importance of setting high ambitions.

## Key claims
- Startups are akin to war, requiring a decade to achieve victory.
- There is a significant adoption issue with newly purchased AI solutions among enterprise customers.
- Founders often push their teams to their limits due to the fast-paced nature of the industry.
- Microsoft's strategy involves quickly entering markets and seizing leadership positions, even if they initially appear to miss opportunities.
- Ambition should be high, and external opinions should not deter founders from their goals.
