## Summary
This video introduces a method to use OpenClaw models without incurring costs, addressing the primary concern of high expense associated with the service. It highlights Kiloclaw, a hosted, 24/7 version of OpenClaw, which currently offers free access to the Minimax M2.5 model. This model is positioned as a high-quality alternative, comparable to Claude Opus, allowing users to reserve more powerful, paid models for complex tasks only.

## Key claims
- OpenClaw can now be used for free through a platform called Kiloclaw.
- Kiloclaw is a hosted, always-on version of OpenClaw that requires no setup.
- The Minimax M2.5 model is available for free on Kiloclaw and performs comparably to Claude Opus.
- Users can set Minimax M2.5 as their default and only use expensive models like Opus or GPT-5.2 for highly complex tasks.
