## Summary
India has launched a new AI platform called AI Kosh, which functions as a repository for public datasets, ML models, toolkits, and real-world AI use cases specifically for India. Similar to Hugging Face but tailored for the Indian ecosystem, it currently allows contributions from startups, government bodies, and research labs. Users can access its resources after logging in via Digilocker using their Aadhaar credentials, with existing datasets covering sectors like healthcare, agriculture, technology, and speech.

## Key claims
- AI Kosh is India's new AI platform and ecosystem hub.
- It serves as a repository for datasets, models, and real-world AI use cases.
- Access requires login via Aadhaar through Digilocker.
- Currently, only startups, government bodies, and research labs can contribute.
- Existing datasets include health care, agriculture, technology, and speech.
