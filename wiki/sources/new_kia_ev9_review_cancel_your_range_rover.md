## Summary
This YouTube review examines the new Kia EV9, a large 7-seater electric SUV. The reviewer highlights its imposing, squared-off 'cyberpunk' design, a well-laid-out interior with a mix of screens and physical buttons, and ample interior storage. Performance options include a 203hp single motor and a 385hp dual-motor GT Line, both with a 100kWh battery, offering ranges of up to 350 miles. Despite its features, the EV9 is positioned as a premium vehicle with a starting price of £65,000.

## Key claims
