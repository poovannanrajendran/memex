## Summary
This YouTube video explores Mugli, a restaurant in Manchester reputed to serve the best Indian food in the city. The reviewer highlights several dishes, including a sizzling mix grill with lamb, chicken, and prawns, noting the smoky flavour from the charcoal pit and the richness of ghee. Other notable items are the scorpion prawns, a leopard roll with seekh kebab and mango chutney, and a highly praised butter chicken served with a cheese-stuffed naan.

## Key claims
- Mugli is considered to serve the best Indian food in Manchester.
- The sizzling mix grill features lamb, chicken, and prawns with a distinct smoky flavour from the charcoal pit.
- Scorpion prawns marinated in tandoori masala and garlic oil are a standout dish.
- The leopard roll, a seekh kebab wrapped in chapati with mango chutney, is noted for its flavour absorption but considered expensive at £10.
- The butter chicken is exceptionally tender and potentially the best the reviewer has ever tasted.
- The cheese-stuffed naan is described as a decadent accompaniment to the butter chicken.
