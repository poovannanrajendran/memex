## Summary
The creator details a remarkably efficient and cost-effective method for producing AI avatar video advertisements. By utilising HeyGen, they can generate a 60-second video for as little as 50 cents, with perfect lip-sync and a quick turnaround time. This process is streamlined to the point where creating a new ad requires only a single line of code, leveraging pre-saved AI actors.

- AI avatar video ads can be produced for as little as 50 cents each.
- Video rendering time for these ads is approximately 60 seconds.
- Creating a new AI avatar ad can be accomplished with just one line of code.
- HeyGen offers a viable solution for cost-effective AI avatar video production with good lip-sync capabilities.
- Kling's image-to-video engine was tested but found to be too slow and lacking essential features like lip-sync.
