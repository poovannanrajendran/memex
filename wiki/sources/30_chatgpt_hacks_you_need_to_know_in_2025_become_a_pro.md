## Summary
This video provides 30 practical tips and hacks to enhance the user experience with ChatGPT. It covers a wide range of techniques, from structuring complex prompts and assigning roles to ChatGPT, to managing chat history, leveraging custom instructions, and utilising advanced features like Canvas and different AI models (GPT-4o, GPT-1). The advice aims to improve efficiency, relevance, and overall effectiveness when using the AI tool for various tasks, including content creation, coding, and learning.

## Key claims
- Breaking down complex prompts into smaller, sequential steps yields better results than single, lengthy prompts.
- Assigning ChatGPT a specific role or persona enhances the relevance and professionalism of its responses.
- Utilising custom instructions and clearing chat memory helps maintain consistent and accurate responses.
- ChatGPT's subscription offers significant advantages, including access to GPT-4o and unlimited image generation, over the free version.
- Features like Canvas, GPT-1 model, and image analysis provide advanced capabilities for content creation, coding, and problem-solving.
- Using ChatGPT effectively can significantly boost productivity across various domains, from personal learning to professional content creation.
- For academic or professional writing, it's crucial to check for plagiarism as ChatGPT can inadvertently piece together existing information.
