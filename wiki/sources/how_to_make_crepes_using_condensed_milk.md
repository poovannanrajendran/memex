## Summary
This video demonstrates a simple recipe for making crepes. It involves dissolving a can of condensed milk in boiling water, then combining it with oil, an egg, salt, and sifted flour to create a batter. The batter is then cooked in a non-stick pan with butter until golden brown on both sides, resulting in delicious and easy-to-make crepes.

## Key claims
- Condensed milk can be dissolved in boiling water to form the base of a crepe batter.
- A simple mixture of condensed milk, oil, egg, salt, and flour can be used to make crepes.
- The resulting crepes are described as delicious and easy to make.
