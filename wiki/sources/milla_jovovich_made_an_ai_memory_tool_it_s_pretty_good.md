## Summary
This video reviews 'MePalace,' an AI memory tool co-created by actress Milla Jovovich and her friend Ben. MePalace aims to provide AI with permanent memory by organising past conversations and projects into a structured 'palace' format, improving retrieval efficiency. The tool uses a custom AI dialect, AAKa, for efficient data compression, and the reviewer demonstrates its installation and integration with AI models like Claude, noting its surprisingly effective performance.

## Key claims
- MePalace is the highest-scoring AI memory system ever benchmarked.
- The structured organisation of data within MePalace improves retrieval by 34%.
- MePalace uses a proprietary AI dialect called AAKa to compress months of conversations into about 120 tokens.
- The tool can be integrated with existing AI models, such as Claude, to enhance their memory capabilities.
- MePalace is free and has gained significant community traction with nearly 5,000 stars.
