## Summary
Apple has unveiled the new Apple Watch Series 10 at the Glow Time Event. Key advancements include a larger, 40% brighter wide-angle OLED display in a thinner design, a new health feature capable of detecting potential sleep apnea by monitoring breathing patterns, and AI-powered enhancements driven by a quad-core neural engine in the S10 chip, including improved background noise suppression for calls.

## Key claims
- The Apple Watch Series 10 features a 40% brighter, larger, wide-angle OLED screen in a thinner package than previous models, including the Apple Watch Ultra.
- The Series 10 can detect potential sleep apnea by monitoring breathing patterns overnight using its accelerometer, providing a report for doctors.
- The new S10 chip includes a four-core neural engine that powers AI features, such as improved background noise suppression for calls.
