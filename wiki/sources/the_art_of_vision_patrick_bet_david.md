## Summary
Patrick Bet-David discusses the concept of "The Art of Vision" inspired by books like "The Art of Seduction" and "4 Laws of Power." The core idea is to create a vision or dream so grand that it appears unbelievable to most people, encouraging perseverance and belief in its eventual realization.

## Key claims
- Cast a vision or dream so big that it seems unbelievable.
- Most people will dismiss an unbelievable vision, but some will persevere.
- Belief and perseverance can turn an unbelievable dream into reality.
