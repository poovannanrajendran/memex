## Summary
This video introduces Hyperframes, an open-sourced framework by HeyGen that allows users to generate professional MP4 videos by writing HTML. The tool, designed for AI agents like Claude Code and Gemini CLI, can create videos in minutes using simple prompts and costs very little to run. The presenter suggests this technology enables the creation of a digital business focused on video production for clients needing product videos or social media content.

## Key claims
- Hyperframes can render HTML into professional MP4 videos.
- The process of creating videos with Hyperframes takes only minutes.
- Hyperframes is specifically built for AI agents and supports tools like Claude Code, Cursor, and Gemini CLI.
- Hyperframes is open-sourced, free, and uses the Apache 2 license.
- This technology can be used to build a digital business by offering video creation services to clients.
