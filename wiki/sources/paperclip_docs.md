## Summary
Paperclip is presented as a control plane designed for companies that leverage AI for their operations. It allows users to hire AI agents, assign them tasks, and provides a mechanism to approve more critical or risky tasks. The documentation serves as a guide, offering a quickstart option and access to various sections for deeper understanding.

## Key claims
- Paperclip acts as a central control plane for AI-driven businesses.
- Users can hire and manage AI agents through Paperclip.
- The platform enables task delegation and oversight.
- It includes a process for approving high-risk tasks.
- Documentation is available for quick starts and detailed exploration.
