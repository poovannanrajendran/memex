## Summary
This video review explores used electric cars offering long range (around 300 miles or more) for under £20,000, with mileage generally under 80,000. It highlights several models, including the Tesla Model 3, BMW i4, Hyundai Kona Electric, Kia Niro EV, Kia Soul EV Max, Hyundai Ioniq 5, VW ID3, Tesla Model S, Polestar 2, Ford Mustang Mach-E, and Skoda Enyaq, discussing their pros, cons, and typical pricing for good value.

## Key claims
- Used electric cars with long range can be found for under £20,000.
- The Tesla Model 3 (2019-2020) offers significant value and was ahead of its time.
- The BMW i4 is a brilliant car with fantastic range, though potentially slightly over budget.
- Hyundai Kona Electric and Kia Niro EV offer excellent value in a slightly more SUV-like package than the Model 3.
- The Hyundai Ioniq 5 and VW ID3 are strong contenders with good range and modern features, though early software issues have largely been resolved.
- Older Tesla Model S vehicles (2015-2016) still offer luxury, performance, and practicality.
- Polestar 2 and Ford Mustang Mach-E offer good range and performance, with software updates addressing early issues.
- Skoda Enyaq is a practical family EV with good range and build quality, preferred by the reviewer over its VW counterparts from the same era.
