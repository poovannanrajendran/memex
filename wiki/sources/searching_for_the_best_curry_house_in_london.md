## Summary
This YouTube short follows a food reviewer's quest to find the best curry house in London. The review highlights Lahore Kebab House, an established Pakistani curry house founded in 1995, praising its sensational mixed grill and game-changing naan breads, particularly the Keema naan. The reviewer emphasizes the bustling atmosphere and the quality of the food, describing the meats as soft and tender with deep spices.

## Key claims
- Lahore Kebab House is an institution among London's curry houses, established in 1995.
- The mixed grill at Lahore Kebab House, featuring lamb chops, tandoori chicken, and seekh kebabs, is sensational.
- The naan breads at Lahore Kebab House are game-changing, with the Keema naan being the best the reviewer has ever tasted.
- The restaurant has a bustling atmosphere, particularly on Saturday evenings.
