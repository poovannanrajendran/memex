## Summary
A customer attempted to cancel a $32,000 watch purchase on eBay due to forgetting a discount code. The seller, a small business, offered to refund the discount amount ($700). The customer suggested receiving a $700 piece of jewelry instead. The seller sent an aquamarine and diamond ring, which the customer's wife loved. This positive interaction led the customer to make another purchase, appreciating the seller's customer-centric approach.

## Key claims
- A $32,000 watch purchase was nearly cancelled due to a forgotten eBay discount code.
