## Summary
A developer built an AI-powered tool that automates the entire job hunting process. This tool, developed using Claude Code, analyzes job descriptions against a user's CV, provides a scoring, generates tailored resumes, and even offers interview preparation with star stories based on experience. The developer successfully used this tool to evaluate numerous job offers and secure a Head of Applied AI position.
