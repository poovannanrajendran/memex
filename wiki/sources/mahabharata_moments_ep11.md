## Summary
Episode 11 serves as the season finale for Season 1 of Mahabharata Moments. It reflects on the first ten episodes, summarizing key stories of the Pandavas and Krishna that illustrate themes of strategic brilliance, honesty, resilience, and the quiet burden of wisdom. It sets the stage for Season 2, which will explore the ancestry of the Kuru dynasty.

## Key claims
- Krishna’s strategy defeated Jarasandha, proving wit triumphs over strength.
- Yudhishthira’s honesty was established early through Dronacharya’s seed test.
- Bhima’s resilience was shown through his poisoning and the defeat of Bakasura.
- Arjuna’s mastery came through nighttime discipline and compassion.
- Nakula demonstrated empathy through his care for horses and daring devotion to family.
- Sahadeva’s foresight is tempered by the restraint of fate.
- The epic is built on the interplay of dharma, duty, and destiny.
