## Summary
This YouTube automation workflow describes a method for creating faceless content efficiently. It involves finding a popular video in a niche, using a tool like Shadow Script to respin the content into a new script with viral elements, and then employing an AI video generator like Sylli.io to produce and schedule videos. The process allows for an entire month's worth of content to be scheduled in just two days.

## Key claims
- A YouTube automation workflow can generate a month's worth of faceless content in two days.
- Shadow Script can respin existing viral videos into new, original scripts while retaining viral elements.
- Sylli.io can be used to generate videos from scripts and schedule them up to two weeks in advance.
- This workflow adheres to YouTube's guidelines.
