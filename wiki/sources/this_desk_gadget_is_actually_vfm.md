## Summary
This video reviews an affordable desk gadget that significantly expands iPhone storage and functionality. The device allows users to insert an SSD for an additional 512GB of storage, enabling ProRes video recording. It also features SD and Micro SD card slots, a fast-charging USB-C port, and a headphone jack, all of which work simultaneously. Additionally, it supports Apple Watch charging and monitor connectivity, and is compatible with Android devices. The gadget is highlighted as excellent value for money, costing only 12,000 rupees (excluding the SSD).
