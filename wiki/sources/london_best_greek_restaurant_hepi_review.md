## Summary
This YouTube video reviews Hepi, a Greek restaurant in London, purported to be the best in the city. The reviewer highlights the restaurant's electric atmosphere, custom-built grill from 1966, and the owner Costa. The review showcases various dishes, including handmade Taziki, exceptionally large scallops, butterfied prawns in garlic butter, and the signature Perennium Mountain milkfed lamb, which sells out daily due to high demand. The lamb is praised for its crispy skin and tender, fall-apart meat.

## Key claims
- Hepi is considered by many to be the best Greek restaurant in London.
- The restaurant has a vibrant atmosphere and a custom-built grill from 1966.
- The Taziki is handmade by the owner, Costa.
- The scallops served are unusually large, handpicked by divers.
- The Perennium Mountain milkfed lamb is a highly sought-after dish that sells out daily.
