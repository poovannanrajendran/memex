## Summary
LTX 2.3 is a new, free, and open-source AI model capable of generating 4K cinematic videos with synchronized audio, sound effects, and music from text and images. It offers a completely free alternative to paid models like Veo 3, Cling, and Sora. Additionally, LTX Desktop is available as a free video editor that allows users to download models, generate videos, and edit them locally and offline, depending on hardware capabilities.

## Key claims

- LTX 2.3 is an alternative to paid video generation models such as Veo 3, Cling, and Sora.
