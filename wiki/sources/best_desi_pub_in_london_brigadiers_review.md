## Summary
This YouTube short reviews Brigadiers, a London-based Indian restaurant owned by the JKS Restaurants group. The reviewer highlights several dishes, including a cheese and onion bhaji, fried chicken lollipops, goat tikka buns, and sizzling meat sharing platters featuring guinea fowl kebabs, chicken, and lamb chops.
