## Summary
This video demonstrates how to prepare a street-style Paneer Cheese Dosa, a popular Indian breakfast or snack. The recipe involves creating a flavourful paneer filling with onions, tomatoes, and spices, which is then spread onto a dosa. The dosa is topped with cheese, folded, and cooked until the cheese is melted and gooey, resulting in a delicious and visually appealing dish.

## Key claims
- Paneer Cheese Dosa is a delicious street-style dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast or as a snack.
- The filling is made by sautéing onions, garlic, green chilies, and tomatoes with a blend of Indian spices and grated paneer.
- Cheese, a combination of mozzarella and cheddar, is generously used both inside and on top of the paneer filling to enhance richness and taste.
- The final dosa is cooked until the cheese is completely melted, creating a gooey and desirable texture.
- The paneer cheese dosa can be served as is or with a side dish like coconut chutney.
