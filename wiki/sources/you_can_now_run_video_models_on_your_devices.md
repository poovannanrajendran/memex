## Summary
This video introduces LTX 2.3, a new AI video generation model that can be run locally on personal devices like PCs and MacBooks. This offers an alternative to expensive subscription-based online AI video tools, providing users with greater control, no usage limits, and improved stability and output quality. The shift from renting computational power online to owning it locally is highlighted as a significant change for content creators, agencies, and those monetising AI.

## Key claims
- AI video generation can now be performed locally on personal devices using models like LTX 2.3, eliminating the need for expensive monthly subscriptions.
- Running AI models locally gives users ownership of computational power, avoiding limitations and throttling common with online tools.
- LTX 2.3 is an improved version of the popular open-source model LTX2, developed based on creator feedback for better output, control, and stability.
- This local AI video model represents a new infrastructure for content creation, impacting cost, speed, and control for users.
- Many individuals and businesses may overlook this shift, missing out on its benefits until it's too late.
