## Summary
This video provides first impressions of the new iPhone 16 and 16 Pro models. The standard models receive aesthetic upgrades, improved Ceramic Shield, and a redesigned camera layout for spatial video recording, along with the action button and a new camera control button. The Pro models boast larger displays with slimmer bezels, enhanced camera sensors for 4K 120fps video and better macro photography, and a new a18 Pro chip. A significant focus is placed on Apple Intelligence features, though their delayed release and current capabilities are discussed.

## Key claims
- The iPhone 16 and 16 Plus feature a new ultramarine color, retain the iPhone 15's screen sizes, and have an upgraded 50% stronger Ceramic Shield.
- The standard iPhone 16 models now have a vertical camera layout to enable spatial video recording for Apple Vision Pro.
- The action button and a new camera control button are introduced on the standard iPhone 16, with the latter offering advanced camera adjustments and a visual intelligence feature similar to Google Lens.
- The iPhone 16 models feature the A18 chip, offering significant performance boosts in CPU, graphics, and machine learning.
- Apple Intelligence is integrated across iOS, offering features like email summarization, photo cleanup, and an improved Siri, but will be released later in the year.
- The iPhone 16 Pro models have larger displays (6.3-inch and 6.9-inch) with slimmer bezels, an updated A18 Pro chip, and significantly upgraded camera systems.
- The Pro models can now shoot 4K video at 120fps, allowing for slow-motion playback, and the ultra-wide camera is upgraded to 48MP.
- The smaller iPhone 16 Pro now includes the five times optical zoom camera previously exclusive to the Pro Max, utilizing a tetra prism design.
- The reviewer expresses mixed feelings about the iPhone 16 generation, noting a reliance on Apple Intelligence but questioning its current groundbreaking nature and the delayed release of hardware features.
