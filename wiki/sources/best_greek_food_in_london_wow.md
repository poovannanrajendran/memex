## Summary
This YouTube Short follows a food enthusiast's visit to Helppi, a popular Greek restaurant in London known for its electric atmosphere and authentic cuisine. The video highlights the custom-built grill, the owner Costa, and dishes like large scops, butterflied prawns in garlic butter, and the highly sought-after perum mountain milkfed lamb, which sells out daily due to high demand and the butcher's inability to keep up.

## Key claims
- Helppi has an electric atmosphere with queues out the door on a Tuesday night.
- The restaurant's grill was custom-built by hand in 1966, the year it opened.
- Princess Diana and Richard Branson have visited Helppi.
- The scops served at Helppi are huge and taste amazing.
- The butterflied prawns in garlic butter are a popular dish.
- The perum mountain milkfed lamb is the star of the show, with 80 portions served daily and selling out due to high demand.
