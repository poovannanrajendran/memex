## Summary
This video discusses strategies for asking for a change in job roles or teams, focusing on effective communication and skill development. It emphasizes the importance of not just having knowledge but also knowing how to present it, developing the right attitude, and possessing the wisdom to know when and how to make a request. The dialogue highlights different scenarios and provides advice on how to approach a manager or senior for a desired change, particularly for career advancement.

## Key claims
- To achieve a desired outcome, one must decide what to say and how to say it.
- Effective communication requires a combination of knowledge, skill, and attitude.
- Asking for a change, especially for promotion, needs careful consideration of the right timing and approach.
- Handling senior team members requires more skill than managing junior ones.
- Wisdom is crucial in deciding whether to voice a request or not.
