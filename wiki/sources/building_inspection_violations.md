## Summary
A detailed account of an IT professional discovering massive safety and legal violations in a supposedly "finished" commercial building. After experiencing poor working conditions (no AC, ongoing construction), the narrator identified missing building permits and non-functional fire safety systems. A city inspection led to the entire building being declared unsafe and the successful termination of a 5-year lease.

## Key claims
- The building was rented out without valid occupancy approval or building permits for major renovations.
- Fire safety systems (sprinklers, alarms) were non-functional or disconnected.
- The landlord attempted to hide defects with temporary, suspicious repairs.
- Regulatory intervention (City Inspector, Fire Department) is the most effective recourse for unsafe working environments.
