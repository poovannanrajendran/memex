## Summary
This video details a t-shirt printing business owner's experience receiving and evaluating a sample of acid wash t-shirts from a supplier in Tiruppur. The owner discusses the quality of the 240 GSM pure cotton t-shirts, noting that while the GSM and material were good, the acid wash finish was too intense. The supplier charged ₹100 for the sample, and the owner then proceeded to place an order for t-shirt printing.

## Key claims
- A t-shirt supplier from Tiruppur manufactures acid wash t-shirts in 240 GSM.
- The sample t-shirts were made of pure cotton and were 240 GSM.
- The acid wash on the sample t-shirts was too intense.
- The supplier charged ₹100 for the sample t-shirt.
- The business owner proceeded to place an order after evaluating the sample.
