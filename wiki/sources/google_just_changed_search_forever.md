## Summary
Google has launched an AI mode integrated into its search engine, initially in India, aiming to consolidate various AI functionalities into a single platform. This new feature provides structured, real-time answers drawing from Google's knowledge graph and user data, offering personalized suggestions and remembering conversational context for follow-up questions. It also integrates capabilities similar to Google Lens, allowing users to identify objects via camera and receive instructions.

## Key claims
- Google's new AI mode consolidates multiple AI functionalities into a single search interface.
- The AI mode provides structured answers using real-time data and personalized suggestions from Google's knowledge graph.
- It supports follow-up questions, maintaining context for personalized responses.
- Integration with Google Lens allows for image-based queries and instructions.
- The AI mode is powered by Gemini 2.5 and Google's extensive user data.
- The AI mode will be free in India, contrasting with paid alternatives.
- Google is embedding popular AI app features directly into Android and Google Search.
