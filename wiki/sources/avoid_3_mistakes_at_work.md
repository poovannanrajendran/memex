## Summary
The video outlines three critical mistakes to avoid at work to become irreplaceable. Firstly, it advises against faking busyness, emphasizing the importance of delivering high-quality, beautiful work over mere speed, as AI can replicate fast but not necessarily beautiful work. Secondly, it suggests a strategy of undercommitting and overdelivering by taking time to analyse and plan effectively. Finally, it cautions against oversharing personal information in the workplace, recommending that employees share only what is necessary to maintain professional boundaries and facilitate leave requests.

## Key claims
- Do not act busy if you are not; focus on the quality and beauty of your work to become irreplaceable.
- Undercommit and overdeliver by taking time to analyse and plan your work effectively.
- Limit sharing personal information at work to maintain professional boundaries and manage leave requests discreetly.
