## Summary
This video demonstrates the installation process for the Thermalright Phantom Spirit 120 SE air cooler on various CPU sockets including AM5, AM4, and LGA 1700. It covers preparing the mounting hardware, attaching the brackets to the motherboard, applying thermal paste, and finally securing the cooler and its fans.

## Key claims
- The installation process involves mounting brackets, screws, and securing the heatsink.
- Thermal paste application is crucial and should cover the entire CPU surface.
- The fans can be attached and adjusted after the main heatsink is secured.
