## Summary
Flowep is an AI design assistant that transforms text-based ideas into professional UI designs, including wireframes and user flows, within seconds. It demonstrates the potential for AI to simplify the design process to a conversational level, although it may not fully replicate the unique creative vision of human designers.

## Key claims
- Flowep can generate complete UI designs, wireframes, and user flows from simple text prompts in seconds.
- The AI understands user intent to create detailed design outputs.
- While efficient, Flowep may lack the unique creative vision and brand personality of experienced human designers.
