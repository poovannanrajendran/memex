## Summary
This video highlights five AI tools that can automate key business functions. It covers tools for research (Manus), website and app building (Replit), sales lead generation and cold emailing (Apollo), content creation (HeyGen and 11 Labs), marketing visual generation (Midjourney), and learning automation (Google AI Studio).

## Key claims
- AI tools can automate significant parts of a business.
- Manus is a general-purpose AI agent for scaled research.
- Replit enables rapid website and app development.
- Apollo automates sales by generating lead lists based on Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).
- HeyGen and 11 Labs can automate content creation without direct camera presence.
- Midjourney can generate marketing visuals from text descriptions.
- Google AI Studio facilitates automated learning through screen sharing.
