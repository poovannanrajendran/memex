## Summary
This is a review of the Tamil movie "DNA," starring Atharvaa. The reviewer found the movie to be a crime thriller that starts strong with good tension but is then bogged down by an unnecessary item song and unrelated fight sequences. Despite these pacing issues, the reviewer appreciated the detailed investigation elements, the corporate villain's portrayal, and the spiritual undertones added by director Nelson.

## Key claims
- The movie starts as a good crime thriller with engaging tension.
- An unnecessary item song and unrelated fight scenes disrupt the flow.
- The portrayal of the corporate villain and the investigation were strong points.
- Director Nelson added interesting details, including hospital scenes and spiritual connections.
- Despite pacing issues, the film concludes satisfactorily by resolving the plot threads.
