## Summary
This video demonstrates a recipe for an eggplant curry inspired by Sri Lankan cuisine. The process involves salting the eggplant to draw out water, charring it, and then adding aromatics like garlic and ginger, spices such as chili and curry powder, and tomato paste for depth of flavour. The dish is described as one of the best the creator has ever made.
