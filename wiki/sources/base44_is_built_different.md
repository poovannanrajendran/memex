## Summary
Base44 is an AI app builder that distinguishes itself from no-code platforms by offering a fully integrated solution. Unlike competitors that require manual setup of databases, email systems, and storage, Base44 has these components preconfigured and built-in. It utilises 'vibe coding', where users describe their app idea in natural language, and the AI generates the complete application, including complex back-end logic, with one-click deployment.

## Key claims
- Base44 solves the "integration hell" problem common in no-code platforms by having all essential components (database, email, analytics, storage) built-in and preconfigured.
- Base44 employs "vibe coding", enabling users to build applications solely by describing their idea in natural language.
- The AI in Base44 understands user intent and translates it into functional apps with complex back-end logic, unlike competitors requiring manual database and workflow configuration.
- Base44 allows for rapid deployment of apps from idea to production in minutes, with one-click hosting on enterprise infrastructure.
- Wix acquired Base44 due to its novel approach to app development, which reimagines the process as explaining an idea rather than manual building.
