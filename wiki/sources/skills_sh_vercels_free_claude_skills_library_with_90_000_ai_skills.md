## Summary
Skills.Sh, a new library from Vercel, offers over 90,000 free AI skills that act as 'superpowers' for AI models like Claude. These skills enable users to instantly transform their AI into specialists for various tasks such as UI design, app testing, and content writing, eliminating the need to repeatedly write complex prompts. The skills can be integrated into multiple AI code editors, including anti-gravity, codeex, and the Claude desktop app, by simply installing them via a command in the terminal.
