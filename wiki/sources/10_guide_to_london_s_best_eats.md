## Summary
This YouTube video provides a guide to affordable and delicious eats in London, focusing on places where you can get a meal for £10 or less. It highlights several establishments, including Bus Cafe for a hearty breakfast, Lanzhou for hand-pulled noodles in Chinatown, Dwat for a loaded kebab naan, Lamar Chu for cheesecake, and Fosdakb for cheap shawarma. The video also features Bread Store for pizza and Roll Boys for fresh cinnamon buns.

## Key claims
- Bus Cafe offers a full English breakfast with double portions for £6.
- Lanzhou in Chinatown serves hand-pulled noodles and is open until 5:00 a.m.
- Dwat in Tooting provides a loaded kebab naan for £2.49.
- Fosdakb in North London offers the cheapest shawarma in London for £3.
- Bread Store sells large, unique pizza slices for £7.
- Roll Boys bakes fresh cinnamon buns, with a popular bacon and maple syrup flavour.
