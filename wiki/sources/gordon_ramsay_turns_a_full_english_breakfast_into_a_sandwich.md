## Summary
Chef Gordon Ramsay demonstrates how to create an elevated breakfast sandwich using a full English breakfast as inspiration. The process involves cooking sausages, bacon, and tomatoes in a cast iron pan, finishing them in the oven, and frying eggs with special seasonings and sauces. The cooked components are then layered between slices of bread to form a decadent sandwich.

## Key claims
- A perfect breakfast sandwich can be made by layering traditional full English breakfast ingredients.
- Cooking tomatoes in the rendered fat from sausages and bacon enhances their flavour.
- Finishing the sausages and tomatoes in the oven after searing improves their texture and flavour.
- Frying eggs with specific seasonings like pepper, salt, chili flakes, and Sriracha adds a unique flavour profile.
- The bread does not require butter as it can absorb flavour from the other ingredients, especially the eggs.
