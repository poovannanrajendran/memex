## Summary
This motivational piece advocates for focusing on action over excessive communication. It suggests that talking too much can stem from a need for external approval, which hinders progress. The core message is to let your results speak for themselves, keeping your efforts private until delivery to maintain an aura of power and effectiveness.

## Key claims
- Excessive talking can be a sign of seeking external validation, which is detrimental to progress.
- Silence and focused action are more powerful than explanations.
- Delivering results without explaining the process is a key to effectiveness and maintaining an advantage.
