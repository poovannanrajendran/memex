## Summary
This YouTube video highlights five essential AI tools that can significantly benefit small businesses by enhancing productivity and efficiency. The author shares personal daily usage of these tools, emphasizing their value and wishing for earlier adoption. The featured tools range from research assistants and internal AI tool development platforms to AI chat systems for sales, virtual team assistants, and advanced video generation models.

## Key claims
- NotebookLM by Google acts as a real-time research assistant, capable of processing various types of information like videos and documents.
- GumLoop enables teams, including finance and HR, to build AI tools without specialized training or external support.
- Revio is an AI chat system that analyses conversations and provides guidance on what to say during sales processes.
- Deli allows teams to query it for information on how to perform tasks, reducing the need for direct human availability.
- A nom is an advanced video generation model with future applications in attending meetings or giving talks virtually.
