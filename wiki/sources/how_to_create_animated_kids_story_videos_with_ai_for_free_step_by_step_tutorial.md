## Summary
This tutorial demonstrates how to create animated kids' story videos entirely for free using readily available AI tools. It guides users through generating a story and image prompts with ChatGPT, creating high-quality images with Leonardo AI, animating these images into 3D videos with Hiyoo AI, and finally editing everything together with voiceovers using CapCut and 11 Labs. The process is presented as accessible even for beginners, with a focus on leveraging free tiers and trial periods to produce engaging content for platforms like YouTube.

## Key claims
- Animated kids' story videos can be created for free using AI tools.
- ChatGPT can be used to generate story ideas, full stories with scene breakdowns, and detailed image prompts.
- Leonardo AI can generate high-quality images from prompts, with a 'seed' feature to maintain visual consistency.
- Hiyoo AI can animate static images into 3D videos, offering a free trial period for unlimited use.
- CapCut is a suitable free tool for editing the animated video clips and integrating voiceovers.
- 11 Labs provides a text-to-speech tool that can be used to generate voiceovers from scripts.
- The entire process, from story to final export, can be achieved without initial financial investment.
