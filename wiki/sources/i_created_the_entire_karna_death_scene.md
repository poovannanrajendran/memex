## Summary
This video details the creation of a scene depicting Karna's death from the Mahabharata using AI tools. The process involved researching and rewriting the narrative, generating a screenplay using an AI co-pilot, defining a visual style with a desaturated colour palette, creating hyperrealistic character models for Karna, Arjuna, and Krishna, and then generating video clips from these models and prompts. The final steps included assembling the clips, adding sound design elements like battle ambience and music, and editing everything within an AI-powered editor to create a cohesive and dramatic sequence.

## Key claims
- The entire Kurukshetra death scene of Karna from the Mahabharata was recreated using AI tools.
- The process involved five key steps: research and screenplay generation, colour tone logging, character creation, image and video generation, and final editing with sound design.
- Morphic, an AI filmmaking tool, was central to the creative process, alongside other AI models and tools like Copilot and Canvas.
- Detailed prompts were used to guide the AI in generating character appearances, actions, and even dialogue synchronization.
- Sound design and music composition were integrated within the AI editor to enhance the scene's dramatic impact.
