## Summary
A Chinese college student developed an AI named Miroofish that predicts future trends by simulating a digital world with intelligent agents interacting and evolving. This project, open-sourced on GitHub, gained significant traction and attracted a $4 million investment from a tech billionaire, transforming the student's graduation project into a startup.

## Key claims
- An AI named Miroofish can predict future trends.
- Miroofish simulates a digital world where intelligent agents interact and evolve.
- The project was open-sourced on GitHub and achieved significant popularity.
- Miroofish received a $4 million investment shortly after a demo video was shown.
- The AI was developed in 10 days using Claude code.
