## Summary
This YouTube video demonstrates how to build a no-code AI research agent in minutes. The agent utilizes OpenRouter to connect to various AI models, serving as its 'brain,' and integrates with Perplexity, a powerful research tool, to conduct in-depth research on user-provided queries. The process involves selecting AI models from a range of providers via OpenRouter and then configuring Perplexity as a research tool. The agent can then perform detailed research, such as on sleep optimization, delivering comprehensive reports with cited sources.

## Key claims
- An AI research agent can be built quickly using no-code tools.
- OpenRouter acts as a central hub to connect to various AI models, providing the 'brain' for the agent.
- Perplexity serves as an advanced research tool, akin to a 'ChatGPT research tool on steroids'.
- The AI agent can perform deep research, citing multiple sources, and generate comprehensive reports.
- Research tasks can vary in complexity and time, with deep research taking longer than basic web searches.
