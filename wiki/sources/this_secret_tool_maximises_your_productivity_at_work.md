## Summary
This video introduces Scribe, a browser extension and desktop app designed to maximise productivity at work. Scribe allows users to capture processes by performing the steps themselves, automatically generating a guide with screenshots and text documentation. This eliminates the need for one-on-one explanations and provides easily shareable guides for colleagues.

## Key claims
- Scribe can significantly increase productivity at work.
- Scribe automates the creation of process documentation with screenshots and text.
- Using Scribe reduces the need for one-on-one training and support.
- Scribe offers both private and public sharing options for generated guides.
