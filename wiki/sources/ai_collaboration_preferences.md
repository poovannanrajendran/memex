## Summary
A foundational document from Poovi's grounding pack detailing his preferred style, technical stack, and workflow when collaborating with AI agents. It emphasizes British English, structured deliverables, and a "95% clarity before building" philosophy.

## Key claims
- Always use British English (organise, analyse).
- Architecture and 95% clarity are required before implementation.
- AI should act as a multi-role partner (Architect, PM, EA, DevOps).
- Avoid end-of-response summaries and unnecessary caveats.
- Context continuity across sessions is expected.
