## Summary
Toolify is a new platform designed to consolidate access to all AI tools and their corresponding websites in one central location. It categorises tools for various functions such as writing, image generation, audio, video, coding, legal, and finance. A significant benefit is that many of these tools are available for free. Toolify is also updated daily with newly launched AI tools, making it a dynamic resource.

## Key claims
- Toolify provides unified access to all AI tools and their websites.
- It categorises AI tools by function (writing, images, audio, video, coding, legal, finance).
- Many AI tools listed on Toolify are free to access.
- Toolify is updated daily with new AI tool launches.
- It serves as a comprehensive resource hub for AI tools.
