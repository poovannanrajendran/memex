## Summary
This video reviews the Nvidia Jetson Orin Nano SUPER development kit, highlighting its compact form factor and impressive AI performance for its price. The reviewer explores its capabilities beyond AI, including basic desktop use, running Linux, and even emulating PS3 games using the RPCS3 emulator. The Orin Nano SUPER offers enhanced TDP and memory bandwidth compared to its predecessor, with a significantly lower price point, making it a more accessible option for developers and enthusiasts.

- The Nvidia Jetson Orin Nano SUPER offers significantly improved performance over the original Orin Nano, especially in AI tasks, due to increased TDP and memory bandwidth.

- The Orin Nano SUPER is capable of basic desktop usage, running Linux, and even emulating PS3 games with the RPCS3 emulator.
