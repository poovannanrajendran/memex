## Summary
Photographer Doug Mills captured a striking image during a recent assassination attempt on Donald Trump. While Trump was delivering a speech in Pennsylvania, a gunman opened fire. Mills, working for the New York Times, managed to photograph the exact moment a bullet passed by Trump's head. Analysis of the photo's EXIF data revealed it was taken with a Sony A1 camera and a Sony 24mm f1.4 G Master lens, with a shutter speed of 1/18000th of a second, allowing for the capture of the fast-moving projectile. The image was slightly cropped to enhance the visibility of the bullet.
