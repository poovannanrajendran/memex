## Summary
A report by Forrester highlights that only 15% of enterprises achieve measurable ROI from AI agent deployments, with the majority struggling to translate AI-generated insights into actionable business outcomes. The report defines the 'agentic action gap' and provides a framework for bridging this divide by reducing friction, improving orchestration, and moving towards an agent-centric operating model.

## Key claims
- Only 15% of enterprises see measurable ROI from AI agent deployments.
- The majority of AI agent initiatives fail to move beyond insight generation to actual business action.
- There is a significant 'agentic action gap' between AI insights and value-driving business activities.
- Bridging this gap requires measuring friction, reducing time to action, introducing orchestration, and adopting an agent-centric operating model.
