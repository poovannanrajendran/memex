## Summary
This video by Brian Tracy outlines a seven-step process for achieving any goal. It emphasizes the importance of clear goal setting, writing goals down, and developing a plan of action. Tracy also highlights the need for persistence, dedication, and a positive attitude throughout the goal achievement journey.

## Key claims
- Clearly define your goals in writing.
- Break down large goals into smaller, manageable steps.
- Develop a detailed action plan.
- Be persistent and dedicated to your goals.
- Maintain a positive mental attitude.
- Take consistent action towards your goals.
- Review your progress regularly and adjust your plan as needed.
